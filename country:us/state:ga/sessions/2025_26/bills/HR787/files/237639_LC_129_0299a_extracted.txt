Section 1:
25 LC 129 0299
House Resolution 787
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Jessica West-Royal, Pepperell High School's 2025 STAR Teacher; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, Jessica West-Royal has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the
6 vital role that she has played in the teaching profession and her deep personal commitment
7 to the education of the students of Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, Jessica West-Royal has earned distinction for teaching excellence and has been
9 honored as Pepperell High School's 2025 STAR Teacher; and
10 WHEREAS, in recognition of her contributions to the education and welfare of young people
11 in this state, it is abundantly fitting and proper for this body to honor the extraordinary
12 accomplishments and academic achievements of this outstanding educator.
H. R. 787
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0299
13 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
14 the members of this body commend Jessica West-Royal, Pepperell High School's 2025
15 STAR Teacher, and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
17 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
18 Jessica West-Royal.
H. R. 787
