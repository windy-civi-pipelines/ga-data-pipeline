25 LC 112 2668
House Resolution 24
By: Representative Cheokas of the 151st
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the Corporate Volunteer Council (CVC) of Atlanta supports member companies
3 in achieving business results through its strategic commitment to civic engagement and
4 social impact; and
5 WHEREAS, founded in 1992, the Corporate Volunteer Council of Atlanta is the only CVC
6 in the nation recognized three times as CVC of the Year by Points of Light, a nonpartisan
7 international social impact organization; and
8 WHEREAS, the CVC of Atlanta is currently led by Yvonne Whitaker of the NCR
9 Foundation as President, Brittany Church of Ryan LLC as Vice President, Kat Reynolds of
10 Veritiv as Secretary, Geneva Hall-Shelton of IHG Hotels and Resorts as Treasurer, and
11 Toccara Arnold Alam as Community Volunteer, all of whom bring diverse expertise and
12 unwavering dedication to corporate excellence; and
13 WHEREAS, members of the CVC benefit from forums that focus on best practices for
14 corporate community engagement and gain access to educational programming, networking,
15 and leadership development opportunities; and
H. R. 24
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2668
16 WHEREAS, the CVC further provides annual benefits such as industry-specific interest
17 groups, access to nonprofit partnerships, and recognition opportunities through the IMPACT
18 Awards and other press features; and
19 WHEREAS, the CVC membership comprises over 90 businesses, including prominent
20 corporations such as Delta Air Lines, Deloitte, and Amazon, alongside nonprofit
21 organizations like United Way of Greater Atlanta; and
22 WHEREAS, the CVC of Atlanta continues to inspire companies to achieve meaningful social
23 impact and professional growth through their commitment to volunteerism and community
24 engagement.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body commend the Corporate Volunteer Council of Atlanta for its
27 longstanding dedication to businesses operating in the State of Georgia and beyond as well
28 as extend best wishes for continued success.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
30 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
31 Corporate Volunteer Council of Atlanta.
H. R. 24
