25 LC 129 0141
Senate Resolution 168
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Jeffrey Cohen; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Jeffrey Cohen is retiring from a long career of public accounting and personal
3 financial planning, representing a significant milestone in his distinguished career; and
4 WHEREAS, he began his career in the accounting industry with a degree in accounting and
5 finance from the University of South Florida; and
6 WHEREAS, upon obtaining his accounting degree, Jeffrey Cohen joined the firm of Metcalf,
7 Frix & Company, where he served as tax manager; and
8 WHEREAS, he embarked thereafter on an entrepreneurial journey to co-found Bromberg &
9 Cohen, PC, with his partner, Sam Bromberg, which provided accounting and tax services to
10 individuals in Atlanta and the surrounding areas; and
11 WHEREAS, he embarked on another entrepreneurial journey to co-found Nova Wealth
12 Management Group LLC, providing personal financial planning to individuals in Atlanta and
13 the surrounding areas; and
S. R. 168
- 1 -

14 WHEREAS, he sold this business to Buckingham Strategic Wealth, making sure the
15 individuals he helped could be served for years to come; and
16 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of
17 Jeffrey Cohen be appropriately honored.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
19 recognize and commend Jeffrey Cohen, CPA/PFS, for his exemplary service and extend best
20 wishes for his continued success in all future endeavors.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
22 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Jeffrey Cohen.
