25 LC 39 4658
House Resolution 427
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life of Dr. Esco Hall, Jr., and dedicating a road in his memory; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Dr. Esco Hall, Jr., was born into a sharecropping family in Wheeler County,
4 Georgia, and graduated as valedictorian of his 1966 class at Wheeler County High School;
5 and
6 WHEREAS, Dr. Hall's love of agriculture began at a young age through his involvement
7 with the New Farmer's Association and Future Farmer's Association, leading him to become
8 one of the first two black students to graduate with a doctorate in veterinary medicine from
9 the University of Georgia in 1973; and
10 WHEREAS, he served counties across southeast Georgia as a large and small animal
11 veterinarian for over 46 years as the sole proprietor of Appling Animal Hospital; and
12 WHEREAS, Dr. Hall made history once again in 1982 by being elected the first black city
13 councilmember of Baxley City Council, a post he held for 36 years until his untimely passing
14 in 2019; and
H. R. 427
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4658
15 WHEREAS, he diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of his time,
16 talents, and energy toward the betterment of his community and state as evidenced
17 dramatically by his superlative service as mayor pro tem for the last 12 years of his service
18 on the city council; and
19 WHEREAS, Dr. Hall embodied the spirit of service and it is abundantly fitting and proper
20 that this remarkable and distinguished Georgian be recognized appropriately by dedicating
21 a road in his memory.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
23 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of State Route 15 from mile point 76.17 to
24 76.716 in Appling County is dedicated as the Dr. Esco Hall, Jr. Highway.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
26 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Dr. Esco Hall, Jr. Highway.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
29 family of Dr. Esco Hall, Jr., and to the Department of Transportation.
