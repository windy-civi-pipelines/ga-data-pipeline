25 LC 112 3284
House Resolution 535
and Romman of the 97th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Four Corners Primary Care Centers; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Four Corners Primary Care Centers (FCPC) is committed to delivering
3 comprehensive primary health care services across all stages of life, ensuring accessible,
4 high-quality care for the residents of its service area; and
5 WHEREAS, FCPC provides primary care, oral health, mental health, substance abuse
6 counseling, specialty care, pharmacy assistance, enabling services, education, and
7 comprehensive outreach through service delivery locations in Norcross, Lawrenceville, and
8 Hoschton; and
9 WHEREAS, FCPC serves all individuals regardless of language, gender, socio-economic
10 status, physical and mental capacity, age, religion, housing status, or ability to pay, with a
11 particular focus on reaching homeless, low-income, uninsured, underinsured, vulnerable,
12 Medicaid and Medicare patients; and
13 WHEREAS, Gwinnett County faces significant challenges in health care access due in part
14 to a shortage of primary care providers, resulting in poor health status indicators; and
H. R. 535
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3284
15 WHEREAS, the unwavering commitment of FCPC to improving health outcomes and
16 reducing disparities in access to care has made a profound impact on the lives of countless
17 individuals and families in Georgia; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable organization be
19 appropriately honored during this special time.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body commend Four Corners Primary Care Centers for its outstanding
22 efforts to address the critical health care needs of underserved communities and extend best
23 wishes for continued success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Four
26 Corners Primary Care Centers.
