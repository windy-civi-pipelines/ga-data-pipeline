Section 1:
25 LC 47 3490S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 1 of Chapter 7 of Title 44 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to landlords and tenants in general, so as to require certain residential landlords to
3 have in-state staff to manage tenant communications related to such properties; to provide
4 for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Article 1 of Chapter 7 of Title 44 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
8 landlords and tenants in general, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as
9 follows:
10 "44-7-25.
11 Any landlord that is not a resident of this state that owns or operates 25 or more
12 single-family or duplex residential rental properties in this state shall employ a broker
13 licensed pursuant to Chapter 40 of Title 43, that may or may not reside within this state,
14 and at least one person, to be located within this state, who shall be responsible for
15 receiving, coordinating, managing, and responding to communications from tenants of such
16 landlord related to maintenance and other issues related to such properties."
H. B. 399 (SUB)
- 1 -
25 LC 47 3490S
17 SECTION 2.
18 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
