Section 1:
25 LC 50 1023
By: Representatives Williamson of the 112th, Smith of the 138th, Kelley of the 16th, Blackmon
of the 146th, Crowe of the 118th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 7 of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 income taxes, so as to change certain definitions regarding such taxes; to provide for related
3 matters; to provide for an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
5 SECTION 1.
6 Chapter 7 of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to income taxes,
7 is amended in Code Section 48-7-1, relating to definitions regarding such taxes, by revising
8 paragraph (1) as follows:
9 "(1) 'Corporation' includes, but is not limited to, all associations, professional
10 associations organized pursuant to Chapter 10 of Title 14, and insurance companies."
11 SECTION 2.
12 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
13 without such approval.
H. B. 276
- 1 -
25 LC 50 1023
14 SECTION 3.
15 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
