Section 1:
25 LC 112 3539
Senate Resolution 491
22nd, Mangham of the 55th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Cindy Duong as an Ambassador of Morrow in Economic
2 Development; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the City of Morrow, Georgia, is committed to fostering a vibrant and
4 sustainable economic environment that encourages growth, innovation, and community
5 development; and
6 WHEREAS, the success and prosperity of communities in the State of Georgia are
7 significantly influenced by the efforts of outstanding individuals who demonstrate
8 exceptional leadership, dedication, and vision; and
9 WHEREAS, Cindy Duong has consistently exemplified these qualities through her tireless
10 work and commitment to enhancing economic opportunities within the City of Morrow; and
11 WHEREAS, she has played a pivotal role in promoting local businesses, attracting new
12 investments, and advocating for policies that stimulate economic growth while preserving
13 the character and heritage of the community; and
S. R. 491
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3539
14 WHEREAS, her contributions have not only bolstered the city's local economy but have also
15 inspired others to take part in its ongoing development and enrichment; and
16 WHEREAS, the City of Morrow recognizes the importance of collaboration and partnerships
17 in promoting economic development, and Cindy Duong has served as a key link among
18 business leaders, government officials, and community members.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 recognize and commend Cindy Duong as an Ambassador of Morrow in Economic
21 Development as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
23 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Cindy Duong.
