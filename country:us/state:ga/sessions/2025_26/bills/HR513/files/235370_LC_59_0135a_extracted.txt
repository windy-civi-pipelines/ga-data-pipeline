Section 1:
25 LC 59 0135
House Resolution 513
of the 4th, Tarvin of the 2nd, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Encouraging the United States Congress to investigate the dispersion of chemicals into the
2 sky by aircraft, so as to understand the effects of such dispersion on air, soil, and water
3 quality in the State of Georgia; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, it is documented that the federal government and other entities acting on the
5 federal government's behalf or at the federal government's request may conduct
6 geoengineering experiments by intentionally dispersing chemicals into the atmosphere, and
7 those activities may occur within the State of Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, dispersing chemicals into the atmosphere has been observed to leave visible
9 trails in the sky; and
10 WHEREAS, the risk to human health and environmental welfare from broadscale
11 geoengineering that leaves visible trails in the sky is currently not well understood; and
12 WHEREAS, the United States has exclusive sovereignty of airspace of the United States; and
H. R. 513
- 1 -
25 LC 59 0135
13 WHEREAS, it is the intent of the House of Representatives to protect the public health and
14 welfare of Georgians.
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
16 the members of this body encourage the United States Congress to investigate the dispersion
17 of chemicals into the sky by aircraft, so as to understand the effects of such dispersion on air,
18 soil, and water quality.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
20 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
21 members of the Georgia delegation to the United States Congress.
