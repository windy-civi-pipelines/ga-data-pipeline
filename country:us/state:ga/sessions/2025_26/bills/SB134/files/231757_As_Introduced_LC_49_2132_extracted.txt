Section 1:
25 LC 49 2132
By: Senators Sims of the 12th, Jones II of the 22nd, Mallow of the 2nd, Harbison of the 15th,
Lucas of the 26th and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Title 20 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to education, so as to
2 repeal Chapter 2B, relating to promise scholarship accounts, including both Articles 1 and
3 2 of said chapter, relating to the Georgia Education Savings Authority and the "Georgia
4 Promise Scholarship Act," respectively; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Title 20 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to education, is amended by
8 repealing Chapter 2B, relating to promise scholarship accounts, in its entirety.
9 SECTION 2.
10 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 134
