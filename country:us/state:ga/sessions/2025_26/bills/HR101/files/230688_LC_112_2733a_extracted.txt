25 LC 112 2733
House Resolution 101
By: Representative Williamson of the 112th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Kolton Teis Sargent has attained the rank of Eagle Scout, the most
3 distinguished rank conferred by the Boy Scouts of America; and
4 WHEREAS, a candidate for the most exceptional award in scouting must complete a
5 rigorous program of training and demonstrate his proficiency in selected scouting techniques
6 and the attainment of the highest ideals of scouting; and
7 WHEREAS, only one scout out of 97 ever reaches this lofty level of achievement, and
8 Kolton has ably evidenced his aptitude and competency in mastering the various special
9 skills taught through the Scouts BSA program; and
10 WHEREAS, this hard-working young man has demonstrated great leadership skills and
11 initiative and has truly exhibited a commitment to the scouting principles of honesty and
12 fairness during his years with the Scouts BSA program; and
H. R. 101
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2733
13 WHEREAS, a member of Troop 705 in Monroe, Georgia, Kolton earned the rank of Eagle
14 Scout by leading the preservation of a historic spring and the creation of hiking trails at the
15 Union Chapel Church Natural Area; and
16 WHEREAS, over his six years of scouting, he has earned an additional 32 Merit Badges and
17 attended four summer camps at Rainy Mountain in Clayton, Georgia, as well as High
18 Adventure Camps at Florida Sea Base and Philmont Scout Ranch; and
19 WHEREAS, the perseverance and determination displayed by this exceptional young leader
20 set an example for all Georgians, and great success is surely in this young man's future.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body commend Kolton Teis Sargent for his diligence and dedication in
23 pursuing the highest honor awarded by the Boy Scouts of America and congratulate him on
24 attaining the prestigious rank of Eagle Scout.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
27 Kolton Teis Sargent.
H. R. 101
