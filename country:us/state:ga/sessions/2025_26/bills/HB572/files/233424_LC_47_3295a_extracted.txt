Section 1:
25 LC 47 3295
By: Representatives Persinger of the 119th, Gaines of the 120th, Efstration of the 104th,
McCollum of the 30th, and Dunahoo of the 31st
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to provide a new charter for the Town of Braselton, approved June 3, 2003
2 (Ga. L. 2003, p. 4319), so as to annex certain properties into the town; to provide for related
3 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
5 SECTION 1.
6 An Act to provide a new charter for the Town of Braselton, approved June 3, 2003 (Ga. L.
7 2003, p. 4319), is amended by adding a new subsection to Section 1.11 to read as follows:
8 "(d) In addition to the other provisions of this section, the corporate limits of the town shall
9 include the following described properties, as described in the records of the Barrow
10 County Board of Tax Assessors on the effective date of this subsection:
11 Barrow County Parcel ID XX023 013
12 Barrow County Parcel ID XX0025 053
13 Barrow County Parcel ID XX025 054
14 Barrow County Parcel ID XX023 014
15 Barrow County Parcel ID XX025 052
16 Barrow County Parcel ID XX025 051
