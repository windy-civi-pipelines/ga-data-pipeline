25 LC 44 3150/AP
By: Representatives Sainz of the 180th, Townsend of the 179th, and DeLoach of the 167th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act creating a board of commissioners of Glynn County, approved
2 February 11, 1937 (Ga. L. 1937, p. 1336), as amended, particularly by an Act approved
3 April 13, 2001 (Ga. L. 2001, p. 3914), so as to change the annual salary of the chairperson
4 and members of the board; to provide for withholding of compensation for certain meeting
5 absences; to provide for related matters; to provide an effective date; to repeal conflicting
6 laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 An Act creating a board of commissioners of Glynn County, approved February 11, 1937
10 (Ga. L. 1937, p. 1336), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 13, 2001 (Ga. L.
11 2001, p. 3914), is amended by striking the first unnumbered paragraph of Section 6 and
12 inserting in its place the following:
13 "The chairperson shall receive an annual salary of $28,000.00, payable in monthly
14 installments, and an expense allowance of $600.00 per month. The vice chairperson shall
15 receive an annual salary of $27,000.00, payable in monthly installments, and an expense
16 allowance of $600.00 per month. Each other member of the board shall receive an annual

17 salary of $26,000.00, payable in monthly installments, and an expense allowance of
18 $550.00 per month. However, that compensation shall not be paid to the chairperson, vice

20 chairperson, or other member of the board failed to attend at least one regularly scheduled
21 meeting of the board unless the board by majority vote determines that the absence should

23 SECTION 2.
24 This Act shall become effective on July 1, 2025.
