25 LC 39 4524
House Resolution 66
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing Mr. Masen "Mase" Aubrey McIntyre II and dedicating a bridge in his memory;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Mr. Masen "Mase" Aubrey McIntyre II was born on July 9, 2001, in Austell,
4 Georgia, the beloved son of Masen McIntyre and Brandy Drinnon McIntyre; and
5 WHEREAS, Mr. McIntyre graduated from Bowdon High School where he played football
6 and tennis; and
7 WHEREAS, he was a stellar athlete as evidenced by his selection to the All-Region Team
8 for football his senior year; and
9 WHEREAS, his talent and leadership on the football field earned him the title of Player of
10 the Game on numerous occasions as well as being honored with the Golden Helmet Award
11 from his coaches; and
12 WHEREAS, after graduation, Mr. McIntyre joined his father in the family business at M3
13 Logging; and
H. R. 66
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4524
14 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous soul, Mr. McIntyre will long be remembered for
15 his bright smile and his love of family and friendship, and this loyal son and friend will be
16 missed by all who had the great fortune of knowing him; and
17 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
18 Georgian be recognized appropriately by dedicating a bridge in his memory.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
20 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the bridge on State Route 100 over Turkey Creek in Carroll
21 County is dedicated as the Masen "Mase" McIntyre Memorial Bridge.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
23 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Masen "Mase" McIntyre
24 Memorial Bridge.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 family of Mr. Masen "Mase" Aubrey McIntyre II and to the Department of Transportation.
H. R. 66
