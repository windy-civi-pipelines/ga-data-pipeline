15 WHEREAS, upon his return home, Mr. Blackwell continued to serve his country as a rural
16 mail carrier with the United States Postal Service in Lula, Georgia, for 34 years and as an
17 assistant magistrate judge in Banks County for more than 30 years; and
18 WHEREAS, Mr. Blackwell was an active member of Homer Baptist Church, where he was
19 on the Sunday School Celebration Committee, and the American Legion Post #215, Phi
20 Delta Masonic Lodge #148, Banks County Chamber of Commerce, and Banks County Soil
21 Conservation; and
22 WHEREAS, Mr. Blackwell demonstrated selfless service to this nation and his unyielding
23 commitment to protecting the people and ideals of the United States should be recognized
24 appropriately by dedicating a road in his memory.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
26 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of State Route 51 from Hill Street to State
27 Route 164 in Banks County is dedicated as the World War II Veteran Bobby Blackwell
28 Memorial Drive.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
30 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the World War II Veteran Bobby
31 Blackwell Memorial Drive.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
33 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
34 family of Mr. Bobby Blackwell and to the Department of Transportation.
