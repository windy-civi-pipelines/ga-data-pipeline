25 LC 129 0342
House Resolution 687
67th, and Dawson of the 65th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Judge William F. Lee Jr.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Judge William F. Lee Jr. on February 7, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Judge William F. Lee Jr. was born on February 13, 1943, in Fort Bragg, North
5 Carolina, a beloved son of William and Martha Lee; and
6 WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, Judge William F. Lee Jr. was a fifth
7 generation, active member of Central Baptist Church, in Newnan, Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife, Emily, and was blessed with two
9 remarkable children, Emily and Martha; and three wonderful grandchildren, Sarah, Thomas,
10 and Caroline; and
11 WHEREAS, a member of the Georgia Bar since 1967, Judge William F. Lee Jr. left an
12 indelible mark on the legal community; and
H. R. 687
- 1 -

13 WHEREAS, in 1980, he was elected Superior Court judge for the Coweta Circuit, where he
14 will be remembered for his dedication and commitment as the longest serving superior court
15 judge in the circuit; and
16 WHEREAS, he gave inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep concern
17 for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding he demonstrated to his
18 family and friends were admired by others; and
19 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
20 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
21 made this world a better place in which to live; and
22 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Judge William F. Lee Jr. will long be
23 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
24 had the great fortune of knowing him.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Judge William F. Lee Jr.
27 and express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
30 family of Judge William F. Lee Jr.
