25 LC 112 3083
Senate Resolution 270
By: Senator Strickland of the 42nd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the South Atlanta Vascular Institute (SAVI) is a multi-specialty vascular
3 practice that provides comprehensive care through the combined expertise of board-certified
4 vascular surgeons and vascular interventional radiologists; and
5 WHEREAS, SAVI's mission is to restore patients' quality of life through advanced
6 techniques performed in its state-of-the-art facility, which allow for faster recovery with less
7 pain and risk; and
8 WHEREAS, SAVI specializes in minimally invasive surgical treatments for arterial diseases
9 such as peripheral vascular disease, diabetic artery disease, limb preservation treatment,
10 abdominal aortic aneurysm, and carotid artery disease as well as venous diseases including
11 varicose veins, superficial and deep venous disease, deep venous thrombosis, and deep
12 venous reconstruction; and
13 WHEREAS, SAVI's outpatient surgical center allows physicians to perform these minimally
14 invasive vascular procedures in-office, providing greater convenience for patients; and

15 WHEREAS, SAVI's board-certified physicians, including Dr. David Allison, Dr. Paul Jones,
16 and Dr. David Penn, have been recognized as Top Doctors for multiple years for their
17 excellence in vascular care; and
18 WHEREAS, SAVI has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to providing unparalleled
19 service, support, and care to its patients in adherence with the highest principles of skill,
20 integrity, and responsibility; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
22 extraordinary healthcare institution be appropriately honored.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
24 commend the South Atlanta Vascular Institute for its many valuable contributions on behalf
25 of the citizens of Georgia and extend best wishes for the future.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the South Atlanta
28 Vascular Institute.
