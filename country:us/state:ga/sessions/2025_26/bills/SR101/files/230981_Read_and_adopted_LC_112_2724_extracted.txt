25 LC 112 2724
Senate Resolution 101
the 44th, Jackson of the 41st and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 6, 2025, as Keep Georgia Beautiful Day at the state capitol; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Keep Georgia Beautiful Foundation was created in 1978 by executive order
4 of Governor George Busbee; and
5 WHEREAS, there are now more than 70 local Keep America Beautiful Affiliates in Georgia,
6 representing one of the largest networks of local affiliates in the nation; and
7 WHEREAS, those affiliates reach more than 80 percent of Georgia's population with litter
8 prevention, waste reduction, recycling, and community beautification programs, including
9 the Great American Cleanup, America Recycles Day, the Bring One for the Chipper
10 Christmas tree recycling campaign, and BRACE (Blight Reduction and Code Enforcement)
11 grants; and
12 WHEREAS, these programs demonstrate measurable results such as reducing litter collected
13 during the Great American Cleanup by 75 percent over the last 15 years, recycling more than
14 6 million Christmas trees since the start of the Chipper program, and providing 77
S. R. 101
- 1 -

15 mini-grants to Georgia communities over the last two years for a total of $300,000, including
16 $77,500 to communities with populations up to 10,000; and
17 WHEREAS, an average of over 99,000 volunteers participate in Keep Georgia Beautiful
18 Foundation and affiliate programs each year, representing an annual value average of $24.7
19 million to Georgia taxpayers; and
20 WHEREAS, the Keep Georgia Beautiful Foundation returns an average of $170.35 each year
21 in statewide annual benefits for every $1 invested by state government to help reduce crime,
22 encourage economic development, and provide clean, green, healthy, and thriving
23 communities for all Georgians.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 recognize February 6, 2025, as Keep Georgia Beautiful Day at the state capitol.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the public and the
28 press.
