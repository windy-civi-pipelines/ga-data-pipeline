Section 1:
25 LC 28 0682
By: Senators Hodges of the 3rd, Gooch of the 51st, Hickman of the 4th, Watson of the 1st,
Albers of the 56th and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 34-9-2 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 applicability of workers' compensation to employers and employees generally, so as to
3 provide that the chapter shall not apply to employees of an employer covered by and
4 providing coverage under the federal Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act,
5 33 U.S.C. Section 901, et seq., as amended, and its extensions; to provide for related matters;
6 to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 Code Section 34-9-2 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to applicability of
10 workers' compensation to employers and employees generally, is amended by adding a new
11 subsection to read as follows:
12 "(g) This chapter shall not apply to employees of an employer covered by and providing
13 coverage under the federal Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act, 33 U.S.C.
14 Section 901, et seq., as amended, and its extensions."
S. B. 321
- 1 -
25 LC 28 0682
15 SECTION 2.
16 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
