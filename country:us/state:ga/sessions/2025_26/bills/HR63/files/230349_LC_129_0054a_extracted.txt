Section 1:
25 LC 129 0054
House Resolution 63
Hong of the 103rd
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Chinese-American Business Association of Atlanta upon its 32nd
2 anniversary; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, founded in 1992, the Chinese-American Business Association of Atlanta
4 (CBAA) increases and enhances business opportunities for Chinese-American business
5 people through meetings, seminars, and information exchanges; and
6 WHEREAS, the CBAA has consistently and diligently supported Chinese-Americans in
7 starting, maintaining, and growing their business endeavors; and
8 WHEREAS, the organization is skillfully led by President Frank Zhou and Vice Presidents
9 Jian Ni, Richard Li, and Xiaoheng Wang; and
10 WHEREAS, the CBAA has further served as a pivotal force in promoting and celebrating
11 Chinese culture in the greater Atlanta area; and
12 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
13 extraordinary association be appropriately recognized during this special time.
H. R. 63
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0054
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
15 the members of this body recognize and congratulate the Chinese-American Business
16 Association of Atlanta upon the grand occasion of its 32nd anniversary and extend best
17 wishes for its continued growth, development, and success.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
19 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
20 Chinese-American Business Association of Atlanta.
H. R. 63
