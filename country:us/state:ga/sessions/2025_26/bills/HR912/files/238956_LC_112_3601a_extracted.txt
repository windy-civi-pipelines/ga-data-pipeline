25 LC 112 3601
House Resolution 912
By: Representative Burns of the 159th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the Conn Family has dedicated a combined total of 173 years of service under
3 the Gold Dome, including the tenures of Roy Conn, Clifford Conn, Jane Conn, Judy Conn,
4 Mandy Conn, Brian Conn, and Barry Conn; and
5 WHEREAS, Roy, the father of Clifford and Jane, set an example of remarkable service for
6 his children through his 16 years as security with the Georgia Building Authority, working
7 from 1970 to 1986; and
8 WHEREAS, the longest-serving member of the Conn Family, Clifford has spent 53 years
9 and counting as part of the Clerk's Office, where he works on the night crew; and
10 WHEREAS, during her 34 years of service, Jane spent 10 and a half years working in the
11 Governor's Office and 23 and a half years as part of the Georgia Building Authority, serving
12 from 1965 to 2002; and
H. R. 912
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3601
13 WHEREAS, Judy has served the Clerk's Office with distinction alongside Clifford, her
14 husband, for 33 years, and the two have continued the Conn Family's legacy of service
15 through their three children, Brian, Barry, and Mandy; and
16 WHEREAS, with 21 years, 11 years, and five years of service to the Clerk's Office
17 respectively, Brian, Barry, and Mandy represent the newest generation of the Conn Family
18 to have served under the Gold Dome; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this extraordinary family be appropriately
20 honored during this special time.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body recognize and commend the Conn Family for their decades of
23 exemplary service to the Georgia State Capitol as well as extend best wishes to each family
24 member for future health, happiness, and success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 Conn Family.
H. R. 912
