25 LC 112 3228
House Resolution 525
of the 118th, Hitchens of the 161st, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Community Supervision Officer John Crew, the Department
2 of Community Supervision's 2024 Officer of the Year; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, founded on the tenets of public safety and progress, the Georgia Department
4 of Community Supervision commenced operations on July 1, 2015, through the passage of
5 House Bill 310; and
6 WHEREAS, Community Supervision Officer John Crew has honorably served the State of
7 Georgia for 24 years and has shown himself to be an outstanding embodiment of the
8 department's core values of selfless service, accountability, integrity, teamwork, and
9 professional development; and
10 WHEREAS, Officer Crew routinely goes above and beyond to provide efficient, effective,
11 unselfish, and dedicated service to his coworkers and the State of Georgia; and
12 WHEREAS, Officer Crew's commitment to public safety while helping create opportunities
13 for successful outcomes for the individuals he supervises serves as an example for his fellow
14 officers; and
H. R. 525
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3228
15 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
16 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
18 the members of this body recognize and commend Community Supervision Officer John
19 Crew on being named the Department of Community Supervision's 2024 Officer of the Year
20 and extend best wishes for continued success.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
22 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
23 Community Supervision Officer John Crew.
