25 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 44 0222
ADOPTED
amendment #1:
1 Amend the Senate Committee on State and Local Governmental Operations - General
2 substitute to HB 513 (LC 44 3173S) by inserting after "To amend" in line 1 the following:
3 Chapter 60 of Title 36 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to general
4 provisions applicable to counties and municipal corporations, so as to authorize continuance
5 of existing local ordinances related to video surveillance at gas stations; to amend
6 By substituting "effective dates" for "an effective date" on line 5.
7 By inserting the following after line 8:
8 Chapter 60 of Title 36 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to general
9 provisions applicable to counties and municipal corporations, is amended by revising Code
10 Section 36-60-32, relating to video surveillance equipment at locations where the retail sale
11 of automotive gasoline occurs, as follows:
12 "36-60-32.
13 A county, municipal corporation, or consolidated government shall not require the
14 placement of video surveillance equipment in the interior or exterior of any business or
15 location where the retail sale of automotive gasoline, as that term is defined in Code
16 Section 10-1-232, occurs; provided, however, that a county, municipal corporation, or
17 consolidated government which, prior to May 6, 2024, has adopted one or more ordinances
18 related to the placement of video surveillance equipment at any business or location where
19 the retail sale of automotive gasoline occurs is authorized to continue regulating the
