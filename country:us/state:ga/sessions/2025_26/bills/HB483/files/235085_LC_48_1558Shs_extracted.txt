25 LC 48 1558S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 2 of Chapter 5 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to assault and battery, so as to provide for protections for inspectors of code
3 enforcement; to provide for a definition; to provide for enhanced penalties for simple assault,
4 aggravated assault, simple battery, battery, and aggravated battery committed upon
5 inspectors of code enforcement in certain circumstances; to provide for related matters; to
6 provide for an effective date and applicability; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other
7 purposes.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
9 SECTION 1.
10 Article 2 of Chapter 5 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
11 assault and battery, is amended by adding a new paragraph to Code Section 16-5-19, relating
12 to definitions, to read as follows:
13 "(4.1) 'Inspector of code enforcement' means any person employed by or under contract
14 with a municipality, city, county, or consolidated government who performs safety
15 inspections of buildings, structures, and businesses to ensure they are operated in
16 accordance with the law."
H. B. 483 (SUB)
- 1 -
25 LC 48 1558S
17 SECTION 2.
18 Said article is further amended by revising subsection (i) of Code Section 16-5-20, relating
19 to simple assault, as follows:
20 "(i) Any person who commits the offense of simple assault upon a utility worker or
21 inspector of code enforcement while such worker or inspector is acting within the course
22 and scope of their his or her employment or is performing official duties at the time of the
23 offense shall, upon conviction thereof, be punished for a misdemeanor of a high and
24 aggravated nature."
25 SECTION 3.
26 Said article is further amended by revising subsection (m) of Code Section 16-5-21, relating
27 to aggravated assault, as follows:
28 "(m) Any person who commits the offense of aggravated assault upon a utility worker or
29 inspector of code enforcement while such worker or inspector is acting within the course
30 and scope of their his or her employment or is performing official duties shall, upon
31 conviction thereof, be punished by imprisonment for not less than three nor more than 20
32 years."
33 SECTION 4.
34 Said article is further amended by revising subsection (j) of Code Section 16-5-23, relating
35 to simple battery, as follows:
36 "(j) Any person who commits the offense of simple battery upon a utility worker or
37 inspector of code enforcement while such worker or inspector is acting within the course
38 and scope of their his or her employment or is performing official duties at the time of the
39 offense shall, upon conviction thereof, be punished for a misdemeanor of a high and
40 aggravated nature."

49 Said article is further amended by revising subsection (i) of Code Section 16-5-24, relating

56 SECTION 7.

58 without such approval and shall apply to all offenses committed on or after July 1, 2025.
