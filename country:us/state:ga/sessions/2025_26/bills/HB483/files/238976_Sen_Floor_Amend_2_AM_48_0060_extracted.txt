25 Sen Floor Amend 2 AM 48 0060
offered the following amendment #2:
1 Amend the Senate Committee on Rules substitute to HB 483 (LC 49 2400S) by replacing lines
2 1 and 2 with "To amend Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
3 crimes and offenses, so as to provide for certain protections; to provide for enhanced
4 penalties; to replace a term; to repeal an".
5 By inserting between lines 9 and 10 "Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
6 relating to crimes and offenses, is amended by adding a new paragraph to Code Section
7 16-5-19, relating to definitions, to read as follows:
8 "(4.1) 'Inspector of code enforcement' or 'inspector' means any person with a high school
9 diploma or equivalent who has completed Level 1 code enforcement training under a
10 professional program."
11 SECTION 2.
12 Said title is further amended by replacing "utility worker while such worker" with "utility
13 worker or inspector of code enforcement while such worker or inspector" wherever the
14 former term appears in Chapter 5, relating to crimes against a person.
15 SECTION 3.".
16 By replacing lines 10 -11 until "amended" with "Said title is further". By replacing "part" on
17 line 18 with "title". By redesignating Sections 2, 3, and 4 as Sections 4, 5, and 6.
