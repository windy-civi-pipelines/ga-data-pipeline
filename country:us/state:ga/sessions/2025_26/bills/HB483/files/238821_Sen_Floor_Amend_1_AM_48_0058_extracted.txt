Section 1:
25 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 48 0058
amendment #1:
1 Amend the Senate Committee on Rules substitute to HB 483 (LC 49 2400S) by replacing lines
2 1 and 2 with "To amend Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
3 crimes and offenses, so as to provide for protections for inspectors of code enforcement; to
4 provide for enhanced penalties in certain circumstances; to replace a term; to repeal an". By
5 inserting between lines 9 and 10 "Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
6 relating to crimes and offenses, is amended by adding a new paragraph to Code Section
7 16-5-19, relating to definitions, to read as follows: "(4.1) 'Inspector of code enforcement' or
8 'inspector' means any person with a high school diploma or equivalent who has completed
9 Level 1 code enforcement training under a professional program and is employed by or under
10 contract with a municipality, city, county, or consolidated government who performs safety
11 inspections of buildings, structures, and businesses to ensure they are operated in accordance
12 with the law." SECTION 2. Said title is further amended by replacing "utility worker while
13 such worker" with "utility worker or inspector of code enforcement while such worker or
14 inspector" wherever the former term appears in Chapter 5, relating to crimes against a person.
15 SECTION 3.". By replacing lines 10 -11 until "amended" with "Said title is further". By
16 replacing "part" on line 18 with "title". By redesignating Sections 2, 3, and 4 as Sections 4,
17 5, and 6.
