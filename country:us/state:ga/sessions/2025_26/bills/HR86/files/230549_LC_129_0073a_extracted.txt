25 LC 129 0073
House Resolution 86
Mitchell of the 88th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing May 15, 2025, as United Nations International Day for Families in the State of
2 Georgia; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the United Nations General Assembly established the International Day for
4 Families in 1993 through Resolution A/RES/47/237 to promote awareness of issues related
5 to families and enhance understanding of the social, economic, and demographic processes
6 affecting families globally; and
7 WHEREAS, families are fundamental units of society essential to the development and
8 well-being of individuals and communities, and their strength and stability contribute to
9 sustainable development, societal harmony, and generational progress; and
10 WHEREAS, the United Nations International Day for Families highlights annual themes that
11 address critical aspects of family dynamics, including health, education, equality, work-life
12 balance, and sustainable development, and serves as a call to action for governments,
13 organizations, and communities to support families in their diverse forms; and
14 WHEREAS, Dr. Jacqueline Mohair, founder of Trinity Girls Network Corp., Trinity
15 International University of Ambassadors (TIUA), Trinity International Chaplaincy Institute,

16 and TIUA School of Business, has exemplified visionary leadership in promoting the
17 importance of families through faith, education, and economic empowerment; and
18 WHEREAS, Trinity Girls Network Corp., a non-governmental organization with
19 Consultative Status from the United Nations Economic and Social Council, along with its
20 affiliated organizations, celebrates the International Day for Families with a special
21 observance in the State of Georgia on May 14, fostering awareness of the significance of
22 family unity, faith, and legacy building; and
23 WHEREAS, the observance highlights the principle that "a family that prays together stays
24 together" while emphasizing that families who create wealth together build strong legacies
25 for generations to come, empowering future leaders and strengthening the social fabric of
26 communities; and
27 WHEREAS, through initiatives such as The Family Business, these organizations underscore
28 the vital role of families in entrepreneurship, economic empowerment, and the creation of
29 sustainable legacies, inspiring families to work collaboratively to achieve financial stability
30 and generational success; and
31 WHEREAS, TIUA, in partnership with The Family Business initiative, hosts an annual
32 Family Day Celebration at the United Nations Headquarters in New York, underscoring the
33 essential role of family legacy and entrepreneurship in fostering a sustainable future; this
34 event aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, empowering families
35 to drive global progress through innovation, leadership, and faith.
36 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
37 the members of this body recognize May 15, 2025, as United Nations International Day for
