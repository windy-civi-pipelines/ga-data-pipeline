Section 1:
25 LC 48 1455
By: Representatives Momtahan of the 17th, Gunter of the 8th, Reeves of the 99th, Efstration
of the 104th, and Smith of the 18th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 3 of Chapter 7 of Title 15 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to practice and procedure relative to state courts of counties, so as to provide for the
3 online publishing of terms of state court; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting
4 laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Article 3 of Chapter 7 of Title 15 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
8 practice and procedure relative to state courts of counties, is amended by adding a new Code
9 section to read as follows:
10 "15-7-41.1.
11 Each state court clerk shall be required to publish and maintain information and dates
12 regarding terms of court in a prominent location on its public website."
13 SECTION 2.
14 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 378
