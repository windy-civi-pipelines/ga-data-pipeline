25 LC 129 0025
Senate Resolution 22
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Scott Justus; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Scott Justus on January 11, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Scott Justus was born on February 24, 1959, in Hendersonville, North Carolina,
5 a beloved son of James Charles and Roberta Griffin Justus; and
6 WHEREAS, he was an accomplished entrepreneur who devoted more than 30 years to the
7 construction industry, building countless quality homes and serving as a member of the
8 National Association of Home Builders, the Home Builders Association of Georgia, and the
9 West Georgia Board of Realtors; and
10 WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, Scott Justus was an active member of Villa
11 Rica First United Methodist Church; and
S. R. 22
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0025
12 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife of 34 years, Lisa, and was
13 blessed with three remarkable children, John, Lauren, and Charles, as well as two wonderful
14 grandchildren, Grady and Madeline; and
15 WHEREAS, he gave inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep concern
16 for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding he demonstrated to his
17 family and friends were admired by others; and
18 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
19 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
20 made this world a better place in which to live; and
21 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Scott Justus will long be remembered for
22 his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who had the great
23 fortune of knowing him.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 join in honoring the life and memory of Scott Justus and express their deepest and most
26 sincere regret at his passing.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
28 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the family of Scott
29 Justus.
S. R. 22
