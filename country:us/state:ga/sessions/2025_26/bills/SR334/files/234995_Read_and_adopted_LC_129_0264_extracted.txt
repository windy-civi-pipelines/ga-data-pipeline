25 LC 129 0264
Senate Resolution 334
44th, Harrell of the 40th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Pamela Collins for receiving the 2025 Yellow Rose Nikki
2 T. Randall Servant Leader Award; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, in 1999, Representative Nikki T. Randall became the first African American
4 female elected to the General Assembly from middle Georgia, and over her 17 years of
5 service as a legislator, she held numerous leadership positions, including chair of the Georgia
6 Legislative Women's Caucus; and
7 WHEREAS, every year during Women's History Month, the Georgia Legislative Women's
8 Caucus recognizes outstanding women in Georgia who exemplify the leadership and
9 inspiration embodied by Representative Randall; and
10 WHEREAS, Pamela Collins is a woman of unassuming greatness whose humble acts of
11 kindness and generosity have uplifted the entire community; and
12 WHEREAS, Pamela has established a glowing reputation of renown throughout Georgia for
13 her selfless service as a volunteer in the South Fulton area for over 30 years; and
S. R. 334
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0264
14 WHEREAS, she is a chartered member and the first President of the National Council of
15 Negro Women; a charter member of the National Women of Achievement, Inc-Southern
16 Crescent; and a Diamond Life Member of Delta Sigma Theta, Inc., East Point/College Park
17 Alumnae Chapter; and
18 WHEREAS, Pamela has diligently and consistently devoted innumerable hours of her time,
19 talents, and energy to energy, to the improvement of her community as the Vice Chair of the
20 African American Democratic Caucus of Clayton County and the Concern Black Citizens
21 of Clayton County; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
23 extraordinary servant leader be appropriately recognized.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 congratulate and commend Pamela Collins for receiving the 2025 Yellow Rose Nikki T.
26 Randall Servant Leader Award and extend best wishes for her continued success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the Secretary of the Senate is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Pamela Collins.
