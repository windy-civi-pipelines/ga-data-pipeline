25 LC 129 0328
House Resolution 647
of the 75th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Latoya Shambo and recognizing March 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing Women in
2 Georgia Day; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day acknowledges the philanthropic, social,
4 and cultural contributions of women in the state; and
5 WHEREAS, Latoya Shambo has long been recognized for the vital role that she has played
6 in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of the citizens of Georgia;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, Latoya is a pioneer in the creator economy and influencer marketing as well as
9 the founder and CEO of Black Girl Digital, an award-winning agency that connects brands
10 with top creators for high-impact campaigns; and
11 WHEREAS, as a testament to her significant organizational and leadership talents,
12 remarkable patience and diplomacy, and keen sense of vision, she has led successful
13 partnerships with Walt Disney World, Subaru, YouTube, and African Pride, driving
14 engagement through innovative, data-driven strategies; and
H. R. 647
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0328
15 WHEREAS, as an early trailblazer in the creator economy, Latoya has shaped the way
16 brands and influencers collaborate; her expertise has been featured in prestigious
17 publications, including Forbes, The Culture, Bloomberg TV, and Adweek, solidifying her as
18 a leading industry voice; and
19 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
20 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
22 individual be appropriately honored.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body commend Latoya Shambo for her efficient, effective, unselfish, and
25 dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; recognize March, 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing
26 Women in Georgia Day; and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and
27 happiness.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Latoya Shambo.
