25 LC 129 0430
House Resolution 867
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Mr. Robert Carnaroli upon his retirement as Principal of
2 North Forsyth High School; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Mr. Robert Carnaroli has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for
4 the vital role that he has played in leadership and his deep personal commitment to the
5 welfare of the citizens of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, he has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of his time,
7 talents, and energy toward the betterment of his community and state as evidenced
8 dramatically by his superlative service with Forsyth County Schools; and
9 WHEREAS, Mr. Carnaroli has been a dedicated member of the Forsyth County Schools
10 family since 1994, when he began his journey as a high school counselor helping to open
11 North Forsyth High School; and
12 WHEREAS, he served as the school's first Cross Country and Track Coach, with his cross
13 country team garnering both county and region championships as well as placing 3rd in the
14 state championship in 1997; and
H. R. 867
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0430
15 WHEREAS, Mr. Carnaroli was instrumental in the opening of Denmark High School and
16 West Forsyth High School; and
17 WHEREAS, he was named Principal of North Forsyth High School in 2020, where he has
18 served for the past 5 years; and
19 WHEREAS, Mr. Carnaroli has dedicated his life to helping students, staff, families, and
20 schools, while working to provide the very best opportunities, experiences, and support for
21 all; and
22 WHEREAS, he is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
23 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
24 WHEREAS, Mr. Carnaroli has served with honor and distinction with Forsyth County
25 Schools, and his vision and unyielding commitment to student success will be sorely missed
26 upon the occasion of his retirement; and
27 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
28 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
30 the members of this body recognize and commend Mr. Robert Carnaroli for his efficient,
31 effective, unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; congratulate him
32 upon the grand occasion of his retirement; and extend the most sincere best wishes for
33 continued health and happiness.
H. R. 867

25 LC 129 0430
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mr.
36 Robert Carnaroli.
