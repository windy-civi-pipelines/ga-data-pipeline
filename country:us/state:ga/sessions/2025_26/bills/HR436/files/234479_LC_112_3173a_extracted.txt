25 LC 112 3173
House Resolution 436
the 76th, and Davis of the 87th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the invaluable contributions of school bus drivers and school food service
2 workers and recognizing February 28, 2025, as Honoring Heroes Day; and for other
3 purposes.
4 WHEREAS, Honoring Heroes Day will be observed on February 28, 2025, at the Georgia
5 State Capitol, bringing together community members and legislators to honor the essential
6 work of school bus drivers and school food service workers; and
7 WHEREAS, school bus drivers play a vital role in ensuring the safety and well-being of
8 Georgia's students, providing secure and reliable transportation that fosters a sense of
9 stability, care, and trust; and
10 WHEREAS, school food service workers are dedicated to nourishing students with healthy,
11 balanced meals that are essential for academic success, physical health, and overall
12 well-being; and
13 WHEREAS, both school bus drivers and school food service workers demonstrate daily
14 commitment, hard work, and compassion, often going beyond their official duties to
15 positively impact students' lives; and
H. R. 436
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3173
16 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the outstanding efforts and dedication of these
17 often-unsung heroes be recognized and celebrated for the essential roles they play in
18 Georgia's educational system and communities.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body commend Georgia's school bus drivers and school food service
21 workers for their invaluable contributions and recognize February 28, 2025, as Honoring
22 Heroes Day.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
25 sponsors of Honoring Heroes Day and to Georgia's school bus drivers and school food
26 service workers.
