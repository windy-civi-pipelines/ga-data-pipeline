25 LC 129 0315
House Resolution 631
75th, and Berry of the 56th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Adrienne Gates and recognizing March 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing Women in
2 Georgia Day; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day acknowledges the philanthropic, social,
4 and cultural contributions of women in the state; and
5 WHEREAS, Adrienne Gates has long been recognized for the vital role that she has played
6 in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of the citizens of Georgia;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, Adrienne is the founder of P.O.W.E.R. Atlanta Inc., a nonprofit organization
9 dedicated to empowering individuals, children, and families in underserved communities
10 through essential resources, financial literacy, and health-focused initiatives; and
11 WHEREAS, under her leadership, P.O.W.E.R. Atlanta Inc., has become a critical hub for
12 Metro-Atlanta, offering holistic support that meets people where they are; and
H. R. 631

25 LC 129 0315
13 WHEREAS, beyond her work at P.O.W.E.R. Atlanta Inc., Adrienne serves on multiple
14 community advisory boards, using her lived experience to advocate for policies and
15 programs that drive real change; and
16 WHEREAS, this distinguished gentlewoman has given inspiration to many through her high
17 ideals, morals, and deep concern for her fellow citizens, and she possesses the vast wisdom
18 which only comes through experience and the strength of character which is achieved
19 through overcoming the many challenges of life; and
20 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
21 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness ; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
23 individual be appropriately honored.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body commend Adrienne Gates for her efficient, effective, unselfish, and
26 dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; recognize March, 20, 2025, as as
27 TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day; and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued
28 health and happiness.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
30 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
31 Adrienne Gates.
