25 LC 112 2740
Senate Resolution 87
Brass of the 6th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Communities in Schools; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Communities in Schools (CIS) has highly trained site coordinators in schools
3 across the state who assess students' needs and partner with local businesses, social service
4 agencies, healthcare providers, and community volunteers to provide disadvantaged students
5 with the resources they need to succeed in school and in life; and
6 WHEREAS, from 2024 to 2025, CIS provided services to over 120,000 students in nearly
7 50 school districts and partnered with 223 schools across Georgia, achieving significant
8 improvements in academic outcomes for case-managed students; and
9 WHEREAS, 98 percent of the students aided by CIS graduated, 73 percent of students with
10 attendance problems showed improved attendance, and 98 percent of of K-8 students were
11 promoted; and
12 WHEREAS, 70 percent of CIS students with discipline problems in school improved,
13 reducing their number of suspensions, and 76 percent of students improved academically;
14 and
S. R. 87

15 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding organization be
16 appropriately honored.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
18 honor Communities in Schools for its tremendous service to the youth of Georgia and extend
19 best wishes for continued success.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Communities in
