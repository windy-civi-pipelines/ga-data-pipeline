25 LC 112 3313
House Resolution 529
By: Representative Carter of the 93rd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Constance Levett Abbott has devoted her life to ministry, serving as a
3 leader, teacher, and spiritual guide who works to transform the lives of others through faith
4 and service; and
5 WHEREAS, she was licensed to preach the Gospel in 1996 at First Agape Christian
6 Fellowship Church, and she has since held numerous ministerial roles throughout her career,
7 including serving under Pastor Keith D. Brooks at All Nations Life & Praise Church; and
8 WHEREAS, her dedication to spiritual leadership led to her appointment as Senior Pastor
9 of Macedonia Baptist Church in Conyers, Georgia, on February 26, 2022, where she
10 continues to minister to her congregation and engage with the broader Rockdale County
11 community through impactful ministries; and
12 WHEREAS, she has demonstrated a passion for discipleship by developing a structured
13 curriculum to guide new believers in their first six months of faith as well as a 10-course
14 Ministerial Training Curriculum designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and
15 confidence to share Biblical teachings; and
H. R. 529
- 1 -

16 WHEREAS, in addition to her ministry, she has made significant contributions to the
17 community through her service as a DMIN Program Advisory Committee member for South
18 University and a P.E.A.R.L. Advisory Board member, advocating for the well-being of the
19 senior population in Rockdale County; and
20 WHEREAS, in November 2023, Dr. Abbott and Macedonia Baptist Church-Conyers were
21 honored as Unsung Heroes by the National Community-Based Organization Network in
22 collaboration with Morehouse School of Medicine, recognizing their efforts to address social,
23 ethical, and health disparities in the community; and
24 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this enduring example of God's message
25 of peace and love be honored and congratulated for her innumerable good works rendered
26 on behalf of persons in need.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body recognize and commend Dr. Constance Levett Abbott for her
29 exceptional contributions to ministry, education, and community service as well as extend
30 best wishes for many more years of successful fellowship.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
33 Constance Levett Abbott.
