25 LC 112 3092
House Resolution 378
of the 28th, Smith of the 138th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 27, 2025, as Georgia Airports Association Day; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, there are 95 general aviation airports in the State of Georgia that are essential
4 to the state's transportation and economic infrastructure, supporting diversified industries
5 such as technology, manufacturing, distribution, tourism, and agriculture; and
6 WHEREAS, these businesses utilize Georgia's airports to transport employees, customers,
7 vendors, and goods, which spurs economic development; and
8 WHEREAS, the Georgia Statewide Aviation System is also home to an additional eight
9 airports that provide for commercial or regularly scheduled airline service, resulting in a
10 grand total of 103 publically owned airports; and
11 WHEREAS, these airports provide the state with a combined $73.7 billion in annual
12 economic activity as well as with more than 450,000 jobs, representing $20.2 billion in
13 payroll; and
H. R. 378
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3092
14 WHEREAS, founded in 1990, the Georgia Airports Association is dedicated to the
15 development, growth, and empowerment of airports in the State of Georgia; and
16 WHEREAS, this remarkable organization provides its members with access to valuable
17 educational and training materials, networking and communication support, and breaking
18 industry news and updates; and
19 WHEREAS, by providing these vital resources, the Georgia Airports Association enables its
20 member airports to make optimal use of their often-limited time and assets, further
21 supporting the state's economic growth; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the Georgia Airports Association be
23 appropriately honored.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body recognize February 27, 2025, as Georgia Airports Association Day.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
28 Georgia Airports Association.
