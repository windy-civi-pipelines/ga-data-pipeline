25 LC 112 2739
Senate Resolution 72
Hufstetler of the 52nd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Braselton Police Chief Terry Esco; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Braselton Police Chief Terry Esco retires in March after 35 years of dedicated
3 service to the town and its community; and
4 WHEREAS, Chief Esco was hired by former Mayor Henry E. Braselton in February 1990
5 after earning his P.O.S.T. certification the year before, and he was promoted to Chief in May
6 1991; and
7 WHEREAS, as the town expanded into four counties, Chief Esco grew the department's
8 personnel to 23 officers; and
9 WHEREAS, alongside the department's methodical growth in personnel, Chief Esco oversaw
10 advancements in technology, training, equipment, vehicle upgrades, and certifications to
11 meet modern law enforcement needs; and
12 WHEREAS, Chief Esco has served with distinction in additional roles such as a GACP
13 All-Hazard Council Representative and President of the Gwinnett Police Chiefs Association;
14 and
S. R. 72
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2739
15 WHEREAS, in his role as Public Safety Director for the Town of Braselton, Chief Esco has
16 ensured a safe and tranquil environment for its residents and visitors.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
18 commend the care, courage, sympathy, and selfless dedication exhibited by Braselton Police
19 Chief Terry Esco in carrying out the difficult duties of his vocation and express their utmost
20 appreciation for having so purely and truly defined for others the meaning of public servant.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
22 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Braselton Police
23 Chief Terry Esco.
