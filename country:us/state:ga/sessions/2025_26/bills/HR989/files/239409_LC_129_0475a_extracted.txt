2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Gloria Dubberly has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the
4 vital role that she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare
5 of the citizens of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, she has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of her time,

15 WHEREAS, Gloria's significant organizational and leadership talents, remarkable patience
16 and diplomacy, keen sense of vision, and sensitivity to the needs of the citizens of this state
17 have earned her the respect and admiration of her colleagues and associates; and
18 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
19 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
20 WHEREAS, Gloria has served with honor and distinction in Tattnall County, and her vision
21 and unyielding commitment to her community will be sorely missed upon the occasion of
22 her retirement; and
23 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
24 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body recognize and commend Gloria Dubberly for her efficient,
27 effective, unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; congratulate her
28 upon the grand occasion of her retirement; and extend the most sincere best wishes for
29 continued health and happiness.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
32 Gloria Dubberly.
