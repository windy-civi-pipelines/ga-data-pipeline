Section 1:
25 LC 39 4631
House Resolution 328
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing Chief Jeffrey E. Turner and dedicating a road in his honor; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Chief Jeffrey E. Turner is highly regarded by the citizens of his community and
4 state and by local government officials as a person of unquestioned integrity and dedication
5 to the sound principles of law enforcement; and
6 WHEREAS, Chief Turner moved to Clayton County with his family in 1978 and graduated
7 from Morrow High School in 1982; and
8 WHEREAS, he earned a bachelor's degree from the University of West Georgia and a
9 master's degree from Columbus State University; and
10 WHEREAS, after dedicating 20 years of service to the Clayton County Police Department,
11 Chief Turner was appointed as the first African American Chief of Police for the department
12 in 2007; and
13 WHEREAS, in 2009, Chief Turner was assigned to the Clayton County Regional Police
14 Academy as the Director and the first African American to lead the academy; and
H. R. 328
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4631
15 WHEREAS, Chief Turner exhibits extraordinary devotion to duty, outstanding loyalty, fine
16 leadership, and meticulous attention to detail in all his duties; and
17 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
18 Georgian be appropriately recognized by dedicating a road in his honor.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
20 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of Interstate 75 from the Riverdale exit to the
21 Tara Boulevard exit in Clayton County is dedicated as the Jeffrey E. Turner Highway.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
23 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Jeffrey E. Turner Highway.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Chief
26 Jeffrey E. Turner and to the Department of Transportation.
H. R. 328
