25 LC 129 0131
Senate Resolution 295
4th, Sims of the 12th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Lowndes County High School baseball team for winning the 2024 GHSA
2 7A State Baseball Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Lowndes County High School baseball team has upheld a proud tradition
4 of excellence, placing equal importance on character development, academic achievement,
5 and athletic performance; and
6 WHEREAS, through remarkable play, cohesive teamwork, and exceptional leadership, the
7 team became a standout presence throughout the 2024 season, culminating in a thrilling
8 championship victory with a final score of 4-2 to claim the GHSA 7A State Baseball title;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, the outstanding performance of this team exemplifies the dedication,
11 perseverance, and unity of its players as well as the strategic guidance of their head coach,
12 Ryan Page, who has led the Vikings to back-to-back state championships; and
13 WHEREAS, the team's extraordinary achievements are further highlighted by its consistency
14 and commitment to excellence, finishing the season with an impressive 25-5 record and
15 featuring 17 seniors, eight of whom have committed to playing at the collegiate level; and
S. R. 295
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0131
16 WHEREAS, the Vikings have brought immense pride to their school and community,
17 earning a reputation for athletic excellence, sportsmanship, and the relentless pursuit of
18 success both on and off the field.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 congratulate the Lowndes County High School baseball team on winning the 2024 GHSA
21 7A State Baseball Championship and extend best wishes for continued success in their future
22 endeavors.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Lowndes
25 County High School baseball team.
S. R. 295
