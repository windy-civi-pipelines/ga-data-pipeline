Section 1:
25 LC 39 4593S (SCS)
By: Senators Echols of the 49th, Kirkpatrick of the 32nd, Payne of the 54th, Brass of the 6th,
Summers of the 13th and others
AS PASSED SENATE
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 40-2-86 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 license plates promoting or supporting certain agencies, funds, or nonprofit corporations with
3 proceeds disbursed to the general fund and the agency, fund, or nonprofit corporation, so as
4 to establish a specialty license plate benefitting the Georgia Veterans Service Foundation;
5 to provide for related matters; to provide for compliance with constitutional requirements;
6 to provide for an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 Code Section 40-2-86 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to license plates
10 promoting or supporting certain agencies, funds, or nonprofit corporations with proceeds
11 disbursed to the general fund and the agency, fund, or nonprofit corporation, is amended by
12 adding a new paragraph to subsection (l) to read as follows:
13 "(71) A special license plate honoring Georgia veterans and their families. The funds
14 raised by the sale of this special license plate shall be disbursed to the Georgia Veterans
15 Service Foundation."
S. B. 76
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4593S (SCS)
16 SECTION 2.
17 In accordance with the requirements of Article III, Section IX, Paragraph VI(n) of the
18 Constitution of the State of Georgia, this Act shall not become law unless it receives the
19 requisite two-thirds' majority vote in both the Senate and the House of Representatives.
20 SECTION 3.
21 This Act shall become effective on January 1, 2026.
22 SECTION 4.
23 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 76
