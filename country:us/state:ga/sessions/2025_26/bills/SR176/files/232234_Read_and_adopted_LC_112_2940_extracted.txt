25 LC 112 2940
Senate Resolution 176
Mangham of the 55th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Laura Wood, Lanier High School's 2024-2025 Teacher of the Year; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Laura Wood has been teaching ninth through twelfth grade orchestra to Sugar
4 Hill students at Lanier High School in Gwinnett County for four years and has 21 years of
5 teaching experience; and
6 WHEREAS, she is a highly respected ninth through twelfth grade teacher within the school
7 community and among her peers and has been recognized as Lanier High School's Teacher
8 of the Year for 2024-2025; and
9 WHEREAS, she has spent endless hours both inside and outside the classroom teaching
10 students and empowering them to think freely, helping them develop skills such as
11 questioning, provoking, and challenging all the things presented to them; and
12 WHEREAS, she has engaged her students by producing innovative lessons that have real-life
13 applications and has thus promoted learning to her students as a lifelong endeavor; and
S. R. 176
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2940
14 WHEREAS, the purpose of the Teacher of the Year award is to honor educators who have
15 made an exceptional difference in the lives of their students and provided inspiration to their
16 peers, recognizing and promoting excellence in education within Gwinnett County Public
17 Schools and helping to promote an educated and vibrant community; and
18 WHEREAS, in recognition of such remarkable contributions to the education and welfare
19 of Georgia's students, it is abundantly fitting and proper for this body to honor the
20 extraordinary accomplishments and academic achievements of this outstanding educator.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 commend Laura Wood, Lanier High School's 2024-2025 Teacher of the Year, and express
23 their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Laura Wood.
S. R. 176
