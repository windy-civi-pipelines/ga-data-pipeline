25 LC 112 3170
House Resolution 443
55th, Burnough of the 77th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Raising Expectations Inc.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, as college students at Georgia State University, Dr. Maria Armstrong and
3 Tangee Allen recognized the challenges faced by African American youth in low-wealth
4 communities and began providing tutoring and mentoring for youth in Decatur; and
5 WHEREAS, what began as a volunteer effort quickly grew into a structured and sustained
6 initiative as Dr. Armstrong, Tangee, and their peers dedicated themselves to supporting
7 children academically and personally, leading the two women to found Raising Expectations
8 Inc.; and
9 WHEREAS, for 27 years, Raising Expectations Inc. has provided impactful youth
10 development programming to students in Atlanta communities, including Decatur, East Lake,
11 Pittsburgh, Vine City, English Avenue, Washington Park, and the Westside; and
12 WHEREAS, this outstanding organization employs a 360-degree approach to youth
13 development that addresses students' academic, social, and mental well-being through
14 tutoring, mentoring, STEM exposure, college preparation, and hands-on learning to improve
15 college readiness; and
H. R. 443
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3170
16 WHEREAS, Raising Expectations Inc. maintains a high-expectation model in which students
17 are required to attend programming four days per week throughout the school year and
18 participate in enrichment activities designed to foster new skills and interests; and
19 WHEREAS, its signature program, the Academic Tutorial Academy, is the focal component
20 of Project D.R.E.A.M. (Developing Raised Expectations for Adolescent Minds), supporting
21 students through individualized learning plans, one-on-one mentorship, and computer-based
22 instruction to bridge academic gaps and improve standardized test performance; and
23 WHEREAS, Raising Expectations Inc. further collaborates with school administrators,
24 parents, and teachers to align its academic support with classroom learning, using ongoing
25 assessment and data-driven instruction to increase student achievement; and
26 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable nonprofit be
27 appropriately honored.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body commend Raising Expectations Inc. for its outstanding
30 contributions to youth development and education in this state as well as extend sincere best
31 wishes for its continued success.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
33 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
34 Raising Expectations Inc.
