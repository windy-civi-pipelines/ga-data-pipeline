25 LC 112 3020
House Resolution 266
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Brandon "Beezy" Waller; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Brandon "Beezy" Waller on February 8, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Brandon "Beezy" Waller was born on February 5, 1985, in Atlanta, Georgia,
5 and was raised in Columbus, Georgia, where he was cherished as a beloved son, brother,
6 uncle, cousin, friend, and community leader; and
7 WHEREAS, known for his vibrant personality and influential presence, he used his platform
8 to advocate for causes that mattered and played a vital role in promoting community service
9 initiatives, supporting nonprofit organizations, and championing anti-violence campaigns;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, Brandon "Beezy" Waller demonstrated a remarkable ability to connect with
12 people from all walks of life, including government officials, media outlets such as Foxie
13 105, and grassroots organizations, earning deep respect for his dedication and commitment
14 to uplifting others; and
H. R. 266
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3020
15 WHEREAS, he gave inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep concern
16 for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding he demonstrated to his
17 family and friends were admired by others; and
18 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
19 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
20 made this world a better place in which to live; and
21 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Brandon "Beezy" Waller will long be
22 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
23 had the great fortune of knowing him.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Brandon "Beezy" Waller
26 and express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
29 family of Brandon "Beezy" Waller.
