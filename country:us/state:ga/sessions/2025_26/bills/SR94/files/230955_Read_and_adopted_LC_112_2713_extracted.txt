25 LC 112 2713
Senate Resolution 94
Payne of the 54th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending and congratulating David Escott "Scotty" Ferber; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, David Escott "Scotty" Ferber has attained the rank of Eagle Scout, the most
3 distinguished rank conferred by the Boy Scouts of America; and
4 WHEREAS, a candidate for the most exceptional award in scouting must complete a
5 rigorous program of training and demonstrate his proficiency in selected scouting techniques
6 and the attainment of the highest ideals of scouting; and
7 WHEREAS, only one scout out of 97 ever reaches this lofty level of achievement, and Scotty
8 has ably evidenced his aptitude and competency in mastering the various special skills taught
9 through the Scouts BSA program; and
10 WHEREAS, this hard-working young man has demonstrated great leadership skills and
11 initiative and has truly exhibited a commitment to the scouting principles of honesty and
12 fairness during his years with the Scouts BSA program; and
13 WHEREAS, a member of Troop 3740, Scotty designed, cut, stained, sealed, assembled, and
14 installed lockers to house 288 musical instruments for North Forsyth Middle School's band,
S. R. 94
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2713
15 completing a project that involved over 1,300 man-hours, including 346 of his own, and the
16 participation of 66 volunteers; and
17 WHEREAS, Scotty further received a Presidential Gold Service Award for his hours of
18 community service through the project, and as additional testament to his dedication to
19 scouting excellence, he has earned induction into the Order of the Arrow; and
20 WHEREAS, the perseverance and determination displayed by this exceptional young leader
21 set an example for all Georgians, and great success is surely in this young man's future.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 commend David Escott "Scotty" Ferber for his diligence and dedication in pursuing the
24 highest honor awarded by the Boy Scouts of America and congratulate him on attaining the
25 prestigious rank of Eagle Scout.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to David Escott
28 "Scotty" Ferber.
S. R. 94
