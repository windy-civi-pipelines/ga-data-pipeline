25 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 49 0267
WITHDRAWN
1 Amend the Senate Committee on Judiciary substitute to HB 268 (LC 49 2396S) by removing
2 the quotation mark at the end of line 1015 and inserting between lines 1015 and 1016 the
3 following:
4 (j)(1) The Department of Education, in collaboration with the Georgia Emergency
5 Management and Homeland Security Agency, shall develop information and other
6 resources regarding the safe storage of firearms. Such information and other resources
7 shall be made available for use by local school systems and other public schools.
8 (2) By July 1, 2026, each local board of education or public school governing body shall
9 make the information and other resources provided for in paragraph (1) of this subsection
10 available to the parents and guardians of all students enrolled in such local school system
11 or public school and shall publish such information on the local school system or public
12 school website."
