25 LC 112 3058
Senate Resolution 232
the 56th, Jones II of the 22nd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Boy Scouts of America in Georgia and recognizing February 26, 2025, as
2 Scouts Day at the Capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Boy Scouts of America is an organization dedicated to training youth in
4 responsible citizenship, character development, and self-reliance through participation in a
5 wide range of outdoor activities, educational programs, and service projects; and
6 WHEREAS, the Boy Scouts of America in Georgia are the most active group in the state
7 with over 40,000 scouts and more than 3,500 adult leaders in more than 600 units; and
8 WHEREAS, the Boy Scouts of America in Georgia have contributed to the state's
9 communities through their service projects and their commitment to the ideals of Scouting;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, the annual Scouts Day at the Capitol provides an opportunity for Scouts to learn
12 about their government and to engage in dialogue with their senators in the Georgia State
13 Senate.
S. R. 232
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3058
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
15 commend the Boy Scouts of America in Georgia for their commitment to the values and
16 principles of Scouting and for their service to the state.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of this body recognize February 26, 2025,
18 as Scouts Day at the Capitol and encourage all elected officials to take part in the events to
19 show appreciation for the Boy Scouts of America in Georgia.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the public and the
22 press.
S. R. 232
