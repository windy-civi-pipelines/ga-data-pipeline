25 LC 112 3006
House Resolution 299
By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13,
3 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and
4 WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight
5 leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence";
6 and
7 WHEREAS, Evan Marie Carr is a pioneering force in AI innovation and educational
8 technology who currently serves as the female co-founder of DarkViolet.ai, a majority
9 woman-owned artificial intelligence company dedicated to advancing AI-driven learning
10 solutions; and
11 WHEREAS, with over two decades of experience in the industry, she has led the
12 development of Learn with Lumi, a groundbreaking educational platform that exemplifies
13 her commitment to creating sophisticated yet human-centered AI solutions that enhance
14 accessibility and personalized learning for all students; and
H. R. 299

15 WHEREAS, Evan Marie Carr blends technical expertise with deep educational insight,
16 driving innovation in AI-powered education and setting new standards for how technology
17 can transform learning while preserving the essential human elements that make education
18 meaningful; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be
20 appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place
21 in which to live and work.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that

24 accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on
