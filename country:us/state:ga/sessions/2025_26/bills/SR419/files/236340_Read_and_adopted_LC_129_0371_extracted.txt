25 LC 129 0371
Senate Resolution 419
Payne of the 54th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Celebrating the birth of Ella Catherine Branch Tippins; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the months of anticipation and excitement shared by expectant parents are
3 always a cherished memory; and
4 WHEREAS, for Chelsea McElveen Tippins and Stephen Branch Tippins, Jr., those months
5 ended on February 11, 2025, at 9:54 A.M., when they became the proud parents of their
6 breathtaking baby, Ella Catherine Branch Tippins; and
7 WHEREAS, Ella arrived into the world, much like her older sister, in the midst of session
8 at five pounds, fourteen ounces and 18 and a half inches; and
9 WHEREAS, she proved a good luck charm for her father, whose bills, great bills for the
10 great people of the great state of Georgia, thereafter made the deadline for Crossover; and
11 WHEREAS, the devotion, patience, and understanding that Chelsea and Stephen demonstrate
12 daily provide the foundation and framework of love and success in which Ella will develop
13 and flourish; and
S. R. 419
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0371
14 WHEREAS, born with two older sisters who love and adore her, in addition to a wealth of
15 family and friends who will cherish, support, and guide her through every step of her life and
16 teach her, among other things, to love Christ, how to hit a draw, and the proper way to make
17 an old-fashioned, "Ella Cate" will never be alone; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the birth of one of this state's newest
19 citizens be recognized in an appropriate manner.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
21 recognize the very happy occasion of the birth of Ella Catherine Branch Tippins and extend
22 to her their most sincere best wishes for a long and healthy life and great happiness and
23 success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the proud parents
26 of Ella Catherine Branch Tippins.
