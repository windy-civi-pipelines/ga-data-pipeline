25 LC 112 3661
House Resolution 941
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating DeKalb County School District 2025 Georgia Scholar Rebecca Larkin; and
2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Rebecca Larkin, a senior at Tucker High School, has been recognized as a 2025
4 Georgia Scholar by the Georgia Department of Education; and
5 WHEREAS, the Georgia Scholar program identifies and honors high school seniors who
6 have excelled academically and contributed significantly to their communities; and
7 WHEREAS, the Georgia Department of Education coordinates this prestigious program each
8 year in partnership with local school districts to highlight students who embody the spirit of
9 scholarship and service; and
10 WHEREAS, these exceptional students have completed rigorous course loads, performed at
11 the highest levels across all subjects, engaged in interscholastic events, and served as leaders
12 in extracurricular activities; and
13 WHEREAS, each Georgia Scholar receives a special seal for their diploma and a
14 commemorative graduation cord in recognition of their outstanding achievements; and
H. R. 941
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3661
15 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
16 important objective of the General Assembly, and Rebecca has shown by academic
17 accomplishment that the pursuit and attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of
18 the students in this state; and
19 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia looks with great anticipation toward the future of this
20 bright and promising young citizen.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body congratulate DeKalb County School District 2025 Georgia Scholar
23 Rebecca Larkin and extend their best wishes for continued success in all future endeavors.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
26 Rebecca Larkin.
