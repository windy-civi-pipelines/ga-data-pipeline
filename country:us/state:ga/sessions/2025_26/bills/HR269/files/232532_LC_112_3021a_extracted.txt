25 LC 112 3021
House Resolution 269
Gilliard of the 162nd, Petrea of the 166th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring and celebrating the Metro Atlanta Chapter of the Savannah State University
2 National Alumni Association on its 65th anniversary; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Metro Atlanta Chapter of the Savannah State University National Alumni
4 Association (SSUNAA) has exemplified unwavering commitment and dedication to
5 Savannah State University (SSU) and the greater Atlanta community for over six decades;
6 and
7 WHEREAS, established with the purpose of fostering alumni engagement and supporting
8 the mission of Savannah State University, the Metro Atlanta Chapter has become a vibrant
9 and inclusive home for alumni spanning many generations, creating a diverse and unified
10 network of Tiger alumni; and
11 WHEREAS, one of the hallmarks of the Metro Atlanta Chapter's storied history is its Annual
12 Scholarship Awards Program, which generously provides annual scholarships to incoming
13 SSU freshmen; this program, sustained through the dedication of its members and matching
14 partnerships, has helped countless students achieve their dreams of higher education; and
H. R. 269
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3021
15 WHEREAS, as a "chapter on the move," the Metro Atlanta Chapter has continuously united
16 SSU alumni in the Atlanta area to propel the mission of Savannah State University forward
17 and ensure the legacy of the institution is preserved for future generations.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body honor and commend the Metro Atlanta Chapter of the Savannah
20 State University National Alumni Association on the occasion of its 65th anniversary for its
21 remarkable achievements, steadfast commitment to education, and immeasurable
22 contributions to Savannah State University and the Metro Atlanta community.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of this body celebrate the members of the
24 Metro Atlanta Chapter, both past and present, for their dedication, leadership, and enduring
25 Tiger spirit, which has positively impacted countless lives and strengthened the bonds of the
26 SSU family.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
29 Metro Atlanta Chapter of the Savannah State University National Alumni Association.
H. R. 269
