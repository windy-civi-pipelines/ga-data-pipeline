25 LC 129 0296
House Resolution 835
99th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Milton Eagles boys golf team for winning the 2024 GHSA 7A Boys Golf
2 State Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Milton Eagles boys golf team has established and maintained a proud
4 tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team has completed another triumphant season of golf
6 competition that included several prestigious tournament invites and wins; and
7 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
8 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
9 2024 campaign, the team captured its fifth boys golf state championship in school history and
10 fourth state championship in a row; and
11 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
12 athletic ability, the dedication and hard work, and the unity of purpose and spirit of each
13 member of the team; its elite skill level and continuing growth; and the astute direction of
14 Head Coach Tom Dell; and
H. R. 835
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0296
15 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
16 performing with confidence, grit, and precision over months of training and competition, it
17 is abundantly proper to call them champions.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body commend the Milton Eagles boys golf team for winning the 2024
20 GHSA 7A Boys Golf State Championship and extend their most sincere best wishes for
21 continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
24 Milton Eagles boys golf team.
