25 LC 112 3243
House Resolution 503
120th, Anderson of the 10th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Delane Roberts on the occasion of her retirement; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Delane Roberts has long been recognized by the citizens and leaders of this
4 state for the vital role she has played in state and local government and her deep personal
5 commitment to the State of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, for the past 32 years, she has diligently and conscientiously devoted
7 innumerable hours of her time, talents, and energy toward the betterment of the daily
8 operations of the Georgia General Assembly, as evidenced by her service to the Georgia
9 House of Representatives; and
10 WHEREAS, she began her career with the Georgia House of Representatives on January 1,
11 1993, and has served numerous members over the years, including 12 years as the Defense
12 & Veterans Affairs Committee Assistant; and
13 WHEREAS, she excelled in each of these roles, demonstrating a keen ability to identify
14 opportunities for her elected officials, maintaining an extensive network of contacts across
15 state agencies and departments, and always upholding a cheerful demeanor; and
H. R. 503
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3243
16 WHEREAS, this stellar woman exemplifies the courage, determination, leadership, and
17 character necessary to attain excellence in government service, and her contributions have
18 been recognized and appreciated by all who work under the Gold Dome; and
19 WHEREAS, a lifelong member of Bremen Church of Christ, she has remained actively
20 involved in her faith community, where her father, Jessie Roberts, and she was a devoted
21 caregiver to her mother, Sue Bearden Roberts, and her aunt, Eloise Bearden Bell, until their
22 passing; and
23 WHEREAS, she has announced her retirement, effective January 1, 2025, and is to be
24 honored for her decades of efficient, effective, unselfish, and dedicated public service to the
25 citizens of this state.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body recognize and commend Delane Roberts for her decades of service
28 to this state; congratulate her upon the grand occasion of her retirement; and extend the most
29 sincere best wishes for continued good health and happiness.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
32 Delane Roberts.
