25 LC 112 3446
House Resolution 667
the 107th, and McClain of the 109th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing July 9, 2025, as Level Up Day; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Level Up Day is a celebration dedicated to promoting progress, achievement,
3 and empowerment for individuals from all walks of life, fostering a culture of growth and
4 resilience; and
5 WHEREAS, the mission of Level Up Day is to inspire individuals to elevate their personal
6 and professional lives, encouraging active engagement in their communities and the pursuit
7 of lifelong learning; and
8 WHEREAS, through its focus on education, awareness, and civic engagement, Level Up Day
9 provides participants with opportunities to gain knowledge, set goals, and connect with
10 like-minded individuals; and
11 WHEREAS, Level Up Day has had a profound impact on both youth and adults, motivating
12 them to take charge of their futures, expand their knowledge, and contribute meaningfully
13 to their communities and businesses; and

14 WHEREAS, this transformative event features workshops, networking opportunities, and
15 initiatives designed to empower individuals to take the next step in their journey toward
16 success and positive social impact; and
17 WHEREAS, by championing values of empowerment, inclusivity, and education, Level Up
18 Day continues to inspire individuals to strive for excellence and make meaningful
19 contributions to society.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body recognize July 9, 2025, as Level Up Day.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
