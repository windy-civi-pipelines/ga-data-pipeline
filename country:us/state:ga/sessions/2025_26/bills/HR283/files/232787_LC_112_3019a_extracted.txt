House Resolution 283
the 128th, Buckner of the 137th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 20, 2025, as Community Health Centers Day and commending the
2 Georgia Primary Care Association; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Federally Qualified Health Centers located in 330 medically underserved
4 communities provide high-quality primary and preventive health care to more than 700,000
5 people in the State of Georgia, including more than 196,000 Medicaid recipients and 186,000
6 uninsured, the working poor, and numerous underinsured, elderly, homeless, and other
7 vulnerable populations, without regard to income status, cultural or ethnic heritage, or
8 disease conditions; and
9 WHEREAS, Community Health Centers contribute more than $1.2 billion per year to
10 Georgia's economy through indirect and induced economic activity, including $488 million
11 in household income and 4,509 Georgia jobs, saving the Georgia Medicaid program up to
12 33 percent in annual spending per Medicaid beneficiary; and
13 WHEREAS, Community Health Centers recruit and retain more than 1,500 health care
14 professionals where they are most needed and offer access to a wide range of cost-effective
15 medical and dental services, behavioral health services, prescription medicines, health

17 other support programs; and
18 WHEREAS, Community Health Centers have a track record spanning nearly six decades of

22 WHEREAS, Community Health Centers work in partnership with other health care
