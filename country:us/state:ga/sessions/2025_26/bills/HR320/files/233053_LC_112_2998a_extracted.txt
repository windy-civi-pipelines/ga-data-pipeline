25 LC 112 2998
House Resolution 320
of the 68th, Scott of the 76th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network in
2 Georgia; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network (ACS CAN) empowers
4 advocates across all states to raise their voices and influence evidence-based public policy
5 changes aimed at reducing the cancer burden in Georgia and beyond; and
6 WHEREAS, since 2001, ACS CAN has served as the nonprofit, nonpartisan advocacy
7 affiliate of the American Cancer Society, successfully securing billions of dollars in cancer
8 research funding, expanding access to quality, affordable health care, and advancing proven
9 tobacco control measures; and
10 WHEREAS, an estimated 66,000 Georgians will be diagnosed with cancer and more than
11 19,000 Georgians will lose their lives to the disease in 2025—ACS CAN is committed to
12 ensuring that these individuals will have access to fair and equitable care; and
13 WHEREAS, cancer patients face a multitude of challenges beyond their medical diagnoses,
14 including physical, emotional, and financial burdens that affect their quality of life; and
H. R. 320
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2998
15 WHEREAS, effective policies aimed at cancer prevention, early detection, and treatment not
16 only have the potential to save countless lives but also significantly reduce the financial
17 strain that this disease places on individuals and the state as a whole; and
18 WHEREAS, as Georgia continues to navigate complex legislative decisions, efforts to
19 combat cancer remain more critical than ever in providing comprehensive support for
20 patients and survivors; and
21 WHEREAS, ACS CAN and its dedicated volunteers—many of whom have been personally
22 impacted by cancer—continue to rally for meaningful policy changes and champion cancer
23 care as a top priority at the federal, state, and local levels.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body recognize and commend the American Cancer Society Cancer
26 Action Network in Georgia for its many outstanding contributions on behalf of the citizens
27 of this state and beyond as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
30 American Cancer Society Cancer Action Network in Georgia.
