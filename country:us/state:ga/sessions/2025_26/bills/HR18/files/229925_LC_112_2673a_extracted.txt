25 LC 112 2673
House Resolution 18
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Ramielle Moorer, Clayton County Public Schools' 2024-2025 Teacher of the
2 Year; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, nothing is more vital to the future of our state than the education of our young
4 people, and Ramielle Moorer exemplifies the selfless dedication that is characteristic of our
5 finest teachers; and
6 WHEREAS, Ramielle Moorer began her teaching career in 2011 at Forest Park Middle
7 School and later served as a music specialist at Suder Elementary for five years, during
8 which time she prepared numerous students for the Clayton County Public Schools (CCPS)
9 Fine Arts Magnet Track; and
10 WHEREAS, since 2017, she has served as the director of choral activities at Riverdale
11 Middle School, leading school wide morale-building initiatives, chairing the Hospitality
12 Committee, and sharing her expertise as the lead teacher mentor; and
H. R. 18
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2673
13 WHEREAS, during her tenure with CCPS, she has obtained Level 1 Orff-Schulwerk
14 Certification and has further served as the fine arts integration lead for the ArtsNow:
15 Engaging Minds Grant; and
16 WHEREAS, in 2020, she contributed to the development of music education as the CCPS
17 fine arts curriculum writing lead for middle school chorus and general music; and
18 WHEREAS, she has additionally played a key role in organizing the Georgia Music
19 Educators Association (GMEA) District VI Middle School Chorus Large Group Performance
20 Evaluation since 2020; and
21 WHEREAS, in recognition of such remarkable contributions to the education and welfare
22 of Georgia's students, it is abundantly fitting and proper for this body to honor the
23 extraordinary accomplishments and academic achievements of this outstanding educator.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body commend Ramielle Moorer, Clayton County Public Schools'
26 2024-2025 Teacher of the Year, and express their most sincere best wishes for continued
27 success.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Ramielle Moorer.
