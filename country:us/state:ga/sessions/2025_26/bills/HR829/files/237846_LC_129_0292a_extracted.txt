25 LC 129 0292
House Resolution 829
and Reeves of the 99th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Joseph Williams of Alpharetta High School for winning the 2024 GHSA 6A
2 Individual Track and Field State Championship in the 110m hurdles; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Joseph Williams, a junior at Alpharetta High School, maintained the school's
4 proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative athlete has completed a triumphant season of track competition
6 that included several prestigious invitationals and meets; and
7 WHEREAS, excellent skill, superior fitness, and extraordinary resilience made Joseph
8 Williams a spectacular runner to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to his
9 remarkable 2024 campaign, Joseph Williams captured the state championship in the 110m
10 hurdles with a time of 14.11 seconds, becoming the first state champion in the 110m hurdles
11 in school history, and
12 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of Joseph Williams is a clear indication of his
13 outstanding athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and his fighting spirit; his superb
14 discipline and commitment to track; and the astute direction of Head Coach AJ Merine; and
H. R. 829
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, as this outstanding athlete earned his well-deserved reputation for excellence
16 by performing with confidence, grit, and precision over months of training, conditioning, and
17 competition, it is abundantly proper to call him a champion.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body commend Joseph Williams of Alpharetta High School for winning
20 the 2024 GHSA 6A Individual Track and Field State Championship in the 110m hurdles and
21 extend their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
24 Joseph Williams.
