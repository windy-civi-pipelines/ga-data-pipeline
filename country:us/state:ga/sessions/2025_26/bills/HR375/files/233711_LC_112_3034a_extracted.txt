25 LC 112 3034
House Resolution 375
By: Representative Lupton of the 83rd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Mike Coleman, a longtime Georgia resident, has been honored as the recipient
3 of a 2024 Georgia Author of the Year Award for his coming-out memoir, The Way from Me
4 to Us; and
5 WHEREAS, published by Riverdale Avenue Books in New York City in 2023, The Way
6 from Me to Us has received widespread recognition for its candor and emotional depth, with
7 a second edition scheduled for publication in spring 2025; and
8 WHEREAS, since the book's initial publication, he has shared his work through readings and
9 signings at bookstores and literary events across New York, Florida, Alabama, South
10 Carolina, and Georgia; and
11 WHEREAS, as an independent writer, editor, and branding consultant in the latter part of his
12 career, Mike Coleman further authored An Aquarium for Georgia: Commemorating the
13 Construction of the Georgia Aquarium for the Brasfield & Gorrie construction firm; and
H. R. 375
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3034
14 WHEREAS, a professional writer for nearly 45 years before retiring in 2020, Mike Coleman
15 has received awards for news reporting and writing for nonprofit organizations and
16 businesses in the technology industry; and
17 WHEREAS, he continues to serve as a member of the Authors Guild, the oldest and largest
18 association of published authors in the United States, and he is active in both the Atlanta
19 Writers Club and the Broadleaf Writers Association; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding individual be
21 appropriately honored during this special time.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Mike Coleman upon receiving his 2024
24 Georgia Author of the Year Award as well as extend sincere best wishes for future health and
25 happiness.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mike
28 Coleman.
