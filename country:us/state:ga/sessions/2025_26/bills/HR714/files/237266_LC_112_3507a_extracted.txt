25 LC 112 3507
House Resolution 714
of the 109th, and Herring of the 145th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Brittany Thompkins, recipient of the 2025 Georgia Single Parent "Legacy
2 Builders" Award; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Brittany Thompkins of Macon, Georgia, has recently been named a recipient
4 of the 2025 Georgia Single Parent "Legacy Builders" Award in recognition of her exemplary
5 dedication to her family, perseverance through adversity, and commitment to building a
6 lasting legacy for her children; and
7 WHEREAS, a devoted single mother of two, Brittany has consistently demonstrated
8 resilience and strength in the face of financial hardship, choosing to overcome challenges
9 with courage, determination, and an unwavering focus on her children's success; and
10 WHEREAS, driven by a desire to provide meaningful opportunities for her family, Brittany
11 founded Sweet Serenity Lemonade as a creative solution to support her son's travel baseball
12 expenses, ultimately transforming it into a thriving business venture through her
13 entrepreneurial spirit and hard work; and
H. R. 714
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3507
14 WHEREAS, in addition to her business, Brittany successfully balances full-time employment
15 with her parenting responsibilities, prioritizing her children's growth, education, and
16 well-being while modeling the values of selflessness, dedication, and responsibility; and
17 WHEREAS, she is a valued member of Bibb Mount Zion Baptist Church, where she remains
18 grounded in her faith and supported by a community that recognizes her perseverance and
19 joy in uplifting others; and
20 WHEREAS, her humility, work ethic, and positive outlook serve as a daily inspiration to her
21 children and community, exemplifying the qualities of leadership and determination that
22 define a true legacy builder; and
23 WHEREAS, through her actions, Brittany Thompkins has instilled in her children the
24 importance of resilience, initiative, and the pursuit of their goals, setting a powerful example
25 of what can be achieved through perseverance and love.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body commend Brittany Thompkins for her outstanding achievements
28 as a mother, entrepreneur, and community role model as well as congratulate her upon being
29 named a recipient of the 2025 Georgia Single Parent "Legacy Builders" Award.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
32 Brittany Thompkins.
