30 innovative work in the areas of technology, manufacturing, or agriculture?"
31 All persons desiring to vote in favor of ratifying the proposed amendment shall vote "Yes."
32 All persons desiring to vote against ratifying the proposed amendment shall vote "No." If
33 such amendment shall be ratified as provided in said Paragraph of the Constitution, it shall
34 become a part of the Constitution of this state.
