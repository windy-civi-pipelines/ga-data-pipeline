25 LC 112 2802
House Resolution 245
McQueen of the 61st, Carson of the 46th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Aaliyah Phillips, Miss Cobb County 2025; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Aaliyah Phillips was crowned Miss Cobb County 2025 at the 66th annual Miss
4 Cobb County Scholarship Competition in Marietta, Georgia, a title that recognizes her
5 dedication to education, leadership, and community service; and
6 WHEREAS, she will go on to compete for the title of Miss Georgia in June 2025, the winner
7 of which will represent the State of Georgia in the Miss America Competition; and
8 WHEREAS, the Miss Cobb County Scholarship Board additionally awarded her with a
9 $10,000 cash scholarship to support her pursuit of a Doctor of Education in education policy
10 as well as her ultimate goal of serving as the Secretary of the United States Department of
11 Education; and
12 WHEREAS, a 2024 magna cum laude graduate of Columbus State University, she earned
13 a Bachelor of Science in elementary education while maintaining consistent placement on
14 the President's List and serving as a Student Government Association senator; and

15 WHEREAS, she has further honed her leadership skills by serving as a communications
16 intern for United States Senator Jon Ossoff, training at the Ron Clark Academy, and
17 attending the Disney Dreamers Academy; and
18 WHEREAS, Aaliyah is currently in her first year as a kindergarten teacher and has dedicated
19 over 500 hours to community service, actively participating in local events as a vocalist,
20 speaker, advocate, and volunteer; and
21 WHEREAS, she further serves as the founder of SPREAD: Spreading Literacy Through
22 Reading, an initiative focused on promoting literacy equality, through which she has
23 partnered with organizations such as Girls, Inc.; United Way of Greater Atlanta; the Literacy
24 Alliance; and Cobb Collaborative, Educating, Engaging and Empowering; and
25 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this distinguished young woman be
26 appropriately honored.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body congratulate Aaliyah Phillips for winning the title of Miss Cobb
29 County 2025, commend her for her outstanding service on behalf of the students of this state,
30 and extend best wishes for success in future competition.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
33 Aaliyah Phillips.
