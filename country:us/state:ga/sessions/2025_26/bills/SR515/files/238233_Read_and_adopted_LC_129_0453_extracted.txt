25 LC 129 0453
Senate Resolution 515
Summers of the 13th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Larry Wayne Grogan; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Larry Wayne Grogan on March 20, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Larry Wayne Grogan was born on November 27, 1937, in Mitchell County, a
5 beloved son of George Hayes Grogan and Ruth Lemacks Grogan; and
6 WHEREAS, he served as a guardian of this nation's freedom and liberty with the United
7 States Air Force, valiantly and courageously protecting his fellow Americans; and
8 WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, Larry Wayne Grogan was an active member
9 of First Baptist Church of Camilla; and
10 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife, Kay, and was blessed with two
11 remarkable children, Paige and Matt, and a wonderful grandchild, Caroline; and
S. R. 515
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0453
12 WHEREAS, he gave inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep concern
13 for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding he demonstrated to his
14 family and friends were admired by others; and
15 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
16 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
17 made this world a better place in which to live; and
18 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Larry Wayne Grogan will long be
19 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
20 had the great fortune of knowing him.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 join in honoring the life and memory of Larry Wayne Grogan and express their deepest and
23 most sincere regret at his passing.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the family of Larry
26 Wayne Grogan.
