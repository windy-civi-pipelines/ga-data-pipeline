25 LC 129 0055
House Resolution 62
Hong of the 103rd
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Mary D'Souza Sequeira; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Mary D'Souza Sequeira, an Indian female Olympian, distinguished herself as
3 a pioneer in international sports, representing India in track and field as well as field hockey;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, she competed in the women's 100 and 200 meters at the 1952 Helsinki Summer
6 Olympics as part of the first female contingent to represent India at the Olympics; and
7 WHEREAS, at the 1951 Asian Games, Mary D'Souza Sequeira earned a Bronze Medal in
8 the 200 meters and a Silver Medal in the 4x100 meter relay; and
9 WHEREAS, she made history at the 1954 Asian Games by competing in the 4x100 meter
10 relay and becoming one of the first Indian women to win a Gold Medal alongside her
11 teammates; and
12 WHEREAS, in 2013, Mary D'Souza Sequeira was honored with the Dhyan Chand Award
13 by the Government of India in recognition of her lifetime achievements in sport and her
14 outstanding contributions to athletics; and

15 WHEREAS, this distinguished gentlewoman has given inspiration to many through her high
16 ideals, morals, and deep concern for her fellow citizens, and she possesses the vast wisdom
17 which only comes through experience and the strength of character which is achieved
18 through overcoming the many challenges of life; and
19 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
20 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
22 remarkable and distinguished individual be appropriately recognized.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body commend Mary D'Souza Sequeira for her many exemplary athletic
25 achievements as well as extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and
26 happiness.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mary
29 D'Souza Sequeira.
