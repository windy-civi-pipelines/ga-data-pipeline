25 LC 112 2664
Senate Resolution 17
Albers of the 56th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Family and Consumer Sciences; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Family and Consumer Sciences (FCS) is a comprehensive body of research,
3 knowledge, and skills that helps people make informed decisions about their well-being,
4 relationships, and quality of life; and
5 WHEREAS, with a unique focus on families, work, and their inter-relationships, FCS
6 professionals have long worked to empower individuals and families in Georgia to better
7 manage the challenges of living and working in a diverse global society; and
8 WHEREAS, national, state, and local policy makers; education administrators; and the public
9 need an understanding of FCS and its direct relevance to the serious societal concerns of
10 today, such as obesity; food safety; financial literacy; the complexities of family life; and the
11 role of parents, caregivers, and other adults in preparing children for school readiness; and
12 WHEREAS, FCS professionals continue to provide relevant and useful resources to help
13 students prepare for careers in teaching, extension, human services, and a variety of other
14 fields; and
S. R. 17
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2664
15 WHEREAS, federal, state, and local decision makers strongly influence the allocation of
16 funds for FCS and the administrative and curricular structure at all levels; and
17 WHEREAS, the Family, Career, Community Leaders of America (FCCLA) and other
18 entities within Family and Consumer Sciences support the development and implementation
19 of initiatives designed to promote uniform name recognition and foster a greater
20 understanding of the important role that the organization plays in preparing families to
21 successfully manage the many and varied challenges of everyday life.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 recognize and commend the affiliates, communities, and entities within Family and
24 Consumer Sciences and support the development and implementation of national, state, and
25 local intervention strategies to sustain Family and Consumer Sciences in the current
26 economic and education environment.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
28 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Family and
29 Consumer Sciences.
