25 LC 112 3025
House Resolution 282
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the 2024 Technical College System of Georgia award
2 winners; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Technical College System of Georgia (TCSG) stands as a beacon of
4 excellence in technical education, contributing significantly to workforce development and
5 economic growth in our state; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Occupational Award of Leadership (GOAL) program annually
7 recognizes outstanding technical college students across the state, celebrating their academic
8 achievements, leadership, and service to their communities; and
9 WHEREAS, Nicholas Rucker, a physical therapist assistant student from Athens Technical
10 College, has been named the 2024 GOAL winner, embodying the dedication, skill, and
11 passion for excellence that the award seeks to honor; and
12 WHEREAS, the Rick Perkins Award for Excellence in Technical Instruction is bestowed
13 upon technical college instructors who demonstrate exceptional teaching abilities, making
14 significant contributions to the education of Georgia's skilled workforce; and
H. R. 282
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3025
15 WHEREAS, Sharoko Farley, an instructor from Central Georgia Technical College, has been
16 distinguished as the 2024 recipient of the Rick Perkins Award, showcasing exemplary
17 dedication to student success and instructional excellence; and
18 WHEREAS, the Dinah Culbreath Wayne Exceptional Adult Georgian in Literacy Education
19 (EAGLE) Award recognizes extraordinary achievements within the state's adult education
20 programs, highlighting individuals who have overcome challenges to pursue lifelong learning
21 and literacy; and
22 WHEREAS, Patria Brown, recognized for exceptional academic achievements within her
23 local education program at Cobb County School District, has been honored as TCSG's 2024
24 Adult Education Student of the Year, reflecting the perseverance, hard work, and
25 commitment to personal and professional growth that the EAGLE Award celebrates; and
26 WHEREAS, the Outstanding Adult Education Teacher of the Year award acknowledges the
27 vital role of educators in adult learning, recognizing those who provide inspiration, support,
28 and high-quality instruction to their students; and
29 WHEREAS, Rhea Brashear, an adult education instructor from Southern Regional Technical
30 College, has been awarded the 2024 Outstanding Adult Education Teacher of the Year,
31 demonstrating exceptional teaching and mentoring abilities that contribute to the success and
32 empowerment of adult learners.
33 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
34 the members of this body recognize and commend the 2024 Technical College System of
35 Georgia award winners and do hereby congratulate Nicholas Rucker, Sharoko Farley, Patria
36 Brown, and Rhea Brashear on their prestigious achievements.
H. R. 282
- 2 -
25 LC 112 3025
37 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
38 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to
39 Nicholas Rucker, Sharoko Farley, Patria Brown, and Rhea Brashear.
