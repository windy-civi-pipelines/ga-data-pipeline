25 LC 129 0300
House Resolution 786
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Karly Nguyen, Rome High School's 2025 STAR Student; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a gifted student at Rome High School, Karly Nguyen has been recognized for
4 her superlative achievement and selected as Rome High School's 2025 STAR Student; and
5 WHEREAS, to be selected as a STAR Student, a student must have obtained the highest
6 score in one administration of the three-part Scholastic Aptitude Test and be in the top 10
7 percent or top ten students of his or her class; and
8 WHEREAS, each school has one STAR Student who in turn has the opportunity to choose
9 a STAR Teacher who has been inspiring and encouraging throughout that student's high
10 school career; and
11 WHEREAS, educator Seth Winstead was chosen by Karly as Rome High School's 2025
12 STAR Teacher for the great support and guidance provided to her throughout the years; and
13 WHEREAS, Karly has personified the selfless dedication, mental agility, verbal adroitness,
14 and personal maturity necessary for superlative academic performance; and
H. R. 786
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0300
15 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
16 important objective of the General Assembly, and Karly has shown by academic
17 accomplishment that the pursuit and attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of
18 the students in this state; and
19 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia looks with great anticipation toward the future of this
20 bright and promising young citizen.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body heartily commend Karly Nguyen for being named Rome High
23 School's 2025 STAR Student and express their most sincere best wishes for continued
24 academic success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Karly
27 Nguyen.
