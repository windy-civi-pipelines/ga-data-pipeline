25 LC 129 0098
Senate Resolution 115
of the 4th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Lanier County High School Lady Bulldogs for winning the GHSA Class
2 A Division II State Softball Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Lanier County High School Lady Bulldogs has established and maintained
4 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary leadership made this team
6 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its extraordinary
7 2024 campaign, the team triumphed in a hard-fought battle to become the GHSA Class A
8 Division II State Softball Champions; and
9 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of MVP Jaden Benefield and her superb pitching
10 ability are a clear display of the outstanding athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity
11 of purpose and spirit among each member of the team as well as the astute direction of head
12 coach Ben Davis; and
13 WHEREAS, the team performed with precision during the season and earned its
14 well-deserved reputation for high standards of athletic achievement, the quality of its play,
15 and the stiff competition it presented to its opponents; and
S. R. 115
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0098
16 WHEREAS, this accomplished team achieved a remarkable 24-4 record and earned Lanier
17 County its first softball championship ever.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
19 congratulate the Lanier County High School Lady Bulldogs on their victory at the GHSA
20 Class A Division II State Softball Championship and extend best wishes for their continued
21 success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
23 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Lanier County
24 High School Lady Bulldogs.
S. R. 115
