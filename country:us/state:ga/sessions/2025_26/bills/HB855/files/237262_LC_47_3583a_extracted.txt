Section 1:
25 LC 47 3583
By: Representative Taylor of the 173rd
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to authorize the governing authority of the City of Thomasville to levy an
2 excise tax pursuant to subsection (b) of Code Section 48-13-51 of the O.C.G.A., approved
3 April 27, 2016 (Ga. L. 2016, p. 3799), so as to revise provisions related to the expenditure
4 of certain proceeds of such tax; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and
5 for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 An Act to authorize the governing authority of the City of Thomasville to levy an excise tax
9 pursuant to subsection (b) of Code Section 48-13-51 of the O.C.G.A., approved April 27,
10 2016 (Ga. L. 2016, p. 3799), is amended by revising paragraph (1) of Section 3 as follows:
11 "(1) In each fiscal year during which a tax is collected pursuant to paragraph (2) of
12 subsection (b) of Code Section 48-13-51 of the O.C.G.A., an amount equal to not less
13 than 50 percent of the total amount of taxes collected that exceeds the amount of taxes
14 that would be collected at the rate to 5 percent shall be expended for promoting tourism,
15 conventions, and trade shows by the destination marketing organization designated by the
16 City of Thomasville or such other entity authorized to administer and expend the
H. B. 855

17 proceeds of such tax under a contract authorized by paragraph (2) of subsection (e) of
18 Code Section 48-13-51 of the O.C.G.A.; and"
19 SECTION 2.
20 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
