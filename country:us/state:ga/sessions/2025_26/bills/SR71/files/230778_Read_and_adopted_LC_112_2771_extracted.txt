25 LC 112 2771
Senate Resolution 71
the 29th, Anderson of the 24th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Employees' Retirement System of Georgia upon its 75th anniversary; and
2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Employees' Retirement System of Georgia (ERSGA) was established in
4 1950, and it has served as a trusted partner in retirement readiness for Georgia's workforce
5 over the past 75 years; and
6 WHEREAS, ERSGA administers five distinct defined benefit pension plans, two life
7 insurance plans, and three defined contribution plans, including the Peach State Reserves
8 (PSR) 401(k) and 457 plans; and
9 WHEREAS, ERSGA further serves as the Social Security Administrator for political
10 subdivisions across Georgia, supporting retirement on a statewide level; and
11 WHEREAS, ERSGA is guided by its mission to promote a dignified retirement and is
12 committed to delivering accurate and timely benefits supported by state-of-the-art
13 technology; and
S. R. 71
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2771
14 WHEREAS, ERSGA remains dedicated to operational excellence, innovation, and superior
15 customer service, upholding its commitment to integrity and continuous improvement for the
16 members, retirees, and beneficiaries it serves.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
18 congratulate the Employees' Retirement System of Georgia upon the grand occasion of its
19 75th anniversary and extend best wishes for continued success.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Employees'
22 Retirement System of Georgia.
