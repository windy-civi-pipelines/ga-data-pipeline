25 LC 112 3488
Senate Resolution 472
Harbison of the 15th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Aligarh Alumni Association of Atlanta (AAAA) and its
2 contributions to education, community service, and Atlanta's largest Muslim community
3 gathering on Eid-ul-Fitr; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Aligarh Alumni Association of Atlanta (AAAA) is a nonprofit organization
5 dedicated to promoting education, social welfare, cultural heritage, and interfaith
6 understanding, inspired by the vision of Sir Syed Ahmed Khan and Aligarh Muslim
7 University (AMU), Aligarh, India; and
8 WHEREAS, the AAAA has made significant contributions to educational advancement by
9 providing scholarships, mentorship, and career counseling for underprivileged and deserving
10 students, thereby equipping youth with knowledge and opportunities for a better future; and
11 WHEREAS, the AAAA actively promotes literary and cultural initiatives, including the
12 preservation and promotion of the Urdu language, literary events, and educational and social
13 seminars, enriching the intellectual and cultural landscape of the community; and
S. R. 472
- 1 -

14 WHEREAS, the AAAA has consistently engaged in social welfare and humanitarian efforts,
15 including support for charity organizations, food distribution programs, and disaster relief
16 initiatives, improving the quality of life for many individuals and families in need; and
17 WHEREAS, the AAAA plays an instrumental role in organizing and supporting Atlanta's
18 largest Muslim community gathering on Eid-ul-Fitr, an event that fosters interfaith
19 understanding, cultural unity, and social cohesion by bringing together hundreds of
20 community members in the spirit of peace and togetherness; and
21 WHEREAS, this annual Eid-ul-Fitr celebration serves as a platform for cultural exchange,
22 philanthropy, and strengthening bonds among diverse communities, furthering the values of
23 inclusivity, mutual respect, and harmony; and
24 WHEREAS, the AAAA's commitment to education, cultural preservation, and social service
25 has left a lasting impact on the local, national, and international levels, making it a beacon
26 of community service and empowerment.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
28 recognize and commend the Aligarh Alumni Association of Atlanta (AAAA) for its
29 outstanding contributions to education, community service, and cultural heritage as well as
30 acknowledge and celebrate the significance of Atlanta's largest Muslim community gathering
31 on Eid-ul-Fitr, recognizing its role in fostering unity and cultural appreciation among diverse
32 populations.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
34 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Aligarh
35 Alumni Association of Atlanta (AAAA).
S. R. 472
