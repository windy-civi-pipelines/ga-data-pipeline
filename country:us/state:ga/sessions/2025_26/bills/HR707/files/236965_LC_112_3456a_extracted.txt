2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Social Work is a profession truly dedicated to helping people; and
4 WHEREAS, Social Workers are dedicated to enhancing well-being and helping meet the
5 basic needs of all people, especially the most vulnerable in our society; and
6 WHEREAS, Social Workers follow a Code of Ethics that calls on them to fight social
