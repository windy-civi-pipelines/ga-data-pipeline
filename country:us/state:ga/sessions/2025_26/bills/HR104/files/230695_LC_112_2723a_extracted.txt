25 LC 112 2723
House Resolution 104
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Hebron Christian Academy football team for winning the 2024 GHSA
2 Class Private A-3A State Football Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Hebron Christian Academy football team has established and maintained
4 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team has completed a triumphant season of football competition
6 by posting an outstanding record of 12 wins and only two losses; and
7 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
8 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
9 2024 campaign, the team captured its first state football championship; and
10 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school by winning the 2024 GHSA
11 Class Private A-3A State Football Championship against Prince Avenue Christian School
12 with a final score of 56-28; and
13 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
14 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
H. R. 104
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2723
15 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Head Coach
16 Jonathan Gess; and
17 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
18 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
19 abundantly proper to call them champions.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body congratulate the Hebron Christian Academy football team for
22 winning the 2024 GHSA Class Private A-3A State Football Championship and extend best
23 wishes for their continued success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
26 Hebron Christian Academy football team.
