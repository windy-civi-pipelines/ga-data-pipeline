25 LC 112 3226
House Resolution 467
132nd, and Mitchell of the 88th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Jack and Jill of America, Inc.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Jack and Jill of America, Inc., is a membership organization of mothers with
3 children ages 2-19, dedicated to nurturing future African-American leaders by strengthening
4 children through leadership development, volunteer service, philanthropic giving, and civic
5 duty; and
6 WHEREAS, the late Marion Stubbs Thomas founded Jack and Jill of America, Inc., on
7 January 24, 1938, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania when 20 mothers came together to discuss
8 creating an organization to provide social, cultural, and educational opportunities for youth
9 between the ages of two and 19 and in 1946, ten chapters were involved in the national
10 restructuring process where a constitution and bylaws were drawn up and the organization
11 was incorporated under the laws of the State of Delaware; and
12 WHEREAS, today, this organization has 262 chapters nationwide, representing 50,000
13 families in seven regions with each chapter planning annual programming activities guided
14 under a national theme, and through service projects, it creates a medium of contact for
15 children to stimulate their growth and development; and
