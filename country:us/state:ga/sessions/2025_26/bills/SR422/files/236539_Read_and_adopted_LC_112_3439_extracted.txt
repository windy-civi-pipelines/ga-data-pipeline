25 LC 112 3439
Senate Resolution 422
By: Senator Still of the 48th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Katie Bagosy has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to supporting
3 veterans and advancing mental health initiatives, guided by her deep-rooted ties to the
4 military as the daughter of two Navy veterans; and
5 WHEREAS, her late husband, Tom, honorably served as a guardian of this nation's freedom
6 and liberty with the United States Marine Corps at Camp Lejeune, courageously and
7 valiantly protecting his fellow Americans during deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan; and
8 WHEREAS, she has been a steadfast advocate for veterans during her years of service with
9 the Mental Health Council in Forsyth County, and she serves as the Outreach Coordinator
10 at AboutFace-USA, a distinguished nonprofit organization dedicated to providing vital
11 support and resources to veterans; and
12 WHEREAS, this remarkable gentlewoman has given inspiration to many through her high
13 ideals, morals, and deep concern for her fellow citizens, and she possesses the vast wisdom
14 which only comes through experience and the strength of character which is achieved
15 through overcoming the many challenges of life; and
S. R. 422

16 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
17 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
19 exemplary individual be appropriately honored.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
21 recognize and commend Katie Bagosy for her dedicated service on behalf of our nation's
22 servicemembers and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and happiness.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Katie Bagosy.
