25 LC 129 0276
House Resolution 500
75th, and Cannon of the 58th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Pharaoh Kelley; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Pharaoh Kelley has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the
3 vital role that he has played in leadership and his deep personal commitment to the welfare
4 of the citizens of Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, as founder of Legendary Visions Realty, Pharaoh guides individuals through
6 the home buying or selling process in the State of Georgia; and
7 WHEREAS, Pharaoh's first book, titled Insurmountable Victory, details his experience from
8 homelessness and an absent father; and
9 WHEREAS, reflecting on his childhood hardship, Pharaoh travels to schools and different
10 organizations to encourage students to rise above obstacles in their own lives; and
11 WHEREAS, his organization Legendary Vision helps students and youth of all ages to bridge
12 the gap from young adulthood to productive self-reliant individuals who are in control of
13 their own lives in the real world; and
H. R. 500
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0276
14 WHEREAS, Legendary Vision teaches the importance of having a positive mindset no
15 matter the current situation and helps students build positive self-esteem; and
16 WHEREAS, Pharaoh has given inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep
17 concern for his fellow citizens, and he possesses the vast wisdom which only comes through
18 experience and the strength of character which is achieved through overcoming the many
19 challenges of life; and
20 WHEREAS, he is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
21 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
23 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body recognize and commend Pharaoh Kelley for his efficient, effective,
26 unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia and extend the most sincere
27 best wishes for continued health and happiness.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Pharaoh Kelley.
