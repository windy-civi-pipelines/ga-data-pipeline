and Carter of the 93rd
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dana Fowle for her outstanding contributions to journalism
2 and the community; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Dana Fowle is an Emmy Award-winning journalist whose investigative
4 reporting has significantly impacted the lives of Georgia's citizens; and
5 WHEREAS, Dana Fowle joined FOX 5 Atlanta in 1995 as a general assignment reporter, and
6 in August 1999, she became the Consumer Reporter for the FOX 5 I-Team, where she has
7 consistently delivered in-depth investigations that have led to meaningful change; and
8 WHEREAS, her investigative work on Georgia's child-welfare system played a pivotal role
9 in prompting changes to state child-care laws and the reorganization of top management at
10 the Department of Family and Children's Services, demonstrating her commitment to the
11 well-being of Georgia's children and families; and
12 WHEREAS, Dana's journalistic integrity and dedication to consumer advocacy have led her
13 to cover a wide range of topics, including health, finance, environmental concerns, fraud, and
14 various consumer protection issues, all of which have empowered and educated the public;
15 and

16 WHEREAS, in addition to her journalism career, Dana is an exceptional storyteller,
17 consultant, pet food business owner, and community organizer, further demonstrating her
18 passion for service and leadership beyond the newsroom; and
19 WHEREAS, over the past two decades at WAGA-TV, Dana has received nearly a dozen
20 Emmy Awards, a testament to her excellence in investigative journalism and consumer
21 reporting; and
22 WHEREAS, Dana Fowle's unwavering commitment to truth, justice, and public service has
23 made a lasting impact on the State of Georgia and beyond, and she continues to be an
24 invaluable asset to the field of journalism and the broader community.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body recognize and commend Dana Fowle for her exemplary
27 contributions to investigative journalism and consumer advocacy, applaud her for the
28 positive change she has fostered through her reporting, and extend their sincere best wishes
29 for continued success in all her endeavors.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dana
32 Fowle.
