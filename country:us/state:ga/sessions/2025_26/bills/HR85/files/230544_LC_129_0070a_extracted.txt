25 LC 129 0070
House Resolution 85
Mitchell of the 88th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Authors Expert Academy for its contributions on World Book and
2 Copyright Day and recognizing April 23, 2025, as the Authors Expert Academy Day in
3 Georgia; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, World Book and Copyright Day, established by UNESCO and observed
5 annually on April 23, celebrates the power of books to promote knowledge, bridge cultural
6 divides, and foster understanding across communities and nations; and
7 WHEREAS, the Authors Expert Academy, an initiative of the TIUA School of Business and
8 TIUA Corp. Florida, has demonstrated exemplary leadership in promoting the enjoyment of
9 books and reading while raising awareness about the importance of intellectual property
10 rights and copyright protections; and
11 WHEREAS, the Authors Expert Academy provides a transformative platform for aspiring
12 and established authors, equipping them with the tools, education, and mentorship needed
13 to elevate their craft and contribute meaningfully to global literature; and
H. R. 85
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0070
14 WHEREAS, the academy actively encourages the celebration of books, authors, and
15 publishers, fostering a deep appreciation for storytelling and creative expression in Georgia
16 and beyond; and
17 WHEREAS, the Authors Expert Academy aligns with the principles of World Book and
18 Copyright Day by raising awareness about the importance of intellectual property rights,
19 advocating for fair recognition and protection of authors' contributions, and supporting
20 efforts to combat copyright infringement; and
21 WHEREAS, the academy has inspired authors and readers to connect through stories that
22 bridge gaps, uplift communities, and drive meaningful change, embodying the spirit of
23 World Book and Copyright Day.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body commend the Authors Expert Academy for its outstanding
26 contributions to the promotion of books, reading, and intellectual property rights in alignment
27 with the goals of World Book and Copyright Day as well as recognize April 23, 2025, as the
28 Authors Expert Academy Day in Georgia in honor of its efforts to inspire creativity,
29 celebrate the written word, and advocate for the rights of authors and publishers.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 Authors Expert Academy.
H. R. 85
