25 LC 112 3072
House Resolution 342
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Shaw Industries Group, the Cartersville-Bartow County
2 Chamber of Commerce 2024 Manufacturer of the Year; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, since beginning its journey in Northwest Georgia, Shaw Industries Group has
4 become a global leader in the floor covering industry while remaining an integral part of
5 Bartow County for more than 40 years; and
6 WHEREAS, Shaw Industries Group has invested nearly $1 billion into the State of Georgia
7 over the past five years, including the installation of a half-mile solar array at its Adairsville
8 facility and the establishment of the Shaw Family Health Center and Hiring Center in
9 Cartersville; and
10 WHEREAS, Shaw Industries Group employs more than 2,700 associates across multiple
11 Bartow County locations and fosters a culture of support and empowerment while
12 contributing significantly to local economic growth and supporting numerous not-for-profit
13 organizations; and
14 WHEREAS, as additional testament to its dedication to the community, Shaw Industries
15 Group was recently recognized by Forbes magazine as a Best-In-State Employer; and
H. R. 342
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3072
16 WHEREAS, Shaw Industries Group exemplifies excellence, innovation, and community
17 commitment, making it a deserving recipient of this prestigious award.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body congratulate Shaw Industries Group upon being named the
20 Cartersville-Bartow County Chamber of Commerce 2024 Manufacturer of the Year;
21 commend Shaw Industries Group for its many outstanding contributions on behalf of the
22 citizens of Georgia; and extend best wishes for its continued growth, development, and
23 success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Shaw
26 Industries Group.
