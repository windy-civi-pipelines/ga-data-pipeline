25 LC 112 3154
House Resolution 420
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Salleigh Grubbs for her dedicated service as Chairwoman of
2 the Cobb County Republican Party; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Salleigh Grubbs has faithfully served as Chairwoman of the Cobb County
4 Republican Party, demonstrating unwavering commitment to conservative values, principled
5 leadership, and tireless advocacy for the citizens of Cobb County; and
6 WHEREAS, during her tenure, Salleigh Grubbs worked diligently to strengthen the party's
7 grassroots efforts, foster unity among members, and support Republican candidates at every
8 level of government, contributing significantly to the success and growth of the party; and
9 WHEREAS, her dedication to empowering local voices and engaging the community in the
10 democratic process has inspired countless individuals to become involved in civic and
11 political life, leaving a lasting legacy of service and leadership; and
12 WHEREAS, Salleigh Grubbs' ability to bring people together, her focus on promoting
13 policies that uplift families and businesses, and her steadfast commitment to the ideals of
14 freedom, limited government, and personal responsibility have made her a respected and
15 admired figure in Cobb County and beyond; and
H. R. 420
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3154
16 WHEREAS, her exceptional service has not only benefited the Cobb County Republican
17 Party but has also positively impacted the broader community by advocating for policies that
18 promote prosperity, security, and opportunity for all; and
19 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that Salleigh Grubbs' significant contributions and her
20 legacy of leadership and dedication to the Cobb County Republican Party be appropriately
21 recognized.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body commend Salleigh Grubbs for her exemplary service as
24 Chairwoman of the Cobb County Republican Party, extend their heartfelt gratitude for her
25 many years of dedication and leadership, and convey their best wishes for her future
26 endeavors.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Salleigh Grubbs.
