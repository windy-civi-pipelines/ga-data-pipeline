25 LC 129 0467
Senate Resolution 550
and Harbison of the 15th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Nikki Glanton on Women Veterans Appreciation Day at the
2 Capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Master Sergeant (Ret.) Nikki Glanton served as a guardian of this nation's
4 freedom and liberty with the United States Army Reserve, valiantly and courageously
5 protecting her fellow Americans from 2001 to 2022; and
6 WHEREAS, over the course of her decorated career, she completed five combat tours in the
7 Middle East and earned more than 25 awards and decorations for her exemplary leadership
8 and unwavering commitment to service; and
9 WHEREAS, currently, Nikki is a respected communications and public affairs executive,
10 bringing over 25 years of experience across the public, private, and nonprofit sectors; and
11 WHEREAS, as the Vice President of Communications and Public Affairs for the Atlanta
12 Police Foundation, she directs impactful, citywide communication strategies that strengthen
13 public safety, elevate community trust, and connect business and civic leaders to initiatives
14 designed to make Atlanta the nation's safest large city; and
S. R. 550
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, her leadership has been pivotal in launching and communicating the mission
16 of the Public Safety Training Center, one of the most significant public-private infrastructure
17 projects in Atlanta's history; and
18 WHEREAS, she was recognized in 2020 as one of the most influential African American
19 businesswomen to follow on LinkedIn, and is widely respected for her successful transition
20 from military to civilian life as well as her ability to inspire others to do the same through
21 storytelling, authenticity, and service; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
23 exemplary veteran are appropriately honored.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 recognize and commend Nikki Glanton for the dedication, bravery, and sacrifices she has
26 made to serve her country and extend sincere best wishes for her continued health, happiness,
27 and success.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
29 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Nikki Glanton.
