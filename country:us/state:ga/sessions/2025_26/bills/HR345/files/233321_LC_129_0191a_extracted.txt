House Resolution 345
By: Representative Glaize of the 67th
A RESOLUTION
2 Yellow Rose Nikki T. Randall Servant Leadership Award; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, in 1999, Representative Nikki T. Randall became the first African American
4 female elected to the General Assembly from middle Georgia, and over her 17 years of
5 service as a legislator, she held numerous leadership positions, including chair of the Georgia
6 Women's Legislative Caucus; and
7 WHEREAS, every year during Women's History Month, the Georgia Women's Legislative
8 Caucus recognizes outstanding women in Georgia who exemplify the leadership and
9 inspiration embodied by Representative Randall; and
10 WHEREAS, since her election, Council Member Angelette Mealing has been a grassroots
11 leader fighting for public safety, youth and senior initiatives, affordable housing, economic
12 recovery, and the overall improvement of the quality of life in Union City; and
13 WHEREAS, Council Member Mealing serves in leadership roles at both the federal and state
14 levels, including as a board member of the Georgia Municipal Association and as a member
15 of the National League of Cities' Small Cities Committee and Public Safety Committee; and

16 WHEREAS, this outstanding individual represents the initiative, integrity, and experience
17 needed to ensure that the citizens of Union City have a seat at the table and that their voices
18 are heard; and
19 WHEREAS, Council Member Mealing is a woman of unassuming greatness whose humble
20 acts of kindness and generosity have uplifted the entire Union City community; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
22 extraordinary servant leader be appropriately recognized.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body congratulate and commend Council Member Angelette Mealing
25 for receiving the 2025 Yellow Rose Nikki T. Randall Servant Leadership Award and extend
26 best wishes for her continued success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Council Member Angelette Mealing.
