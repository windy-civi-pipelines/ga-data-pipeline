15 WHEREAS, Head Coach Ashley Flavin was recognized as NAIA Coach of the Year for her

28 Championship, commend the men's wrestling team for its outstanding performance, and
29 recognize the leadership of coaches Ashley Flavin and Omi Acosta and the dedication of all
30 student-athletes and staff who contributed to this historic season.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
32 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Life University.
