25 LC 112 3344
House Resolution 563
By: Representative Reese of the 140th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, since his election, Senator Jon Ossoff has championed economic development
3 in Georgia, passing legislation that has attracted thousands of advanced manufacturing jobs
4 and billions of dollars in investment, strengthening the state's economy and workforce; and
5 WHEREAS, through the historic bipartisan infrastructure law, he has secured funding to
6 greatly expand broadband internet access; upgrade Georgia's ports, airports, roads, bridges,
7 and water infrastructure; and invest in coastal resilience to protect communities from storm
8 surge, flooding, and tropical storms; and
9 WHEREAS, he has led bipartisan investigations exposing the mistreatment of military
10 families in privatized housing, corruption in federal prisons, the sexual assault of female
11 inmates, and the medical mistreatment of women in federal detention; and
12 WHEREAS, he has upgraded and strengthened healthcare facilities across Georgia as well
13 as helped pass legislation capping the cost of insulin for seniors at $35 per month; and
H. R. 563

25 LC 112 3344
14 WHEREAS, deeply committed to supporting Georgia's veterans, servicemembers, and
15 military families, he has passed bipartisan legislation to expand veterans' access to mental
16 health care, upgrade child development centers for military families, and enhance VA
17 healthcare services for Iraq and Afghanistan veterans; and
18 WHEREAS, his legislative achievements further include laws to strengthen mental health
19 care services, public safety, and opioid epidemic response; investigate unsolved lynchings
20 and civil rights murders; combat corruption and improve security in federal prisons; and
21 safeguard children from online exploitation; and
22 WHEREAS, through his unwavering commitment to service, bipartisanship, and the
23 well-being of Georgians, Senator Ossoff continues to deliver critical reforms and investments
24 that improve the lives of families, veterans, and communities across the state.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body recognize and commend Senator Jon Ossoff for his efficient,
27 effective, unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia and extend the most
28 sincere best wishes for continued health and happiness.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
30 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
31 Senator Jon Ossoff.
