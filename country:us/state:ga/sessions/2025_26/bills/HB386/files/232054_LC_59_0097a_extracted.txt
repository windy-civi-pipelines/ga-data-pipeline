Section 1:
25 LC 59 0097
By: Representatives Barnes of the 86th, Hugley of the 141st, Burnough of the 77th, Jackson
of the 165th, Drenner of the 85th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 48-8-3 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 exemptions from sales and use taxes, so as to exempt sales of clothing and school related
3 supplies during the last weekend of July each year; to provide for a short title; to provide for
4 a sunset date; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other
5 purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "Back to School Relief Act."
9 SECTION 2.
10 Code Section 48-8-3 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to exemptions from
11 sales and use taxes, is amended in paragraph (75) by revising subparagraph (A) and adding
12 a new subparagraph to read as follows:
13 "(75)(A) The sale of eligible property. The exemption provided by this paragraph
14 applies only to sales occurring during the period commencing at 12:01 A.M. on July 30,

17 "(D) This paragraph shall stand repealed and reserved on December 31, 2028."
18 SECTION 3.
19 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
