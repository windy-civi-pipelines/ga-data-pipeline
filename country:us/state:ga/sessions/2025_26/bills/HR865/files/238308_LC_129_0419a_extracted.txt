25 LC 129 0419
House Resolution 865
of the 24th, Clark of the 100th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Emanuel Pitts on winning the 2025 GHSA Class 4A State
2 Diving Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Emanuel Pitts of East Forsyth High School earned the top title in the state with
4 his win at the 2025 GHSA Class 4A State Diving Championship; and
5 WHEREAS, he completed his senior season with a historic performance and a top score of
6 617.90; and
7 WHEREAS, this stellar young man epitomizes the courage, determination, leadership, and
8 character necessary to attain excellence in athletic prowess and performance; and
9 WHEREAS, Emanuel's victory was the culmination of a season of hard work, dedication,
10 and excellent sportsmanship; and
11 WHEREAS, his superior skill, strategy, stamina, and control have earned him the respect and
12 admiration of his teammates and challengers; and
H. R. 865
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0419
13 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the superlative abilities of this
14 accomplished and gifted student-athlete, as well as the pride, honor, and respect he has
15 brought to himself, his school, and his community, be appropriately recognized.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
17 the members of this body recognize and commend Emanuel Pitts on winning the 2025 GHSA
18 Class 4A State Diving Championship and extend best wishes for success in the future.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
20 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
21 Emanuel Pitts.
