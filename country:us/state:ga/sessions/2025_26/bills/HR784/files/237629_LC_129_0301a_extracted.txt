Section 1:
25 LC 129 0301
House Resolution 784
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Seth Winstead, Rome High School's 2025 STAR Teacher; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, Seth Winstead has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the vital
6 role that he has played in the teaching profession and his deep personal commitment to the
7 education of the students of Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, Seth Winstead has earned distinction for teaching excellence and has been
9 honored as Rome High School's 2025 STAR Teacher; and
10 WHEREAS, in recognition of his contributions to the education and welfare of young people
11 in this state, it is abundantly fitting and proper for this body to honor the extraordinary
12 accomplishments and academic achievements of this outstanding educator.
H. R. 784
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0301
13 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
14 the members of this body commend Seth Winstead, Rome High School's 2025 STAR
15 Teacher, and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
17 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Seth
18 Winstead.
H. R. 784
