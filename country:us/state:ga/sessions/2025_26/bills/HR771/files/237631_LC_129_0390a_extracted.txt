25 LC 129 0390
House Resolution 771
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending David Hill, Rockdale County High School's Class of 2025 salutatorian; and
2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, David Hill has shown by academic accomplishment that the pursuit and
6 attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of the students in this state; and
7 WHEREAS, this remarkable senior has earned distinction through his notable achievements
8 as a student at Rockdale County High School where he is a member of the marching band,
9 the national honor society, and the technology student association; and
10 WHEREAS, he has been selected as salutatorian by earning exemplary grades in the most
11 challenging academic courses while maintaining a substantial extracurricular schedule
12 throughout his academic career; and
H. R. 771
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0390
13 WHEREAS, this achievement is the culmination of David Hill's hard work and
14 determination, and he deserves much credit for his energy and dedication to academic
15 excellence; and
16 WHEREAS, he is an outstanding representative of the young people of this state and it is
17 only fitting and proper that he be recognized for his many accomplishments.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body heartily commend David Hill for his spectacular scholastic
20 endeavors and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
22 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to David
23 Hill.
