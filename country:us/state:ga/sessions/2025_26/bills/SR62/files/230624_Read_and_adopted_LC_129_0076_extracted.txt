Senate Resolution 62
the 52nd, Moore of the 53rd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Reinhardt University for serving the students of North
2 Georgia for over 140 years; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Reinhardt University was founded in 1883 and has provided 142 years of
4 educational service in Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, the University is a top producer of public school teachers and leaders in
6 Cherokee County; and
7 WHEREAS, the University boasts graduating top performing students in the nursing
8 profession, with a 93 percent NCLEX pass rate compared to the national average of 76
9 percent; and
10 WHEREAS, the University has made significant contributions to law enforcement leadership
11 through offering a high quality online criminal justice degree since 2013, an MPA in law
12 enforcement leadership, and is the only private college in Georgia to offer a Mandate Police
13 Academy for working adults; and

14 WHEREAS, the University has made significant contributions to the workforce in Georgia
15 in the areas of arts, sciences, business, and cybersecurity; and
16 WHEREAS, the University has contributed greatly to the cultural life of Northwest Georgia
17 by bringing professional performances to the Falany Performing Arts Center and Flint Hall;
18 and
19 WHEREAS, the University has demonstrated a commitment to keeping the history of
20 Georgia settlers and native Cherokee communities alive through the Funk Heritage Museum
21 and Bennett History Center.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 commend Reinhardt University as well as its administration, faculty, staff, and trustees for
24 the important contributions they have made to the State of Georgia as well as extend best
25 wishes for continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Reinhardt
28 University.
