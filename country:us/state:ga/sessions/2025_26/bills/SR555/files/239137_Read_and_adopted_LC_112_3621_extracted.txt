25 LC 112 3621
Senate Resolution 555
By: Senator Mallow of the 2nd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Quentina M. Miller-Fields, a native of Savannah, Georgia, has distinguished
3 herself as an educator, advocate, and leader dedicated to uplifting youth and strengthening
4 her community through decades of public service; and
5 WHEREAS, she currently serves the Savannah-Chatham County Public School System as
6 Director of Student Affairs, supporting more than 36,000 students through compassionate
7 leadership and transformative programming; and
8 WHEREAS, she began her career with the Savannah-Chatham County Public School System
9 as the first social worker assigned to the Teen Age Parenthood Program, during which time
10 she provided vital support to students navigating the challenges of early parenthood and
11 served as President of the First District of School Social Workers of Georgia; and
12 WHEREAS, her next position was as Job Placement Specialist and Assistant Site
13 Administrator at the Riley Learning Center, a school designated for students charged with
14 or adjudicated of specific felony offenses; and

15 WHEREAS, Dr. Miller-Fields developed and coordinated several influential programs and
16 initiatives during her tenure, including the Male Readiness Camp—a three-week summer
17 program for third through fifth grade boys—as well as the district's first "Promoting Peace"
18 Violence Prevention Conference and its annual "Character Counts" Conference; and
19 WHEREAS, her dedication to youth and education has earned her significant recognition,
20 including the Gwendolyn Goodman "Champion for Children" Award, selection as a featured
21 presenter at the National Youth At-Risk Conference, and honors for "Excellence in
22 Education" by the 100 Black Men of Savannah Inc.; and
23 WHEREAS, her dedication extends beyond the classroom as she contributes to numerous
24 civic and health organizations, including service on the Board of Directors for the Chatham
25 County Health Department, the Department of Family and Children Services, and the Coastal
26 Empire Disaster Relief Committee; and
27 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable individual be
28 appropriately honored during this special time.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
30 commend Dr. Quentina M. Miller-Fields for her unwavering service to education, youth
31 development, and her community as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
33 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr. Quentina M.
34 Miller-Fields.
