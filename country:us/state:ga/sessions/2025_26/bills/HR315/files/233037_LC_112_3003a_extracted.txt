25 LC 112 3003
House Resolution 315
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Jacob Crumbley; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, a native of Harris County and graduate of Harris County High School, Jacob
3 Crumbley has been named U.S. Soccer's 2024 Male Cerebral Palsy Player of the Year; and
4 WHEREAS, he joined the United States Cerebral Palsy Men's National Team (CP MNT) in
5 2018 during his senior year of high school and has since represented the United States in
6 international competitions across Spain, England, Ecuador, Peru, Chile, Australia, and
7 Ireland; and
8 WHEREAS, in 2024, he demonstrated exceptional versatility and leadership, excelling in
9 multiple positions and leading the CP MNT with five goals and eight total goal contributions,
10 including standout performances at the IFCPF World Cup in Salou, Spain; and
11 WHEREAS, Jacob played a pivotal role in Team USA's seventh-place finish at the IFCPF
12 World Cup, delivering a crucial equalizer and scoring the game-winning goal in a dramatic
13 comeback victory against Germany; and

14 WHEREAS, as a dedicated member of Team USA, Jacob has contributed to the success of
15 the United States Olympic and Paralympic Committee, earning two bronze medals at the
16 Parapan American Games; and
17 WHEREAS, a talented multi-sport athlete, Jacob played on the Harris County High School
18 Varsity Soccer Team and served as a kicker for the Tigers football team, where he was
19 ranked the third-best kicker in the nation for his graduating class by the Ray Guy Kicking
20 Camp program; and
21 WHEREAS, Jacob is the third recipient of the Male Cerebral Palsy Player of the Year award
22 since its inception in 2022, earning 59 percent of the weighted vote; and
23 WHEREAS, his achievements reflect his hard work, resilience, and passion for soccer,
24 bringing pride to Harris County and the State of Georgia, and it is abundantly fitting and
25 proper that he be appropriately honored.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body recognize and commend Jacob Crumbley, U.S. Soccer's 2024 Male
28 Cerebral Palsy Player of the Year, and extend best wishes for continued success both on and
29 off the field.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Jacob
32 Crumbley.
