Section 1:
25 LC 44 3189ER
By: Representatives Anderson of the 10th, Williamson of the 112th, New of the 40th, Clark of
the 100th, Stephens of the 164th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 36-83-4 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 authorized investments, delegation of investment authority to financial officer, and objective
3 of investment, so as to provide for additional authorized investments; to provide for related
4 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Code Section 36-83-4 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to authorized
8 investments, delegation of investment authority to financial officer, and objective of
9 investment, is amended by revising paragraph (1) of subsection (a) as follows:
10 "(a)(1) Subject to the procedures set forth in this chapter, the governing authority of any
11 local government may invest and reinvest any money subject to its control and
12 jurisdiction in:
13 (A) Obligations of this state or of other states;
14 (B) Obligations issued by the United States government;
15 (C) Obligations fully insured or guaranteed by the United States government or a
16 United States government agency;
H. B. 871

17 (D) Obligations of any corporation of the United States government;
18 (E) Prime bankers' acceptances;
19 (F) The local government investment pool established by Code Section 36-83-8;
20 (G) Repurchase agreements; and
21 (H) Obligations of other political subdivisions of this state;
22 (I) Securities of or other interests in any no-load, open-end management type
23 investment company or investment trust registered under the federal Investment
24 Company Act of 1940, as authorized by Code Section 36-82-7;
25 (J) A commingled stable net asset value investment trust established by an
26 intergovernmental agreement by two or more units of local government pursuant to
27 Article IX, Section III, Paragraph I(a) of the Constitution of Georgia; and
28 (K) Commercial paper issued by domestic corporations carrying ratings no lower than
29 P-1 by Moody's Investors Service, Inc., A-1 by Standard and Poor's Corporation, or F-1
30 by Fitch, Inc."
31 SECTION 2.
32 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
