House Resolution 665
the 76th, and Hugley of the 141st
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Georgia Association of Senior Centers in honor of
2 National Senior Center Month; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, September is recognized as National Senior Center Month, a time to celebrate
4 the invaluable contributions of senior centers in enhancing the lives of older adults; and
5 WHEREAS, the Georgia Association of Senior Centers (GASC) was founded to bring senior
6 center professionals together from across Georgia's rural, urban, and suburban communities
7 to strengthen the operations, programming, and leadership of senior centers statewide; and
8 WHEREAS, GASC is committed to supporting senior center administrators, managers,
9 programmers, and professionals in their mission to enhance the well-being and quality of life
10 for Georgia's older adults by fostering collaboration, professional development, and
11 advocacy; and
12 WHEREAS, GASC is establishing itself as a guiding force for senior centers by providing
13 leadership, resources, and support to ensure that Georgia's senior centers remain vibrant and
14 accessible hubs for health, wellness, education, and social engagement; and

15 WHEREAS, the founding board of GASC has demonstrated extraordinary vision, leadership,
16 and dedication in forming the organization and laying the foundation for its continued
17 success, with the founding board members including Tori Strawter-Tanks, President and
18 Founder; Konswella Gilchrist, Vice President; Tammy Le, Secretary; Adrianne Jones,
19 Treasurer; Katie Howard, Board Member; and Shawn Taylor, Board Member; and
20 WHEREAS, the members of this distinguished board bring a wealth of experience and a
21 shared commitment to the advancement of Georgia's senior centers, ensuring they serve as
22 beacons of excellence for older adults and their communities; and
23 WHEREAS, the establishment of GASC marks a significant milestone in Georgia's efforts
24 to strengthen senior services, providing a statewide network that fosters innovation, best
25 practices, and advocacy to support the aging population; and
26 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of GASC and its
27 founding board be recognized and celebrated in honor of National Senior Center Month for
28 their work in elevating the quality and sustainability of senior centers across Georgia.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
30 the members of this body commend the Georgia Association of Senior Centers for its
31 exemplary service, leadership, and commitment to enhancing the lives of older adults
32 throughout the state and recognize the dedication of its founding board members in
33 establishing this vital organization.
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
36 Georgia Association of Senior Centers.
