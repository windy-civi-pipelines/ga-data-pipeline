25 LC 112 2762
Senate Resolution 90
22nd, Harrell of the 40th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Ms. Rhea Brashear; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Ms. Rhea Brashear has been selected to compete for the honor of becoming the
3 state recipient of the Technical College System of Georgia's 2024 Adult Education Teacher
4 of the Year award; and
5 WHEREAS, with this award, Ms. Brashear has earned the distinction of being named the
6 Technical College System of Georgia's 2024 Adult Education Teacher of the Year; and
7 WHEREAS, Ms. Brashear, an instructor for the adult education program at Southern
8 Regional Technical College, has exemplified excellence in instructional knowledge and
9 practices; and
10 WHEREAS, in recognition of such, Ms. Brashear was selected from an elite group of
11 Georgia technical college adult education instructors who have each demonstrated innovative
12 teaching practices and exceptional leadership skills at their respective institutions; and
S. R. 90
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2762
13 WHEREAS, through dedication and professionalism, Ms. Brashear has brought honor to
14 herself, her family, Southern Regional Technical College, and the entire Technical College
15 System of Georgia.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
17 congratulate and commend Ms. Rhea Brashear for her outstanding achievements and her
18 efforts to promote adult and technical education in Georgia as well as extend best wishes for
19 continued success.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Ms. Rhea
22 Brashear.
