Section 1:
25 LC 60 0166S
substitute to HB 117:
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 13 of Chapter 2 of Title 26 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to food service establishments, so as to require notification of foreign imported
3 shrimp; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
5 SECTION 1.
6 Article 13 of Chapter 2 of Title 26 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
7 food service establishments, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
8 "26-2-378.1.
9 All food service establishments in this state that serve imported shrimp shall conspicuously
10 display on their menus a disclosure by each menu item containing shrimp stating
11 'FOREIGN IMPORTED,' or display 'FOREIGN IMPORTED SHRIMP' on placards visible
12 to the public. This Code section shall not apply to a food service establishment operated
13 by a state agency, as such term is defined in Code Section 48-8-14."
14 SECTION 2.
15 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
