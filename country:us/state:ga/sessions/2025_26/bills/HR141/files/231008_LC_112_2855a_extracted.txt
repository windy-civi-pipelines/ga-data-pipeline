25 LC 112 2855
House Resolution 141
of the 156th, Dubnik of the 29th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing the week of February 3-7, 2025, as National School Counseling Week; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, National School Counseling Week focuses on increasing public attention on the
4 unique contributions school counselors make by helping students achieve success in school
5 and plan for the future; and
6 WHEREAS, this week highlights the enormous impact that school counselors make in
7 helping students reach their goals; and
8 WHEREAS, school counselors are employed in public and private schools to help students
9 reach their full potential and are actively committed to helping students explore their
10 interests, abilities, strengths, and talents relating to development and career awareness; and
11 WHEREAS, school counselors help parents focus on ways to further the educational,
12 personal, and social growth of their children and work with teachers and other educators to
13 help students explore their potential and set realistic goals for themselves; and
H. R. 141
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2855
14 WHEREAS, school counselors seek to identify and utilize community resources that can
15 enhance and complement comprehensive school counseling programs to help students
16 become productive members of society; and
17 WHEREAS, comprehensive school counseling programs are considered an integral part of
18 the educational process, which enables all students to achieve success in school.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body recognize the week of February 3-7, 2025, as National School
21 Counseling Week.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
24 Georgia School Counselor Association.
