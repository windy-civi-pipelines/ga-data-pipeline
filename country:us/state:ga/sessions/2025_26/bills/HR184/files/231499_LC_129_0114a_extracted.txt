25 LC 129 0114
House Resolution 184
McClain of the 109th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Grayson High School football team for winning the 2024 State Football
2 Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Grayson High School football team has established and maintained a proud
4 tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team has completed another triumphant season of football
6 competition by posting an outstanding record; and
7 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
8 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
9 2024 campaign, the team captured its fourth state football championship; and
10 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school by winning the 2024 State
11 Football Championship against Carrollton High School with a final score of 38-24; and
12 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
13 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
14 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Head Coach
H. R. 184
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0114
15 Santavious Bryant, who was ably assisted by Keith Barefield and his offensive staff as well
16 as Greg Carswell and his defensive staff; and
17 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
18 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
19 abundantly proper to call them champions.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body congratulate the Grayson High School football team for winning
22 the 2024 State Football Championship and extend best wishes for their continued success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
25 Grayson High School football team.
H. R. 184
