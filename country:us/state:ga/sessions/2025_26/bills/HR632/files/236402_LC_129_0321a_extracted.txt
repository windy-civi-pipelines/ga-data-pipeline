25 LC 129 0321
House Resolution 632
61st, and Willis of the 55th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Cocoa Brown and recognizing March 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing Women in
2 Georgia Day; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day acknowledges the philanthropic, social,
4 and cultural contributions of women in the state; and
5 WHEREAS, Cocoa Brown has long been recognized for the vital role that she has played
6 in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of the citizens of Georgia;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, Cocoa is an actress, comedienne, entrepreneur, and Screen Actors Guild award
9 Nominee best know her for her roles in Tyler Perry's "The Single Mom Club," OWN
10 Network's "For Better or For Worse," and her comedy special "Famous Enough"; and
11 WHEREAS, Cocoa serves her community though her partnerships with the Social Butterflies
12 Foundation, which supports women with lupus and fibromyalgia; the Wigs of Love
13 Foundation, which celebrates and honors women battling hair loss from lupus; and though
14 From Stage 2 Screen and Little Oscars where she teaches children and adults acting, stand-up
15 comedy, and show business; and
H. R. 632
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0321
16 WHEREAS, with her passion and talent, Cocoa Brown remains an unstoppable force in the
17 entertainment industry, continuing to deliver captivating performances across a variety of
18 genres; and
19 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
20 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
22 individual be appropriately honored.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body commend Cocoa Brown for her efficient, effective, unselfish, and
25 dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; recognize March, 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing
26 Women in Georgia Day; and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and
27 happiness.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Cocoa Brown.
