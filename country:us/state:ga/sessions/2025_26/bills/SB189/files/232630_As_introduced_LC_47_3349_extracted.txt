25 LC 47 3349
By: Senators Williams of the 25th, Anderson of the 24th, Davenport of the 17th, Rhett of the
33rd and Esteves of the 35th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 8 of Title 50 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 general provisions related to the Department of Community Affairs, so as to provide for the
3 department to develop and maintain a publicly accessible data base of legal notices that are
4 otherwise required by law to be published in a newspaper; to provide that such data base
5 shall be an additional, mandatory, manner of posting legal notices; to make such data base
6 contingent upon appropriation; to provide requirements for such data base; to authorize fees;
7 to provide that posting on such data base shall serve as an alternative to other publication
8 requirements in certain circumstances; to provide a definition; to authorize rules and
9 regulations; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
11 SECTION 1.
12 Chapter 8 of Title 50 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to general
13 provisions related to the Department of Community Affairs, is amended by adding a new
14 Code section to read as follows:
S. B. 189
- 1 -
25 LC 47 3349
15 "50-8-19.
16 (a) Subject to appropriation by the General Assembly, the department shall develop and
17 operate a publicly accessible online data base of legal notices required by law to be
18 published in either a legal organ or other newspaper of general circulation.
19 (b) Posting legal notices on such data base shall be mandatory and in addition to any other
20 required public notice. Except as provided for in this subsection, posting of such legal
21 notices on such data base shall not be in lieu of publication requirements otherwise
22 provided by law. No posting on the data base provided for in this subsection shall operate
23 to serve as any form of notice required by Code Section 28-1-14.
24 (c) The data base provided for in subsection (a) of this Code section shall:
25 (1) Be designed to allow for legal notices to be uploaded using commonly accessible
26 electronic file formats;
27 (2) Allow for the online payment of reasonable publication fees that shall not exceed
28 $25.00 per legal notice;
29 (3) Post in a secure and unalterable format an uploaded legal notice within 24 hours of
30 the uploading of such notice;
31 (4) Allow the public to search for and view posted legal notices in an internet based
32 format that is also easily accessible and readable from mobile devices;
33 (5) Allow for the establishment of accounts that can receive notifications of the posting
34 of certain legal notices designated by the account holder; provided, however, that the
35 department may charge additional fees for such service; and
36 (6) Provide a mechanism for users to obtain certified copies of posted legal notices.
37 (d) Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, in the event that the legal organ of a
38 county does not maintain an office within such county to administer the publication of legal
39 notices, when a person is otherwise required by law to publish a legal notice in such
40 county's legal organ, posting such public notice on the data base provided for in this
41 subsection shall serve as lawful publication of such notice and may serve in lieu of
S. B. 189
- 2 -
25 LC 47 3349
42 publication in the legal organ and shall satisfy any and all legal requirements of adequate
43 notice to the public; provided, however, that such posting on the data base occurs no later
44 than the publication date otherwise provided by law.
45 (e) In the event that an error is made in the text or publication date of a legal notice
46 required to be published in a legal organ or other newspaper of general circulation, and a
47 correct version of such notice is posted on the data base provided for in this subsection
48 within the time provided by law, posting on such data base shall satisfy any and all legal
49 requirements of adequate notice to the public.
50 (f)(1) For the purpose of this paragraph, the term 'archival notice' means any notice
51 posted on the data base provided for in this subsection for a period of over one year.
52 (2) The department is authorized to establish and collect reasonable fees related the
53 search and retrieval of archival notices.
54 (g) The department is authorized to adopt rules and regulations related to this Code section
55 and the data base required herein."
56 SECTION 2.
57 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
