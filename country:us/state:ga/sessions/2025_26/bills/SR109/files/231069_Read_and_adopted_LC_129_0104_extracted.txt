25 LC 129 0104
Senate Resolution 109
55th, Esteves of the 35th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the winners of the 2025 Miss Atlanta Competition; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the Miss Atlanta Competition is a scholarship pageant that recognizes
3 outstanding young women in three areas of competition: Talent, Interview, and Evening
4 Gown; and
5 WHEREAS, the following young women have been recognized as the winners of the 2025
6 Miss Atlanta Competition and have earned scholarship awards for their performance: Audrey
7 Kittila, Miss Atlanta 2025; AnnaKate Robinson, Miss Capital City 2025; Lindsay Jones,
8 Miss Greater Atlanta 2025; Peyton Christen, Miss Empire of the South 2025; Claire Zhang,
9 Miss Atlanta Teen 2025; Ansleigh Rose, Miss Capital City Teen 2025; Whitney Hartsock,
10 Miss Greater Atlanta Teen 2025; and Delaney Lanham, Miss Empire of the South Teen
11 2025; and
12 WHEREAS, each of these distinguished individuals will go on to represent greater Atlanta
13 at the Miss Georgia and Miss Georgia Teen competitions in Columbus, Georgia, the winners
14 of which will advance to the national level; and
S. R. 109
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0104
15 WHEREAS, these young women serve as role models who advocate for the four points of
16 the Miss America crown: Service, Style, Scholarship, and Success; and
17 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the winners of this competition be
18 appropriately honored.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 congratulate Audrey Kittila, AnnaKate Robinson, Lindsay Jones, Peyton Christen, Claire
21 Zhang, Ansleigh Rose, Whitney Hartsock, and Delaney Lanham upon their exemplary
22 performance and extend best wishes for continued success in future competition.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Audrey Kittila,
25 AnnaKate Robinson, Lindsay Jones, Peyton Christen, Claire Zhang, Ansleigh Rose, Whitney
26 Hartsock, and Delaney Lanham.
