25 LC 112 3067
House Resolution 301
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Barrow County's 2025 STAR Students and STAR Teachers; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the following students have been recognized as Barrow County's 2025 STAR
4 Students: Christopher Skyler Mack, Apalachee High School; Lucy Grace Bugg, Barrow Arts
5 & Sciences Academy; Bennett Logan, Bethlehem Christian Academy; and Alexander
6 Liangyu Hou, Winder-Barrow High School; and
7 WHEREAS, these students were granted the opportunity to name a STAR Teacher from their
8 respective schools whose guidance and support played a vital role in their success: Mrs.
9 Holly Behzadi, tenth grade Honors and AP Chemistry, Apalachee High School; Mr. Jeffery
10 Lalaian, 12th grade AP Calculus, Barrow Arts & Sciences Academy; Mrs. Tammy
11 Suertefelipe, 12th grade AP Literature, Bethlehem Christian Academy; and Mr. Cason
12 Dowdy, 11th grade Physics, Physics Honors, and AP Physics, Winder-Barrow High School;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, these exemplary students have personified the selfless dedication, mental
15 agility, and personal maturity necessary for superlative academic performance; and
H. R. 301
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3067
16 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
17 important objective of the Georgia General Assembly, and these distinguished individuals
18 have shown that the pursuit and attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of the
19 students in this state; and
20 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia looks with great anticipation toward the future of these
21 bright and promising young students and honors the efforts of educators who go above and
22 beyond in order to impact the lives of our youth.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body heartily commend Barrow County's 2025 STAR Students and
25 STAR Teachers and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
28 public and the press.
