Section 1:
25 LC 112 3604
House Resolution 878
Chastain of the 7th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, behavioral health conditions, including mental and substance use disorders, are
3 critical public health issues, and 988 was established to improve access to crisis services in
4 response to the country's growing suicide and mental health-related crisis care needs; and
5 WHEREAS, the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline is a direct, national three-digit number that
6 connects individuals with suicide prevention and mental health crisis resources, which are
7 distinct from the public safety purposes of 911; and
8 WHEREAS, the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline provides crucial support for people in
9 distress, reducing the burden on emergency rooms and law enforcement; and
10 WHEREAS, 988 calls, texts, and chats in the State of Georgia are answered by the Georgia
11 Crisis and Access Line (GCAL) 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year; and
12 WHEREAS, this vital service has the potential to save lives and foster healthier communities
13 by reducing stigma, promoting help-seeking behavior, and ensuring that people in crisis
14 receive timely and appropriate care; and
H. R. 878
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3604
15 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable mental health resource
16 be appropriately honored during this special time.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
18 the members of this body commend the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline for its outstanding
19 service on behalf of citizens in need and express best wishes for continued success.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
21 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
22 public and the press.
