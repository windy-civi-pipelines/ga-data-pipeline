25 LC 112 3594
Senate Resolution 513
By: Senator James of the 28th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, a native of Atlanta and the daughter of two distinguished public servants, the
3 late civil rights leader Rev. Joseph E. Boone and longtime Atlanta Public Schools educator
4 Alethea W. Boone, Andrea L. Boone is currently serving her second term as a member of the
5 Atlanta City Council, where she proudly represents the citizens of District 10; and
6 WHEREAS, she has previously served the citizens of Atlanta through her work in the office
7 of former Atlanta City Councilmember C.T. Martin, where she held the role of Chief of Staff
8 and advanced initiatives supporting affordable housing, public safety, and youth
9 empowerment throughout District 10; and
10 WHEREAS, she further served for over seven years as the City of Atlanta Commissioner in
11 the Mayor's Office of Constituent Services, collaborating with churches, senior centers,
12 neighborhoods, businesses, and community stakeholders to ensure city leaders remained
13 attentive to the needs of District 10; and
S. R. 513
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3594
14 WHEREAS, she has consistently advocated for collaboration between the Atlanta Public
15 School System, the City Council, and the Mayor's Office to prioritize the needs of local
16 neighborhoods and communities; and
17 WHEREAS, her leadership has supported key citywide goals, including assisting in the
18 successful hiring of 2,000 officers for the Atlanta Police Department and ensuring the city's
19 budget has been balanced each year without increasing taxes; and
20 WHEREAS, Andrea L. Boone's decades of service reflect a profound and unwavering
21 dedication to the betterment of Atlanta, and her work continues to uplift and empower the
22 residents of District 10; and
23 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
24 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
26 recognize and commend Andrea L. Boone for her efficient, effective, unselfish, and
27 dedicated public service to the State of Georgia and extend the most sincere best wishes for
28 continued health and happiness.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
30 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Andrea L. Boone.
