Section 1:
25 LC 50 1165S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to revenue and
2 taxation, so as to provide for a standard deduction from income taxes; to provide for related
3 matters; to provide for an effective date and applicability; to repeal conflicting laws; and for
4 other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to revenue and taxation, is
8 amended in Code Section 48-7-27, relating to computation of taxable net income, by revising
9 paragraph (1) of subsection (a) as follows:
10 "(1) At the taxpayer's election, either:
11 (A) The sum of all itemized nonbusiness deductions used in computing such taxpayer's
12 federal taxable income; or
13 (B) A standard deduction in an amount as follows:
14 (i)(I) In the case of a married couple filing a joint return, $24,000.00; or
H. B. 165 (SUB)
- 1 -
25 LC 50 1165S
15 (ii)(II) In the case of a single taxpayer, head of household, or married taxpayer
16 filing a separate return, $12,000.00; and
17 (ii) An additional amount equal to the net disaster loss as defined under Section
18 304(b) of Title III of Division EE of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021, P.L.
19 116-260, as amended by Section 2 of the Federal Disaster Tax Relief Act of 2023,
20 P.L. 118-148;"
21 SECTION 2.
22 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
23 without such approval and shall be applicable to all taxable years beginning on or after
24 January 1, 2024.
25 SECTION 3.
26 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
