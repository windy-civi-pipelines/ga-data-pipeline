25 LC 112 3017
House Resolution 486
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Brantley County girls tennis team for winning the 2024 GHSA Class AA
2 State Girls Tennis Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Brantley County girls tennis team has established and maintained a proud
4 tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
6 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
7 2024 campaign, the team captured the GHSA Class AA State Girls Tennis Championship;
8 and
9 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
10 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
11 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Coach Patrick
12 Stieve, Coach Dalton Lairsey, Coach Reid Teatsorth, and Coach Kate Melvin; and
13 WHEREAS, this all-star team included Brooklyn Wildes, Raylee Strickland, Kaleigh Oakie,
14 Jacie Riggins, Chasadee Waldron, Summer Colley, Savannah Carroll, Bri Crews, Harlee
15 Driggers, and Cheryl Tencza; and
H. R. 486
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3017
16 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
17 competing with determination and precision throughout the season, it is abundantly proper
18 to call them champions.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body congratulate the Brantley County girls tennis team for winning the
21 2024 GHSA Class AA State Girls Tennis Championship and extend best wishes for their
22 continued success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
25 Brantley County girls tennis team.
