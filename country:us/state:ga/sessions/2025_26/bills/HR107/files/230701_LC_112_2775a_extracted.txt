Section 1:
25 LC 112 2775
House Resolution 107
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring and commending American Heritage Girls (AHG); and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, American Heritage Girls (AHG) is currently chartered in every state except
3 Utah; and
4 WHEREAS, AHG has 39,839 national members in all 50 states; 1,220 troops nationally;
5 1,520 members in Georgia; and 43 troops in the State of Georgia, and its purpose is to instill
6 spiritual and character development in young women; and
7 WHEREAS, on June 25, 2015, AHG and Trail Life USA signed a Memorandum of Ministry
8 Alliance, which is the highest form of relationship each organization can hold, and it was
9 approved by both boards; and by sharing skills, talents, knowledge, and common goals, both
10 groups will be able to empower and support their respective memberships in order to
11 accomplish great things in their communities while spreading the love of God to others; and
12 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the efforts of American Heritage Girls
13 toward improving the lives of the young women of this state be appropriately recognized.
H. R. 107
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2775
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
15 the members of this body honor and commend American Heritage Girls (AHG) for its
16 tireless efforts in shaping the youth in Georgia and the United States and extend sincere best
17 wishes for continued success.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
19 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
20 American Heritage Girls (AHG).
H. R. 107
