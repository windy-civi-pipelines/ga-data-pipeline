25 LC 129 0072
House Resolution 84
Mitchell of the 88th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing October 17, 2025, as International Day for the Eradication of Poverty in the
2 State of Georgia; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, October 17 has been established by the United Nations as the International Day
4 for the Eradication of Poverty to emphasize the need to address and combat poverty
5 worldwide through innovative approaches; and
6 WHEREAS, this day serves as a call to action to address poverty through financial inclusion,
7 access to financial resources, and the promotion of economic empowerment, micro-finance,
8 and building financial resilience in vulnerable communities; and
9 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia recognizes the critical importance of educating and
10 empowering the next generation with innovative and out-of-the-box financial thinking to
11 break cycles of poverty and create sustainable change; and
12 WHEREAS, Dr. Jacqueline Mohair, founder of Trinity International University of
13 Ambassadors School of Business (TIUA), and Dr. Sonya Hamm, renowned as "The Wealth
14 Coach" with a Doctor of Philosophy in Kingdom Finance and Ministry Stewardship, have
H. R. 84
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0072
15 exemplified leadership and vision in addressing poverty through education and financial
16 empowerment; and
17 WHEREAS, the PhD program in Kingdom Finance and Ministry Stewardship at TIUA
18 equips leaders with biblical and financial stewardship principles, emphasizing
19 Kingdom-focused leadership, ministry strategies, and practical financial education to
20 empower communities worldwide; and
21 WHEREAS, this program prepares students for impactful careers in ministry finance
22 management, church administration, Kingdom stewardship education, and biblical
23 economics consultation, integrating theological principles with financial excellence; and
24 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia acknowledges the vital role of faith-based organizations,
25 innovative educational programs, and visionary leaders in eradicating poverty and fostering
26 economic empowerment across communities.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body recognize October 17, 2025, as International Day for the
29 Eradication of Poverty in the State of Georgia and honor the work of Dr. Jacqueline Mohair,
30 Dr. Sonya Hamm, and the Trinity International University of Ambassadors School of
31 Business for their commitment to education, financial empowerment, and transformative
32 leadership.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of this body encourage all citizens,
34 organizations, and institutions to observe this day through acts of service, education, and
35 community engagement, thereby fostering greater awareness and collective action toward
36 poverty eradication.
