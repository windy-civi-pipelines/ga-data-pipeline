25 LC 129 0421
House Resolution 873
of the 24th, Clark of the 100th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Logan Otteson on winning the 2024 Gymnastics State
2 Championship in balance beam; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Logan Otteson of North Forsyth High School earned the top title in the state
4 with her balance beam win in the 2024 Gymnastics State Championship; and
5 WHEREAS, this stellar young woman epitomizes the courage, determination, leadership, and
6 character necessary to attain excellence in athletic prowess and performance; and
7 WHEREAS, her months of training been rewarded with an impressive win, and it is
8 appropriate that she holds the title of champion; and
9 WHEREAS, Logan's victory was the culmination of a season of hard work, dedication, and
10 excellent sportsmanship; and
11 WHEREAS, her superior skill, strategy, stamina, and control have earned her the respect and
12 admiration of her teammates and challengers; and
H. R. 873
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0421
13 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the superlative abilities of this
14 accomplished and gifted student-athlete, as well as the pride, honor, and respect she has
15 brought to herself, her school, and her community, be appropriately recognized.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
17 the members of this body recognize and commend Logan Otteson on winning the 2024
18 Gymnastics State Championship and extend best wishes for success in the future.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
20 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
21 Logan Otteson.
H. R. 873
