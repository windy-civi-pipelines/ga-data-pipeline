25 LC 129 0370
Senate Resolution 409
2nd, Halpern of the 39th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Darren Myles; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Darren Myles has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the vital
3 role that he has played in leadership and his deep personal commitment to the success of the
4 athletes of Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, he has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of his time,
6 talents, and energy toward the betterment of his community and state as evidenced
7 dramatically by his recognition as the 2024 Atlanta Falcons Coach of the Year; and
8 WHEREAS, for 18 seasons, Darren Myles has coached at various Atlanta high schools,
9 including Frederick Douglass High School, Alonzo Crim High school, and George
10 Washington Carver High School; and
11 WHEREAS, Darren Myles' exemplary coaching, mentoring, and dedication to his students
12 has led several of them to the NFL and other prestigious professional endeavors; and
13 WHEREAS, this distinguished gentleman has given inspiration to many through his high
14 ideals, morals, and deep concern for his fellow citizens, and he possesses the vast wisdom
S. R. 409
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0370
15 which only comes through experience and the strength of character which is achieved
16 through overcoming the many challenges of life; and
17 WHEREAS, he is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
18 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
20 remarkable and distinguished coach be appropriately recognized.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 recognize and commend Darren Myles for his dedication to the betterment of Georgia's
23 athletes and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and happiness.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Darren Myles.
