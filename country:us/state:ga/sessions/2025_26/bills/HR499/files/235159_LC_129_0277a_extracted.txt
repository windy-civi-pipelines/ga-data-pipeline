House Resolution 499
75th, and Cannon of the 58th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Neil "Iwer" George; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, hailing from South Trinidad, Neil 'D Boss Iwer' George is one of Trinidad and
3 Tobago's most prolific cultural and Soca icons; and
4 WHEREAS, since the inception of his career, in 1987, Iwer George's name has been
5 synonymous with the Carnival season; and
6 WHEREAS, he performs at all the major carnival events in Trinidad & Tobago such as Hyatt
7 Lime, UWI All Inclusive, I UP, Jam naked, Lara All Inclusive, Candy Coated Events,
8 Passage to Asia, Army Fete, Yuma events, Dimanche Gras, Jouvert Carnival Monday and
9 Parade of the Bands on the road carnival Tuesday, as well as for numerous prestigious
10 corporate events; and
11 WHEREAS, he has performed regionally and internationally in Jamaica, Grenada, St. Kitts
12 and Nevis, New York, Toronto, Bahamas, Germany, Barbados, Cayman Islands, Miami, and
13 on the Uber Soca Cruise; and
H. R. 499
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0277
14 WHEREAS, most recently, Iwer George was an actor, writer, and executive producer of the
15 movie Soca Prison; and
16 WHEREAS, his exceptional musical talents have been recognized though numerous awards,
17 including earning the title of Soca Monarch in 2002, 2003, 2007, and 2020, as well as
18 winning both the titles of International Soca Monarch & Road March in 2020; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the accomplishments of this talented and
20 devoted musician be appropriately recognized.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body recognize and commend the significant cultural and musical
23 contributions of Neil "Iwer" George and extend their best wishes for his continued success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Neil
26 "Iwer" George.
