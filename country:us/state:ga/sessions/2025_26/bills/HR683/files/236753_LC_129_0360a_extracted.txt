25 LC 129 0360
House Resolution 683
of the 102nd, Lim of the 98th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Joy Monroe; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Joy Monroe has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the vital
3 role that she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of
4 the citizens of Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, she has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of her time,
6 talents, and energy toward the betterment of her community and state as evidenced
7 dramatically by her superlative service as the founder and CEO of Single Parent Alliance and
8 Resource Center; and
9 WHEREAS, Single Parent Alliance and Resource Center (SPARC) is a nonprofit dedicated
10 to empowering single-parent families through housing stability, financial literacy, and
11 holistic support; and
12 WHEREAS, with a deep commitment to addressing housing insecurity and economic
13 challenges, Joy has led SPARC in creating innovative programs that provide real solutions
14 for families in need; and
H. R. 683
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0360
15 WHEREAS, under her leadership, SPARC has grown into a trusted resource, fostering
16 collaboration among nonprofits, businesses, and government agencies to drive systemic
17 change; and
18 WHEREAS, Joy's significant organizational and leadership talents, remarkable patience and
19 diplomacy, keen sense of vision, and sensitivity to the needs of the citizens of this state have
20 earned her the respect and admiration of her colleagues and associates; and
21 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
22 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
23 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
24 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body recognize and commend Joy Monroe for her efficient, effective,
27 unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia and extend the most sincere
28 best wishes for continued health and happiness.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
30 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Joy
31 Monroe.
