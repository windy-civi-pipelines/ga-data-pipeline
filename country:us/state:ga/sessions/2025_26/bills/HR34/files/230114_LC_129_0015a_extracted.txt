25 LC 129 0015
House Resolution 34
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Reverend Dr. David L. Stallion Sr. on the occasion of his
2 ninth pastoral anniversary; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Reverend Dr. David L. Stallion Sr. has demonstrated his commitment to
4 teaching the Gospel, witnessing Christ through word and deed, and addressing the physical,
5 psychological, intellectual, and spiritual needs of others; and
6 WHEREAS, Reverend Dr. Stallion was called by the Lord nine years ago to lead the
7 congregation of Mt. Pilgrim Baptist Church; and
8 WHEREAS, his vision and direction have helped Mt. Pilgrim Baptist Church grow in size
9 and influence, uplifting the community like the light of Bethlehem and serving as a spiritual
10 leader and place for fellowship; and
11 WHEREAS, Reverend Dr. Stallion's ministry has encompassed preaching and teaching at
12 numerous revivals, worship services, and Bible studies as well as community service through
13 organizations such as the United Way of Chattahoochee Valley; and
H. R. 34
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0015
14 WHEREAS, whether the task is preparing a sermon, visiting the sick, counseling the
15 troubled, baptizing a new believer, or acting as a theologian, educator, administrator, or
16 humanitarian, Reverend Dr. Stallion has shown the energy, devotion, and commitment truly
17 reflective of divine guidance; and
18 WHEREAS, the unmatched spiritual assistance offered by Reverend Dr. Stallion is a source
19 of strength and direction for persons in all walks of life; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this enduring example of God's message
21 of peace and love be recognized and congratulated for his innumerable good works rendered
22 on behalf of persons in need.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body recognize and commend Reverend Dr. David L. Stallion Sr. on the
25 wonderful occasion of his ninth pastoral anniversary and extend best wishes for many more
26 years of successful fellowship and leadership.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Reverend Dr. David L. Stallion Sr.
