25 LC 112 3366
House Resolution 633
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Fannin County Lady Rebels basketball team for winning the 2025 GHSA
2 Class A Division I State Basketball Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Fannin County Lady Rebels basketball team has established and maintained
4 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school and to Fannin County by
6 winning the 2025 GHSA Class A Division I State Basketball Championship against the
7 Banks County Lady Leopards with a final score of 51-42; and
8 WHEREAS, the Lady Rebels secured their victory through exceptional execution and
9 disciplined control, overcoming a strong defensive press from their opponents while turning
10 key stops into scoring opportunities; and
11 WHEREAS, standout performances included crucial contributions from Maggie Ledford,
12 Izzie Jabaley, and Reese Lewis, whose early fourth-quarter offensive surge helped propel the
13 Lady Rebels to victory, as well as tremendous defensive efforts from Avi Ethington, Callie
14 Ensley, Emma Buchanan, Emma Holloway, and Albany Cole, which limited Banks County's
15 top scorers well below their season average; and
H. R. 633
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3366
16 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
17 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit demonstrated by each
18 team member as well as the astute direction of Head Coach Ryan Chastain; and
19 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
20 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
21 abundantly proper to call them champions.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body congratulate the Fannin County Lady Rebels basketball team for
24 winning the 2025 GHSA Class A Division I State Basketball Championship and extend best
25 wishes for their continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
28 Fannin County Lady Rebels basketball team.
H. R. 633
