25 LC 112 3337
House Resolution 677
143rd, and Carpenter of the 4th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing April 22, 2025, as DJ Day; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, DJs play a vital role in the music industry by curating and introducing fresh
3 tracks and emerging artists to audiences worldwide; and
4 WHEREAS, through their performances in clubs, festivals, and on the radio, DJs are
5 instrumental in helping new sounds gain traction and reach wider audiences, shaping musical
6 trends and expanding the influence of diverse genres; and
7 WHEREAS, DJs are often at the forefront of discovering and popularizing new music,
8 fostering innovation and creativity within the industry; and
9 WHEREAS, their ability to read crowds, mix tracks, and create unique musical experiences
10 brings people together, making them key contributors to the cultural and social landscape;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, DJs play a crucial role in determining the success of a record, influencing which
13 songs become hits and shaping the sound of contemporary music; and
H. R. 677
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3337
14 WHEREAS, the following individuals are to be honored on this year's DJ Day: DJ Toni K.,
15 DJ Encore ATL, DJ Silk Provibes, DJ Roots Queen, DJ Waffles, DJ D. Rocc, DJ Big X, DJ
16 Dereck Sea, DJ Knotts, DJ Calvin Da Coordinator, DJ Swaye, and DJ Makaveli Dre; and
17 WHEREAS, DJ Day honors the contributions of DJs in shaping music, entertainment, and
18 culture while celebrating their impact on artists, audiences, and communities worldwide.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body recognize April 22, 2025, as DJ Day.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
22 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
23 public and the press.
H. R. 677
