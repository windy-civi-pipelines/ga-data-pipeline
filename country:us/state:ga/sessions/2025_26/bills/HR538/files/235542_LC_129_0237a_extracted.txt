the 165th, and Beckles of the 96th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Catherine Hardrick for receiving the 2025 Yellow Rose
2 Nikki T. Randall Servant Leadership Award; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, in 1999, Representative Nikki T. Randall became the first African American
4 female elected to the General Assembly from middle Georgia, and over her 17 years of
5 service as a legislator, she held numerous leadership positions, including chair of the Georgia
6 Women's Legislative Caucus; and
7 WHEREAS, every year during Women's History Month, the Georgia Women's Legislative
8 Caucus recognizes outstanding women in Georgia who exemplify the leadership and
9 inspiration embodied by Representative Randall; and
10 WHEREAS, Catherine Hardrick is a distinguished community leader, entrepreneur, and
11 disabled veteran who honorably served in the United States Army before transitioning to a
12 career in the financial industry, where she worked for more than 20 years; and
13 WHEREAS, she has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to service by leading a
14 community feeding program for homeless veterans and individuals in need; mentoring youth;

15 advocating for racial, environmental, and social justice; and supporting survivors of human
16 trafficking and sexual trauma; and
17 WHEREAS, she has further extended her dedication to community engagement by
18 co-founding "Conversations with Karen and Cat," a solutions-driven podcast focused on
19 addressing local issues; and
20 WHEREAS, Catherine Hardrick is a woman of unassuming greatness whose humble acts of
21 kindness and generosity have uplifted the entire community; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
23 extraordinary servant leader be appropriately recognized.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body congratulate and commend Catherine Hardrick for receiving the
26 2025 Yellow Rose Nikki T. Randall Servant Leadership Award and extend best wishes for
27 her continued success.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Catherine Hardrick.
