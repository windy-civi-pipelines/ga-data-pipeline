Section 1:
25 LC 47 3224/AP
By: Representative Yearta of the 152nd
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to reconstitute the Board of Education of Lee County, approved
2 April 9, 1993 (Ga. L. 1993, p. 5130), as amended, so as to revise the compensation of the
3 members of such board of education; to provide for an effective date; to provide for related
4 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 An Act to reconstitute the Board of Education of Lee County, approved April 9, 1993
8 (Ga. L. 1993, p. 5130), as amended, is amended by revising Section 6 as follows:
9 "SECTION 6.
10 Members of the Board of Education of Lee County shall be compensated in the amount
11 of $175.00 per month. Members of the board, including the chairperson, as approved by
12 the board, shall be reimbursed for the actual expenses necessarily incurred for attending
13 meetings and nonathletic events in the performance of their official duties for the Lee
14 County School District."
H. B. 720
- 1 -
25 LC 47 3224/AP
15 SECTION 2.
16 This Act shall become effective on the first day of the month following the month in which
17 it is approved by the Governor or in which it becomes law without such approval.
18 SECTION 3.
19 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
