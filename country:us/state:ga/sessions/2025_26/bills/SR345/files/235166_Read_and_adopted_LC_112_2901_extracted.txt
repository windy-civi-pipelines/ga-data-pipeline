25 LC 112 2901
Senate Resolution 345
13th, Echols of the 49th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the 2025 Senate Aides and Senate Academic Aides for their exemplary service;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, each year, the Georgia Senate relies on citizens of Georgia who are interested
4 in the legislative process to work as aides for the Senate committees and leadership; and
5 WHEREAS, these individuals hail from varied backgrounds and from all areas of this state
6 and have demonstrated a commitment to ensuring the efficient and effective administration
7 of Senate committee hearings; and
8 WHEREAS, the Georgia Senate has been very fortunate to have had the exceptional service
9 of legislative aides, whose work has been marked by a devotion to helping the citizens of
10 Georgia and a constant commitment to the highest standards of excellence; and
11 WHEREAS, the chairpersons and members of the standing committees and Senate leadership
12 salute and commend each of the following Senate Aides for their hard work and a job well
13 done: Adeline Bennett, AJ Murray, Amadi Gaines, Annaliese Mundt, Anthony Lecca, Ashley
14 Ogega, Audrey McNeal, Benjamin Michaud, Blake Yselonia, Brooke Partridge, Caleb
15 Morse, Claire Ivey, Cole Simmons, Devin Bell, Dylan Kautz, Ella Salter, Emma Beck,
S. R. 345
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2901
16 Emorie Holland, Francesca Almeida, Grace King, Hannah Davenport, Hannah McDaniel,
17 Harrison Caswell, Harry Greer, Iman Ibraheem, Isabel Crosby, Jaelyn Gamble, John Tomlin,
18 Justin Lancaster, Kai Ward, Kendall Griffin, Lauren Pederson, Manaswi Kolla, Matthew
19 Hurt, Michelle Silos, Molly Roberts, Nahomy Limon, Nick Henson, Olivia Davis, Robert
20 Persons, Samuel Martinez, Sanya Kaushal, Stephanie Rivera, Thomas Nilsen, Valencia John,
21 and Victoria Oltean; and
22 WHEREAS, the hard work and dedication of these distinguished Georgians have been
23 enormous assets to the citizens of Georgia; and
24 WHEREAS, all the members of the Senate greatly appreciate the invaluable service of aides
25 who enable their business to run smoothly and successfully.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
27 commend the Senate Aides and Senate Academic Aides for the 2025 regular session on their
28 superb performance and convey their best wishes to each of them for success and happiness
29 in their future pursuits.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
31 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the 2025 Senate
32 Aides and Senate Academic Aides.
