Senate Resolution 196
By: Senator Sims of the 12th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Phoebe Putney Health System is the largest employer in southwest Georgia as
3 well as a leading provider of comprehensive medical care, ensuring that residents have access
4 to advanced healthcare close to home; and
5 WHEREAS, on January 15, 2025, Phoebe Putney Health System opened its new $150
6 million Trauma & Critical Care Tower, featuring a vastly expanded Emergency & Trauma
7 Center—the second-only Level II Trauma Center in Georgia south of Atlanta—as well as a
8 new Neonatal Intensive Care Unit that is more than four times the size of the previous facility
9 and a new Surgical Intensive Care Unit with 20 additional critical care beds; and
10 WHEREAS, Phoebe Putney Health System is a leader in healthcare workforce development,
11 as exemplified by the Phoebe Living & Learning Community, an innovative partnership with
12 Albany Technical College that provides housing and training for students, helping to
13 quadruple the college's number of nursing graduates; and
14 WHEREAS, the Phoebe Health Science Pathway allows high school students at Commodore
15 Conyers College & Career Academy to earn nursing assistant certification and dual

17 University and Albany Technical College; and
18 WHEREAS, Phoebe Putney Health System supports medical education by housing regional
