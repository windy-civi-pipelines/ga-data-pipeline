Section 1:
25 LC 50 0979
By: Senators Islam Parkes of the 7th, Jones II of the 22nd, Parent of the 44th, Rahman of the
5th, Esteves of the 35th and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 48-8-3 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 exemptions from sales and use taxes, so as to exempt sales of clothing and school related
3 supplies during the second week of August each year; to provide for related matters; to repeal
4 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Code Section 48-8-3 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to exemptions from
8 sales and use taxes, is amended by revising subparagraph (A) of paragraph (75) as follows:
9 "(75)(A) The sale of eligible property. The exemption provided by this paragraph
10 applies only to sales occurring during the period commencing at 12:01 A.M. on July 30,
11 2016, August 7 and concluding at 12:00 Midnight on July 31, 2016 August 13 of each
12 year."
13 SECTION 2.
14 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 115
