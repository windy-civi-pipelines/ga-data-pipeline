Section 1:
25 LC 48 1414
By: Representatives McQueen of the 61st, Bell of the 75th, Berry of the 56th, Cannon of the
58th, and Willis of the 55th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 6 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to sexual
2 offenses, so as to revise penalties for violations of certain offenses; to provide for fines for
3 such violations and to provide that such collected fines be made available to the Safe Harbor
4 for Sexually Exploited Children Fund Commission; to provide for related matters; to repeal
5 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 Chapter 6 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to sexual offenses,
9 is amended by revising subsection (a) of Code Section 16-6-13, relating to penalties for
10 violating Code Sections 16-6-9 through 16-6-12, as follows:
11 "(a) Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) of this Code section, a person
12 convicted of violating:
13 (1) Code Section 16-6-10 16-6-9 shall be punished as for a misdemeanor of a high and
14 aggravated nature, and at the sole discretion of the judge, all but 24 hours of any term of
15 imprisonment imposed may be suspended, stayed, or probated fined $75.00, and such

17 Harbor for Sexually Exploited Children Fund Commission;
18 (2) Code Section 16-6-9 16-6-10 shall be punished as for a misdemeanor fined $75.00,

20 the Safe Harbor for Sexually Exploited Children Fund Commission;
21 (3) Code Section 16-6-11 for a:
22 (A) First offense shall be punished as for a misdemeanor of a high and aggravated
