House Resolution 880
and Momtahan of the 17th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring Lydia Hallmark; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Lydia Hallmark has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the
3 vital role that she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare
4 of the citizens of Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, Mrs. Hallmark is a member of the Floyd County, Paulding County, and Cobb
6 County Republican Women, as well as the Paulding County Republican Party and the Cobb
7 County Republican Party; and
8 WHEREAS, she served the Paulding County GOP as assistant treasurer, chair of the
9 nominating committee, and corresponding secretary; and
10 WHEREAS, in 2008, she co-founded the Paulding County Republican Women’s Club where
11 she has held numerous positions; and
12 WHEREAS, currently, she holds the title of President of the Paulding County Republican
13 Women's Club; and
H. R. 880
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0456
14 WHEREAS, Mrs. Hallmark's exemplary service with the Georgia Federation of Republican
15 women has earned her the respect and admiration of her colleagues and associates; and
16 WHEREAS, in 2016, she was elected as delegate to the Republican National Convention to
17 represent the 14th District; and
18 WHEREAS, in 2019, Mrs. Hallmark served as 14th District Chair for “Women for Trump,"
19 and further supported the president as a Trump 47 Captain, in 2024; and
20 WHEREAS, she is the proud Recipient of the only two awards given by the Paulding County
21 Republican Party, the 2011 Grassroots Volunteer of the year award and the 2015 Betty Finch,
22 Lifetime Achievement Award; and
23 WHEREAS, she received the Republican of the Year award at District Convention 2021 and
24 the Heritage Action We the People Award, in Orlando, in November 2021, for her work on
25 the Georgia Election Integrity Bill; and
26 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
27 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
28 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
29 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body recognize and commend Lydia Hallmark for her efficient,
32 effective, unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia and extend the most
33 sincere best wishes for continued health and happiness.
H. R. 880

34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Lydia
36 Hallmark.
