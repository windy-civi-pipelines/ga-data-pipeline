25 LC 112 3119
House Resolution 353
of the 96th, and McClain of the 109th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Kayode Adekunle; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Kayode Adekunle has diligently and conscientiously dedicated over 30 years
3 to community leadership and service, playing a vital role in uplifting and advocating for the
4 Nigerian and African diaspora communities in Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, he currently serves as president of the Alliance of Nigerian Organizations in
6 Georgia (ANOG), an umbrella organization representing over 40 diverse Nigerian diaspora
7 associations, where he has worked to expand the organization's visibility, strengthen
8 community engagement, and cultivate relationships with elected officials; and
9 WHEREAS, under his leadership, ANOG has elevated its signature events, including the
10 Annual Nigeria Cultural Day and Banquet Awards, launched a quarterly newsletter to
11 promote diaspora businesses, and expanded outreach to local county leadership to champion
12 causes and foster collaborations; and
13 WHEREAS, he has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to humanitarian efforts, leading
14 initiatives to provide food, shelter, and employment assistance to individuals and families in
H. R. 353
- 1 -

15 need, including raising over $12,000 to support those impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, beyond ANOG, he has been an active leader in numerous community
18 organizations, including over a decade of service to the Nigerian American Public Affairs
19 Committee; and
20 WHEREAS, in addition to his service to the community, he is a highly regarded
21 cybersecurity consultant with expertise in cyber threat management, regulatory compliance,
22 layered defense strategies, computing platforms, and network design who holds multiple
23 professional certifications; and
24 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable individual be
25 appropriately honored during this special time.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body recognize and commend Kayode Adekunle for his many
28 outstanding accomplishments and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health
29 and happiness.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
32 Kayode Adekunle.
