25 LC 129 0343
House Resolution 758
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the for the North Oconee High School boys basketball team for winning the
2 2025 GHSA Class 4A State Basketball Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the North Oconee High School boys basketball team has established and
4 maintained a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics;
5 and
6 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
7 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
8 2025 campaign, the team captured its second consecutive state basketball championship; and
9 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school by winning the 2025 GHSA
10 Class 4A State Basketball Championship against Pace Academy with a final score of 60 to
11 51; and
12 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
13 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
14 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Head Coach Rick
15 Rasmussen; and
H. R. 758
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0343
16 WHEREAS, this all-star team included starting five Justin Wise, Thrasher Wilkins, Khamari
17 Brooks, Blake Bracco, and Banks Pourchier; and
18 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
19 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
20 abundantly proper to call them champions.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body congratulate the North Oconee High School boys basketball team
23 for winning the 2025 GHSA Class 4A State Basketball Championship and extend best
24 wishes for their continued success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 North Oconee High School boys basketball team..
