A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending DJ Aone on the occasion of District 58 Day; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, presented by GOAT Ave Inc. and The Corporate Influencer Academy, District
4 58 Day is a community initiative dedicated to uplifting individuals and recognizing
5 outstanding leaders who have made a significant impact in their respective fields; and
6 WHEREAS, DJ Aone is a highly respected and influential DJ known for his ability to blend
7 diverse musical genres, create unforgettable experiences, and elevate the cultural landscape
8 through his artistry; and
9 WHEREAS, with a career spanning over a decade, he has performed at major festivals,
10 events, and venues; collaborated with top-tier artists; and used his platform to promote
11 creativity, unity, and inclusivity; and
12 WHEREAS, beyond his success as a DJ, he is a dedicated mentor and advocate for aspiring
13 musicians and producers, providing guidance and resources to help them navigate the music
14 industry; and

15 WHEREAS, as a strong ally of the LGBTQ+ community, DJ Aone actively supports
16 initiatives that promote acceptance, equality, social justice, and the empowerment of

18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this distinguished individual be
19 appropriately honored during this special time.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body recognize and commend DJ Aone on the occasion of District 58

23 continued success.
