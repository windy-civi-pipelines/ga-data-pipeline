25 LC 129 0487
House Resolution 976
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the 2025 Heart of the Community Young Heart Award
2 recipient, Ciara McKnight; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Ciara McKnight is a Rome, Georgia, native, the youngest of seven siblings, and
4 combines her passion for family, community, and personal growth in all she does; and
5 WHEREAS, Ciara began her career in 2013, working with adolescents and later transitioning
6 to roles supporting adults in the Georgia Department of Corrections; and
7 WHEREAS, Ciara serves as a Field Coordinator and Adjunct Teacher at Kennesaw State
8 University and supports local nonprofits by sharing her expertise as a certified trainer; and
9 WHEREAS, her heart for youth empowerment has lead her to found A Teen's Choice, Inc.,
10 A Dream Ball Gala, and The CASH Program; and
11 WHEREAS, Ciara is an Open Gym Coordinator, Trainer for Teen Communication, Senior
12 Program Leader, Substance Abuse Group Facilitator, Mental Health Advocate, and Youth
13 Mentor; and
H. R. 976
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0487
14 WHEREAS, Ciara serves as the Board Chair for Chattooga Family Connection and the
15 Family Well-being Workshop Host to strengthen family and community support systems;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, Ciara is the organizer of Home for the Holidays and the Back-to-School Drive
18 as well as a Prevention Group Facilitator, leading wellness and self-care programs and a
19 Community Service Collaborator, working with agencies to support local needs; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding achievements of this
21 exemplary Georgian be appropriately recognized.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize Ciara McKnight for her outstanding dedication to the
24 community, commend her as a recipient of the 2025 Heart of the Community Young Heart
25 Award, and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Ciara
28 McKnight.
