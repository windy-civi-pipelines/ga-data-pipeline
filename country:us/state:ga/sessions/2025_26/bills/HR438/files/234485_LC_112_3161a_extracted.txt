25 LC 112 3161
House Resolution 438
67th, Davis of the 87th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Spelman College Glee Club on its 100th anniversary for its outstanding
2 contributions to the arts and community; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Spelman College Glee Club, established in 1924, has a proud and rich
4 tradition of musical excellence, serving as a beacon of artistic achievement for over a
5 century; and
6 WHEREAS, through its commitment to the pursuit of musical excellence, the Glee Club has
7 become a cornerstone of Spelman College's cultural and artistic identity, enriching the lives
8 of its members and audiences within their mission to amaze and inspire; and
9 WHEREAS, the Glee Club has performed with distinguished artists and organizations such
10 as the Atlanta Symphony Orchestra, Aretha Franklin, Stevie Wonder, President Barack
11 Obama, the Mormon Tabernacle Choir, and India.Arie, among many others, demonstrating
12 its global reach and impact in the field of music; and
13 WHEREAS, in celebration of its 100th anniversary, the Spelman College Glee Club has
14 continued to build bridges through its performances, uniting communities, cultures, and
15 generations in song and spirit; and
H. R. 438
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3161
16 WHEREAS, the Glee Club's unwavering commitment to its mission, fostering artistic talent,
17 discipline, and leadership, has set a standard for excellence and has produced remarkable
18 leaders who have carried the legacy of the Spelman spirit to every corner of the world; and
19 WHEREAS, the Glee Club's remarkable performances and community outreach, including
20 its participation in high-profile events such as Jimmy Carter's 100th birthday celebration and
21 the King Celebration, reflect the Glee Club's role as an ambassador for Spelman College and
22 the broader community; and
23 WHEREAS, under the direction of Dr. Kevin P. Johnson, the Glee Club has flourished and
24 expanded its influence, continuing to inspire and touch the lives of many through music and
25 dedication.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body commend the Spelman College Glee Club for its 100 years of
28 musical excellence, its contributions to the arts, and its role as a powerful force for cultural
29 enrichment, unity, and social change as well as extend their heartfelt congratulations and best
30 wishes for continued success in the years to come.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
33 Spelman College Glee Club.
H. R. 438
