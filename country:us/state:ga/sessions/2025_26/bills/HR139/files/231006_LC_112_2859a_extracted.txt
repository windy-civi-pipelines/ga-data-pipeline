25 LC 112 2859
House Resolution 139
17th, and Smith of the 18th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 4, 2025, as Georgia Association of Voter Registration and Election
2 Officials Day at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Voter Registrar's Association of Georgia (VRAG) was established in 1969
4 for the purpose of educating, assisting, and communicating the Georgia Election Code to
5 registrars throughout the State of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Election Officials Association (GEOA) was established in 1985 for
7 the purpose of educating, assisting, and communicating the Georgia Election Code to all
8 election officials and probate judges serving as election superintendents throughout the State
9 of Georgia; and
10 WHEREAS, in 2017, VRAG and GEOA voted to combine and become a joined association,
11 the Georgia Association of Voter Registration and Election Officials, with the majority of
12 Georgia's counties combining boards individually; and
13 WHEREAS, it is a registrar's responsibility to register individuals to vote, to keep records
14 of these individuals, and to issue absentee ballots; and
H. R. 139
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2859
15 WHEREAS, it is the responsibility of election superintendents and probate judges to conduct
16 elections and maintain voting equipment; and
17 WHEREAS, election officials and registration officials pledge to freedom and democracy
18 and uphold strict and high standards of conduct within their profession so that the public may
19 have due confidence in them while exercising their right to vote; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the hard work and high standards of all
21 election officials and registrars in the State of Georgia be appropriately recognized.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize February 4, 2025, as Georgia Association of Voter
24 Registration and Election Officials Day at the state capitol.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 Georgia Association of Voter Registration and Election Officials.
