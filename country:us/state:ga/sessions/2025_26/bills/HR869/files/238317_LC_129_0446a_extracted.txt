25 LC 129 0446
House Resolution 869
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Samantha Patton on winning the State Championship in the
2 Girls 130LB Weight Class Division at the 2024 GHSA Traditional Wrestling State
3 Championship; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, Samantha Patton of Cook High School earned the top title in the state with her
5 win in the Girls 130LB Weight Class Division at the 2024 GHSA Traditional Wrestling State
6 Championship; and
7 WHEREAS, Samantha's performance at the state tournament and championship victory have
8 earned her the title of first Lady Hornet to become a state champ in wrestling, and are a
9 fitting end to an exceptional high school wrestling career; and
10 WHEREAS, this stellar young woman epitomizes the courage, determination, leadership, and
11 character necessary to attain excellence in athletic prowess and performance; and
12 WHEREAS, her months of training and competition have been rewarded with an impressive
13 win, and it is appropriate that she holds the title of champion; and
H. R. 869
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0446
14 WHEREAS, Samantha's victory was the culmination of a season of hard work, dedication,
15 and excellent sportsmanship; and
16 WHEREAS, her superior skill, strategy, stamina, and control have earned her the respect and
17 admiration of her teammates and challengers; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the superlative abilities of this
19 accomplished and gifted student-athlete, as well as the pride, honor, and respect she has
20 brought to herself, her school, and her community, be appropriately recognized.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body recognize and commend Samantha Patton on winning the State
23 Championship in the Girls 130LB Weight Class Division at the 2024 GHSA Traditional
24 Wrestling State Championship and extend best wishes for success in the future.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
27 Samantha Patton.
H. R. 869
