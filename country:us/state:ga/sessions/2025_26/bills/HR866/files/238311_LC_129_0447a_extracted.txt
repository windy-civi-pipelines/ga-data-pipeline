25 LC 129 0447
House Resolution 866
47th, and Holland of the 54th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Holy Innocents' Episcopal School girls and boys basketball teams for
2 winning the 2025 GHSA Class A-AAA Private State Championships; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Holy Innocents' Episcopal School girls and boys basketball teams have
4 established and maintained a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphasis on character,
5 academics, and athletic achievement; and
6 WHEREAS, the girls basketball team secured its fifth state championship in program history,
7 finishing an outstanding season with a record of 25 wins and 5 losses, while the boys
8 basketball team captured its first-ever state championship with an impressive record of 27
9 wins and 4 losses; and
10 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made both
11 teams remarkable to observe throughout the season, culminating in their triumphant victories
12 at the 2025 GHSA Class A-AAA Private State Championships; and
13 WHEREAS, several players and coaches received prestigious honors from the Atlanta
14 Tip-Off Club, including senior Haley Swain being named Miss Georgia Basketball, Caleb
15 Wilson earning the title of Mr. Georgia Basketball, and Head Coaches Nichole Dixon and
H. R. 866
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0447
16 Mario Mays receiving the esteemed Garland-Pinholster Girls and Boys Coach of the Year
17 awards, respectively; and
18 WHEREAS, Nakhai Worthy was recognized for her exceptional talent and contributions to
19 the girls basketball team by earning a spot on the All-Metro Third Team; and
20 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performances of these teams are a testament to the outstanding
21 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose of each team member; the skillful
22 coaching of their leaders; and the unwavering support of their school and community; and
23 WHEREAS, as these magnificent teams earned their well-deserved reputations for
24 excellence by performing with courage, precision, and determination over months of rigorous
25 training and competition, it is abundantly proper to call them champions.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body congratulate the Holy Innocents' Episcopal School girls and boys
28 basketball teams for winning the 2025 GHSA Class A-AAA Private State Championships
29 and extend best wishes for their continued success.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 Holy Innocents' Episcopal School girls and boys basketball teams.
