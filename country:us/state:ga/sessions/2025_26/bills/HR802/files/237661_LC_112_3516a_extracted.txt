House Resolution 802
the 86th, Willis of the 55th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Lisa Cunningham; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Lisa Cunningham is a distinguished marketing and communications executive
3 whose award-winning leadership has advanced national conversations around women's
4 health through bold, innovative campaigns that deliver measurable impact; and
5 WHEREAS, she currently serves as executive vice president of marketing &
6 communications at the Black Women's Health Imperative, where she has spearheaded
7 high-profile campaigns addressing breast and cervical cancer awareness and diabetes
8 prevention; and
9 WHEREAS, her cervical cancer awareness campaign, produced in partnership with Ciara
10 and Hologic, earned an Anthem Award and garnered more than 58 million organic
11 impressions, while her strategic leadership fueled a more than 2,700 percent increase in
12 audience engagement and a 211 percent growth in social media followers; and
13 WHEREAS, Lisa began her professional journey in the entertainment industry, directing and
14 producing over 1,000 branded and music videos for major artists and entertainment
15 companies before transitioning into purpose-driven marketing work for organizations such

16 as the CDC, McDonald's, Ford Motors, W Hotels, ViiV Healthcare, and a host of nonprofit
17 partners; and
18 WHEREAS, her most recent project, Me Period—a documentary sponsored by Procter &

20 explores inter-generational health conversations between Black mothers and daughters; and
21 WHEREAS, her civic engagement includes appointments by two Atlanta mayors to the city's

24 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this distinguished individual be

28 marketing, steadfast advocacy for women's health, and ongoing contributions to her
29 community as well as extend best wishes for continued success.

31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Lisa
32 Cunningham.
