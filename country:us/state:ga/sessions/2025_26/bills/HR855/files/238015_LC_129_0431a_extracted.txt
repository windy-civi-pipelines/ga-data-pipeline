25 LC 129 0431
House Resolution 855
the 78th, and Hugley of the 141st
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Charles R. Drew Public Charter School varsity boys golf team for
2 winning the State Golfing Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Charles R. Drew Public Charter School varsity boys golf team has
4 established and maintained a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character
5 and academics; and
6 WHEREAS, this superlative team has all Black players and an all-Black coaching staff,
7 making them the first all-Black team, and first Atlatna Public Schools Team, to win a State
8 Golf Championship in Georgia; and
9 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
10 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season; and
11 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school y beating the two-time
12 defending state champions by more than 15 strokes; and
H. R. 855
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0431
13 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
14 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
15 team, and the astute direction of Head Coach Nyre Williams; and
16 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
17 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
18 abundantly proper to call them champions.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body congratulate the Charles R. Drew Public Charter School varsity
21 boys golf team for winning the State Golfing Championship and extend best wishes for their
22 continued success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
25 Charles R. Drew Public Charter School varsity boys golf team.
