16th, and Smith of the 18th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending East Paulding High School's Career and Technical Student
2 Organizations; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, East Paulding High School's Career and Technical Student Organization
4 (CTSO) students are visiting the state capitol on February 5, 2025, for CTSO Day,
5 representing such outstanding organizations as DECA, HOSA, SkillsUSA, and FFA; and
6 WHEREAS, East Paulding's DECA students have demonstrated continued excellency
7 through their many achievements, including earning Industry Certification in 2024, winning
8 the 2024 Region Championship, attaining Gold Certified School-Based Enterprise status, and
9 ranking among the Top 10 Largest Chapters in Georgia; and
10 WHEREAS, DECA officers Mamie Jeffries, Alyssa Gurley, Kendylle Jones, Zakiya
11 Castello, and Angelina Arce, along with advisors Halie Bissell and Megan Dupree, have
12 provided leadership and guidance in fostering student success within the organization; and
13 WHEREAS, East Paulding's HOSA students have made a significant impact through their
14 healthcare education initiatives, maintaining over 100 active members annually for the past
