Section 1:
25 Floor Amend 1 AM 49 0241
ADOPTED
1 Amend the Senate Committee on Education and Youth substitute to SB 124 (LC 49 2250S)
2 by inserting "to require the publication and reporting of certain information relating to federal
3 guidance documents;" following "families;" on line 5.
4 By replacing lines 37 through 38 with the following:
5 (c)(1) Within 15 calendar days of receiving a guidance document from a federal agency
6 which the State School Superintendent or the State Board of Education deems relevant
7 and applicable to the duties, responsibilities, or business of the Department of Education,
8 the Department of Education shall publish such guidance document in accordance with
9 the requirements of subsection (d) of this Code section.
10 (2) Within 45 calendar days of receiving a guidance document provided for in
11 paragraph (1) of this subsection, the Department of Education shall include on the
12 dedicated web page provided for in subsection (d) of this Code section a written response
13 to such guidance document that includes, at a minimum:
14 (A) Each federal or state statute, regulation, or rule that is addressed either expressly
15 or by reasonable implication, interpretation, or application in such guidance document;
16 and
17 (B) A description of how such guidance document will be implemented or objected to.
18 (3) Within 15 calendar days of a guidance document provided for in paragraph (1) of this
19 subsection being rescinded, the Department of Education shall:
20 (A) Clearly indicate on the dedicated web page provided for in subsection (c) of this
21 Code section that such guidance document has been rescinded; and
22 (B) Add the rescinded guidance document along with the date such guidance document
23 was rescinded to a separate and clearly identifiable list of rescinded guidance
- 1 -

24 documents to be maintained on the dedicated web page provided for in subsection (c)
25 of this Code section.
26 (d) All guidance documents and rescinded guidance documents which are required to be
27 published by the Department of Education under subsection (c) of this Code section shall
28 be published on a dedicated web page on the Department of Education's public website that
29 is clearly identifiable, readily searchable using the search tool included on such public
30 website, and accessible via a link that is included on the home page of such public website.
31 (e) Annually by August 1, the State School Superintendent shall provide in writing the
32 following information to the General Assembly: a complete list of guidance documents and
33 rescinded guidance documents that are currently published on the dedicated web page
34 provided for in subsection (d) of this Code section and a complete copy of each rule
35 adopted by the State Board of Education pursuant to subsection (f) of this Code section.
36 (f) The State Board of Education shall be authorized to adopt rules as necessary to
37 implement the requirements of this Code section. Reserved."
