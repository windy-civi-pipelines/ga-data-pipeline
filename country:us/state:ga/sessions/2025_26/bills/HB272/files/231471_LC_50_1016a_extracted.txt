Section 1:
25 LC 50 1016
By: Representatives Blackmon of the 146th, Williamson of the 112th, Smith of the 138th,
Kelley of the 16th, Crowe of the 118th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 1 of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 general provisions regarding revenue and taxation, so as to change certain provisions
3 regarding definitions; to provide for an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for
4 other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Chapter 1 of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to general
8 provisions regarding revenue and taxation, is amended in Code Section 48-1-2, relating to
9 definitions, by revising paragraph (1) as follows:
10 "(1) 'Agency' means any department, commission, institution, office, or officer of this
11 state."
12 SECTION 2.
13 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
14 without such approval.
H. B. 272
- 1 -
25 LC 50 1016
15 SECTION 3.
16 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
