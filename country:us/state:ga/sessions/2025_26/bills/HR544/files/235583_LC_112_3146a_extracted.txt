25 LC 112 3146
House Resolution 544
the 61st, and Santos of the 117th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dr. Paula Greaves; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Paula Greaves has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable
3 hours of her time, talents, and energy toward the betterment of her community and state, as
4 evidenced dramatically by her distinguished service and leadership in women's health; and
5 WHEREAS, since completing her residency in obstetrics and gynecology at the University
6 of Tennessee in Knoxville in 1997 and moving to Marietta, Georgia, Dr. Greaves has
7 provided exceptional care to women of diverse backgrounds; and
8 WHEREAS, she has served as a practicing physician at Wellstar Northwest Women's Care
9 and as chief of the Wellstar Health System Women's Service Line, where she has worked to
10 improve maternal health outcomes and care for menopausal women; and
11 WHEREAS, Dr. Greaves is a fellow of the American Board of Obstetrics and Gynecology
12 and a frequent speaker for numerous community organizations who uses her platform to
13 advocate for improved maternal health and patient outcomes; and
H. R. 544
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3146
14 WHEREAS, Dr. Greaves is deeply committed to decreasing maternal morbidity and
15 mortality and providing care to women in accordance with her service motto, "Every woman
16 every time, at all phases of life"; and
17 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
18 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and she has given inspiration to many through
19 her high ideals, morals, and deep concern for her fellow citizens; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
21 remarkable and distinguished physician be appropriately honored.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Dr. Paula Greaves for her many
24 exemplary contributions on behalf of the citizens of this state as well as extend the most
25 sincere best wishes for her continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
28 Paula Greaves.
