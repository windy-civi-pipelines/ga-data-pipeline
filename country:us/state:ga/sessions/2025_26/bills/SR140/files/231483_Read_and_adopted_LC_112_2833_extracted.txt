25 LC 112 2833
Senate Resolution 140
Harbison of the 15th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Roosevelt Robinson; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Roosevelt Robinson on January 5, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Roosevelt Robinson was born on November 11, 1936, in Pike County, Georgia,
5 a beloved son of Charlie and Lillie Mae Alexander Robinson; and
6 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife, Blanche, and was blessed with
7 three remarkable children, Roosevelt Jr., Randy, and Deedrea; seven wonderful
8 grandchildren, Jason, Justin, Tyler, Jordan, Patrick, William Jr., and Kaelon; and four
9 outstanding great-grandchildren, Jaida, Justin Jr., Amirah, and Naliyah; and
10 WHEREAS, Roosevelt Robinson found joy in the simple pleasures of life, particularly
11 fishing and gardening, and he remained a source of love and strength for his family despite
12 his declining health; and
S. R. 140
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2833
13 WHEREAS, he gave inspiration to many through his high ideals, morals, and deep concern
14 for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding he demonstrated to his
15 family and friends were admired by others; and
16 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
17 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
18 made this world a better place in which to live; and
19 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Roosevelt Robinson will long be
20 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
21 had the great fortune of knowing him.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 join in honoring the life and memory of Roosevelt Robinson and express their deepest and
24 most sincere regret at his passing.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
26 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the family of
27 Roosevelt Robinson.
S. R. 140
