25 LC 129 0259
House Resolution 783
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Yixin "Linda" Pan, Darlington High School's 2025 STAR Student; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a gifted student at Darligton High School, Yixin "Linda" Pan has been
4 recognized for her superlative achievement and selected as Darligton High School's 2025
5 STAR Student; and
6 WHEREAS, to be selected as a STAR Student, a student must have obtained the highest
7 score in one administration of the three-part Scholastic Aptitude Test and be in the top 10
8 percent or top ten students of his or her class; and
9 WHEREAS, each school has one STAR Student who in turn has the opportunity to choose
10 a STAR Teacher who has been inspiring and encouraging throughout that student's high
11 school career; and
12 WHEREAS, educator Owen Kinney was chosen by Yixin "Linda" Pan as Darlington High
13 School's 2025 STAR Teacher for the great support and guidance provided to her throughout
14 the years; and
H. R. 783
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0259
15 WHEREAS, Yixin "Linda" Pan has personified the selfless dedication, mental agility, verbal
16 adroitness, and personal maturity necessary for superlative academic performance; and
17 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
18 important objective of the General Assembly, and Yixin "Linda" Pan has shown by academic
19 accomplishment that the pursuit and attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of
20 the students in this state; and
21 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia looks with great anticipation toward the future of this
22 bright and promising young citizen.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body heartily commend Yixin "Linda" Pan for being named Darlington
25 High School's 2025 STAR Student and express their most sincere best wishes for continued
26 academic success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Yixin
29 "Linda" Pan.
