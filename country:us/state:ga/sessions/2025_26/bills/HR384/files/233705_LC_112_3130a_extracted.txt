25 LC 112 3130
House Resolution 384
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending the Ben Hill County School District, recipient of the
2 Georgia Reads Community Award, for its dedication to literacy advancement; and for other
3 purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council on Literacy is dedicated to improving literacy from birth
5 through adulthood and recognizes that communities play a critical role in addressing low
6 literacy rates; and
7 WHEREAS, the Georgia Reads Community Award was established to recognize
8 communities across Georgia that have formed partnerships of two or more organizations,
9 sustained for at least three years, with a strategic plan and measurable results in literacy
10 improvement; and
11 WHEREAS, the Ben Hill County School District is a recipient of the Georgia Reads
12 Community Award for its collaborative efforts to address barriers that students face, such as
13 poverty, lack of resources, and academic challenges; and
H. R. 384
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3130
14 WHEREAS, Communities in Schools collaborates with the Ben Hill County School District
15 to place site coordinators within schools to build relationships with students and families,
16 offering mentorship, counseling, and academic support; and
17 WHEREAS, its meaningful partnerships include the Ben Hill County School District,
18 Communities in Schools, L4GA, CLCP, Fitzgerald-Ben Hill Chamber of Commerce,
19 Dorminy Medical Center, the Monitor Enrichment Program, civic organizations, PTO, and
20 numerous private businesses and industries in Fitzgerald and Ben Hill County, demonstrating
21 a strong commitment to literacy development through community collaboration; and
22 WHEREAS, the Ben Hill County School District and its partners have diligently developed
23 and implemented innovative programs that have increased literacy rates within their
24 communities; and
25 WHEREAS, these initiatives have led to measurable improvements in reading proficiency,
26 ensuring greater opportunities for success in education and career development; and
27 WHEREAS, the dedication and hard work of the Ben Hill County School District and its
28 partners serve as an inspiration to others, demonstrating the power of community-driven
29 solutions to address literacy challenges.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body congratulate and commend the Georgia Reads Community Award
32 recipients, particularly the Ben Hill County School District, for their exemplary service and
33 unwavering commitment to improving literacy in their communities.
H. R. 384
- 2 -
25 LC 112 3130
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
36 Ben Hill County School District.
