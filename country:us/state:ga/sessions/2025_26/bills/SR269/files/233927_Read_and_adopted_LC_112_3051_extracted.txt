25 LC 112 3051
Senate Resolution 269
Merritt of the 9th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the University of Georgia women's tennis team for winning the 2025 ITA
2 National Team Indoor Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the University of Georgia women's tennis team has established and maintained
4 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team continues its triumphant season of tennis competition by
6 securing the 2025 ITA National Team Indoor Championship, marking the seventh national
7 championship and fifth national indoor title in program history; and
8 WHEREAS, the Bulldogs demonstrated their strength in doubles play with Guillermina
9 Grant and Anastasiia Lopata earning a dominant 6-1 victory on court two, while Dasha
10 Vidmanova and Mell Reasco fought back to claim a 7-5 win on court one; and
11 WHEREAS, in singles competition, Sofia Rojas delivered a commanding 6-2, 6-4 victory
12 on court six to extend the Bulldogs' lead, followed by Aysegul Mert's hard-fought 6-2, 7-5
13 win on court four, putting Georgia one point away from clinching the title; and
S. R. 269
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3051
14 WHEREAS, top-ranked Dasha Vidmanova secured the championship for the Bulldogs with
15 a clutch performance, defeating No. 3 Mary Stoiana in a tense second-set tiebreaker to win
16 6-1, 7-6(5) and seal Georgia's place as national champions; and
17 WHEREAS, Dasha Vidmanova made history by becoming the first college women's tennis
18 player since 2013 to achieve the championship trifecta, winning national titles in team,
19 singles, and doubles competition during her collegiate career; and
20 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
21 athletic ability, dedication, hard work in the right direction, use of tools, and unity of purpose
22 and spirit of each member of the team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the
23 ability to fit Capability to Opportunity under the astute direction of Head Coach Drake
24 Bernstein, the fastest head coach in program history to win a national championship at the
25 helm; and
26 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
27 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
28 abundantly proper to call them champions.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
30 congratulate the University of Georgia women's tennis team for winning the 2025 ITA
31 National Team Indoor Championship and extend best wishes for continued success.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
33 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the University of
34 Georgia women's tennis team.
S. R. 269
