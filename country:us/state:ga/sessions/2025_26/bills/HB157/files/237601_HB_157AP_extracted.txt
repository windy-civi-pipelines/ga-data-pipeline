Section 1:
25 LC 59 0020/AP
By: Representatives Dunahoo of the 31st, Persinger of the 119th, Gaines of the 120th, and
Erwin of the 32nd
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to provide a new charter for the Town of Braselton, approved June 3, 2003
2 (Ga. L. 2003, p. 4319), so as to annex certain properties into the town; to provide for related
3 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; to provide an effective date; and for other purposes.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
5 SECTION 1.
6 An Act to provide a new charter for the Town of Braselton, approved June 3, 2003 (Ga. L.
7 2003, p. 4319), is amended by adding a new subsection to Section 1.11 to read as follows:
8 "(c) In addition to the other provisions of this section, the corporate limits of the town shall
9 include the following described properties:
10 All that tract or parcel of land lying and being in the 1765 and 1407, being Tracts 1 and
11 2 as shown on that certain survey for Town of Braselton Georgia dated November 21,
12 2023 by DES Davis Engineering & Surveying, Dusty L. Lowman, PLS 3216 recorded
13 11-22-2023 in Plat Book 87, page 51, Jackson County, Georgia records.
14 LESS AND EXCEPT Jackson County Tax Parcel 019 122 and B01 016A."
H. B. 157
- 1 -
25 LC 59 0020/AP
15 SECTION 2.
16 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
17 without such approval.
18 SECTION 3.
19 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 157
