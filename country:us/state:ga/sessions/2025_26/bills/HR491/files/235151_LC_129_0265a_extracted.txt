25 LC 129 0265
House Resolution 491
of the 11th, and Cox of the 28th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the South Forsyth High School co-ed cheer team, the 2024 GHSA Class 6A Cheer
2 State Champions; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the South Forsyth High School co-ed cheer team has demonstrated outstanding
4 skill, dedication, and perseverance, culminating in their victory as the 2024 GHSA Class 6A
5 Cheer State Champions; and
6 WHEREAS, the team's achievement is a testament to their hard work, teamwork, and the
7 unwavering support of their coaches, families, and school community; and
8 WHEREAS, through their commitment to excellence, the team has brought great pride and
9 recognition to South Forsyth High School and Forsyth County; and
10 WHEREAS, the following student-athletes have exemplified dedication, sportsmanship, and
11 talent in securing this championship: Lyla Boleware, Shea Cheek, Lizzie Crane, Lizzy
12 Gaffney, Chloe Handschumacher, McKenzie Heath, Ellie Heglin, Reilly Hill, Addison
13 Litzlinger, Madison Malave, Emma Mason, Dania Nacy, Layla Newbold, Amanda Ricci,
14 Jackson Schmuhl, Rylie Shull, Emma Smith, and Ella Yu; and
H. R. 491
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0265
15 WHEREAS, the dedicated leadership and guidance of the coaching staff were instrumental
16 in the team's success, coaches Stacey Schmuhl, Lyndsey Phelps, and Lyndi McCloud are
17 commended for their efforts; and
18 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
19 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
20 abundantly proper to call them champions.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body recognize and honor the South Forsyth High School co-ed cheer
23 team for their exceptional achievement as the 2024 GHSA Class 6A Cheer State Champions.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
26 South Forsyth High School co-ed cheer team.
