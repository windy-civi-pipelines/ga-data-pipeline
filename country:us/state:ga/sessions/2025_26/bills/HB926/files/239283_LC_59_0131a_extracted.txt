Section 1:
25 LC 59 0131
By: Representative Campbell of the 35th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 1 of Title 28 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 general provisions relative to the General Assembly, so as to revise the oath of office to
3 require that members conduct themselves with civility; to provide for related matters; to
4 repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Chapter 1 of Title 28 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to general
8 provisions relative to the General Assembly, is amended by revising subsection (a) of Code
9 Section 28-1-4, relating to form and administration of oath of office to members, as follows:
10 "(a) In addition to any other oath prescribed by law, each Senator and Representative,
11 before taking the seat to which elected, shall take the following oath:
12 'I do hereby solemnly swear or affirm that I will support the Constitution of this state and
13 of the United States and, on all questions and measures which may come before me, I will
14 so conduct myself with civility, as will, in my judgment, be most conducive to the
15 interests and prosperity of this state.'"
H. B. 926
- 1 -
25 LC 59 0131
16 SECTION 2.
17 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 926
