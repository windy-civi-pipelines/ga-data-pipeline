25 LC 112 3074
House Resolution 341
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Monica Whitfield, the Cartersville-Bartow County
2 Chamber of Commerce 2024 Volunteer of the Year; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Monica Whitfield was recently named the Cartersville-Bartow County Chamber
4 of Commerce 2024 Volunteer of the Year in recognition of her exceptional dedication to the
5 chamber and her community; and
6 WHEREAS, she has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to nurturing future leaders
7 through her service as chair of the Youth Leadership Bartow committee, where her efforts
8 have inspired and guided countless young individuals; and
9 WHEREAS, her leadership as both a chamber ambassador and head of the Events
10 sub-committee has played a vital role in the success of numerous chamber events, including
11 the Annual Cookout; and
12 WHEREAS, her selfless contributions further include her participation in county-wide
13 initiatives such as the upcoming Cartersville Dancing with the Stars, which supports the
14 Good Neighbor Homeless Shelter and Backpack Buddies; and
H. R. 341
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3074
15 WHEREAS, Monica Whitfield's outstanding service, leadership, and compassion have made
16 a lasting impact on her community and beyond, making her a well-deserved recipient of this
17 prestigious award.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body congratulate Monica Whitfield upon being named the
20 Cartersville-Bartow County Chamber of Commerce 2024 Volunteer of the Year; commend
21 her for her many exemplary contributions on behalf of the citizens of Georgia; and extend
22 best wishes for her continued success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
25 Monica Whitfield.
