25 LC 112 3404
House Resolution 696
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Tenisha Lynette Dixon; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Tenisha Lynette Dixon has exemplified dedication and commitment as a public
3 servant, serving as Council Aide to the Mayor and City Council Members for five years,
4 tirelessly working to support the needs of the community and ensure the efficient operation
5 of city governance; and
6 WHEREAS, she was born on January 4, 1976, in Daytona Beach, Florida, to a loving and
7 supportive family who profoundly influenced her character; and
8 WHEREAS, her mother, Sara J. Dixon, provided unwavering love and guidance while her
9 father, Wilford Dixon, a United States Navy veteran, instilled in her the values of service,
10 discipline, and perseverance, shaping her into the person she is today; and
11 WHEREAS, she has been a proud resident of Forest Park, Georgia, for over 25 years,
12 demonstrating her deep roots and commitment to the community; and
H. R. 696
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3404
13 WHEREAS, Tenisha is a devoted mother to her two daughters, Aiy'Anna and Kennedy King,
14 and a longtime partner to their father, Jormel King, who has been a constant source of
15 reassurance, support, and encouragement; and
16 WHEREAS, she skillfully balances her demanding professional responsibilities with her
17 unwavering dedication as a loving and supportive parent; and
18 WHEREAS, she is an active member of New Vision Christian Church, where she plays a
19 vital role in fostering spiritual growth and fellowship within her congregation; and
20 WHEREAS, Tenisha is a passionate Atlanta Falcons fan who enjoys spending quality time
21 with her family, watching movies, and traveling, all of which showcase her vibrant and
22 well-rounded personality; and
23 WHEREAS, her dedication, hard work, and positive spirit have made a lasting impact on the
24 city and its residents, earning her the respect and admiration of her colleagues and
25 community members alike.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body recognize and commend Tenisha Lynette Dixon for her
28 outstanding contributions to the City of Forest Park and her unwavering commitment to
29 public service, extend their deepest gratitude for her efforts, and celebrate her as a valued
30 member of the community.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
33 Tenisha Lynette Dixon.
