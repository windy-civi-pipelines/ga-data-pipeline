House Resolution 920
By: Representative Carter of the 93rd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Stacey Garel has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the vital
3 role that she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of
4 the citizens of Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, Stacey, an Atlanta Native who now resides in Stonecrest, Georgia, is a certified
6 Event Planner and professional writer; and
7 WHEREAS, she is an Bestselling published author with the 9 times best-selling book Peeling
8 Off The Layers To Unmask New Beginnings; and
9 WHEREAS, she has worked on National conferences, trade shows, and events such as the
10 2012 Democratic National Convention for former President Barack Obama as well as the
11 2013 and 2019 Super Bowls; and
12 WHEREAS, in 2023, Stacey was nominated for a RICE Award as Founder's Rising and
13 received an Outstanding Citizen Award from the state of Georgia; and

14 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
15 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
