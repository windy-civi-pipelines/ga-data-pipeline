25 LC 129 0324
House Resolution 644
of the 75th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Monesha Clark and recognizing March 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing Women in
2 Georgia Day; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day acknowledges the philanthropic, social,
4 and cultural contributions of women in the state; and
5 WHEREAS, Monesha Clark has long been recognized for the vital role that she has played
6 in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of the citizens of Georgia;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, known as "Ms. Atl Doll," Monesha is a multi-talented event promoter, media
9 personality, model, and entrepreneur based in Atlanta, Georgia; and
10 WHEREAS, Monesha is the founder of a revolutionary lipstick and lashes company; the host
11 of DaPostUp, an acclaimed radio and television show centered on life in Atlanta; as well as
12 a writer and red carpet reporter with several prestigious publications; and
H. R. 644
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0324
13 WHEREAS, in addition to her media efforts, Monesha recently released her first guidebook
14 titled HustleHer's Playbook: Guide for Boss Babes to Earn Extra Income, aimed at
15 empowering women to achieve financial independence; and
16 WHEREAS, she thrives in the entertainment industry, using her platform to connect with
17 others, share her journey, and inspire fellow entrepreneurs; and
18 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
19 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
21 individual be appropriately honored.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body commend Monesha Clark for her efficient, effective, unselfish, and
24 dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; recognize March, 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing
25 Women in Georgia Day; and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and
26 happiness.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Monesha Clark.
