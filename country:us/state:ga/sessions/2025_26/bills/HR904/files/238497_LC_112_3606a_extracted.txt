25 LC 112 3606
House Resolution 904
89th, Dawson of the 65th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life, work, and contributions of Patrick Muhammad; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Patrick Muhammad, known as the Principal Farmer, is a pioneering leader in
3 Atlanta's homesteading and agricultural education, dedicating over a decade to transforming
4 his 31-acre Your Faith Farms into a thriving hub for sustainable living and agricultural
5 learning; and
6 WHEREAS, through his dedication and expertise, he has empowered over 30 families to
7 purchase and develop their own farms, fostering a new generation of landowners and
8 sustainable farmers; and
9 WHEREAS, as the Principal of Chattahoochee Hills Charter School, an agriculture-focused
10 institution, he inspires young students through hands-on agricultural lessons, instilling in
11 them the values of sustainability, self-sufficiency, and environmental stewardship; and
12 WHEREAS, his impact extends beyond the classroom as his educational videos on
13 sustainable farming and homesteading have gone viral, reaching millions of viewers and
14 sparking widespread enthusiasm for agricultural education; and
H. R. 904
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3606
15 WHEREAS, his contributions to the field of agriculture and education have been recognized
16 with an Emmy nomination, and his work has been featured on prominent platforms such as
17 Netflix's Chicken Soup for the Soul, FOX's Home Free, and a John Deere campaign; and
18 WHEREAS, Patrick Muhammad is also a published author, and his forthcoming third book,
19 Farming by Faith, is set to further inspire and educate individuals on the principles of
20 farming, faith, and self-reliance; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding achievements and lasting
22 contributions of Patrick Muhammad be appropriately recognized and celebrated.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body honor Patrick Muhammad for his exemplary leadership, his
25 commitment to agricultural education, and his dedication to empowering families through
26 farming and homesteading.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Patrick Muhammad.
