25 LC 112 3136
House Resolution 381
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Believe Greater Dalton, recipient of the Georgia Reads
2 Community Award, for its dedication to literacy advancement; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council on Literacy is dedicated to improving literacy from birth
4 through adulthood and recognizes that communities play a critical role in addressing low
5 literacy rates; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Reads Community Award was established to recognize
7 communities across Georgia that have formed partnerships of two or more organizations,
8 sustained for at least three years, with a strategic plan and measurable results in literacy
9 improvement; and
10 WHEREAS, Believe Greater Dalton, the strategic planning initiative of the Greater Dalton
11 Chamber of Commerce, is a recipient of the Georgia Reads Community Award for its
12 dedicated efforts in improving school readiness and ensuring all children are reading at grade
13 level by third grade; and
14 WHEREAS, over the past decade, Believe Greater Dalton has built a framework of
15 collaborative partners whose sole purpose is to achieve these literacy goals; and

- 1 -

16 WHEREAS, its meaningful partnerships include the B8 team, which meets bi-monthly and
17 includes leaders from Dalton Public Schools and Whitfield County Schools, Dalton State
18 College and Georgia Northwestern Technical College, United Way, Family Connections,
19 local childcare centers, healthcare professionals from Vitruvian Health and the Anna Shaw
20 Children's Institute, faith-based organizations, and various nonprofit organizations and
21 governmental agencies vital to serving children; and
22 WHEREAS, these initiatives have led to measurable improvements in reading proficiency,
23 ensuring greater opportunities for success in education and career development; and
24 WHEREAS, the dedication and hard work of Believe Greater Dalton and its partners serve
25 as an inspiration to others, demonstrating the power of community-driven solutions to
26 address literacy challenges.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body congratulate and commend the Georgia Reads Community Award
29 recipients, particularly Believe Greater Dalton, for their exemplary service and unwavering
30 commitment to improving literacy in their communities.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
33 Believe Greater Dalton.
