34 SECTION 1.
35 It is the intention of the General Assembly to recognize that every individual has the
36 fundamental right to reproductive freedom and to determine their own life course, that the
37 right to reproductive freedom is central to an individual's privacy, liberty, dignity, and
38 autonomy, and that every individual should be free to exercise that right free from
39 government interference.
40 SECTION 2.
41 This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "Reproductive Freedom Act."
