25 LC 112 3032
House Resolution 326
the 29th, McCollum of the 30th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the North Hall Middle School boys and girls cross country teams for winning
2 the 2024 GHSA Middle School State Cross Country Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the North Hall Middle School boys and girls cross country teams have
4 established and maintained a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphasis on character
5 and academics; and
6 WHEREAS, excellent performance, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made
7 these teams a spectacular force throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to their
8 remarkable 2024 campaign, they captured their third straight state cross country
9 championships, along with their third Grand Champion title for best overall cross country
10 school in the state; and
11 WHEREAS, the girls team delivered a strong performance, placing five of their seven
12 runners in the top 25, including three in the top 10, as well as securing a six-point victory in
13 the championship race; and
14 WHEREAS, the girls team was led by Chandler Sosebee, who finished third overall, with
15 key contributions from Challis Latta and Emmy Edwards in the top 10, along with Malorie

16 Wyse, Macy Michael Hill, and Marianna Elizarraraz rounding out the title-winning roster;
17 and
18 WHEREAS, the boys team demonstrated equal dominance, placing five runners in the top
19 15 and securing a commanding 25-point victory over the runner-up team; and
20 WHEREAS, Hunter Morrison led the boys team with a fifth-place overall finish, with
21 Thaddeus Ivy, Isaac Borg, Hudson Webb, Angelo Lugo, Clay Brown, and Ben Erickson
22 completing the championship-winning roster; and
23 WHEREAS, four boys runners—Isaac Borg, Hudson Webb, Bodi Glover, and Levi
24 Miller—and three girls runners—Chandler Sosebee, Malorie Wyse, and Macy Michael
25 Hill—achieved the remarkable feat of competing on all three consecutive state championship
26 teams from sixth through eighth grade; and
27 WHEREAS, as these magnificent teams earned their well-deserved reputation for excellence
28 by performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
29 abundantly proper to call them champions.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body congratulate the North Hall Middle School boys and girls cross
32 country teams for winning the 2024 GHSA Middle School State Cross Country
33 Championship and extend best wishes for their continued success.
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
36 North Hall Middle School boys and girls cross country teams.
