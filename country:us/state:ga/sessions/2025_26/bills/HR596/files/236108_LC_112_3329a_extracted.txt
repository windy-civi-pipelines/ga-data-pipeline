25 LC 112 3329
House Resolution 596
54th, Cannon of the 58th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Techbridge Inc. on the occasion of its 25th anniversary and recognizing March
2 17, 2025, as Techbridge Day at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, since its founding in 2000, Techbridge Inc. has been a leader in nonprofit
4 capacity building, providing cutting-edge technology solutions and strategic support to
5 empower organizations in their missions to alleviate poverty and create pathways to
6 self-sufficiency; and
7 WHEREAS, Techbridge Inc. has had a profound impact through its four core pillars: Hunger
8 Relief, supporting food banks and pantries to distribute over 6.6 billion pounds of food;
9 Social Justice, facilitating over $131 million in pro bono legal services to underserved
10 communities; Workforce Development, creating economic opportunities and career pathways
11 in the technology sector with a $9.6 million impact; and Homelessness, securing over $30
12 million in housing assistance for individuals and families in need; and
13 WHEREAS, Techbridge Inc. has fostered strategic partnerships with businesses, nonprofit
14 organizations, and government entities to expand its reach and increase its impact, helping
15 bridge the digital divide and ensuring equitable access to technology-driven solutions; and
H. R. 596
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3329
16 WHEREAS, Techbridge's commitment to innovation has positioned it as a national model
17 for leveraging technology to solve pressing social challenges, making a tangible difference
18 in the lives of countless individuals and families; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper to honor Techbridge Inc. as well as its
20 leadership, staff, and partners for their outstanding contributions over the past 25 years and
21 their continued commitment to social impact and economic empowerment.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body commend Techbridge Inc. upon the momentous occasion of its
24 25th anniversary, recognize March 17, 2025, as Techbridge Day at the state capitol, and
25 extend sincere best wishes for continued success in the years to come.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
28 Techbridge Inc.
