25 LC 129 0032
Senate Resolution 59
25th, Hatchett of the 50th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and congratulating Georgia Peace Officers Standards and Training Council's
2 Executive Director Michael T. Ayers upon being awarded Georgia's Outstanding Police
3 Chief of the Year; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, Executive Director Michael T. Ayers began his career in 1989 with the Hartwell
5 Police Department and subsequently joined the Georgia Bureau of Investigation in 1990,
6 rising to the rank of Special Agent in Charge; and
7 WHEREAS, he was appointed to the position of Executive Director of the Georgia Peace
8 Officers Standards and Training Council (POST) by Governor Brian P. Kemp on May 15,
9 2019; and
10 WHEREAS, Director Ayers graduated from the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of
11 Arts in criminal justice; and
12 WHEREAS, Director Ayers has graduated from advanced law enforcement leadership
13 programs such as the FBI National Academy, the Georgia Law Enforcement Professional
14 Management Program, and the Georgia International Law Enforcement Exchange Program;
15 and
S. R. 59
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0032
16 WHEREAS, Director Ayers is a member of and has served as President of the International
17 Association of Directors of Law Enforcement Standards and Training; and
18 WHEREAS, under the leadership of Director Ayers, POST operations were modernized, and
19 he significantly contributed to the safety and efficiency of law enforcement throughout
20 Georgia while acknowledging the recruiting and retention challenges facing law enforcement
21 executives; and
22 WHEREAS, Director Ayers retired from service to the citizens of Georgia on January 2,
23 2025; and
24 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper to recognize the impactful changes at POST,
25 which benefit all law enforcement as well as the citizens of the State of Georgia, brought
26 about by the visionary leadership of Executive Director Michael T. Ayers.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
28 recognize and congratulate Georgia Peace Officers Standards and Training Council's
29 Executive Director Michael T. Ayers upon being awarded Georgia's Outstanding Police
30 Chief of the Year.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
32 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Executive Director
33 Michael T. Ayers.
