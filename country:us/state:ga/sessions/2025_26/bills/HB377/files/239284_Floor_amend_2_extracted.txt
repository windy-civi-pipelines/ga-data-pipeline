Raw Text:
25 Senate Floor Amend 2
Senator Setzler of the 37th offered the following amendment # 2:
Amend the Senate Committee on Rules substitute (LC 47 3698S) to HB 377:
by Adding to Code Section 43-21-3.2 a new subsection (B) stating the following
“Notwithstanding any other provision of law, when an innkeeper furnishes accommodations
to a guest on a day-to-day or week-to-week basis, an innkeeper-guest relationship shall be
presumed to exist provided that such guest entered into a valid written contract designating
the relationship to be an innkeeper and guest agreement. An innkeeper’s acceptance of a
missed or late payment tendered by a guest shall not create a landlord-tenant relationship nor
waive an innkeeper’s right to pursue immediate removal of a guest pursuant to this code
section or any other provision of law.
