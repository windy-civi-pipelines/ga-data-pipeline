25 LC 112 2712
House Resolution 44
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Barry A. Brown; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Savannah native Barry A. Brown was born on November 6, 1954, to the late
3 Henry J. and Bernardine Osbourne Brown; and
4 WHEREAS, he graduated from Savannah High School and dedicated over 20 years to the
5 City of Tybee, serving on the Planning Commission and City Council as well as Mayor Pro
6 Tempore; and
7 WHEREAS, as a longtime resident of Tybee Island, Barry A. Brown was a committed
8 member of the Marine Rescue Squadron for over 25 years; and
9 WHEREAS, Barry A. Brown belonged to the American Legion Post 154, and professionally,
10 he was the proud owner and operator of Barry A. Brown Co. Inc.; and
11 WHEREAS, his family was the most important thing in his life; his greatest treasures were
12 his grandchildren; and
H. R. 44
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2712
13 WHEREAS, Barry A. Brown enjoyed hunting, fishing, and watching chef, restauranteur, and
14 television host Guy Fieri; and
15 WHEREAS, his dry sense of humor made him beloved by all, and he will long be
16 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
17 had the great fortune of knowing him; and
18 WHEREAS, he never met a stranger; Barry A. Brown loved helping others and his kindness
19 was boundless; and
20 WHEREAS, his wife of 51 years, Sunni; his children, siblings, nieces, and nephews; and his
21 "greatest treasures," grandchildren Colby, Tyler, Brayden, and Phoenix, meant the world to
22 Barry A. Brown.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body honor the life and memory of Barry A. Brown and express their
25 deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
28 family of Barry A. Brown.
H. R. 44
