25 LC 129 0086
House Resolution 334
of the 24th, Clark of the 100th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Forsyth Central High School One Act Play program for winning the 2024
2 GHSA Class 6A State Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Forsyth Central High School One Act Play program has established and
4 maintained a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics;
5 and
6 WHEREAS, as a spectacular conclusion to the 2024 theater season, this exemplary program
7 claimed the school's first state championship victory since 2005 for its production of "Red";
8 and
9 WHEREAS, this exceptional cast included Gavin Haslett, who won Best Actor, and
10 Christian Rippe, who made All-Star Cast; and
11 WHEREAS, the Forsyth Central High School One Act Play program has grown since its
12 inception in the late 1980s to become a major component of the community known for
13 producing high-quality theater performances; and
H. R. 334
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0086
14 WHEREAS, the program has continued to flourish due to the dedicated efforts of Director
15 Kevin Whitley, who strives for excellence in educational theater; and
16 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable program be appropriately
17 honored during this special time.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body congratulate the Forsyth Central High School One Act Play
20 program for winning the 2024 GHSA Class 6A State Championship as well as extend sincere
21 best wishes for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
24 Forsyth Central High School One Act Play program.
