25 LC 112 3306
House Resolution 531
97th, and Santos of the 117th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Larry Campbell and Corners Outreach; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Larry Campbell has demonstrated exceptional leadership and dedication as the
4 President and CEO of Corners Outreach, an organization committed to breaking cycles of
5 generational poverty through educational development and economic opportunities; and
6 WHEREAS, he founded Corners Outreach over 12 years ago, growing it into a
7 professionally staffed nonprofit that has built trusted relationships with over 1,500 families
8 through its two leading programs, Corners Academy and Corners Social Enterprise; and
9 WHEREAS, under his leadership, Corners Academy has provided tutoring to more than
10 3,000 students, achieving significant improvements in literacy and math, and has partnered
11 with over 20 Title I schools to support early education, teen programs, and parenting
12 workshops; and
13 WHEREAS, launched in 2018, Corners Social Enterprise has created stable employment
14 opportunities for parents and young adults through skill development programs, job

16 organization's mission; and
17 WHEREAS, prior to his work in the nonprofit sector, he founded and led CD Group, a

19 WHEREAS, his expertise spans business strategy, profit management, analytics, operations,
20 and relationship building, all of which have contributed to his success in both corporate and
