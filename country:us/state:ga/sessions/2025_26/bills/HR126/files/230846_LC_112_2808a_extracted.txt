25 LC 112 2808
House Resolution 126
of the 96th, Okoye of the 102nd, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Sirish Subash, the winner of the 3M Young Scientist
2 Challenge; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, on October 16, 2024, 3M and Discovery Education named Sirish Subash, a
4 ninth grader at the Gwinnett School of Mathematics, Science, and Technology, the winner
5 of the 2024 3M Young Scientist Challenge, the nation's premier middle school science
6 competition; and
7 WHEREAS, Sirish demonstrated ingenuity, innovative thinking, a strong application of
8 STEM principles, a passion for research, exceptional presentation skills, and the ability to
9 inspire others; and
10 WHEREAS, he spent four months competing against nine other finalists and secured his
11 victory during the final challenge events held at 3M global headquarters in St. Paul,
12 Minnesota, on October 14 and 15, 2024; and
13 WHEREAS, his winning project, Pestiscand, is a handheld device designed to detect
14 pesticide residues on produce using a non-destructive method that employs
15 spectrophotometry to measure how light of various wavelengths is reflected off the surface

16 of fruits and vegetables, with a machine learning model analyzing this data to determine the
17 presence of pesticides; and
18 WHEREAS, Pestiscand consists of a sensor, a power supply, a display screen, and a
19 processor; during testing, the device achieved an accuracy rate of greater than 85 percent in
20 identifying pesticide residues on spinach and tomatoes, meeting the project's objectives for
21 effectiveness and speed; and
22 WHEREAS, such achievements contribute to the advancement of scientific knowledge and
23 highlight the importance of supporting educational endeavors in the sciences; and
24 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia, with its rich tradition of fostering excellence and
25 innovation, takes immense pride in celebrating the achievements of its young scholars, whose
26 brilliance and dedication illuminate a bright future not only for this great state but for the
27 nation and the world.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body recognize and commend Sirish Subash for his outstanding efforts
30 and extend best wishes for his continued success in all future endeavors.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
33 public and the press.
