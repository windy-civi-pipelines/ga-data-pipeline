Section 1:
25 LC 48 1438
By: Representatives Clark of the 100th, Gaines of the 120th, Sainz of the 180th, Jasperse of the
11th, Petrea of the 166th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 5 of Chapter 11 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia, relating to
2 offenses involving illegal aliens, so as to provide for penalties for state and local officials and
3 employees for noncompliance; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and
4 for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Article 5 of Chapter 11 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia, relating to offenses
8 involving illegal aliens, is amended by revising subsection (d) of Code Section 16-11-201,
9 relating to definitions, offense of concealing, harboring, or shielding an illegal alien,
10 penalties, and exceptions, as follows:
11 "(d) It shall be unlawful for any state or local official or employee to knowingly and
12 willfully violate any state law related to deterring the concealing, harboring, or shielding
13 of an illegal alien guilty of a criminal offense in violation of this Code section. In addition
14 to the penalties provided in subsection (c) of this Code section, any state or local official
15 or employee found to have violated this Code section shall be subject to prosecution by the
16 Attorney General, and may be subject to removal and replacement by the Governor. This
H. B. 319
- 1 -
25 LC 48 1438
17 Code section shall not apply to a government employee or any person acting at the express
18 direction of a government employee who conceals, harbors, or shelters an illegal alien
19 when such illegal alien is or has been the victim of a criminal offense or is a witness in any
20 civil or criminal proceeding or who holds an illegal alien in a jail, prison, or other detention
21 facility."
22 SECTION 2.
23 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
