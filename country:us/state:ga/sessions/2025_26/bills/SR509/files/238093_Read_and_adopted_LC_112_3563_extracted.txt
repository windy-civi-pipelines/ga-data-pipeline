Section 1:
25 LC 112 3563
Senate Resolution 509
44th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating Dabney Bond; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the Midtown High School girls swim relay team has established and maintained
3 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
4 WHEREAS, the Midtown High School girls swim relay team brought special recognition to
5 its school and to Fulton County by winning first place in the 400 Free Relay, breaking the
6 school record by an impressive seven seconds with a time of 3:31.75; and
7 WHEREAS, this record-breaking relay team included the exceptional talents of Rhea
8 El-Deiry, Dabney Bond, Katya Tumanova, and Sarah Paisley Owen; and
9 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
10 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
11 team; and
12 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
13 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
14 abundantly proper to call them champions.
S. R. 509
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3563
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
16 congratulate Dabney Bond for claiming first place in the 400 Free Relay and extend best
17 wishes for continued success.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
19 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dabney Bond.
S. R. 509
