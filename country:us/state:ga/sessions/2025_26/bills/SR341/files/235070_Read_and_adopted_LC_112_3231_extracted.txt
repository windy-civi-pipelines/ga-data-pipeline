25 LC 112 3231
Senate Resolution 341
Wicks of the 34th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Atlanta Apartment Association upon its 50th anniversary;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Atlanta Apartment Association has proudly served the State of Georgia
4 since 1975, representing housing providers and professionals in the multifamily housing
5 industry; and
6 WHEREAS, the Association has played a crucial role in ensuring that quality housing
7 remains accessible to all Georgians, providing a vital resource for individuals and families
8 across the state; and
9 WHEREAS, the Association has continually supported efforts to improve the housing
10 industry, from promoting best practices and professional development to advocating for
11 policies that strengthen their communities and the state's economy, driving job creation, and
12 enhancing the quality of life for millions of Georgians; and
13 WHEREAS, the Association has made a lasting impact in fostering safe, affordable, and
14 high-quality housing for the residents of Georgia, and it continues to lead efforts in the
15 housing industry with innovation and integrity; and
S. R. 341
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3231
16 WHEREAS, the members of the Association have contributed more than 140 million meals
17 to the Atlanta Community Food Bank through its decades-long philanthropic partnership,
18 representing one of the nation's largest annual food drives of its kind; and
19 WHEREAS, the 50th anniversary of the Atlanta Apartment Association provides an
20 opportunity to celebrate the Association's success, acknowledge the invaluable contributions
21 of its members, and recognize their commitment to building and supporting vibrant
22 communities throughout the State of Georgia.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
24 recognize and commend the Atlanta Apartment Association upon the grand occasion of its
25 50th anniversary for its many exemplary contributions on behalf of the citizens of this state
26 as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
28 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Atlanta
29 Apartment Association.
