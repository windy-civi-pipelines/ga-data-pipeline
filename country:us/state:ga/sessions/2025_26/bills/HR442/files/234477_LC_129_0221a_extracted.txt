25 LC 129 0221
House Resolution 442
By: Representative Lupton of the 83rd
A RESOLUTION
2 T. Randall Servant Leadership Award; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Natasha Byrd, CEO and Founder of Dream Teens Community Foundation Inc.,
4 has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to serving and empowering her community
5 through mentorship, youth development, and philanthropic efforts; and
6 WHEREAS, as an Atlanta native and the eldest of 12 siblings, Natasha embraced leadership
7 and care giving from an early age, dedicating her life to uplifting others, particularly young
8 athletes, underserved individuals, and families in need; and
9 WHEREAS, through her extensive experience in recruiting, human resources, and
10 community relations, Natasha has provided invaluable support to job seekers, fostering
11 second-chance employment opportunities and guiding individuals toward success; and
12 WHEREAS, Natasha's work at UPS as a District Community Relations representative for the
13 UPS Foundation allowed her to collaborate with nonprofits across Georgia, North Carolina,
14 and South Carolina, strengthening community programs and initiatives; and
H. R. 442
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0221
15 WHEREAS, recognizing the need for youth empowerment in education, counseling, health
16 and wellness, life skills, and community exposure, Natasha founded the Dream Teens
17 Community Foundation Inc. to equip young people with the knowledge and resources to
18 build a brighter future; and
19 WHEREAS, her dedication to service embodies the spirit of collaboration and the philosophy
20 that "It Takes a Village," ensuring that the next generation is nurtured, supported, and
21 prepared for success; and
22 WHEREAS, in recognition of her outstanding contributions to the community, her leadership
23 in youth development, and her lifelong mission to uplift others, we are honored to present
24 Natasha Byrd with the Yellow Rose Award for her exemplary service and profound impact.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body congratulate and commend Natasha Byrd for receiving the 2025
27 Yellow Rose Nikki T. Randall Servant Leadership Award, celebrate her selfless dedication
28 and work as an inspiration to all who strive to make a difference in the lives of others, and
29 extend best wishes for her continued success.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
32 Natasha Byrd.
