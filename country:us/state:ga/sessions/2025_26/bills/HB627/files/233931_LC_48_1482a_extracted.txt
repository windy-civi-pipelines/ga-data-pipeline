Section 1:
25 LC 48 1482
By: Representatives Persinger of the 119th, Cannon of the 172nd, Hitchens of the 161st, Smith
of the 18th, Lumsden of the 12th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Title 15 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to courts, so as to
2 revise a provision relating to the use of a deadly weapon; to provide for additional offenses
3 where the superior court has exclusive jurisdiction in juvenile cases; to provide for related
4 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Title 15 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to courts, is amended by revising
8 division (12)(A)(iii) of Code Section 15-11-2, relating to definitions regarding the juvenile
9 code, as follows:
10 "(iii) Aggravated assault upon an individual or situation described in subsection (d),
11 (h), or (k) of Code Section 16-5-21 or assault with a deadly weapon or with any
12 object, device, or instrument which, when used offensively against a person, actually
13 does result in serious bodily injury, provided that such deadly weapon is not a
14 firearm; and provided, further; provided, however, that such injured person is not a
15 public safety officer as defined in Code Section 16-5-19 and such acts are not
16 prohibited under subsection (c) of Code Section 16-5-21;"
H. B. 627
- 1 -
25 LC 48 1482
17 SECTION 2.
18 Said title is further amended by revising paragraphs (9) and (10) of and by adding new
19 paragraphs to subsection (b) of Code Section 15-11-560, relating to concurrent and original
20 jurisdiction of superior court, to read as follows:
21 "(9) Aggravated assault if committed with a firearm; upon a public safety officer as such
22 acts are prohibited under subsection (c) of Code Section 16-5-21; or
23 (10) Aggravated battery upon a public safety officer as such acts are prohibited under
24 subsection (c) of Code Section 16-5-24;
25 (11) Terroristic threats or acts if the threat or act involves or is against a public or private
26 school, transportation furnished by a school, a school function, or a person who, at the
27 time of the threat or act, is physically present in a public or private school, transportation
28 furnished by a school, or a school function; or
29 (12) Criminal attempt to commit the offenses provided in paragraph (1) and paragraphs
30 (4) through (8) of this subsection."
31 SECTION 3.
32 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
