25 LC 112 3134
House Resolution 387
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Dooly County Schools, recipient of the Georgia Reads
2 Community Award, for its dedication to literacy advancement; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council on Literacy is dedicated to improving literacy from birth
4 through adulthood and recognizes that communities play a critical role in addressing low
5 literacy rates; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Reads Community Award was established to recognize
7 communities across Georgia that have formed partnerships of two or more organizations,
8 sustained for at least three years, with a strategic plan and measurable results in literacy
9 improvement; and
10 WHEREAS, Dooly County Schools was selected as a recipient of the Georgia Reads
11 Community Award for its efforts in leveraging the L4GA grant to support literacy initiatives
12 aimed at improving student reading outcomes; and
13 WHEREAS, collaboration with Family Connections has enhanced literacy efforts by
14 organizing reading events, establishing Little Libraries, and promoting reading through
15 incentives that foster a love for reading; and
H. R. 387
- 1 -

16 WHEREAS, Albany Primary Health has contributed by providing free vision and hearing
17 screenings, ensuring students have the necessary resources to succeed in literacy
18 development; and
19 WHEREAS, Dooly County Schools and its partners have diligently developed and
20 implemented innovative programs that have increased literacy rates within their
21 communities; and
22 WHEREAS, these initiatives have led to measurable improvements in reading proficiency,
23 ensuring greater opportunities for success in education and career development; and
24 WHEREAS, the dedication and hard work of Dooly County Schools and its partners serve
25 as an inspiration to others, demonstrating the power of community-driven solutions to
26 address literacy challenges.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body congratulate and commend the Georgia Reads Community Award
29 recipients, particularly Dooly County Schools, for their exemplary service and unwavering
30 commitment to improving literacy in their communities.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dooly
33 County Schools.
