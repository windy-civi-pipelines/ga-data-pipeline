25 LC 112 3417
House Resolution 700
of the 81st, New of the 40th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Happy Hitters of North Atlanta; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the Happy Hitters of North Atlanta pickleball team was co-founded by Karla
3 Blaginin and Arthur Jackson to provide athletes with special needs the opportunity to
4 develop skills, build friendships, and compete in pickleball; and
5 WHEREAS, the team's roster was created with 12 athletes from Peachtree Corners, Roswell,
6 Alpharetta, Dunwoody, and the surrounding area with the support of six community
7 volunteers, five parent volunteers, and generous donors who helped cover game fees, team
8 shirts, and refreshments; and
9 WHEREAS, ACE Pickleball has donated space to the team for two seasons in a row for
10 practice, events, and meetings, and the Ace Pickleball Club of Roswell has lent its indoor
11 courts and space; and
12 WHEREAS, the team includes dedicated players Cici Rose, Colin Cooperider, Dana Lu,
13 Elijah Nunn, Gracie King, Jack Chaplin, Jake Jeffries, Kirtana Bulusu, Luke Harriman, Max
14 Bartholomai, Mimi Ngo, and Myles Henderson, all of whom have exhibited outstanding
15 sportsmanship; and
H. R. 700
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3417
16 WHEREAS, under the leadership of Coach Karla Blaginin, athlete Mimi Ngo has advanced
17 to mainstream pickleball tournaments, expanding opportunities for special needs athletes
18 within the sport; and
19 WHEREAS, the Happy Hitters of North Atlanta continue to be a shining example of
20 inclusivity, perseverance, and the positive impact of adaptive sports programs, fostering
21 personal growth and community support for athletes with special needs.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body commend the Happy Hitters of North Atlanta for their excellence
24 in pickleball and extend best wishes for their continued success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 Happy Hitters of North Atlanta.
H. R. 700
