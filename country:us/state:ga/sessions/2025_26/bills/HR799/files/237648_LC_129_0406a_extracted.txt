25 LC 129 0406
House Resolution 799
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Terry Walker Moore; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, March is celebrated as Women's History Month, a time to honor and recognize
3 the invaluable contributions of women throughout history and across all fields and trades;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, Terry Walker Moore's contributions to the community reflect the core values
6 of Women's History Month; and
7 WHEREAS, a woman of faith and a devoted wife, mother, godmother, grandmother, and
8 mentor to many, Terry is dedicated to inspiring others to finding their purpose and living
9 happy prosperous, holy, and righteous lives; and
10 WHEREAS, she is widely recognized for her leadership and unwavering commitment to the
11 Boys and Girls Clubs community for 24 years; and
12 WHEREAS, she is a recipient of numerous honors, including the C.A.R.E. Award from the
13 Boys and Girls Club of Metro Atlanta, the Maytag Dependable Leader Award from Boys and
H. R. 799

14 Girls Clubs of America, and the 2020 Paul Harris Fellow recognition from the Rotary Club
15 of Rockdale; and
16 WHEREAS, her leadership reaches the broader community, where she is a dedicated
17 advocate for youth development and people who are in need of opportunity; and
18 WHEREAS, a hardworking and passionate leader, Terry remains committed to guiding
19 future generations toward their full potential while sharing the good news of the Gospel; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
21 remarkable woman are recognized during Women's History Month.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Terry Walker Moore for her unwavering
24 dedication to service and extend sincere best wishes for her continued health, happiness, and
25 success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Terry
28 Walker Moore.
