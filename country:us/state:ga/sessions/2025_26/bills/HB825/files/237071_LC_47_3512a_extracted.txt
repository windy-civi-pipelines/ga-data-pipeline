Section 1:
25 LC 47 3512
By: Representative Washburn of the 144th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to reconstitute the Board of Education of Monroe County, Georgia,
2 approved February 25, 1993 (Ga. L. 1993, p. 3857), as amended, so as to change the
3 compensation of the members and the chairperson of such board; to provide for an effective
4 date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 An Act to reconstitute the Board of Education of Monroe County, Georgia, approved
8 February 25, 1993 (Ga. L. 1993, p. 3857), as amended, is amended by revising Section 6 as
9 follows:
10 "SECTION 6.
11 Members and the chairperson of the Board of Education of Monroe County, Georgia, shall
12 be compensated in the amount of $600.00 per month. Members and the chairperson of the
13 board shall also be reimbursed for actual expenses necessarily incurred."
H. B. 825
- 1 -
25 LC 47 3512
14 SECTION 2.
15 This Act shall become effective on the first day of the month following the month in which
16 it is approved by the Governor or in which it becomes law without such approval.
17 SECTION 3.
18 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 825
