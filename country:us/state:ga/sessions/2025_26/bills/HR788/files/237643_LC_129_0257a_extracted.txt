25 LC 129 0257
House Resolution 788
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Elan Ason McClain, Armuchee High School's 2025 STAR Student; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a gifted student at Armuchee High School, Elan has been recognized for his
4 superlative achievement and selected as Armuchee High School's 2025 STAR Student; and
5 WHEREAS, to be selected as a STAR Student, a student must have obtained the highest
6 score in one administration of the three-part Scholastic Aptitude Test and be in the top 10
7 percent or top ten students of his or her class; and
8 WHEREAS, each school has one STAR Student who in turn has the opportunity to choose
9 a STAR Teacher who has been inspiring and encouraging throughout that student's high
10 school career; and
11 WHEREAS, educator Emily Mowery was chosen by Elan as Armuchee High School's 2025
12 STAR Teacher for the great support and guidance provided to him throughout the years; and
13 WHEREAS, Elan has personified the selfless dedication, mental agility, verbal adroitness,
14 and personal maturity necessary for superlative academic performance; and
H. R. 788
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0257
15 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
16 important objective of the General Assembly, and Elan has shown by academic
17 accomplishment that the pursuit and attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of
18 the students in this state; and
19 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia looks with great anticipation toward the future of this
20 bright and promising young citizen.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body heartily commend Elan Ason McClain for being named Armuchee
23 High School's 2025 STAR Student and express their most sincere best wishes for continued
24 academic success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Elan
27 Ason McClain.
