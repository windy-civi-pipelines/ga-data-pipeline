25 LC 129 0202
House Resolution 543
62nd, and Newton of the 127th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dr. Teresa T. Byrd; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Teresa T. Byrd, MD, FACOG, MSCI, has recently joined Wellstar
3 Kennestone as director of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Residency Program, where she
4 works to provide innovations in resident and medical student education as well as conducts
5 ongoing research on pre-invasive diseases of the cervix; and
6 WHEREAS, Dr. Byrd has published significant studies on the use of Raman spectroscopy
7 in detecting cervical dysplasia and the potential of active hexose correlated compounds
8 (AHCC) to block the expression of human papillomavirus (HPV) in patients with abnormal
9 Pap tests; and
10 WHEREAS, her clinical expertise has focused on minimally invasive gynecology and office
11 gynecology, and her outstanding contributions have been recognized through numerous
12 institutional and national teaching awards; and
13 WHEREAS, Dr. Byrd completed her residency training in obstetrics and gynecology at the
14 University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston and later returned to Meharry Medical

15 College as a faculty member, where she earned a master's degree in clinical investigation;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, Dr. Byrd has made a lasting impact on the field of obstetrics and gynecology

19 receives high-quality education and training; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
