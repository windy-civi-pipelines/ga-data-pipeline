Section 1:
25 LC 55 0547
By: Senators Summers of the 13th, Strickland of the 42nd, Beach of the 21st, Jones II of the
22nd, Jackson of the 41st and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 4 of Title 3 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to distilled
2 spirits, so as to authorize the sale of consumable hemp products by retail dealers in distilled
3 spirits; to provide for a definition; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws;
4 and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Chapter 4 of Title 3 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to distilled spirits,
8 is amended by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
9 "3-4-4.
10 (a) As used in this Code section, the term 'consumable hemp product' and 'retail
11 consumable hemp establishment license' shall have the same meanings as set forth in Code
12 Section 2-23-3.
13 (b) Nothing in this chapter nor any rules and regulations promulgated by the commissioner
14 pursuant to this chapter shall prohibit a licensed retail dealer in distilled spirits from
15 obtaining a retail consumable hemp establishment license and, upon obtaining such license,
16 selling or offering for sale consumable hemp products to consumers in accordance with the
S. B. 294
- 1 -
25 LC 55 0547
17 provisions of Chapter 23 of Title 2, the 'Georgia Hemp Farming Act,' and the rules and
18 regulations promulgated by the Department of Agriculture pursuant to said chapter. The
19 sale or offering for sale of consumable hemp products to consumers by a licensed retail
20 dealer in distilled spirits shall be governed and regulated exclusively by the provisions of
21 said chapter and the rules and regulations promulgated by the Department of Agriculture
22 pursuant to said chapter."
23 SECTION 3.
24 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 294
