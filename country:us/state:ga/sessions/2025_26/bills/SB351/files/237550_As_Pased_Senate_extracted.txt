Section 1:
25 LC 61 0216
By: Senator Walker III of the 20th
AS PASSED SENATE
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to establish the State Court of Treutlen (formerly known as the City Court
2 of Soperton), in and for the County of Treutlen, approved August 18, 1919 (Ga. L. 1919, p.
3 519), as amended, so as to change the salary of the judge and solicitor-general; to provide for
4 related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 An Act to establish the State Court of Treutlen (formerly known as the City Court of
8 Soperton), in and for the County of Treutlen, approved August 18, 1919 (Ga. L. 1919, p.
9 519), as amended, is amended by adding a new section to read as follows:
10 "SECTION 3.1
11 Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Act, beginning on July 1, 2025, the judge
12 shall receive an annual salary of not less than $55,000.08. Beginning on July 1, 2025, the
13 solicitor-general shall receive an annual salary of not less than $49,000.08. Payments shall
S. B. 351
- 1 -
25 LC 61 0216
14 be made in equal monthly installments from the funds of Treutlen County. The judge and
15 the solicitor-general may succeed themselves in office."
16 SECTION 2.
17 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
