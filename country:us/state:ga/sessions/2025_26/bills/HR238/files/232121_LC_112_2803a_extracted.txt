25 LC 112 2803
House Resolution 238
McQueen of the 61st, Carson of the 46th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Melony Tidmore, Miss Cobb County's Teen 2025; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a high school sophomore at Liberty University Online Academy, Melony
4 Tidmore was crowned last year as Miss Cobb County's Teen 2025 and was additionally
5 honored as the competition's Talent Winner for her ballet en pointe performance of the
6 "Esmerelda Variation"; and
7 WHEREAS, she will go on to participate in the Miss Georgia's Teen competition in June
8 2025, the winner of which will represent the State of Georgia and compete for the title of
9 Miss America's Teen; and
10 WHEREAS, she has distinguished herself as an exemplary role model, speaker, advocate,
11 and volunteer, particularly through her community service initiative "Dance: A Movement
12 Towards Better Health," which promotes the mental and physical health benefits of dance
13 for all ages with a special emphasis on senior citizens; and
14 WHEREAS, she has expanded the reach of her initiative through her social media campaign
15 "Melody's Movement Monday" as well as through collaborations with the Wellstar Health

16 System, the Salvation Army's Silver Sneakers program, and the Atlanta Ballet Children's
17 Outreach Program; and
18 WHEREAS, a dedicated and accomplished dancer, she has completed over 13 years of dance
19 training, including classical ballet, and she is currently a pre-professional ballerina at the
20 Tolbert Yilmaz School of Dance; and
21 WHEREAS, through her service as a member of the 2025 Cobb County Youth Commission,
22 Melony works closely with local government, develops leadership skills, and participates in
23 service projects throughout Cobb County; and
24 WHEREAS, she has demonstrated outstanding academic achievement by maintaining a 4.12
25 GPA, serving as president of the National Beta Club, being selected for the Duke University
26 Summer Program, and earning recognition as one of 52 National Beta Club All-Star
27 Community Service winners; and
28 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
29 remarkable and distinguished young Georgian be appropriately recognized.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body congratulate Melony Tidmore upon her win as Miss Cobb County's
32 Teen 2025, commend her for her outstanding work on behalf of her community, and extend
33 best wishes for future success in competition.
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
36 Melony Tidmore.
