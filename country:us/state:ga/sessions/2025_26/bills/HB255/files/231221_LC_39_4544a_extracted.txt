Section 1:
25 LC 39 4544
By: Representatives McClain of the 109th, Prince of the 132nd, Alexander of the 66th, Taylor
of the 92nd, and Jackson of the 128th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 40-2-86 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 special license plates promoting or supporting certain worthy agencies, funds, or nonprofit
3 corporations with proceeds disbursed to the general fund and the agency, fund, or nonprofit
4 corporation, so as to establish a specialty license plate honoring Southern University Alumni;
5 to provide for related matters; to provide for an effective date; to provide for compliance with
6 constitutional requirements; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 Code Section 40-2-86 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to special license
10 plates promoting or supporting certain worthy agencies, funds, or nonprofit corporations with
11 proceeds disbursed to the general fund and the agency, fund, or nonprofit corporation, is
12 amended by adding a new paragraph to subsection (l) to read as follows:
13 "(71) A special license plate honoring Southern University Alumni. The funds raised by
14 the sale of this special license plate shall be disbursed to the Atlanta Metropolitan Chapter
15 of Southern University Alumni Federation of Georgia, Inc."
H. B. 255
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4544
16 SECTION 2.
17 In accordance with the requirements of Article III, Section IX, Paragraph VI(n) of the
18 Constitution of the State of Georgia, this Act shall not become law unless it receives the
19 requisite two-thirds' majority vote in both the Senate and the House of Representatives.
20 SECTION 3.
21 This Act shall become effective on January 1, 2026.
22 SECTION 4.
23 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
