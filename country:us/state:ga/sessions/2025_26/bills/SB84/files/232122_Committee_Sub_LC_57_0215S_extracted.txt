Section 1:
25 LC 57 0215S
substitute to SB 84:
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 40-2-86 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 special license plates promoting or supporting certain worthy agencies, funds, or nonprofit
3 corporations with proceeds disbursed to the general fund and the agency, fund, or nonprofit
4 corporation, so as to establish a specialty license plate honoring the Shepherd Center; to
5 establish a specialty license plate benefiting the Georgia Transplant Foundation; to provide
6 for related matters; to provide for effective dates; to provide for compliance with
7 constitutional requirements; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
9 SECTION 1.
10 Code Section 40-2-86 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to special license
11 plates promoting or supporting certain worthy agencies, funds, or nonprofit corporations with
12 proceeds disbursed to the general fund and the agency, fund, or nonprofit corporation, is
13 amended in subsection (l) by adding a new paragraph to read as follows:
14 "(71) A special license plate honoring the Shepherd Center. The funds raised by the sale
15 of this special license plate shall be disbursed to Shepherd Center, Inc."
- 1 -
25 LC 57 0215S
16 SECTION 2.
17 Said Code section is further amended in subsection (l) by adding a new paragraph to read as
18 follows:
19 "(72) A special license plate recognizing the importance of organ donation. The funds
20 raised by the sale of this special license plate shall be disbursed to the Georgia Transplant
21 Foundation."
22 SECTION 3.
23 (a) Except as otherwise provided for in subsection (b) of this section, this Act shall become
24 effective on July 1, 2025.
25 (b) Section 2 of this Act shall become effective on January 1, 2026.
26 SECTION 4.
27 In accordance with the requirements of Article III, Section IX, Paragraph VI(n) of the
28 Constitution of the State of Georgia, this Act shall not become law unless it receives the
29 requisite two-thirds' majority vote in both the Senate and the House of Representatives.
30 SECTION 5.
31 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
