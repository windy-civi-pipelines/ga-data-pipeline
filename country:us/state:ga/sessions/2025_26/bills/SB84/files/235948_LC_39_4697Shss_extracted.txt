Section 1:
25 LC 39 4697S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 40-2-86.1 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 license plates promoting or supporting certain agencies, funds, or nonprofit corporations or
3 issued to qualified motor vehicles or owners with proceeds deposited in the general fund, so
4 as to establish a specialty license plate recognizing the importance of organ donation; to
5 provide for related matters; to provide for an effective date; to provide for compliance with
6 constitutional requirements; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 Code Section 40-2-86.1 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to license plates
10 promoting or supporting certain agencies, funds, or nonprofit corporations or issued to
11 qualified motor vehicles or owners with proceeds deposited in the general fund, is amended
12 in subsection (l) by adding a new paragraph to read as follows:
13 "(12) A special license plate recognizing the importance of organ donation. The design
14 of the special plate shall include the phrase 'Organ Donors Save Lives.' The funds raised
15 by the sale of this license plate shall be deposited in the general fund."
S. B. 84 (SUB)
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4697S
16 SECTION 2.
17 This Act shall become effective on January 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 3.
19 In accordance with the requirements of Article III, Section IX, Paragraph VI(n) of the
20 Constitution of the State of Georgia, this Act shall not become law unless it receives the
21 requisite two-thirds' majority vote in both the Senate and the House of Representatives.
22 SECTION 4.
23 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 84 (SUB)
