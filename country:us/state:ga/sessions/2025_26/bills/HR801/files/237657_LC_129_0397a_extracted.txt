House Resolution 801
the 79th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dr. Ebonee Gresham; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Ebonee Gresham has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for
3 the vital role that she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the
4 welfare of the citizens of Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, graciously known as Dr. Ebonee Empowers, Dr. Ebonee is a Doctor of Nursing
6 Practice; and
7 WHEREAS, she is the CEO and founder of Dr. Ebonee Empowers, LLC, and Promise
8 Health Medical Institute, a post-secondary medical school in Decatur, GA, as well as
9 Co-owner of HealthCare Academy; and
10 WHEREAS, she is the author of the Amazon best seller, The Grit, The Grind and The Glory,
11 and co-author of Gracefully Broken-Heavenly Restored, she is also a Global Empowerment
12 Keynote Speaker and mentor to many; and
13 WHEREAS, this distinguished gentlewoman has given inspiration to many through her high
14 ideals, morals, and deep concern for her fellow citizens, and she possesses the vast wisdom

15 which only comes through experience and the strength of character which is achieved
16 through overcoming the many challenges of life; and
17 WHEREAS,Her passionate response and advocacy for issues such as domestic violence,
18 single motherhood, mental health of black women, and women empowerment has been
19 featured on several media outlets and print publications such as SHEEN magazine, IMPACT
20 magazine, CDC, NurseDeck, VoyageATL, the Atlanta Journal Constitution, NBC Nightly
21 News, and the SisAundra television show; and
22 WHEREAS, in 2023, Dr. Ebonee was awarded the Presidential Lifetime Achievement

27 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
28 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
29 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body recognize and commend the extraordinary achievements of Dr.
32 Ebonee Gresham and extend sincere best wishes for her continued health and happiness.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
34 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
35 Ebonee Gresham.
