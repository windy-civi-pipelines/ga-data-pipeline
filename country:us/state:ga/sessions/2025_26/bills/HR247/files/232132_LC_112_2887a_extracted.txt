25 LC 112 2887
House Resolution 247
of the 24th, Clark of the 100th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the East Forsyth High School eSports team for winning the 2024-2025 3A-4A
2 Super Smash Bros. Ultimate State Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the East Forsyth High School eSports team has established and maintained a
4 proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
6 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
7 2024 campaign, the team captured the school's first ever state championship; and
8 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school by winning the 2024-2025
9 3A-4A Super Smash Bros. Ultimate State Championship against Northview High School
10 with a final score of 3-2; and
11 WHEREAS, this all-star team included players Ethan Wakefield, Victor Wissmann, Jackson
12 Wickey, and Bobby Brown as well as head coach Justin Hodges; and
13 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
14 dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the team; and
H. R. 247
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2887
15 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
16 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
17 abundantly proper to call them champions.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body congratulate the East Forsyth High School eSports team for
20 winning the 2024-2025 3A-4A Super Smash Bros. Ultimate State Championship and extend
21 best wishes for their continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
24 East Forsyth High School eSports team.
H. R. 247
