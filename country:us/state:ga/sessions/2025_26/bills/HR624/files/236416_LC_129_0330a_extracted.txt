25 LC 129 0330
House Resolution 624
142nd, and Reese of the 140th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Reverend John H. Herring; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Reverend John H. Herring has demonstrated his commitment to teaching the
3 Gospel, witnessing Christ through word and deed, and addressing the physical,
4 psychological, intellectual, and spiritual needs of others; and
5 WHEREAS, On April 25, 2010, Reverend Herring was licensed to preach the gospel of Jesus
6 Christ and in March 2011, was ordained as a Preacher of the Gospel; and
7 WHEREAS, in 2012, upon the retirement of his father, Pastor Wallace B. Herring, Sr.,
8 Reverend John Herring became the 4th and youngest pastor of Glorious Hope Baptist Church
9 in Macon, Georgia; and
10 WHEREAS, his vision and direction have helped Glorious Hope Baptist Church grow in size
11 and influence, uplifting the community like the light of Bethlehem and serving as a spiritual
12 leader and place for fellowship; and
13 WHEREAS, with the heart for children and youth, Reverend Herring has been instrumental
14 in the success of the widely popular Men About Change program, an after-school and
H. R. 624
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0330
15 summer program for boys and girls that focuses on educational and entrepreneurial
16 initiatives, character development, and community service; and
17 WHEREAS, whether the task is preparing a sermon, visiting the sick, counseling the
18 troubled, baptizing a new believer, or acting as a theologian, educator, administrator, or
19 humanitarian, Reverend Herring has shown the energy, devotion, and commitment truly
20 reflective of divine guidance; and
21 WHEREAS, the unmatched spiritual assistance offered by Reverend Herring is a source of
22 strength and direction for persons in all walks of life; and
23 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper to recognize the outstanding accomplishments
24 of the dynamic Christian ministry of this devout and pious man.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body recognize and commend Reverend John H. Herring and extend best
27 wishes for many more years of successful fellowship and leadership.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Reverend John H. Herring.
