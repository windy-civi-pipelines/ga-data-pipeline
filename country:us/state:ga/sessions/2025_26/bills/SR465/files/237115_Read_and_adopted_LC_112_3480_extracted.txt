25 LC 112 3480
Senate Resolution 465
Harrell of the 40th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending and congratulating Calvin George Kemp Sr.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Calvin George Kemp Sr. was born on June 2, 1923, in Atlanta, Georgia, and
3 this year he enjoys the rare distinction of celebrating his 102nd birthday; and
4 WHEREAS, the devotion, patience, and understanding he has demonstrated have provided
5 the foundation of success from which his family members have developed and flourished,
6 as evidenced by his two wonderful children, four outstanding grandchildren, five exceptional
7 great-grandchildren, and three cherished great-great-grandchildren; and
8 WHEREAS, he honorably served as a guardian of this nation's freedom and liberty with the
9 United States Navy during World War II, during which he was stationed in the South Pacific
10 Islands and Pearl Harbor; and
11 WHEREAS, a man of great faith, Calvin George Kemp Sr. has remained an active and
12 devoted member of Saint Philip African Methodist Episcopal Church, where he serves on the
13 Usher and Steward Boards, Finance Committee, and as director of the Seniors and Retirees
14 Program; and
S. R. 465
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, he has dedicated much of his life to mentoring and guiding younger generations
16 through his service as a scoutmaster with the Boy Scouts of America and as a little league
17 baseball coach, helping to instill the values of leadership, teamwork, and perseverance in the
18 youth of his community; and
19 WHEREAS, he has further shared his knowledge and skills as a brick mason instructor at
20 Atlanta Medical College, helping to train and empower individuals in skilled trades that
21 provide lasting career opportunities; and
22 WHEREAS, he has witnessed a dramatic change in the world over the course of his lifetime,
23 and we salute his contribution to its progress through his tireless efforts on behalf of his
24 community, state, and nation; and
25 WHEREAS, by the example Calvin George Kemp Sr. has made of his life, he makes this
26 world a better place in which to live, and it is only fitting and proper that he be appropriately
27 recognized during this very special and memorable time.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
29 commend Calvin George Kemp Sr. for his unselfish, dedicated, and loving service to the
30 members of his family and community; congratulate him on the remarkable occasion of his
31 102nd birthday; and extend their most sincere best wishes for continued health and
32 happiness.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
34 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Calvin George
35 Kemp Sr.
