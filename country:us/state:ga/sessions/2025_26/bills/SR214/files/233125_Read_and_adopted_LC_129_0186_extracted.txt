25 LC 129 0186
Senate Resolution 214
42nd, Walker III of the 20th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Phillip Hampton Sims; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Phillip Hampton Sims on February 7, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Phillip Hampton Sims was born in 1956 in Forest Park, Georgia, to the late
5 H.B. Sims and Theresa Staples Sims; and
6 WHEREAS, he and his family moved to Tyrone, Georgia, in 1966 and put down roots on a
7 gorgeous farm; and
8 WHEREAS, after college, he opened a family business, S&S Glass, in Tyrone and served
9 the community at the College Park Fire Department, where he was a fireman for 26 years,
10 retiring in 2007; and
11 WHEREAS, he will be remembered for his big fish personality, providing an endless source
12 of stories; for his desire to not only embrace the outdoors but to instill that passion in others;
13 and for his selfless, unconditional love of family and friends, for whom he was always
14 willing to provide anything he could.
S. R. 214
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0186
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
16 join in honoring the life and memory of Phillip Hampton Sims and express their deepest and
17 most sincere regret at his passing.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
19 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the family of
20 Phillip Hampton Sims.
S. R. 214
