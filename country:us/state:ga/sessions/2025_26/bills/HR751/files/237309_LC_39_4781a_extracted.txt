3 WHEREAS, Mr. William A. Fickling, Jr., has long been recognized for his contributions to

5 WHEREAS, Mr. Fickling graduated from Auburn University, where he was a three-year

14 WHEREAS, he has been recognized with numerous honors and awards, including the 1985
15 Walter Gilbert Award from Auburn University, the Distinguished Alumnus Award of the
16 Auburn School of Business, Award for Management Excellence and Achievement from the
17 University of Georgia, Business Executive of the Year by the Georgia Security Dealers
18 Association, and a Gold Award from The Wall Street Transcript; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
20 Georgian be recognized appropriately by dedicating a bridge in his honor.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
22 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the bridge on State Route 83 over the Towaliga River in
23 Monroe County is dedicated as the William A. Fickling, Jr. Bridge.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
25 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the William A. Fickling, Jr.
26 Bridge.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Mr.
29 William A. Fickling, Jr., and to the Department of Transportation.
