25 LC 112 3572
House Resolution 872
of the 24th, Clark of the 100th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Jackson Moffit on winning the 2025 GHSA Class 4A State
2 Wrestling Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Jackson Moffit of East Forsyth High School recently earned the top title in the
4 state with his win in the 175-pound weight class at the 2025 GHSA Class 4A State Wrestling
5 Championship; and
6 WHEREAS, Jackson Moffit's remarkable season, wherein he earned his second regional
7 championship title and second consecutive state championship title, marks a fitting end to
8 an exceptional high school wrestling career; and
9 WHEREAS, Jackson Moffit boasts a record of 36 wins and only 2 losses during the 2025
10 season, with 25 of those victories coming by pin; and
11 WHEREAS, this stellar young man epitomizes the courage, determination, leadership, and
12 character necessary to attain excellence in athletic prowess and performance; and
13 WHEREAS, his months of training and competitive discipline have been rewarded with an
14 impressive win, and it is appropriate that he holds the title of champion; and
H. R. 872
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3572
15 WHEREAS, Jackson Moffit's victory was the culmination of a season of hard work,
16 dedication, and excellent sportsmanship; and
17 WHEREAS, his superior skill, strategy, stamina, and control have earned him the respect and
18 admiration of his teammates and challengers; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the superlative abilities of this
20 accomplished and gifted student-athlete, as well as the pride, honor, and respect he has
21 brought to himself, his school, and his community, be appropriately recognized.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Jackson Moffit on winning the 2025
24 GHSA Class 4A State Wrestling Championship and extend best wishes for continued success
25 in the future.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
28 Jackson Moffit.
