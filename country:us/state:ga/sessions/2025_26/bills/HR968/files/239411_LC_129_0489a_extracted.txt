25 LC 129 0489
House Resolution 968
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the 2025 Awards of Honor recipient, Mimi Weed; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Mimi is a Rome native, a graduate of Coosa High School and co-owner of Mel
4 & Mimi; and
5 WHEREAS, Mimi is very close with her family and family is very important to her; and
6 WHEREAS, Mimi enjoys traveling and finding new experiences and adventures with dear
7 friends , as well as fashion; and
8 WHEREAS, Mimi is a Board member of Friends of the Marshall Forest, Rome Area
9 Heritage Foundation, Relay for Life, and Cancer Navigators; and
10 WHEREAS, Mimi continues the legacy of her husband, Robert Weed, through her support
11 of the Robert Weed Memorial Marshall Forest Walk in partnership with the Nature
12 Conservancy and Federated Garden Clubs of Georgia, 10th Walk 2024; and
H. R. 968
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0489
13 WHEREAS, Mimi serves her community through her volunteerism with Rome Celebrity
14 Dance Challenge 2023, Rome Symphony Debutante Ball, Floyd Chamber of Commerce,
15 Open Door Home, Junior Service League and Heart of the Community; and
16 WHEREAS, Mimi further serves the community through her business, Mel & Mimi, by
17 fundraising for Brighter Birthdays Dessert Challenge 2022, Keep Rome Floyd Beautiful, and
18 Historic Desoto Theatre Foundation An Evening on the Runway.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body recognize Mimi Weed for her outstanding dedication to the
21 community, commend her as a recipient of the 2025 Heart of the Community Awards Honor
22 Recipient, and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mimi
25 Weed.
