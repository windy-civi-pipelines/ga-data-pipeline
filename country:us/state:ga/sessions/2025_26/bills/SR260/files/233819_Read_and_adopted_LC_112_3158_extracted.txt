Senate Resolution 260
By: Senator Mangham of the 55th
A RESOLUTION
2 Community Service Award; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of the Sherman Town community in Stone Mountain,
4 Georgia, James S. Clark has faithfully served his fellow citizens as a chauffeur, care giver,
5 handyman, and dedicated provider of meals for the homebound and those in need, preparing
6 weekly meals and cooking for the Forever Young Senior Group twice a month; and
7 WHEREAS, he has further served his community as president of the Stone Mountain Day
8 Committee for over 30 years; actively participated in Piney Grove Lodge 585, where he
9 served a term as Worshipful Master; and contributed to local sports as a player-coach for the
10 Stone Mountain A's softball team for 15 years; and
11 WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, he is a lifelong member of St. Paul A.M.E.
12 Church who has served as part of the Trustee Board and the St. Paul Male Chorus; and
13 WHEREAS, a proud veteran, James S. Clark has also served as a guardian of this nation's
14 freedom and liberty with the United States Army, completing two tours in Vietnam and
15 earning two Purple Hearts and the Bronze Star; and

16 WHEREAS, as additional testament to his dedication to those in need, he has received

32 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to James S. Clark.
