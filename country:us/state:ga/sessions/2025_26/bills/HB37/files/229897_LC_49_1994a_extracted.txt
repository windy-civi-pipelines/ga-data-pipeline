Section 1:
25 LC 49 1994
By: Representatives Leverett of the 123rd, Ballard of the 147th, and Hilton of the 48th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Subpart 2 of Part 6 of Article 6 of Chapter 2 of Title 20 of the Official Code of
2 Georgia Annotated, relating to conditions of employment in elementary and secondary
3 education, so as to require local school systems to provide notice to employees regarding
4 social security withholdings and eligibility for inclusion in certain benefits, pension, or
5 retirement plans; to provide for an effective date; to provide for related matters; to repeal
6 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 Subpart 2 of Part 6 of Article 6 of Chapter 2 of Title 20 of the Official Code of Georgia
10 Annotated, relating to conditions of employment in elementary and secondary education, is
11 amended by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
12 "20-2-219.1.
13 (a) Beginning July 1, 2025, and continuing thereafter, each local school system shall, upon
14 hiring a new employee, provide such new employee the following:
15 (1) Notice of whether:
16 (A) Social security taxes will be withheld from his or her pay;

17 (B) He or she is eligible to be included in a plan with coverage for the benefits of Title
18 II of the Social Security Act; and
19 (C) He or she is eligible to be included in any other pension or retirement plan; and
20 (2) If he or she is eligible to be included in one or more plans provided for in
21 subparagraph (B) or (C) of paragraph (1) of this subsection, information regarding such
22 plan or plans.
23 (b) No later than December 31, 2025, and at least once every five years thereafter, each
24 local school system shall provide each current employee with the notice required in
25 paragraph (1) of subsection (a) of this Code section.
26 (c) Beginning July 1, 2025, and continuing thereafter, each local school system shall notify
27 each employee at or near the time of such employee's separation whether social security
28 taxes were withheld from his or her pay during any part of his or her employment."
29 SECTION 2.
30 This Act shall become effective on July 1, 2025.
31 SECTION 3.
32 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
