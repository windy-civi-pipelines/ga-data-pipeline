25 LC 129 0173
House Resolution 279
By: Representative Griffin of the 149th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Noris Price has served as the Superintendent of Baldwin County Schools
3 since July 1, 2014, demonstrating unwavering dedication to student success and academic
4 excellence; and
5 WHEREAS, under her exemplary leadership, the Baldwin County School District has
6 implemented instructional initiatives and strategies that have significantly improved student
7 performance, increasing the high school graduation rate from 66 percent to 85 percent; and
8 WHEREAS, Dr. Price has successfully secured over $10 million in grant funding, greatly
9 benefiting the students and programs of Baldwin County Schools, and has led the school
10 district through the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools/Council on Accreditation
11 and School Improvement district accreditation process; and
12 WHEREAS, prior to her tenure as Superintendent, Dr. Price served as Deputy
13 Superintendent of the Clarke County School District, where she played a pivotal role in the
14 development and implementation of school and system-wide improvement efforts, leading

15 to the district being named the Title I Distinguished District in 2010 for closing the
16 achievement gap; and
17 WHEREAS, Dr. Price has devoted her career to public education, serving as an elementary
18 school teacher in New York City and Fairfax, Virginia, and later as an Assistant Principal
19 and Principal in Fairfax, Virginia, and Fulton County, Georgia, where she led Woodland
20 Elementary Charter School to multiple state and national recognitions; and
21 WHEREAS, Dr. Price has been widely recognized for her contributions to education,
22 receiving accolades such as the 2022 Georgia Superintendent of the Year, Georgia School
23 Superintendents Association President's Award, Georgia Charter System of the Year, and the
24 Communities in Schools National "All In For Students" Award, among many others; and
