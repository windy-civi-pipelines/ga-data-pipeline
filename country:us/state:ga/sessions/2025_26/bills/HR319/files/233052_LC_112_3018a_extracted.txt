25 LC 112 3018
House Resolution 319
87th, Scott of the 76th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 19, 2025, as Reach Out and Read Day at the state capitol to recognize
2 the power of reading to young children every day; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read's early literacy intervention began in 1989 at Boston
4 Medical Center and has expanded to 50 states, serving 5.7 million families today. Georgia's
5 affiliate office launched in 2012 and now serves 194,258 children and families in 81 counties
6 at 208 hospitals, pediatric practices, clinics, and home visiting program sites with 108
7 medical sites that have applications in progress to become Reach Out and Read sites; and
8 WHEREAS, over 1,000 medical providers and staff have been trained in the evidence based
9 early literacy intervention program of Reach Out and Read Georgia, offering each child an
10 opportunity to be read to and to learn to read; and
11 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read has trained 36,000 pediatricians and medical providers
12 who prescribe reading as a daily activity during wellness checkups; and
13 WHEREAS, over the past 12 months, 388,516 well child checkups (engaging approximately
14 194,258 children and families) were conducted across Georgia with pediatricians and
15 providers implementing the Reach Out and Read program; and

16 WHEREAS, in that same time, 289,422 new children's books were distributed through the
17 program's pediatric network to children aged five years old and younger; and
18 WHEREAS, since the program's inception, over 2.7 million new children's books have been
19 given to young children in Georgia by pediatric providers beginning at the very first newborn
20 visit through a child's fifth birthday, and children in the program demonstrate a six-month
21 developmental edge compared to children not enrolled in the program when arriving at
22 kindergarten; and
23 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read is the only early literacy intervention endorsed by the
24 American Academy of Pediatrics and has the most extensive body of research supporting the
25 efficacy of the program model with 26 studies supporting its work; and
26 WHEREAS, pediatricians prescribe reading as a daily activity and parents take these
27 prescriptions to heart, increasing social and emotional bonds with their children while
28 increasing their exposure to positive interactions and creating special, meaningful moments;
29 and
30 WHEREAS, positive emotional bonding between a parent and child has been demonstrated
31 to help mitigate the damaging effects of Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACES); and
32 WHEREAS, pediatric offices implementing Reach Out and Read have cited an increase in
33 wellness visit compliance during the earliest years of life, a time when most immunizations
34 occur; and

- 2 -

35 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read has the ability to serve as the intersection of education and
36 healthcare, offering unprecedented access to the pediatric community, children, and families;
37 and
38 WHEREAS, Reach Out and Read Georgia will provide quality, diverse, and developmentally
39 and linguistically appropriate books in 30 languages to medical sites across Georgia; and
40 WHEREAS, 1,500 medical providers and staff implement the Reach Out and Read Georgia
41 program every day across the state, offering each child an opportunity to be read to and to
42 learn to read; and
43 WHEREAS, medical providers prescribe reading at every wellness visit beginning at birth
44 through age five.
45 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
46 the members of this body commend the efforts of Reach Out and Read to give young
47 children a foundation for success by incorporating books into the existing pediatric care
48 infrastructure, encourage families in Georgia to read aloud together each and every day, and
49 recognize February 19, 2025, as Reach Out and Read Day at the state capitol.
50 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
51 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Reach
52 Out and Read Georgia.
