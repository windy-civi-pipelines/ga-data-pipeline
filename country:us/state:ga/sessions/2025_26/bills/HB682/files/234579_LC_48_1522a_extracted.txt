Section 1:
25 LC 48 1522
By: Representative Mitchell of the 88th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 2 of Chapter 10 of Title 17 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to the death penalty generally, so as to provide that the death penalty shall not be
3 imposed if the only evidence of the defendant's guilt is the testimony of a single eyewitness;
4 to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Article 2 of Chapter 10 of Title 17 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to the
8 death penalty generally, is amended by revising Code Section 17-10-30, relating to the
9 procedure for imposition of death penalty generally, by adding a new subsection to read as
10 follows:
11 "(d) The death penalty shall not be imposed in cases in which the only evidence of a
12 defendant's guilt is the testimony of a single eyewitness."
13 SECTION 2.
14 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 682
