25 LC 112 2867
House Resolution 156
18th, and Kelley of the 16th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Anna Borsh; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Anna Borsh received the 2024 Inclusion Award from Georgia Tech's K-12
3 InVenture Prize State and National Finals at the Raytheon Technologies Invention
4 Convention United States Nationals; and
5 WHEREAS, Ms. Borsh is a diligent, hard-working student from Panter Elementary School;
6 and
7 WHEREAS, with the help of Nellie Klodner, Ms. Borsh created the app "Grocery Eyes,"
8 which helps visually impaired people navigate through the grocery store more easily; and
9 WHEREAS, both Ms. Borsh and Ms. Klodner created the app in the hope that visually
10 impaired people can gain more independence through using the app's navigation,
11 microphone, and camera features to help locate items; and
12 WHEREAS, prior to winning the 2024 Inclusion Award at the United States Nationals,
13 "Grocery Eyes" won the same title from the state level competition where each student won
14 an Amazon tablet; and

- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, as first prize winners at the United States Nationals, each student received a
16 trophy, certificate, and $1,000.00 prize; and
17 WHEREAS, this remarkable young student has personified the selfless dedication, mental
18 agility, verbal adroitness, and personal maturity necessary for such an honorary award; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
20 extraordinary student be appropriately recognized.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body congratulate Anna Borsh upon receiving the 2024 Inclusion Award
23 from Georgia Tech's K-12 InVenture Prize State and National Finals, commend her for her
24 many outstanding contributions on behalf of the citizens of Georgia, and extend best wishes
25 for continued growth, development, and success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Anna
28 Borsh.
