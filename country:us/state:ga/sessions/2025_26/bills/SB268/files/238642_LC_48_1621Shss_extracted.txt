25 LC 48 1621S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to crimes and
2 offenses, so as to include step-grandparent and step-grandchild relationship regarding the
3 offense of incest; to provide for the crime of interference with a first responder; to provide
4 for penalties; to provide for definitions; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting
5 laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to crimes and offenses, is
9 amended in Code Section 16-6-22, relating to incest, by revising paragraph (4) of subsection
10 as follows:
11 "(4) Grandparent and grandchild of the whole blood or the half blood or by virtue of
12 adoption or step-grandparent and step-grandchild;"
13 SECTION 2.
14 Said title is further amended in Article 2 of Chapter 11, relating to offenses against public
15 order, by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
S. B. 268 (SUB)
- 1 -
25 LC 48 1621S
16 "16-11-46.
17 (a) As used in this Code section, the term:
18 (1) 'First responder' means any person or agency who provides on-site care until the
19 arrival of a duly licensed ambulance service. This shall include, but not be limited to,
20 persons who routinely respond to calls for assistance through an affiliation with law
21 enforcement agencies, fire suppression agencies, rescue agencies, and others.
22 (2) 'Harass' means to willfully engage in any conduct directed at a first responder which
23 intentionally causes substantial emotional distress in that first responder and serves no
24 legitimate purpose.
25 (b) A person commits the offense of interference with a first responder when he or she,
26 after receiving a verbal warning not to approach from a person he or she knows or
27 reasonably should know is a first responder, who is engaged in the lawful performance of
28 a legal duty, knowingly and willfully violates such warning and approaches or remains
29 within 25 feet of the first responder with the intent to:
30 (1) Impede or interfere with the first responder's ability to perform such duty;
31 (2) Threaten the first responder with physical harm; or
32 (3) Harass the first responder.
33 (c) A person convicted pursuant to this Code section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor of
34 a high and aggravated nature."
35 SECTION 3.
36 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 268 (SUB)
