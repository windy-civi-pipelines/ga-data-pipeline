25 LC 129 0363
Senate Resolution 488
Payne of the 54th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Mr. Jim Battles upon his retirement; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Mr. Jim Battles has served in vocational ministry for over forty years; and
3 WHEREAS, Mr. Battles received his Bachelors of Science from Auburn University; and
4 WHEREAS, Mr. Battles later received his Master's degree in Religious Education from
5 Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary; and
6 WHEREAS, Mr. Battles began his full-time service in vocational ministry in education and
7 youth at Parkview Baptist Church, in Florida, in 1981; and
8 WHEREAS, Mr. Battles continued working in ministry after moving to Georgia in 1994, to
9 work as the Associate Pastor for Adults and Youth Education at First Baptist Church in
10 Fayetteville, before becoming the Youth Minister; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Battles' influence spans decades, mentoring students who later became
12 youth ministers themselves, including Belinda Jenkins, whose daughter and son-in-law are
13 now in ministry; and
S. R. 488
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0363
14 WHEREAS, despite fighting a battle with leukemia, Mr. Battles remains grateful for the
15 opportunity to serve long enough to see generational change in his ministry; and
16 WHEREAS, Mr. Battles had the ability to make every student feel included and valued
17 serving as a key part of his success; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
19 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
21 recognize and commend Mr. Jim Battles for his decades of ministry and service, congratulate
22 him upon his retirement, and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and
23 happiness
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mr. Jim Battles.
