25 Senate Floor Amend 1 AM 50 0133
OUT OF ORDER
29th offered the following amendment #1:
1 Amend the Senate Committee on Rules substitute to HB 652 (LC 39 4810S) by inserting after
2 line 7 the following:
3 to revise the definition of the term "tourism product development" relative to excise tax on
4 rooms, lodgings, and accommodations to include air service product expansion at certain
5 air carrier airports;
6 By inserting after line 93 the following:
7 Said title is further amended in paragraph (6) of Code Section 48-13-50.2, relating to
8 definitions relative to excise tax on rooms, lodgings, and accommodations, by striking "or"
9 at the end of subparagraph (O), by replacing the period with "; and" at the end of
10 subparagraph (P), and by adding a new subparagraph to read as follows:
11 "(Q) Air service product expansion at air carrier airports located in any coastal county
12 in which one or more barrier islands are operated by a state authority as a state park."
13 SECTION 7.
14 By replacing "subsection (b)" with "subsections (b) and (c)" on line 94.
15 By inserting after line 97 the following:
16 (c) Section 6 of this Act shall become effective on July 1, 2025.
