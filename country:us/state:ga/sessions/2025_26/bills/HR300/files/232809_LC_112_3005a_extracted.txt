25 LC 112 3005
House Resolution 300
By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13,
3 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and
4 WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight
5 leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence";
6 and
7 WHEREAS, Denise N. Smith is a visionary leader with over two decades of experience in
8 technology and business strategy who currently serves as CEO of DNS Consulting, where
9 she collaborates with small businesses, private equity, and venture capital portfolio
10 companies to drive innovation, scalability, and transformative growth; and
11 WHEREAS, her distinguished career spans prior leadership roles at firms such as EY, Cox
12 Automotive, and Hewitt Associates, during which time she has spearheaded initiatives to
13 optimize performance, scale operations, and advance technological solutions; and

14 WHEREAS, a passionate advocate for leveraging technology to bridge societal gaps, Denise
15 N. Smith has traveled to 152 countries, gaining a global perspective on business innovation

18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be
