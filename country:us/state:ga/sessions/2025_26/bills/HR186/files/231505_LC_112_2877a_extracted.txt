25 LC 112 2877
House Resolution 186
the 89th, and Olaleye of the 59th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Urban League of Greater Atlanta Young Professionals and
2 the Urban League of the River Valley Young Professionals; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Urban League of Greater Atlanta Young Professionals (ULGA-YP) is an
4 organization dedicated to empowering young professionals in the metro Atlanta area,
5 fostering leadership development, and advocating for social and economic equality; and
6 WHEREAS, the Urban League of the River Valley Young Professionals (ULRV-YP) is
7 dedicated to serving the young professionals working in the River Valley region; and
8 WHEREAS, this year, the Columbus Chapter of the ULRV-YP is celebrating its 10th
9 anniversary; and
10 WHEREAS, the ULGA-YP and ULRV-YP have consistently demonstrated a commitment
11 to community service, education, and civic engagement, thereby contributing to the
12 betterment of this state; and
H. R. 186
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2877
13 WHEREAS, the ULGA-YP and ULRV-YP have played pivotal roles in addressing pressing
14 issues such as education, workforce development, and social justice, working towards
15 creating a more equitable and inclusive society.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
17 the members of this body recognize and commend the Urban League of Greater Atlanta
18 Young Professionals and the Urban League of the River Valley Young Professionals for their
19 unwavering dedication to community service, leadership development, and advocacy for
20 social and economic equality.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of this body express their gratitude to the
22 Urban League of Greater Atlanta Young Professionals and the Urban League of the River
23 Valley Young Professionals for their ongoing efforts to improve the lives of young
24 professionals and underserved communities in this state.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of this body extend best wishes for
26 continued success to the Urban League of Greater Atlanta Young Professionals and the
27 Urban League of the River Valley Young Professionals in all their future endeavors.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
30 Urban League of Greater Atlanta Young Professionals and the Urban League of the River
31 Valley Young Professionals.
