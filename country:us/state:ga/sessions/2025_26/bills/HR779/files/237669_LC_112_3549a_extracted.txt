25 LC 112 3549
House Resolution 779
103rd, and Efstration of the 104th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Aligarh Alumni Association of Atlanta; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, established in 1984, the Aligarh Alumni Association of Atlanta (AAAA) is a
4 dedicated nonprofit organization composed of graduates of Aligarh Muslim University
5 (AMU), India, and supporters of the Aligarh educational movement; and
6 WHEREAS, AAAA has played a crucial role in supporting education by providing
7 assistance to students through various programs, such as the Sir Syed Global Scholars Award
8 (SGSA), the Direct Early Intervention Center at AMU (DEIC), and the Indo-US APJ Abdul
9 Kalam Center for STEM Education & Research of OSU-AMU, reflecting its commitment
10 to academic and financial support for deserving students; and
11 WHEREAS, AAAA is also deeply involved in community engagement, contributing to the
12 well-being of the greater Atlanta community through collaborative efforts, social issue
13 dialogues, and promoting interfaith understanding; and
14 WHEREAS, since 2022, AAAA has hosted annual Eid Milan Picnics, becoming a cherished
15 tradition in the Atlanta area, fostering cultural connection and providing an opportunity for

16 thousands of community members to gather and celebrate Eid together in a festive,
17 family-friendly environment; and
18 WHEREAS, AAAA has been instrumental in preserving and promoting the Urdu language
19 and its art forms through the Jashn-e-Urdu Festival, which has featured renowned literary
20 figures like poet and lyricist Javed Akhtar, showcasing poetic performances, literary
21 discussions, and fostering a greater appreciation for the richness of Urdu culture; and
22 WHEREAS, the contributions of AAAA to education, community engagement, and cultural
23 preservation have had a lasting and meaningful impact on the people of Georgia and beyond.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body recognize and commend the Aligarh Alumni Association of
26 Atlanta for its outstanding efforts in advancing education, supporting the community, and
27 preserving cultural heritage.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
30 Aligarh Alumni Association of Atlanta.
