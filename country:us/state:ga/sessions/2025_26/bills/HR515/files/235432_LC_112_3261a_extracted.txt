25 LC 112 3261
House Resolution 515
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Larry Max Cox; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Larry Max Cox on October 4, 2024; and
4 WHEREAS, Larry Max Cox safeguarded his community as a Gwinnett County police officer
5 in the 1970s and served as a guardian of this nation's freedom and liberty with the United
6 States Army, valiantly and courageously protecting his fellow Americans; and
7 WHEREAS, he was an active member of the Rotary Club of Madison County, the
8 Cattlemen's Association of Madison County, the Chamber of Commerce, the T J and Friends
9 Cancer Foundation, the Food Bank of Madison County, and Food to Kids, selflessly giving
10 his time and talents to many causes and nonprofits; and
11 WHEREAS, he was instrumental in implementing a scholarship program for high school
12 seniors in Madison County and was a generous contributor to the fund, ensuring greater
13 educational opportunities for local students; and
H. R. 515
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3261
14 WHEREAS, he additionally served as a mentor to students at Ila Elementary School, making
15 a profound difference in their lives by helping to improve their reading skills and fostering
16 a love for learning; and
17 WHEREAS, Larry Max Cox enjoyed a 27-year-long professional career with UPS, where
18 he worked his way up from driver to vice president of telecommunications; and
19 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife, Linda, with whom he shared 55
20 wonderful years, and the two were blessed with an outstanding son, Jason, and three
21 cherished grandchildren, Jared, Justin, and Kathryn; and
22 WHEREAS, a man of deep love for the land, he found great joy in his farm and cattle, often
23 spending his days on his tractor or caring for his livestock; and
24 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Larry Max Cox will long be remembered
25 for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who had the great
26 fortune of knowing him.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Larry Max Cox and
29 express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 family of Larry Max Cox.
H. R. 515
