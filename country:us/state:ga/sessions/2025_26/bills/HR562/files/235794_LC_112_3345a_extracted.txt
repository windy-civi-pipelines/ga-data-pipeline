25 LC 112 3345
House Resolution 562
By: Representative Reese of the 140th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Senator Raphael G. Warnock has dedicated innumerable hours of his time and
3 energy to public service, advocacy, and ministry, addressing critical issues such as healthcare
4 access, voting rights, economic opportunity, and criminal justice reform; and
5 WHEREAS, he was elected as Georgia's first Black United States Senator in 2021 and
6 successfully defended his seat in December 2022, where he has since continued his work to
7 create jobs, address the maternal mortality crisis, and expand healthcare access; and
8 WHEREAS, he currently serves on the Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry Committee;
9 Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs Committee; Finance Committee; and the Special
10 Committee on Aging, supporting policies that strengthen the economy, support rural
11 communities, and protect vulnerable populations; and
12 WHEREAS, during his exemplary tenure, he has used his moral voice to champion policies
13 that serve Georgians, including efforts to reduce gun violence, close the healthcare gap,
14 strengthen economic opportunities, and protect the fundamental right to vote; and
H. R. 562
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3345
15 WHEREAS, since 2005, he has further served as senior pastor of Ebenezer Baptist Church
16 in Atlanta, the former pulpit of Reverend Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.; he is the youngest
17 pastor ever selected to serve in this role; and
18 WHEREAS, through his leadership, advocacy, and legislative accomplishments, Senator
19 Warnock has demonstrated an enduring commitment to improving the lives of Georgians and
20 advancing justice and equity across the nation; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
22 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately honored.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body recognize and commend Senator Raphael G. Warnock for his
25 efficient, effective, unselfish, and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia as well as
26 extend the most sincere best wishes for continued success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Senator Raphael G. Warnock.
