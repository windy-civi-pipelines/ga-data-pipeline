25 LC 112 2815
Senate Resolution 111
Harbison of the 15th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 10, 2025, as Blue and White Day at the state capitol; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Men of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Incorporated, continue to exemplify the
4 high ideals of their Distinguished Founders' belief of Culture for Service and Service for
5 Humanity with 56 active chapters throughout the great State of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia chapters of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., continuously strive to
7 improve the communities they work and reside in by establishing service projects designed
8 to eliminate physical and mental health inequities, mobilize and educate voters, and empower
9 a previously marginalized citizenry; and
10 WHEREAS, the Ladies of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Incorporated, a community conscious,
11 action-oriented organization, continue to fulfill the vision of their Founders with 60 active
12 chapters throughout the great State of Georgia; and
13 WHEREAS, the Georgia chapters of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc., fervently strive to improve
14 their communities by establishing projects and programs that advocate for social well-being,
15 mental and physical health, and economic justice; and
S. R. 111
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2815
16 WHEREAS, the Georgia chapters of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., and Zeta Phi Beta
17 Sorority, Inc., continue to build on their rich legacy of shared purpose for the benefit of
18 improved service to the communities we reside in.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 recognize February 10, 2025, as Blue and White Day at the state capitol; commend the
21 combined efforts of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., and Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc.; and
22 extend the most sincere best wishes for future success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Phi Beta Sigma
25 Fraternity, Inc., and Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc.
