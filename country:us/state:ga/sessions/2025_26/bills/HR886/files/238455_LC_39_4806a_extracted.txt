2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Ms. Rachel Hodge was highly regarded by the citizens of her community and
4 state and by local government officials as a person of unquestioned integrity with a
5 commitment to serving others; and
6 WHEREAS, Ms. Hodge was born in St. Marys, Georgia, the beloved daughter of Christopher
7 and Melissa Crosby Hodge and graduated from Camden County High School; and
8
9 WHEREAS, as the daughter of a law enforcement officer with the Department of Natural
10 Resources, Ms. Hodge grew up to follow in her father's footsteps to serve as an Emergency
11 9-1-1 Communications Officer with the Camden County Sheriff's Office; and
12 WHEREAS, she exhibited extraordinary devotion to duty, outstanding loyalty, fine
13 leadership, and meticulous attention to detail in all her duties; and
14 WHEREAS, Ms. Hodge's life was tragically cut short on April 18, 2020, on her way home
15 from work; and
H. R. 886
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4806
16 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
17 Georgian be recognized appropriately by dedicating an intersection in her memory.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
19 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the intersection of State Route 25 Spur/Satilla Bluff Road
20 and Pine Forest Drive in Camden County is dedicated as the Rachel Hodge Memorial
21 Intersection.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
23 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Rachel Hodge Memorial
24 Intersection.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 family of Ms. Rachel Hodge and to the Department of Transportation.
