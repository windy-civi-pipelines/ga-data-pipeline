25 LC 129 0255
House Resolution 791
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Sophia Dubs, Unity Christian School's 2025 STAR Student; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a gifted student at Unity Christian School, Sophia Dubs has been recognized
4 for her superlative achievement and selected as Unity Christian School's 2025 STAR
5 Student; and
6 WHEREAS, to be selected as a STAR Student, a student must have obtained the highest
7 score in one administration of the three-part Scholastic Aptitude Test and be in the top 10
8 percent or top ten students of his or her class; and
9 WHEREAS, each school has one STAR Student who in turn has the opportunity to choose
10 a STAR Teacher who has been inspiring and encouraging throughout that student's high
11 school career; and
12 WHEREAS, educator Susan Payne was chosen by Sophia as Unity Christian School's 2025
13 STAR Teacher for the great support and guidance provided to her throughout the years; and
H. R. 791
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0255
14 WHEREAS, Sophia has personified the selfless dedication, mental agility, verbal adroitness,
15 and personal maturity necessary for superlative academic performance; and
16 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
17 important objective of the General Assembly, and Sophia has shown by academic
18 accomplishment that the pursuit and attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of
19 the students in this state; and
20 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia looks with great anticipation toward the future of this
21 bright and promising young citizen.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body heartily commend Sophia Dubs for being named Unity Christian
24 School's 2025 STAR Student and express their most sincere best wishes for continued
25 academic success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
28 Sophia Dubs.
