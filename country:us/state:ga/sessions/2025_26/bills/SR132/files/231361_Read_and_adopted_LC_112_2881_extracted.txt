25 LC 112 2881
Senate Resolution 132
and Kemp of the 38th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Georgia Council of Court Administrators for its dedication
2 to advancing court administration and enhancing the efficiency of judicial systems
3 throughout the State of Georgia; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council of Court Administrators (GCCA) was established in 1997
5 to promote the professional development of court administrators and ensure excellence in
6 judicial administration across the State of Georgia; and
7 WHEREAS, GCCA is dedicated to providing training, resources, and professional
8 development opportunities to its members, enabling them to serve the courts and the citizens
9 of Georgia more effectively; and
10 WHEREAS, the council is recognized for its unwavering commitment to fostering
11 collaboration among court professionals, justice partners, and stakeholders to address
12 challenges and implement innovative solutions in judicial administration; and
13 WHEREAS, GCCA plays a pivotal role in offering educational programs based on the
14 National Association for Court Management (NACM) Core Competencies, equipping court

15 administrators with the knowledge and skills necessary to meet the evolving needs of the
16 judiciary; and
17 WHEREAS, the council has been instrumental in developing leadership within the courts by
18 organizing conferences, workshops, and training sessions as well as by promoting best
19 practices to enhance the efficiency and accessibility of Georgia's judicial system; and
20 WHEREAS, the dedication of GCCA's leadership and members to the principles of fairness,
21 professionalism, and innovation has significantly contributed to the improvement of court
22 operations in Georgia; and
23 WHEREAS, GCCA has provided valuable support and guidance to court professionals in
24 adapting to challenges, including the integration of technology and the implementation of
25 remote court services during unprecedented times; and
26 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this distinguished organization and its
27 members be appropriately recognized for their outstanding contributions to the judicial
28 system in Georgia.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
30 recognize and commend the Georgia Council of Court Administrators for its exemplary
31 service and dedication to the administration of justice as well as extend their most sincere
32 best wishes for continued success.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
34 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Georgia
35 Council of Court Administrators.
