25 LC 112 2864
Senate Resolution 134
and Gooch of the 51st
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Professional Association of Georgia Educators (PAGE) on its 50th
2 anniversary and recognizing February 18, 2025, as PAGE Day at the state capitol; and for
3 other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Professional Association of Georgia Educators (PAGE) is the state's largest
5 organization for professional educators with more than 93,000 members who are teachers,
6 administrators, and support personnel employed in every school system in Georgia; and
7 WHEREAS, PAGE was founded in 1975 with the mission to make Georgia a better place
8 for students to learn and for teachers to teach, and 2025 marks the 50th anniversary of its
9 founding; and
10 WHEREAS, the PAGE Foundation sponsors the Academic Bowl for middle grades, the
11 Georgia Academic Decathlon for high school students, and the Student and Teacher
12 Achievement Recognition (STAR) program; and
13 WHEREAS, PAGE has promoted teaching as a profession by sponsoring local chapters of
14 the Future Georgia Educators in middle and high schools across the state; and
S. R. 134
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2864
15 WHEREAS, PAGE provides legal protection and professional learning to enhance
16 competence and confidence, build leadership, and increase student achievement; and
17 WHEREAS, PAGE actively participates in the legislative process each year, working to
18 improve education and student achievement while promoting a positive and professional
19 working relationship with legislative representatives across the state; and
20 WHEREAS, this year marks PAGE's 50th anniversary of continual service and advocacy for
21 public educators and public education in Georgia; and
22 WHEREAS, on February 18, 2025, PAGE members and leaders will gather at the state
23 capitol to meet with their local legislators.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 commend the Professional Association of Georgia Educators (PAGE) for 50 years of
26 advocacy and its support of educators and education as well as recognize February 18, 2025,
27 as PAGE Day at the state capitol.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
29 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Professional
30 Association of Georgia Educators (PAGE).
