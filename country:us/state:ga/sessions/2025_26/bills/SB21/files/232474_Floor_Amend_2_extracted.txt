Section 1:
25 Floor amend 2
LOST
Amend the Senate Committee on Public Safety substitute to SB 21 (LC 57 0224S) by:
striking “the” on line 34 and inserting “any” in its place
and after “notice;” inserting on line 34
“provided, however, that, when the facility of a custodial authority has reached capacity
limits for the housing of inmates and the person subject to the immigration detainer notice
is in the custody for a misdemeanor charge, such person shall be held no longer than 100
days from the last date of contact from the federal government;”
