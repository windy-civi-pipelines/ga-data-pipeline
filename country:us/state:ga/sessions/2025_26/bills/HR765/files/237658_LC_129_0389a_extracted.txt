25 LC 129 0389
House Resolution 765
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Oluwasore Edward, Rockdale County High School's Class of 2025
2 valedictorian; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, Oluwasore Edward has shown by academic accomplishment that the pursuit and
6 attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of the students in this state; and
7 WHEREAS, this remarkable senior has earned distinction through her notable achievements
8 as a student at Rockdale County High School where she is a member of the beta club, the
9 color guard, the national honor society, G.I.F.T.S, the national art honor society, and RCA
10 ambassadors; and
11 WHEREAS, she has been selected as valedictorian by earning exemplary grades in the most
12 challenging academic courses while maintaining a substantial extracurricular schedule
13 throughout her academic career; and

14 WHEREAS, this achievement is the culmination of Oluwasore Edward's hard work and
15 determination, and she deserves much credit for her energy and dedication to academic
16 excellence; and
17 WHEREAS, she is an outstanding representative of the young people of this state and it is
18 only fitting and proper that she be recognized for her many accomplishments.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body heartily commend Oluwasore Edward for her spectacular scholastic
21 endeavors and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
24 Oluwasore Edward.
