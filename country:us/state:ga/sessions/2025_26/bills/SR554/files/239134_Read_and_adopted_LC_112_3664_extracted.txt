25 LC 112 3664
Senate Resolution 554
and Wicks of the 34th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring Lawrence A. Reeves Jr. for his service and leadership; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Lawrence A. Reeves Jr. honorably served in the United States Air Force from
3 1964 to 1968, earning an honorable discharge and carrying forward the values of duty,
4 integrity, and leadership throughout his life; and
5 WHEREAS, as a service-disabled veteran, he has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to
6 empowering fellow veterans, notably through his leadership at Aldridge Services Inc., where
7 he positioned the firm as a viable service-disabled veteran-owned small business in
8 environmental consulting and geotechnical engineering; and
9 WHEREAS, his distinguished career spans decades of executive leadership, business
10 development, and public service, including roles as President of R&D Environmental
11 Consultants and Director of Business Development at Tri-Tech Associates, where he played
12 a pivotal role in shaping economic opportunities and environmental initiatives; and
13 WHEREAS, his dedication to uplifting communities is evident in his extensive ministry
14 work, serving as Pastor of Trinity Fellowship Ministries and Outreach Pastor at Cascade
S. R. 554

15 United Methodist Church, where he provided spiritual guidance and tangible resources to
16 those in need, particularly the homeless and underserved populations; and
17 WHEREAS, his contributions extend to education, having served as an instructor at Atlanta
18 Metropolitan College, where he shaped the minds of future leaders in business, management,
19 and administration; and
20 WHEREAS, his service has been recognized through numerous awards, including the Office
21 of Workforce Development's Outstanding Support and Service Award and his appointment
22 to the Fulton County Private Industry Council Board, among others, demonstrating his
23 lasting impact on workforce development, minority business advancement, and community
24 empowerment.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
26 honor and commend Lawrence A. Reeves Jr. for his unwavering dedication to service both
27 in uniform and beyond, his exemplary leadership in business and ministry, and his lifelong
28 commitment to fostering economic opportunity and social progress for all.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
30 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Lawrence A.
31 Reeves Jr.
