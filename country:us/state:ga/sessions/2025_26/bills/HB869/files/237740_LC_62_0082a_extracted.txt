Section 1:
25 LC 62 0082
By: Representatives Gambill of the 15th and Scoggins of the 14th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act creating and establishing a joint airport authority for the City of
2 Cartersville and Bartow County, Georgia, approved February 28, 1966
3 (Ga. L. 1966, p. 2270), so as to provide for the legislative delegation of Bartow County to
4 appoint a member; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other
5 purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 An Act creating and establishing a joint airport authority for the City of Cartersville and
9 Bartow County, Georgia, approved February 28, 1966 (Ga. L. 1966, p. 2270), is amended
10 by revising Section 2 as follows:
11 "Section 2. Such authority shall be composed of three members, one of whom shall be
12 appointed by the board of aldermen of the City of Cartersville in the same manner as in the
13 appointment of the chief of police, and one member shall be appointed by the
14 commissioner of roads and revenues for Bartow County, Georgia. One member shall be
15 appointed by the local delegation consisting of all members of the General Assembly
H. B. 869

16 whose districts are wholly or partially within Bartow County. The authority so constituted
17 shall annually elect its chairperson from its number."
18 SECTION 2.
19 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
