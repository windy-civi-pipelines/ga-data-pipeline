House Resolution 844
and Cannon of the 58th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Pastor Troy for his outstanding contributions to Atlanta's
2 musical sound, culture, and his lasting impact on the global music industry; and for other
3 purposes.
4 WHEREAS, Micah LeVar Troy, known professionally as Pastor Troy, is a trailblazing
5 rapper, songwriter, and producer whose influence on Atlanta's hip-hop scene has been
6 instrumental in shaping the crunk movement and the Southern rap genre; and
7 WHEREAS, born and raised in College Park, Georgia, Pastor Troy rose to prominence in the
8 late 1990s with his electrifying energy, hard-hitting lyrics, and a commanding presence that
9 set him apart as a leader in the hip-hop community; and
10 WHEREAS, his debut album, We Ready (I Declare War), featuring the anthem "No Mo Play
11 in GA," became a rallying cry for Southern hip-hop and solidified his place as one of the
12 defining voices of Atlanta's rap movement; and
13 WHEREAS, Pastor Troy played a pivotal role in the expansion and popularization of crunk
14 music, collaborating with industry heavyweights such as Lil Jon, Three 6 Mafia, and
15 Ludacris, further cementing Atlanta's dominance in the hip-hop world; and

16 WHEREAS, throughout his career, he has remained an independent force in the industry,
17 consistently releasing music that resonates with his dedicated fanbase and inspiring countless
18 artists to maintain creative control over their work; and
19 WHEREAS, Pastor Troy's music embodies the raw, unfiltered energy of Atlanta's streets,
20 blending intensity, passion, and lyrical dexterity to create timeless anthems that continue to
21 influence new generations of artists; and
22 WHEREAS, beyond his contributions to music, Pastor Troy has been a symbol of resilience,
23 entrepreneurship, and authenticity, representing the values that have made Atlanta a cultural
24 hub for artistic innovation; and
25 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this body recognize the exceptional
26 achievements of Pastor Troy and extend gratitude for his contributions to Georgia's musical
27 legacy and the global hip-hop culture.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body recognize and commend Pastor Troy for his outstanding
30 contributions to music and culture and express their sincere appreciation for his lasting
31 influence on the State of Georgia and beyond.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
33 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Pastor
34 Troy.
