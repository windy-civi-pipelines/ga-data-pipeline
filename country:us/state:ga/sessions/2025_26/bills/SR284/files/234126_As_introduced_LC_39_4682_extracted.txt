Section 1:
25 LC 39 4682
Senate Resolution 284
37th and Robertson of the 29th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring fallen peace officers of Georgia and dedicating a road in their memory; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, all residents of Georgia rely on the men and women who put their personal
4 safety on the line to keep communities safe from harm; and
5 WHEREAS, peace officers in this state devote innumerable hours of their time, talents, and
6 energy toward keeping criminals off the streets and uplifting their communities through their
7 service; and
8 WHEREAS, peace officers exhibit devotion to duty, outstanding loyalty, fine leadership, and
9 meticulous attention to detail in their duties; and
10 WHEREAS, fallen peace officers have given the ultimate sacrifice to this state and its
11 citizens and deserve the utmost respect and condolences; and
12 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that these remarkable and distinguished
13 Georgians be recognized appropriately by dedicating a road in their memory.
S. R. 284
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4682
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
15 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of State Route 92 from the Douglas County line
16 to the Cobb County line is dedicated as the Peace Officer Memorial Highway.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
18 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Peace Officer Memorial
19 Highway.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Department
22 of Transportation.
S. R. 284
- 2 -
