25 LC 112 3121
Senate Resolution 250
Anderson of the 43rd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 26, 2025, as DeKalb Day at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, established on December 9, 1822, as Georgia's 56th county, DeKalb County has
3 a legacy of progress, economic development, and civic leadership, making it a cornerstone
4 of Georgia's cultural and economic landscape; and
5 WHEREAS, covering 269 square miles, DeKalb County is strategically positioned between
6 Atlanta and Athens, serving as a key corridor for commerce, education, and innovation along
7 Georgia's Continental Divide; and
8 WHEREAS, DeKalb County is home to world-class educational institutions, including
9 Emory University, Agnes Scott College, Oglethorpe University, and Georgia Piedmont
10 Technical College, reinforcing its status as a leader in higher education and workforce
11 development; and
12 WHEREAS, DeKalb County serves as a global hub for healthcare and scientific research,
13 housing the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, Emory Healthcare, and Children's
14 Healthcare of Atlanta, while also contributing to Grady Hospital's mission of accessible and
15 high-quality healthcare; and
S. R. 250
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3121
16 WHEREAS, DeKalb County is one of Georgia's most diverse communities, representing
17 over 100 nationalities and more than 140 spoken languages, which enriches the county's
18 cultural, economic, and social fabric; and
19 WHEREAS, DeKalb County is an economic powerhouse, leading in technology, film and
20 entertainment, logistics, and advanced manufacturing while also being home to Georgia's
21 second-busiest airport, DeKalb-Peachtree Airport; and
22 WHEREAS, under the historic leadership of CEO Lorraine Cochran-Johnson, the first
23 African American woman elected as Chief Executive Officer of DeKalb County, the county
24 continues to set the standard for strong governance and economic prosperity.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
26 recognize February 26, 2025, as DeKalb Day at the state capitol, celebrating the county's rich
27 history, residents, dynamic diversity, and ongoing commitment to innovation, economic
28 development, and excellence in governance.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
30 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the public and the
31 press.
