25 LC 129 0279
House Resolution 496
75th, and Cannon of the 58th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dr. Contessa Metcalfe; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Contessa Metcalfe is a triple board certified physician, veteran, and star of
3 serval movies and television shows, including Married to Medicine; and
4 WHEREAS, after graduating from St. Louis School of Medicine, she served as a guardian
5 of this nation's freedom and liberty with the United States Navy, valiantly and courageously
6 protecting her fellow Americans and traveling the world as a flight surgeon; and
7 WHEREAS, Dr. Metcalfe owns HauteDoc, a lifestyle brand and a supplier of high quality
8 vitamins and supplements, as well as Chastain Integrative Medicine, located in the
9 prestigious Buckhead region of Atlanta, where she specializes in medical aesthetics as well
10 as preventive, occupational, and addiction medicine; and
11 WHEREAS, in addition to being a successful entrepreneur, Dr. Metcalfe selflessly serves as
12 the Georgia Chair of the American Diabetes Association and advocates for criminal justice
13 reform and health equity, especially in marginalized communities and for veterans; and
H. R. 496
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0279
14 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
15 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
16 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
17 remarkable and distinguished medical professional be appropriately recognized.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body recognize and commend Dr. Contessa Metcalfe for her efficient,
20 effective, unselfish, and dedicated commitment to the health and well being of citizens of
21 Georgia and extend the most sincere best wishes for her continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
24 Contessa Metcalfe.
