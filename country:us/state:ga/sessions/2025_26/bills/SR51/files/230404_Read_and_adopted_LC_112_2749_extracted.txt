25 LC 112 2749
Senate Resolution 51
Goodman of the 8th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Georgia Association of Broadcasters and their radio and television station
2 members; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Association of Broadcasters, the nation's first and oldest broadcast
4 association, works diligently to represent the interests of radio and television licensees and
5 is composed of hundreds of radio and television broadcasters; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Association of Broadcasters and its television and radio station
7 members have consistently demonstrated exceptional dedication to their communities by
8 providing timely and accurate local news, engaging content, and life-saving information; and
9 WHEREAS, during times of crisis, such as Hurricane Helene, broadcasters have played a
10 crucial role in ensuring the safety and well-being of Georgia residents by delivering critical
11 updates, emergency alerts, and essential information; and
12 WHEREAS, the commitment of these broadcasters to public service and their unwavering
13 efforts to keep the public informed have significantly contributed to the resilience and
14 preparedness of communities across Georgia; and
S. R. 51
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2749
15 WHEREAS, the Georgia Association of Broadcasters and its members have shown
16 exemplary professionalism, integrity, and a deep sense of responsibility in their reporting and
17 broadcasting, earning the trust and respect of their audiences; and
18 WHEREAS, the Georgia State Senate recognizes the invaluable contributions of the Georgia
19 Association of Broadcasters and its television and radio station members to the State of
20 Georgia and its residents.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 commend the Georgia Association of Broadcasters and their television and radio station
23 members for their outstanding service, dedication, and commitment to their communities,
24 especially during times of crisis like Hurricane Helene.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
26 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Georgia
27 Association of Broadcasters.
S. R. 51
