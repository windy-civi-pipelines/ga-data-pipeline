25 LC 112 3593
Senate Resolution 511
By: Senator James of the 28th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Michelle Taylor Willis is a distinguished graduate of the University of Florida
3 and a dynamic trailblazer who has earned accolades as a media executive, author, speaker,
4 actress, and strategic advisor; and
5 WHEREAS, as Founder and CEO of Gagnant Media and Publisher of SoFu Lifestyle
6 Magazine—a three-time Publication of the Year recipient—she has amplified community
7 voices and advanced storytelling throughout metro Atlanta; and
8 WHEREAS, Michelle Taylor Willis has become a respected voice on national platforms,
9 sharing her insight as a speaker, panelist, and moderator for organizations such as the
10 National Urban League, Blacks in Tech, National Bar Association, and United Way; and
11 WHEREAS, she has additionally championed positive parenting through her nationally
12 recognized book, Raising Significance: A Guide to Well-Rounded, Independent, and
13 Confident Kids, which was featured on The Tamron Hall Show; and
