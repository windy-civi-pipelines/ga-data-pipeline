14 WHEREAS, Mother Arnold was known for her gardening talents and "green thumb,"
15 impressing her Fairburn community neighbors with her impressive yard and earning her yard
16 of the year recognition on numerous occasions; and
17 WHEREAS, she was recognized with numerous awards and honors, including a Housing

19 a Crown Peace Award, and a Christian City Service Award; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
21 Georgian be appropriately recognized by dedicating a road in her memory.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
23 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of State Route 92 from State Route 29 to Bay
24 Street in Fulton County is dedicated as the Mother Frankie Mae Arnold Highway.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
26 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Mother Frankie Mae Arnold
27 Highway.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
30 family of Mother Essie Frankie Mae Stegall Arnold and to the Department of Transportation.
