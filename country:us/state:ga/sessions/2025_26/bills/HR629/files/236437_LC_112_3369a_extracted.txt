25 LC 112 3369
House Resolution 629
the 149th, and Washburn of the 144th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending D.J. Hurm for being named Monroe County's Teacher of the Year for 2024 and
2 recognizing his exceptional contributions to education and the community; and for other
3 purposes.
4 WHEREAS, D.J. Hurm, a dedicated educator at Mary Persons High School, has been
5 honored as Monroe County's Teacher of the Year for 2024, reflecting his commitment to
6 excellence in teaching and his positive impact on students; and
7 WHEREAS, D.J. Hurm has demonstrated outstanding leadership and innovation in the
8 classroom, fostering an engaging and supportive learning environment that encourages
9 student growth and achievement; and
10 WHEREAS, his dedication extends beyond the classroom as he actively participates in
11 extracurricular activities and community initiatives, further enriching the educational
12 experience for his students and strengthening community ties; and
13 WHEREAS, his passion for education and unwavering commitment to his students serve as
14 an inspiration to his colleagues, students, and the broader community.
H. R. 629
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3369
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
16 the members of this body commend D.J. Hurm for his exemplary service as an educator, his
17 recognition as Monroe County's Teacher of the Year for 2024, and his significant
18 contributions to the academic and personal development of his students.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
20 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to D.J.
21 Hurm.
