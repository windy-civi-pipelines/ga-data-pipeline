Section 1:
25 LC 129 0434
Senate Resolution 503
James of the 28th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating Midtown High School for winning the GHSA 4A State Literary
2 Championship in Domestic and International Extemporaneous Speaking; and for other
3 purposes.
4 WHEREAS, Midtown High School has established and maintained a proud tradition of
5 excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
6 WHEREAS, these superlative students have completed another triumphant season of literary
7 competition by winning the state championship in both the Domestic and International
8 Extemporaneous Speaking categories; and
9 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
10 dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the team; and
11 WHEREAS, this all-star team included seniors Charlotte Keenan and Henry Carter; and
12 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
13 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
14 abundantly proper to call them champions.
S. R. 503
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0434
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
16 congratulate Midtown High School for winning the GHSA 4A State Literary Championship
17 in Domestic and International Extemporaneous Speaking and extend best wishes for their
18 continued success.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
20 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Midtown High
21 School.
S. R. 503
