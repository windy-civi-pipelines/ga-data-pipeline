25 LC 129 0307
Senate Resolution 354
44th, Jackson of the 41st and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Ms. Catherine Ann Smith; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Ms. Catherine Ann Smith on February 25, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Ms. Catherine Ann Smith was born on May 29, 1957, in Columbus, Ohio, a
5 beloved daughter of Dr. Warren L. Smith and Ann Schwartz Smith; and
6 WHEREAS, she learned the art of political organizing from her mother, who turned their
7 basement into a call center for the Democratic Party; and
8 WHEREAS, Ms. Catherine Ann Smith shared her knowledge with thousands of budding
9 activists and organizers in both Michigan and Georgia, dedicating countless hours, energy,
10 and resources to improving her community through the Democratic Party and progressive
11 causes; and
12 WHEREAS, she was a person of intelligence, passion, caring spirit, generosity, and was full
13 of laughter and wit; by the example she made of her life, she made the world a better place
14 in which to live; and
S. R. 354
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0307
15 WHEREAS, Ms. Catherine Ann Smith will long be remembered for her love of her chosen
16 family and friends, and will be missed by all who had the great fortune of knowing her; and
17 WHEREAS, Ms. Catherine Ann Smith embodied the spirit of service and was always willing
18 to find meaning in something greater than herself, making it is abundantly fitting and proper
19 that this remarkable and distinguished American is recognized upon a life well lived.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
21 join in honoring the life and memory of Ms. Catherine Ann Smith and express their deepest
22 and most sincere regret at her passing.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the family of Ms.
25 Catherine Ann Smith.
