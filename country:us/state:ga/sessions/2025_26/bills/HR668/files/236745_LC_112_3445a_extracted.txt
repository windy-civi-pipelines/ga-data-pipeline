A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Georgia Haitian-American Chamber of Commerce Inc.;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Haitian-American Chamber of Commerce Inc. (GAHCCI) has
4 demonstrated outstanding leadership and commitment to the economic and cultural
5 advancement of the Haitian-American community in Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, GAHCCI has launched the PPRNE (Pinnacle Project: Reaching Nonprofit
7 Excellence) program, an initiative dedicated to elevating nonprofit organizations by
8 providing them with the tools and guidance needed to thrive and expand their impact; and
9 WHEREAS, GAHCCI has established Eritaj, a visionary program designed to train,
10 recognize, and elevate businesses that are building lasting legacies for themselves and the
11 community while celebrating those who embody excellence, resilience, and community
12 impact; and
13 WHEREAS, GAHCCI will hold its 10th annual Pwochen Lide/Eritaj Gala to honor and
14 award businesses and organizations that embody excellence, resilience, and community
