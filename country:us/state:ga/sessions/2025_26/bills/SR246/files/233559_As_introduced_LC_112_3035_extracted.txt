25 LC 112 3035
Senate Resolution 246
50th, Beach of the 21st and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending President Donald J. Trump; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, President Donald J. Trump was inaugurated January 20, 2025, following the
3 2024 Presidential Election, winning the electoral and popular vote, as the 47th President of
4 the United States, the second candidate in American history to be elected to the office in two
5 non-consecutive terms, gaining the confidence of millions of American voters; and
6 WHEREAS, President Trump has appointed and nominated a record number of women,
7 minorities, and individuals from a wide range of backgrounds to key leadership positions,
8 including appointing the first female White House Chief of Staff and youngest White House
9 Press Secretary in history; and
10 WHEREAS, President Trump has made several key commitments in line with his America
11 First agenda, to include sealing the United States border, keeping our communities safe,
12 ending high inflation, extending American worker tax cuts, and making America the leading
13 energy producer in the world; and
14 WHEREAS, within hours of taking the oath of office, President Trump began delivering on
15 his promises to the American people with a series of memorandums and executive orders,

16 including but not limited to enhancing border security and targeting dangerous cartels as
17 terrorist organizations, mandating that United States foreign policy be aligned with core
18 American interests, addressing the increased cost-of-living, and ending the weaponization
19 of the federal government towards United States citizens; and
20 WHEREAS, in his first term as President, he led the United States into energy independence
21 for the first time in decades with initiatives like opening federal lands to oil and gas
22 exploration, approving the Keystone XL and Dakota Access pipelines, and ending undue
23 reliance on foreign energy sources; and
24 WHEREAS, President Trump appointed over 200 federal judges, including 54 federal
25 appellate judges and three Supreme Court justices—Neil Gorsuch, Brett Kavanaugh, and
26 Amy Coney Barrett—whose decisions will shape the legal landscape for generations; today,
27 more than a quarter of active federal court judges are Trump appointees; and
28 WHEREAS, he adopted a "peace through strength" approach to foreign policy to restore
29 international American leadership, hold our adversaries accountable at the United Nations,
30 and bring peace to the Middle East through the Abraham Accords; and
31 WHEREAS, the President has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to restoring
32 American sovereignty and ending the abuse of our immigration system through the
33 implementation of the "Remain in Mexico" policy, the construction over 450 miles of new
34 border wall, and cracking down on illegal sanctuary cities.
35 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
36 commend the 45th and 47th President of the United States Donald J. Trump for his
