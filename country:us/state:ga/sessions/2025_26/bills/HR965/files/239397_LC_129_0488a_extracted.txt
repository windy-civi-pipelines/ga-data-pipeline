25 LC 129 0488
House Resolution 965
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the 2025 Awards of Honor recipient, Marquita Levette
2 Wright-Davis; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Marquita Levette Wright-Davis is the owner and founder of 4D Computer
4 Repairs, a business that provides free repair services to nonprofits and churches; and
5 WHEREAS, Marquita is Founder and Director of Testimony Board Ministries, and
6 Co-Founder of AIM at Main Mentorship Program; and
7 WHEREAS, Marquita received a grant from the South Rome Alliance to strengthen the
8 community through free tutoring services, which led to the establishment of the South Rome
9 Free Tutoring Program, offering free tutoring for Rome City School students in kindergarten
10 through 12th grade; and
11 WHEREAS, Marquita was recognized in 2024 by the Floyd County Board of
12 Commissioners during Black History Month for her significant contributions to the
13 community; and
H. R. 965
- 1 -

14 WHEREAS, Marquita collaborates with organizations including One Community United and
15 the Rome NAACP to promote diversity, equity, and inclusion in Rome; and
16 WHEREAS, Marquita is a passionate community leader, activist, educator, and advocate
17 committed to education, mentorship, and social justice; and
18 WHEREAS, Marquita further serves her community through her volunteerism with South
19 Rome Community Association, Rome NAACP, A Voice for Votes, One Community United,
20 REACH Ministries/Bloodline Believers, Healing Scars with Scarves, iFocus Mentoring
21 Program, and Keion Harper Foundation.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize Marquita Levette Wriight-Davis for her outstanding
24 dedication to the community, commend her as a recipient of the 2025 Heart of the
25 Community Awards Honor Recipient, and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued
26 success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Marquita Levette Wright-Davis.
