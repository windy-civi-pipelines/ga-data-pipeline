3 WHEREAS, Evetta B. Washington has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to service,

5 WHEREAS, in recognition of her outstanding contributions to the Rockdale community,
6 Evetta B. Washington has been selected as a recipient of the Women on Purpose award

15 Committee; and
