25 LC 112 3126
House Resolution 406
of the 111th, and Williams of the 148th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 27, 2025, as Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council Day at the state
2 capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council (GMCCC), established in 2006,
4 is an organization composed of dedicated municipal court clerks from across the State of
5 Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, the mission of the Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council is to act as an
7 administrative support mechanism by assisting municipal court clerks throughout the State
8 of Georgia in their endeavor to ensure the administrative efficiency of the court, protect the
9 court's ethical integrity, and help maintain public confidence in the court's fairness in
10 dispensing justice impartially; and
11 WHEREAS, the Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council focuses on the development and
12 training of municipal court clerks, ensuring the continuous improvement of municipal courts
13 and the justice system, thereby supporting the judiciary and court administration to better
14 serve the public and promote the efficient administration of justice; and
H. R. 406
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3126
15 WHEREAS, municipal court clerks are integral to preserving the judiciary, court
16 administration, and public safety as well as upholding the quality of life in local
17 municipalities across the State of Georgia; and
18 WHEREAS, the members of the Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council are court
19 professionals who embody a profound commitment to the ethical and effective management
20 of the Georgia Judicial System; and
21 WHEREAS, municipal court clerks are at the forefront of direct interactions with the public,
22 with more people engaging with these clerks than with all other courts combined, making
23 them vital to shaping the public's impression of the Georgia Judicial System; and
24 WHEREAS, municipal court clerks conduct their duties with unwavering neutrality and
25 impartiality, ensuring equal service to all and steadfastly promoting public confidence in the
26 integrity and fairness of the judiciary; and
27 WHEREAS, it is both fitting and proper to recognize the efforts, accomplishments, and
28 significant role of the Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council in preserving the public trust
29 and fostering confidence, integrity, and impartiality within the judiciary.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body commend the Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council for its
32 invaluable service and dedication to advancing the administration of justice, its unwavering
33 commitment to court administration and public safety, and its contributions to the betterment
34 of the Georgia Judicial System.
H. R. 406
- 2 -
25 LC 112 3126
35 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of this body recognize February 27, 2025,
36 as Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council Day at the state capitol in honor of its service as
37 an essential partner in upholding the principles of fairness, justice, and integrity that underpin
38 the judicial system of the State of Georgia.
39 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
40 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
41 Georgia Municipal Court Clerks Council.
