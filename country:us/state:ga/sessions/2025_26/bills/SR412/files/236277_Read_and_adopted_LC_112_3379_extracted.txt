25 LC 112 3379
Senate Resolution 412
55th, Jones of the 10th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Atlanta Court Appointed Special Advocates on the occasion
2 of its 30th anniversary; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Atlanta Court Appointed Special Advocates (CASA) has faithfully served the
4 children and families of Atlanta for 30 years, advocating for the best interests of children
5 who have experienced abuse or neglect; and
6 WHEREAS, since its founding in 1995, Atlanta CASA has worked tirelessly to ensure that
7 every child in foster care has a safe, permanent, and loving home, leveraging the dedication
8 of trained volunteer advocates to provide critical support and guidance; and
9 WHEREAS, Atlanta CASA has positively impacted the lives of thousands of children,
10 empowering them to thrive through advocacy, mentorship, and compassionate service; and
11 WHEREAS, the organization has strengthened communities by collaborating with local
12 agencies, courts, and stakeholders to enhance child welfare outcomes, promoting a future
13 where every child has a voice and the opportunity to succeed; and
S. R. 412
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3379
14 WHEREAS, the volunteers, staff, and supporters of Atlanta CASA have demonstrated
15 unwavering commitment to the well-being of vulnerable youth, exemplifying the spirit of
16 service and advocacy that defines the great State of Georgia; and
17 WHEREAS, on this milestone anniversary, it is fitting and proper to honor Atlanta CASA
18 for its legacy of impact and its continued dedication to the children and families of Atlanta.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 recognize and commend Atlanta Court Appointed Special Advocates on the occasion of its
21 30th anniversary, express profound gratitude for its invaluable service, and extend best
22 wishes for continued success in its mission to advocate for Atlanta's most vulnerable
23 children.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Atlanta Court
26 Appointed Special Advocates.
