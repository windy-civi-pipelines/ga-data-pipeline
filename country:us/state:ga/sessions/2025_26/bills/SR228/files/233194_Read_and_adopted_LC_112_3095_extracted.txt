Section 1:
25 LC 112 3095
Senate Resolution 228
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Joyce Lewis; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Joyce Lewis; and
4 WHEREAS, she gave inspiration to many through her high ideals, morals, and deep concern
5 for her fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding she demonstrated to
6 her family and friends were admired by others; and
7 WHEREAS, she was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
8 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example she made of her life,
9 she made this world a better place in which to live; and
10 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous woman, Joyce Lewis will long be remembered
11 for her love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who had the
12 great fortune of knowing her.
S. R. 228
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3095
13 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
14 join in honoring the life and memory of Joyce Lewis and express their deepest and most
15 sincere regret at her passing.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
17 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the family of
18 Joyce Lewis.
S. R. 228
