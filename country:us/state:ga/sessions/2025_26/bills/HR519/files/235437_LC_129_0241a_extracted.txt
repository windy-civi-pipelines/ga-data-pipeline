25 LC 129 0241
House Resolution 519
the 109th, Adeyina of the 110th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Kinsley White; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, fourth-grader Kinsley White was recently honored by the Georgia Art
3 Education Association with her inclusion in the 2025 Capitol Art Exhibit; and
4 WHEREAS, her artwork, titled "Super Kin," shows herself as a superhero saving children
5 with a variety of health challenges, serving as a representation of her dream to one day
6 become a pediatrician; and
7 WHEREAS, Kinsley's favorite subjects are math, science, social studies, and art, and she
8 participates in Girls on the Run and the Art Club; and
9 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
10 young artist be appropriately honored.
11 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
12 the members of this body recognize and commend the exemplary artistic and academic
13 abilities of Kinsley White and extend sincere best wishes for her continued success.
H. R. 519

14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
15 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
