Section 1:
25 LC 46 0944
By: Representatives Silcox of the 53rd, Hilton of the 48th, Jones of the 47th, Stephens of the
164th, and Reeves of the 99th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 3 of Chapter 3 of Title 50 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to other state symbols, so as to change the state floral emblem to the sweetbay
3 magnolia; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
5 SECTION 1.
6 Article 3 of Chapter 3 of Title 50 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to other
7 state symbols, is amended by revising Code Section 50-3-53, relating to state floral emblem,
8 as follows:
9 "50-3-53.
10 The Cherokee rose sweetbay magnolia (Magnolia virginiana) is adopted as the floral
11 emblem of the State of Georgia."
12 SECTION 2.
13 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 145
