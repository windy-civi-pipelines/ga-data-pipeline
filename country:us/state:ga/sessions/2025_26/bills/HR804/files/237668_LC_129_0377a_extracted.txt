25 LC 129 0377
House Resolution 804
By: Representative Jackson of the 128th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Cookie Wright is an award-winning radio personality, pioneering broadcaster,
3 and dedicated community advocate; and
4 WHEREAS, she is a trailblazer in the radio and music industry, known for her distinctive
5 voice and dynamic presence; and
6 WHEREAS, her remarkable career highlights include co-hosting the “Saturday Night
7 Throwback Party Show” on KISS 104.1 FM alongside Georgia Radio Hall of Famer Mitch
8 Faulkner and launching the groundbreaking “Soul Blues Live” on Classix 102.9 FM; and
9 WHEREAS, in 2019, she took a bold step by founding her own internet radio station,
10 broadcasting from Atlanta and London to over 50 countries; the station offers 24/7
11 programming and has become a global hub for Soul Blues, Old School, R&B, and more; and
12 WHEREAS, known as the “Maven of Soul Blues,” Cookie has broken new music for
13 countless artists, including Tucka, King George, and Karen Wolfe; and
H. R. 804

25 LC 129 0377
14 WHEREAS, she has received numerous accolades, including ATL’s Hottest Female DJ,
15 Media Radio Personality of the Year, Best Online Network, and induction into the National
16 Black Radio Hall of Fame; and
17 WHEREAS, Cookie's achievements extend far beyond her professional career, as evidenced
18 dramatically by her air-time profits donations, implementation of food programs for
19 neighborhoods in need, and her dedication to advocating for pressing social issues; and
20 WHEREAS, Cookie Wright has not only amplified Atlanta’s presence in the global music
21 scene but has also become a beacon of inspiration for aspiring artists and broadcasters; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
23 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body recognize and commend Cookie Wright for her extraordinary
26 contributions to Atlanta’s cultural, musical, and social landscape and extend sincere best
27 wishes for her continued health and happiness.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
30 Cookie Wright.
H. R. 804
