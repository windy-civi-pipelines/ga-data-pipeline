25 LC 112 3106
House Resolution 417
Cummings of the 39th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring and commending Anat Sultan-Dadon, Consul General of Israel to the Southeastern
2 United States, for her exemplary service in strengthening the bonds between the State of
3 Israel and the State of Georgia and for her steadfast friendship to the people of Georgia; and
4 for other purposes.
5 WHEREAS, Anat Sultan-Dadon has served with distinction as the Consul General of Israel
6 to the Southeastern United States, demonstrating exceptional leadership, diplomacy, and
7 commitment to fostering a strong and mutually beneficial relationship between Israel and
8 Georgia; and
9 WHEREAS, throughout her tenure, Consul General Sultan-Dadon has played a pivotal role
10 in advancing political, economic, cultural, and academic partnerships between the State of
11 Georgia and the State of Israel, further deepening the historical ties that unite our
12 communities; and
13 WHEREAS, she has been instrumental in promoting trade and investment opportunities,
14 strengthening technological collaboration, and supporting innovation between Georgia-based
15 companies and Israeli enterprises, significantly benefiting both economies; and
H. R. 417
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3106
16 WHEREAS, Consul General Sultan-Dadon has worked tirelessly to enhance educational and
17 cultural exchanges, fostering mutual understanding and appreciation between the people of
18 Georgia and the people of Israel; and
19 WHEREAS, her unwavering dedication to public service, her exemplary diplomatic skills,
20 and her steadfast commitment to shared democratic values have earned her the admiration
21 and respect of leaders, businesses, and citizens throughout the State of Georgia; and
22 WHEREAS, under her leadership, the relationship between Georgia and Israel has
23 flourished, ensuring a prosperous and enduring partnership; and
24 WHEREAS, she has represented the State of Israel with integrity, professionalism, and grace,
25 embodying the spirit of true friendship and international cooperation; and
26 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding service of this
27 distinguished diplomat be appropriately recognized and honored by this body.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body commend Anat Sultan-Dadon, Consul General of Israel to the
30 Southeastern United States, for her dedicated service and exceptional contributions to the
31 strengthening of ties between the State of Georgia and the State of Israel, extend their
32 heartfelt gratitude for her unwavering commitment to friendship and collaboration, and wish
33 her continued success in all her future endeavors.
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Anat
36 Sultan-Dadon.
H. R. 417
