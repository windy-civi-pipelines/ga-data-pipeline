25 LC 112 3190
Senate Resolution 289
28th, Anderson of the 43rd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 28, 2025, as Georgia Caregivers Day; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, created in 2015, National Caregivers Day serves to recognize caregivers across
3 the country whose compassionate care enables millions of Americans who are aging or who
4 have a disability to remain as independent as possible in their own homes; and
5 WHEREAS, professional caregivers, who number nearly 4.6 million in the United States,
6 and family caregivers, who are estimated at 44 million, are lifelines to millions of Americans
7 who need assistance with medical, personal, and end-of-life care; and
8 WHEREAS, caregiving has been proclaimed the ultimate form of love and respect, and as
9 a country, we should support and demonstrate our gratitude for caregivers and their role in
10 fulfilling an individual's desire to remain at home; and
11 WHEREAS, the home care industry is among the fastest growing health care industries in
12 the United States, and the Home Care Association Georgia Chapter continuously advocates
13 on behalf of our frontline caregivers; and
S. R. 289
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3190
14 WHEREAS, during the COVID-19 pandemic, caregivers stepped up to the challenge by not
15 only caring for their clients but also risking their own personal health and safety while doing
16 so; and
17 WHEREAS, home care workers allow families to stay together at home safely and with
18 dignity as they age.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 recognize February 28, 2025, as Georgia Caregivers Day and express their gratitude to
21 caregivers for their unwavering commitment to the care of their clients and families.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
23 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the public and the
24 press.
