25 LC 112 3307
House Resolution 537
Park of the 107th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dr. Jung Ha Kim and the Community Resource Corporation;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Dr. Jung Ha Kim has dedicated more than three decades to academia and
4 nonprofit leadership, specializing in community education, program development,
5 curriculum design, research, and advocacy; and
6 WHEREAS, she is the founder and president of the Community Resource Corporation
7 (CRC), a nonprofit organization committed to addressing racial and gender disparities in
8 immigrant and refugee communities; and
9 WHEREAS, the CRC has played a pivotal role in fostering community partnerships,
10 collaborating with organizations such as the Lucky Shoals Community Association to
11 develop dementia respite care programs and working with the Fielding School of Public
12 Health at UCLA on the BRAVE study; and
13 WHEREAS, the CRC has further led efforts to promote cultural heritage and representation
14 by supporting events such as the AAPI Cultural Heritage Festival in Suwanee and organizing
15 Korean cultural performances at Georgia State University; and
H. R. 537
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3307
16 WHEREAS, her extensive experience in nonprofit management includes serving as CEO and
17 President of the Center for Pan Asian Community Services Inc. (CPACS), where she
18 oversaw a budget expansion from $11 million to $14 million and contributed to over 20
19 published articles, interviews, and documentaries highlighting community projects; and
20 WHEREAS, Dr. Jung Ha Kim has long been an advocate for social justice and equity,
21 serving on national mentoring committees, securing grants to address intimate partner
22 violence in immigrant communities, and working to improve data collection on Asian
23 American health and well-being; and
24 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this distinguished leader be appropriately
25 honored during this special time.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body recognize and commend Dr. Jung Ha Kim for her invaluable
28 contributions to community engagement, research, and nonprofit leadership as well as extend
29 best wishes for continued success.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
32 Jung Ha Kim.
