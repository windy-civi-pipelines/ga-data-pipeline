25 LC 129 0455
House Resolution 900
91st, and Kendrick of the 95th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Andre Anthony Cross Jr.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Andre Anthony Cross Jr. is a graduating senior at Georgia Gwinnett College
3 whose journey is marked by resilience, transformation, and a deep commitment to growth;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, a first-generation American and the son of a Liberian immigrant, Andre has
6 consistently defied the odds to achieve academic and personal success; and
7 WHEREAS, beginning his undergraduate career as a student-athlete, Andre faced significant
8 challenges after a sports injury and academic struggles that threatened his future; and
9 WHEREAS, refusing to be defined by setbacks, he transferred to Georgia Gwinnett College
10 for a fresh start where he went on to make the Dean’s List, serve as a Student Ambassador,
11 and intern with the Georgia House of Representatives; and
12 WHEREAS, beyond his academic success, Andre is known for his leadership, humility, and
13 dedication to public service; and
H. R. 900
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0455
14 WHEREAS, Andre’s story is a testament to perseverance, second chances, and the power of
15 education to unlock potential; his character, achievements, and impact have left a lasting
16 impression on the GGC community, and he stands as a proud representative of the Class of
17 2025; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding achievements of this
19 exemplary Georgian are appropriately honored.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body recognize and commend Andre Anthony Cross Jr. and extend
22 sincere best wishes for his continued health, happiness, and success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Andre
25 Anthony Cross Jr.
