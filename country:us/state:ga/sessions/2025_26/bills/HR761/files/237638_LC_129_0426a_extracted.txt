25 LC 129 0426
House Resolution 761
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending and congratulating Fieldon Marcus Chapman; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Fieldon Marcus Chapman has attained the rank of Eagle Scout, the most
3 distinguished rank conferred by the Boy Scouts of America; and
4 WHEREAS, a candidate for the most exceptional award in scouting must complete a
5 rigorous program of training and demonstrate his proficiency in selected scouting techniques
6 and the attainment of the highest ideals of scouting; and
7 WHEREAS, only one scout out of 97 ever reaches this lofty level of achievement, and
8 Fieldon has ably evidenced his aptitude and competency in mastering the various special
9 skills taught through the Scouts BSA program; and
10 WHEREAS, an outstanding Georgian, Fieldon has truly exhibited a commitment to the
11 scouting principles of honesty and fairness and has consistently conducted himself in a
12 manner becoming of an Eagle Scout; and
13 WHEREAS, a member of Troop 705, he has served as Senior Patrol Leader, Patrol Leader,
14 Troop Guide, and Chaplain's Aide as well as earned 24 merit badges; and
H. R. 761
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, for his Eagle Scout Project, Fieldon installed a 40 foot flagpole at George
16 Walton Football Field, in Monroe, Georgia; and
17 WHEREAS, the perseverance and determination displayed by this exceptional young leader
18 set an example for all Georgians, and great success is surely in this young man's future.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body commend Fieldon Marcus Chapman for his diligence and
21 dedication in pursuing the highest honor awarded by the Boy Scouts of America and
22 congratulate him on attaining the prestigious rank of Eagle Scout.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
25 Fieldon Marcus Chapman.
