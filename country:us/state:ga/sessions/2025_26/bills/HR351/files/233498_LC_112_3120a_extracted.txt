25 LC 112 3120
House Resolution 351
of the 96th, and McClain of the 109th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Nancy Taiye Aragbaye; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Nancy Taiye Aragbaye has diligently and conscientiously dedicated over 15
3 years to education, leadership, and entrepreneurship, playing a vital role in advancing social
4 impact and community development; and
5 WHEREAS, she currently serves as a diaspora relations advisor to foreign and local entities,
6 fostering economic development by strengthening connections between Africa and the
7 diaspora; and
8 WHEREAS, she is the founder of Strategic Solutions Global, an EdTech Global Leadership
9 Institute that equips women across Africa and the diaspora with education, tools, and
10 resources to disrupt outdated systems and promote innovation; and
11 WHEREAS, she has further distinguished herself as the USA National Head for Africa's
12 Young Entrepreneurs, and she serves on the advisory boards of the Global Entrepreneurship
13 Festival and the Entrepreneurship Village, also known as Africa's Silicon Valley; and
H. R. 351
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3120
14 WHEREAS, Nancy Taiye Aragbaye continues to uplift and empower women-led
15 organizations through initiatives such as the LeadHERship Gala, which highlights women
16 making a meaningful impact in their communities; and
17 WHEREAS, in addition to her leadership and entrepreneurial work, she is a global speaker
18 committed to inspiring women, youth, and business leaders by promoting economic mobility,
19 stability, and transformation across Africa and the diaspora; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable individual be
21 appropriately honored during this special time.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Nancy Taiye Aragbaye for her many
24 outstanding accomplishments and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
27 Nancy Taiye Aragbaye.
