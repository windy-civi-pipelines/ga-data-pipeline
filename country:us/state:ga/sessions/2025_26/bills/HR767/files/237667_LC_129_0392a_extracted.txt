25 LC 129 0392
House Resolution 767
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Prakash Gupta, Rockdale Magnet School for Science and Technology's Class
2 of 2025 salutatorian; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, Prakash Gupta has shown by academic accomplishment that the pursuit and
6 attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of the students in this state; and
7 WHEREAS, this remarkable senior has earned distinction through his notable achievements
8 as a student at Rockdale Magnet School for Science and Technology (RMSST) where he is
9 a member of RMSST Ambassadors, SkillsUSA, Camera Club, Rockdale Magnet Math
10 Team, RMSST Peer Tutors, RCA Robotics, RMSST Student Council, and a RMSST
11 Summer STEAM camp counselor; and
12 WHEREAS, he has been selected as salutatorian by earning exemplary grades in the most
13 challenging academic courses while maintaining a substantial extracurricular schedule
14 throughout his academic career; and

15 WHEREAS, this achievement is the culmination of Prakash Gupta's hard work and
16 determination, and he deserves much credit for his energy and dedication to academic
17 excellence; and
18 WHEREAS, he is an outstanding representative of the young people of this state and it is
19 only fitting and proper that he be recognized for his many accomplishments.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body heartily commend Prakash Gupta for his spectacular scholastic
22 endeavors and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
24 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
