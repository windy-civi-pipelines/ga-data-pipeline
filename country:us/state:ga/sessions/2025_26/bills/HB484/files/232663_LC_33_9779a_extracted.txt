Section 1:
25 LC 33 9779
By: Representatives Yearta of the 152nd, Efstration of the 104th, Scoggins of the 14th,
Anderson of the 10th, and Leverett of the 123rd
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Title 28 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to the General
2 Assembly, so as to provide for the digitization and electronic publication of the Georgia
3 Laws; to provide for assistance and oversight by the Georgia Code Revision Commission;
4 to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Title 28 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to the General Assembly, is
8 amended in Code Section 28-4-3, relating to the creation, qualifications, and powers and
9 duties of the Office of Legislative Counsel, by revising subsection (e) as follows:
10 "(e) The legislative counsel shall provide for the compiling, indexing, editing, and
11 publication of the Georgia Laws containing the Acts and resolutions of the General
12 Assembly and other appropriate materials. Except as otherwise authorized in Code Section
13 50-18-2, such Acts and resolutions shall be published electronically or in hardbound
14 volumes in a manner which is suitable for retention as permanent records. In the case of
15 any special session of the General Assembly, however, the separate publication and
16 distribution of the Acts and resolutions enacted at that special session may be omitted, and
H. B. 484
- 1 -
25 LC 33 9779
17 in such case the Acts and resolutions enacted at the special session shall be published
18 electronically or in print and distributed together with those enacted at the subsequent
19 regular session. Distribution of the Georgia Laws shall be carried out by the Secretary of
20 State as provided for in Code Section 45-13-22; and the Secretary of State shall notify the
21 legislative counsel of the numbers of volumes required to carry out such distribution."
22 SECTION 2.
23 Said title is further amended in Code Section 28-9-3, relating to and powers and duties of the
24 Code Revision Commission, by adding a new subsection to read as follows:
25 "(17.1) To provide assistance and oversight for the digitization and electronic publication
26 of the Georgia Laws so as to create a permanent digital repository of the session laws of
27 Georgia and to provide single-point public access to the local and general Acts and the
28 resolutions of the General Assembly;"
29 SECTION 3.
30 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
