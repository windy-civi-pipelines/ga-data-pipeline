Section 1:
25 LC 44 3166S
following substitute to HB 443:
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 27-2-23 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 license, permit, tag, and stamp fees, so as to provide for fees for reef and migratory fish
3 endorsements; to provide for recreational and commercial fishing; to provide for retention
4 of certain fees; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other
5 purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 Code Section 27-2-23 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to license, permit,
9 tag, and stamp fees, is amended by adding new subparagraphs to paragraph (4), revising
10 paragraph (11), and adding a new paragraph to read as follows:
11 "(K) Resident reef/migratory fish Annual 10.00
12 endorsement
13 (L) Nonresident reef/migratory fish Annual 20.00"
14 endorsement
- 1 -
25 LC 44 3166S
15 "(11) For wildlife that may be legally taken by recreational or commercial fishing gear
16 as authorized in this title or by board rule, the board may designate which species of
17 wildlife or species grouping of wildlife require a recreational or commercial fishing
18 species endorsement for a fee shown in subparagraphs (K) and (L) of paragraph (4) or
19 subparagraphs (C) and (D) of paragraph (6) of this Code section in addition to the
20 required recreational or commercial fishing license. Each species or group of species so
21 designated shall require a separate recreational or commercial fishing species
22 endorsement."
23 "(12.1) Any fees collected pursuant to subparagraphs (K) and (L) of paragraph (4) of this
24 Code section shall be retained by the department pursuant to Code Section 45-12-92.1."
25 SECTION 2.
26 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
