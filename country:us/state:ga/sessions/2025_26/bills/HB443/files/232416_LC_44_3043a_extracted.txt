Section 1:
25 LC 44 3043
By: Representatives Petrea of the 166th, Rhodes of the 124th, DeLoach of the 167th, Stephens
of the 164th, Franklin of the 160th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 27-2-23 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 license, permit, tag, and stamp fees, so as to provide for fees for reef and migratory fish
3 endorsements; to provide for recreational and commercial fishing; to provide for related
4 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Code Section 27-2-23 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to license, permit,
8 tag, and stamp fees, is amended by adding new subparagraphs to paragraph (4) and revising
9 paragraph (11) to read as follows:
10 "(K) Resident reef/migratory fish endorsement Annual 10.00
11 (L) Nonresident reef/migratory fish endorsement Annual 20.00"
12 "(11) For wildlife that may be legally taken by recreational or commercial fishing gear
13 as authorized in this title or by board rule, the board may designate which species of
14 wildlife or species grouping of wildlife require a recreational or commercial fishing
15 species endorsement for a fee shown in subparagraphs (K) and (L) of paragraph (4) or
16 subparagraphs (C) and (D) of paragraph (6) of this Code section in addition to the
H. B. 443
- 1 -
25 LC 44 3043
17 required recreational or commercial fishing license. Each species or group of species so
18 designated shall require a separate recreational or commercial fishing species
19 endorsement."
20 SECTION 2.
21 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
