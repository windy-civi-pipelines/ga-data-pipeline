3 WHEREAS, Mr. Johnny Floyd has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for the

6 WHEREAS, Mr. Floyd completed Forest Ranger School at the University of Florida, had a

17 WHEREAS, during his tenure with the State Transportation Board, Mr. Floyd has focused

29 is dedicated as the Honorable Johnny Floyd Interchange.

31 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Honorable Johnny Floyd
32 Interchange.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
34 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Mr.
35 Johnny Floyd Interchange and the Department of Transportation.
