25 LC 129 0387
House Resolution 772
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Christian Bridges, Heritage High School's Class of 2025 valedictorian; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, Christian Bridges has shown by academic accomplishment that the pursuit and
6 attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of the students in this state; and
7 WHEREAS, this remarkable senior has earned distinction through his notable achievements
8 as a student at Heritage High School where he is a member of the orchestra, the marching
9 band, and the beta club, as well as has served as a Rockdale County Pubic Schools liaison
10 and host of the Teen Leadership Summit; and
11 WHEREAS, he has been selected as valedictorian by earning exemplary grades in the most
12 challenging academic courses while maintaining a substantial extracurricular schedule
13 throughout his academic career; and
H. R. 772

14 WHEREAS, this achievement is the culmination of Christian Bridges' hard work and
15 determination, and he deserves much credit for his energy and dedication to academic
16 excellence; and
17 WHEREAS, he is an outstanding representative of the young people of this state and it is
18 only fitting and proper that he be recognized for his many accomplishments.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body heartily commend Christian Bridges for his spectacular scholastic
21 endeavors and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
24 Christian Bridges.
