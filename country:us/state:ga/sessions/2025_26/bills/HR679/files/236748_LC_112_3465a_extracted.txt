25 LC 112 3465
House Resolution 679
By: Representative Jones of the 47th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the 2025 Legislative Session marks Wesley Nichols' 25th year as a dedicated
3 member of the Capitol Press Corps, where he has played a vital role in documenting
4 Georgia's legislative process; and
5 WHEREAS, he serves as the lead capitol photographer for Georgia Public Broadcasting's
6 Lawmakers, helping to provide comprehensive coverage of legislative proceedings and
7 informing countless viewers about the formation of laws in Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, over the past two and a half decades, Wesley Nichols has conducted exclusive
9 interviews, covered press conferences, captured historic moments, and built lasting
10 relationships with legislators and members of the state capitol community; and
11 WHEREAS, his commitment to journalistic excellence and visual storytelling has made a
12 profound impact on the public's understanding of state government, ensuring transparency
13 and accessibility in legislative reporting; and
H. R. 679
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3465
14 WHEREAS, he is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
15 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
16 WHEREAS, Wesley Nichols has served with honor and distinction as part of the Capitol
17 Press Corps, and his vision and unyielding commitment to excellence in reporting have set
18 the standard for exemplary service; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
20 remarkable and distinguished individual be appropriately honored.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body recognize and commend Wesley Nichols for his 25 years of
23 effective, efficient, and dedicated service to the Capitol Press Corps and extend the most
24 sincere best wishes for continued health and happiness.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
27 Wesley Nichols.
