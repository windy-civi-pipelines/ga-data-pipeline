25 LC 112 2807
House Resolution 148
Neal of the 79th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the National African American Gun Association upon its 10th anniversary;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the National African American Gun Association was founded in 2015 by Philip
4 Smith, who established its first chapter, the Bass Reeves Gun Club, in metro Atlanta; and
5 WHEREAS, this exemplary nonprofit organization will celebrate its 10th anniversary on
6 February 28, 2025, honoring a decade of dedicated advocacy, education, and community
7 engagement in defense of the rights and responsibilities of African American firearm owners;
8 and
9 WHEREAS, since its founding, the National African American Gun Association has grown
10 to over 120 chapters in more than 40 states, welcoming individuals from diverse religious,
11 political, social, cultural, and racial backgrounds while serving as a hub for African
12 American firearm owners, organizations, and gun clubs; and
13 WHEREAS, the organization advocates for the Second Amendment, educates on the
14 historical significance of firearm ownership within the African American community, and
H. R. 148
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2807
15 provides youth education, safety training, best practices, social advocacy, and cultural
16 inspiration; and
17 WHEREAS, the National African American Gun Association further advocates for the legal
18 rights of African Americans to protect their homes, families, and property from all forms of
19 crime or domestic terrorism via firearm ownership; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable organization be
21 appropriately honored during this special time.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body congratulate the National African American Gun Association upon
24 the grand occasion of its 10th anniversary as well as extend best wishes for continued
25 success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
28 National African American Gun Association.
