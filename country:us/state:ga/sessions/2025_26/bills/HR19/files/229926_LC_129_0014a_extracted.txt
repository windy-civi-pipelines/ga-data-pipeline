25 LC 129 0014
House Resolution 19
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the City of Cartersville upon its 175th anniversary; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, founded in 1850 and named for Georgia entrepreneur Colonel Farish Carter,
3 Cartersville, Georgia, has grown from a small railroad town into a vibrant community with
4 a rich history and bright future; and
5 WHEREAS, located 45 miles northwest of Atlanta, Cartersville perfectly blends small town
6 charm with the advantages of city access; and
7 WHEREAS, know as the "Museum City," Cartersville boasts world-class institutions like
8 the Booth Western Art Museum, the Tellus Science Museum, and the Savoy Automobile
9 Museum, showcasing art, science, and history; and
10 WHEREAS, Cartersville hosts a robust industrial base and full-service city utilities, which
11 have long provided local jobs and economic stability, and the city's public utilities have
12 served as a backbone for both businesses and residents; and
H. R. 19
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0014
13 WHEREAS, the Cartersville City School System provides excellent education and
14 opportunities for local students, playing an essential role in shaping the future of the
15 community; and
16 WHEREAS, Cartersville's beautiful parks and outdoor spaces, including Dellinger Park, Pine
17 Mountain, the Appalachian foothills, and the Etowah River, provide residents and visitors
18 with opportunities to enjoy the beauty of nature and outdoor recreation; and
19 WHEREAS, the city's revitalized downtown area serves as the community's hub, featuring
20 locally owned shops, restaurants, and regular events that bring people together; and
21 WHEREAS, as Cartersville celebrates its 175th anniversary, we honor the city's rich past and
22 look forward to a future filled with growth, unity, and pride.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body congratulate the City of Cartersville upon the grand occasion of
25 its 175th anniversary and extend best wishes to each of its citizens for future health,
26 happiness, and prosperity.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
29 public and the press.
H. R. 19
