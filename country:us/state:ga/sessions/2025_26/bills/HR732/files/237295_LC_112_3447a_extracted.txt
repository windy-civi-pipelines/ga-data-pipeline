25 LC 112 3447
House Resolution 732
Burchett of the 176th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Woodbine Public Library Inc.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Woodbine Public Library Inc. was established as a 501(c)(3) non-profit public
3 charity on November 16, 2016, following its incorporation in the State of Georgia; and
4 WHEREAS, the library originated as the Woodbine Library Initiative in January 2016 with
5 the support of the Woodbine Woman's Club, uniting the community in the pursuit of creating
6 a lending library for Woodbine and North Camden County; and
7 WHEREAS, through the dedication of its staff and the Woodbine Woman's Club, the
8 Woodbine Public Library Inc. has since grown into a thriving institution that fosters literacy,
9 education, and civic engagement for residents of all ages; and
10 WHEREAS, the Woodbine Public Library Inc. has played a vital role in promoting both
11 child and adult literacy in Camden County, providing essential resources, programs, and
12 services that empower individuals to expand their knowledge and enhance their
13 opportunities; and
H. R. 732
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3447
14 WHEREAS, libraries serve as centers of public education, knowledge, and community
15 building, offering access to literature, technology, and learning opportunities that enrich lives
16 and strengthen society.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
18 the members of this body commend the Woodbine Public Library Inc., its staff, and the
19 Woodbine Woman's Club for their dedication to literacy and community enrichment as well
20 as extend best wishes for many more years of success.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
22 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
23 Woodbine Public Library Inc.
