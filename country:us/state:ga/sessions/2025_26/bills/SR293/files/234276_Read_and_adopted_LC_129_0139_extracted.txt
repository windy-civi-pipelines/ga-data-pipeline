25 LC 129 0139
Senate Resolution 293
4th, Sims of the 12th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Pierce County girls golf team for winning the GHSA Girls Golf AA State
2 Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Pierce County girls golf team has demonstrated exceptional skill,
4 dedication, and teamwork in securing the state championship title; and
5 WHEREAS, through perseverance, determination, and an unwavering commitment to
6 excellence, the members of this outstanding team have earned a place in Georgia sports
7 history; and
8 WHEREAS, the exemplary performance of the team is a result of the hard work and
9 guidance of Head Coach Stuart Stipe and Assistant Coach Kim Spivey as well as the
10 dedication of team members Mackenzie Connell, Mollie Kate Dixon, Chloe Lynn, Belle
11 Sinclair, and Lily Spivey; and
12 WHEREAS, Mackenzie Connell and Lily Spivey were named to the GHSA AA All-State
13 team; and
14 WHEREAS, Coach Stipe was voted GHSA/NFHS Girls Golf Coach of the Year; and
S. R. 293
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0139
15 WHEREAS, this remarkable accomplishment is a source of great pride for the athletes, their
16 families, their school, and the entire Pierce County community; and
17 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding achievements of this
18 extraordinary team be appropriately recognized.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 congratulate the Pierce County girls golf team on winning the state championship for the
21 third consecutive year and commend them for their hard work, perseverance, and
22 commitment to excellence.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Pierce County
25 girls golf team.
