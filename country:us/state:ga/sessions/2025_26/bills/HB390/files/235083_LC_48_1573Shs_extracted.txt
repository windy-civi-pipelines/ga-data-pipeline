Section 1:
25 LC 48 1573S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 17-6-12 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 unsecured judicial release, requirement, effect of failure of person charged to appear for trial,
3 and consideration of criminal record, so as to provide for the crime of interference with a first
4 responder; to revise a definition; to provide for a short title; to provide for related matters;
5 to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "HALO Act."
9 SECTION 2.
10 Code Section 17-6-12 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to unsecured
11 judicial release, requirement, effect of failure of person charged to appear for trial, and
12 consideration of criminal record, is amended by revising subsection (a) by striking "or" at
13 the end of subparagraph (DDD), by replacing the period with a semicolon at the end of
14 subparagraph (EEE), and by adding new subparagraphs to read as follows:
H. B. 390 (SUB)
- 1 -
25 LC 48 1573S
15 "(FFF) A felony violation of obstructing or hindering law enforcement officers, as
16 described in Code Section 16-10-24;
17 (GGG) A felony violation of obstructing or hindering firefighters, as described in Code
18 Section 16-10-24.1; or
19 (HHH) A felony violation of obstructing or hindering emergency medical technicians
20 or emergency medical professionals, as described in Code Section 16-10-24.2."
21 SECTION 3.
22 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 390 (SUB)
