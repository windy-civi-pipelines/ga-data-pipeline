25 LC 129 0243
House Resolution 469
of the 110th, and Beckles of the 96th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending LaShonia Turner; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, LaShonia Turner, owner of Posh a Mireille Salon and co-owner of
3 Scrum-did-dly-ump-tious, has long been recognized by her peers and fellow citizens as a
4 dedicated leader and entrepreneur; and
5 WHEREAS, after overcoming great adversity, LaShonia opened Posh a Mireille Salon in
6 2016, in Dacula, Georgia, which grew in size, clientele, and influence over the next eight
7 years and became Gwinnett's go-to hair care destination; and
8 WHEREAS, the salon was named Best of Gwinnett from 2017 to 2021, a testament to its
9 commitment to providing exceptional customer service and its strong presence in the
10 community; and
11 WHEREAS, LaShonia further demonstrated her exceptional entrepreneurial leadership in
12 2023 with the opening of Scrum-did-dly-ump-tious, an upscale brunch restaurant located in
13 the heart of downtown Lawrenceville; and
H. R. 469
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0243
14 WHEREAS, despite her recent health challenges, LaShonia remains committed to her
15 community through her love of God, philanthropy, and her passion for fostering unity among
16 people; and
17 WHEREAS, as the founder of Posh Cares, she has held Back to School drives to help
18 provide students and teachers in need with school supplies, including Chromebooks; and
19 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
20 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
22 remarkable and distinguished individual be appropriately honored.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body recognize and commend LaShonia Turner for her many exemplary
25 career accomplishments as well as extend best wishes for future health, happiness, and
26 success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 LaShonia Turner.
