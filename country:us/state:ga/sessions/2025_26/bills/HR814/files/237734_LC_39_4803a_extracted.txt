25 LC 39 4803
House Resolution 814
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing the Honorable Susan Holmes and dedicating a road in her honor; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Honorable Susan Holmes has long been recognized by the citizens of this
4 state for the vital role that she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment
5 to the welfare of the citizens of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, a seventh generation citizen of Jasper County, Georgia, Representative Holmes
7 was raised on a cotton farm in the community of Farrar in northern Jasper County, graduated
8 from Monticello High School, and earned her bachelor's degree from the University of
9 Georgia; and
10 WHEREAS, she has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of her time,
11 talent, and energy to the citizens of Butts, Jones, and Monroe counties since 2010 as the State
12 Representative for House District 129; and
13 WHEREAS, Representative Holmes serves as vice chairman of the Agriculture and
14 Consumer Affairs and Special Rules committees, as secretary of the Energy, Utilities, and
H. R. 814
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4803
15 Telecommunications and Legislative and Congressional Reapportionment committees, and
16 as a member of the Economic Development and Tourism Committee; and
17 WHEREAS, prior to her service in the Georgia House of Representatives, she served as
18 mayor of the City of Monticello for 12 years, serving as the first female mayor for the city;
19 and
20 WHEREAS, Representative Holmes has been recognized with numerous honors and awards,
21 including Monticello-Jasper County Citizen of the Year by the Chamber of Commerce, 100
22 Most Influential Georgians by Georgia Trend Magazine, Northeast Georgia's Most
23 Outstanding Citizen, and the Georgia Downtown Association's Outstanding Leadership
24 Award; and
25 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
26 Georgian be appropriately recognized by dedicating a road in her honor.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
28 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of State Route 11 from Maddox Street to West
29 Greene Street in Jasper County is dedicated as the Honorable Susan Holmes Highway.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
31 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Honorable Susan Holmes
32 Highway.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
34 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
35 Honorable Susan Holmes and to the Department of Transportation.
H. R. 814
