25 LC 39 4719
Senate Resolution 371
Rhett of the 33rd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life of Investigator Eric Anthony Minix and dedicating a road in his memory;
2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Investigator Eric Anthony Minix was born in Covington, Georgia, on June 8,
4 1992, a beloved son of Mark Minix and Angela Mitchell and his life was tragically cut short
5 in the line of duty on January 4, 2024; and
6 WHEREAS, Investigator Minix was highly regarded by the citizens of his community and
7 state and by local government officials as a person of unquestioned integrity and dedication
8 to the sound principles of law enforcement; and
9 WHEREAS, he served with honor and distinction as a dedicated investigator and K9 handler
10 for the Coweta County Sheriff's Department where he exhibited extraordinary devotion to
11 public service, outstanding loyalty, fine leadership, and meticulous attention to detail in all
12 of his duties; and
13 WHEREAS, he will long be remembered for his inspiring commitment to the welfare of
14 others, which stands as a shining example of the positive effect law enforcement
15 professionals have on the lives and well-being of others; and
S. R. 371
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4719
16 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable and distinguished
17 Georgian be recognized appropriately by dedicating a road in his memory.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
19 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the portion of State Route 16 from State Route 14 to State
20 Route 54 in Coweta County is dedicated as the Investigator Eric Anthony Minix Highway.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
22 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Investigator Eric Anthony
23 Minix Highway.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the family of
26 Investigator Eric Anthony Minix and to the Department of Transportation.
S. R. 371
