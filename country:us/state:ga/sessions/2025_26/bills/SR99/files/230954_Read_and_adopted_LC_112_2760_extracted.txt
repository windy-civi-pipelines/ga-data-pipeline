25 LC 112 2760
Senate Resolution 99
22nd, Harrell of the 40th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Mr. Sharoko Farley; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Mr. Sharoko Farley has been selected as the state recipient of the Technical
3 College System of Georgia's 2024 Rick Perkins Award for Excellence in Technical
4 Instruction; and
5 WHEREAS, with this award, Mr. Farley has earned the distinction as the Technical College
6 System of Georgia's Instructor of the Year; and
7 WHEREAS, Mr. Farley, an instructor for the commercial truck driving program at Central
8 Georgia Technical College, has exemplified excellence in instructional knowledge and
9 practices; and
10 WHEREAS, in recognition of such, Mr. Farley was selected from an elite group of Georgia
11 technical college instructors who have each demonstrated innovative teaching practices and
12 exceptional leadership skills at their respective technical colleges; and
S. R. 99
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2760
13 WHEREAS, through his dedication and professionalism, Mr. Farley has brought honor to
14 himself, his family, Central Georgia Technical College, and the entire Technical College
15 System of Georgia.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
17 congratulate and commend Mr. Sharoko Farley for his outstanding achievements and his
18 efforts to promote technical education in Georgia as well as extend best wishes for continued
19 success.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mr. Sharoko
22 Farley.
