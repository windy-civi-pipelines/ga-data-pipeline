25 LC 61 0058
House Resolution 272
the 142nd, Campbell of the 35th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Encouraging the United States Congress to adopt legislation requiring the United States
2 Department of Agriculture to modify eligibility requirements for the Supplemental Nutrition
3 Assistance Program (SNAP) for students enrolled in institutions of higher education; and for
4 other purposes.
5 WHEREAS, under existing federal regulations, students enrolled in institutions of higher
6 education are ineligible for SNAP benefits unless they qualify for certain exemptions; and
7 WHEREAS, one such exemption is that a student must be employed for at least 20 hours per
8 week and paid for such employment; and
9 WHEREAS, many students are often involved in unpaid work and other activities that are
10 similar to employment; and
11 WHEREAS, students engaged in such unpaid work and other activities are meaningfully
12 contributing to their campus communities and should have the same eligibility for SNAP
13 benefits as students engaged in paid employment.
H. R. 272
- 1 -
25 LC 61 0058
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
15 the members of this body encourage the United States Congress to adopt legislation requiring
16 the United States Department of Agriculture to modify SNAP eligibility criteria for students
17 so that the following would count toward the employment exemption, regardless of whether
18 a student was paid:
19 (1) Work done as part of a research assistantship;
20 (2) Work done by teaching assistants;
21 (3) Work with student organizations, including work done by officers of such
22 organizations;
23 (4) Volunteer and community service hours; and
24 (5) Work done by student ambassadors.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 United States Congress.
H. R. 272
