1 Amend HB 296 (LC 39 4667S) by replacing line 8 with the following:
2 lifetime sportsman's license; to amend Code Section 33-3-28 of the Official Code of Georgia
3 Annotated, relating to request by claimant for information as to name of insurer, name of
4 each insured, and limits of coverage, so as to revise disclosure requirements; to amend
5 Article 2 of Chapter 5 of Title 40 of the Official Code
6 By inserting between lines 68 and 69 the following:
7 Code Section 33-3-28 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to request by
8 claimant for information as to name of insurer, name of each insured, and limits of coverage,
9 is amended by revising paragraph (1) of subsection (a) as follows:
10 "(a)(1) Every insurer providing liability or casualty insurance coverage in this state and
11 which is or may be liable to pay all or a part of any claim shall provide, within 60 days
12 of receiving a written request from the claimant, a statement, under oath, of a corporate
13 officer or the insurer's claims manager stating with regard to each known policy of
14 insurance issued by it, including excess or umbrella insurance, the name of the insurer,
15 the name of each insured, and the limits of coverage that the coverage provides at least
16 the minimum required coverage required by subparagraph (a)(1)(A) of Code Section
17 33-7-11. Such insurer may provide a copy of the declaration page of each such policy
18 in lieu of providing such information. The claimant's request shall set forth under oath
19 the specific nature of the claim asserted and shall be mailed to the insurer by certified
20 mail or statutory overnight delivery."
