25 LC 129 0288
House Resolution 536
96th, and Sanchez of the 42nd
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the contributions of Leslie Palomino; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Leslie Palomino serves as one of the youngest Latina state directors in the state
3 of Georgia, representing Poder Latinx, a national civic and social justice nonprofit
4 organization advocating on behalf of the Latino community in the United States; and
5 WHEREAS, Leslie has long been recognized by the community for the important role that
6 she has played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to empowering the
7 community to become active actors of democracy in Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, she has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of her time,
9 talents, and energy toward the betterment of her community and state, as evidenced by her
10 superlative service as the Georgia State Programs Director for Poder Latinx; and
11 WHEREAS, as a Georgia native who grew up in Gwinnett County, she has built an extensive
12 background in political campaigning, legislative advocacy, and community grassroots
13 organizing; and
H. R. 536
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0288
14 WHEREAS, Leslie, a proud product of Gwinnett County Public Schools, earned her
15 bachelor's degree in Political Science with a minor in Journalism from Georgia State
16 University; and
17 WHEREAS, Leslie is a champion for change who is deeply passionate about increasing the
18 civic participation of Latinos in Georgia, and works tirelessly to empower and engage the
19 next generation of leaders to become civically engaged and politically active changemakers;
20 and
21 WHEREAS, she exemplifies exceptional strength of character and has an outstanding
22 reputation for integrity, wisdom, and compassion; and
23 WHEREAS, Leslie was recognized in 2024 as one of the 50 Most Influential Latinos in
24 Georgia by the Georgia Hispanic Chamber of Commerce; and
25 WHEREAS, Leslie has served with honor and distinction with Poder Latinx, and her vision
26 and unyielding commitment to empowering her fellow citizens have set the standard for
27 activism and civic engagement; and
28 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments and
29 contributions of this distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
30 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
31 the members of this body recognize and commend Leslie Palomino for her effective
32 leadership, dedication, and service to the State of Georgia and extend the most sincere best
33 wishes for continued success and happiness.

34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Leslie
36 Palomino.
