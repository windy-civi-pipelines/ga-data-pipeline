25 LC 129 0009
House Resolution 43
the 147th, Hagan of the 156th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Bryce Alan Johnson; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of district attorney Bryce Alan Johnson on December 2, 2024; and
4 WHEREAS, Bryce Alan Johnson was born on January 6, 1972, in Sylvester, Georgia, a
5 beloved son of Donnie Lee Johnson and Terry Raines Achord; and
6 WHEREAS, Bryce Alan Johnson showed an affinity for politics from a young age, and his
7 commitment to his beliefs as well as his ability to debate the most divisive political topics
8 with respect and good humor were inspirational to those around him; and
9 WHEREAS, in his adult life, he served in various capacities in local, district, and state
10 Republican organizations; as solicitor-general for several towns; and as district attorney of
11 the Tifton Judicial Circuit; and
12 WHEREAS, outside of politics, Bryce Alan Johnson found joy in watching his favorite
13 teams play, coaching his son's sports endeavors, and serving as "The Professor" to the
14 Chick-Fil-A Friday Night Football radio show; and
H. R. 43
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0009
15 WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, Bryce Alan Johnson was an active member of
16 the First Methodist Church of Tifton, where he served as president of the church council and
17 was a member of several committees; and
18 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife, Erika Stribling Johnson, and was
19 blessed with a remarkable child, William Alan Johnson; and
20 WHEREAS, he will be remembered for his encyclopedic knowledge of facts both important
21 and trivial; Bryce Alan Johnson could always recite a team roster, a school's mascot, a county
22 seat, or a school's most famous player; and
23 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Bryce Alan Johnson will long be
24 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
25 had the great fortune of knowing him.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Bryce Alan Johnson and
28 express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
30 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
31 family of Bryce Alan Johnson.
H. R. 43
