25 Sen Floor Amend 1A AM 39 0475
ADOPTED
1 Amend Amendment 1 to HB 308 (AM 39 0459) by replacing line 8 with the following:
2 tinting or unlawful installation of such; to revise and provide for a definition; to provide for
3 the issuance of a refusal to sign citation; to provide for a penalty; to amend Code Section
4 48-5-473 of the Official Code
5 By inserting the following between lines 43 and 44:
6 Said title is further amended in Code Section 40-13-2.1, relating to signature on citations
7 required, effect of failure to sign, exemption for out-of-state drivers, and electronic capture
8 of signature, by revising subsection (a) as follows:
9 "(a) A person who is issued a citation as provided in this chapter or Code Section 17-6-11,
10 relating to display of driver's license in lieu of bail, shall may sign the citation to
11 acknowledge receipt of the citation and of his or her obligation to appear for trial. The
12 officer shall advise the person that signing the citation is not an admission of guilt and that
13 failure to sign will result in the person having to post a cash bond appear for trial. If the
14 person refuses to sign the citation, it shall constitute reasonable cause to believe that the
15 person will not appear at trial and the officer shall write 'Refusal to Sign' on such citation
16 and shall give the citation to such person. The officer may bring the person before a
17 judicial officer or traffic violations bureau to post a bond as is otherwise provided by law."
