25 LC 112 3426
House Resolution 620
of the 154th, Martin of the 49th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Atlantic Institute; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute is a Georgia-based nonprofit organization committed to
3 fostering dialogue, education, and cultural understanding; and
4 WHEREAS, the Institute promotes mutual respect and peace by increasing public awareness
5 of diverse cultures, beliefs, traditions, and perspectives; and
6 WHEREAS, the Institute organizes and hosts educational programs, panel discussions, and
7 cultural events that encourage dialogue on global and societal issues; and
8 WHEREAS, the organization actively engages in interfaith and intercultural initiatives to
9 promote cooperation and harmony among communities; and
10 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute fosters civic engagement and social responsibility through
11 community service projects and collaborative initiatives; and
12 WHEREAS, the organization provides opportunities for youth engagement and leadership
13 development through programs that nurture cross-cultural understanding; and
H. R. 620
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3426
14 WHEREAS, the Institute's commitment to peacebuilding is demonstrated through its efforts
15 to address social divisions and foster connections between different communities; and
16 WHEREAS, the Institute builds strong partnerships with academic institutions, faith-based
17 organizations, and civic leaders to create spaces for constructive dialogue; and
18 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute works to counter prejudice, discrimination, and
19 misunderstanding by emphasizing shared values and common ground among people of all
20 backgrounds; and
21 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute's annual events, including interfaith dinners, cultural
22 exchange programs, and academic discussions, serve as platforms for meaningful
23 conversations and collaboration; and
24 WHEREAS, the organization enhances societal enrichment by fostering discussions on
25 critical social issues such as democracy and the promotion of peaceful coexistence; and
26 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute actively contributes to peaceful coexistence in the
27 Southeastern United States by fostering dialogue and building bridges between communities;
28 and
29 WHEREAS, the Institute honors and highlights diverse cultural traditions through artistic and
30 culinary events that foster appreciation and cross-cultural exchange; and
31 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute's commitment to fostering dialogue aligns with the
32 principles of unity, inclusivity, and respect for all individuals; and
H. R. 620
- 2 -
25 LC 112 3426
33 WHEREAS, through its many initiatives, the organization has had a significant and positive
34 impact on the communities it serves; and
35 WHEREAS, the work of the Atlantic Institute is deserving of recognition for its continued
36 efforts in fostering peace, understanding, and cultural appreciation; and
37 WHEREAS, the Atlantic Institute has established branches in Florida, Alabama, Georgia,
38 Tennessee, and South Carolina, expanding its outreach and impact across the Southeastern
39 United States.
40 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
41 the members of this body recognize and commend the Atlantic Institute for its dedication to
42 fostering dialogue, cultural understanding, and peacebuilding as well as extend best wishes
43 for continued success.
44 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
45 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
46 Atlantic Institute.
