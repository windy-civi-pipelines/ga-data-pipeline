25 LC 129 0122
Senate Resolution 145
26th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Minister Berry Vernon Wren upon his retirement; and for
2 other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Minister Berry Vernon Wren has demonstrated his commitment to teaching the
4 Gospel, witnessing Christ through word and deed, and addressing the physical,
5 psychological, intellectual, and spiritual needs of others; and
6 WHEREAS, Minister Berry Vernon Wren was called by the Lord in 1979 to lead the
7 congregation of Culverton Baptist Church, where he served until September 1982; and
8 WHEREAS, in 1982, he began his 43-year-long tenure of dedicated service to Fairmont
9 Baptist Church in Sparta, Georgia; and
10 WHEREAS, his vision and direction have helped Fairmont Baptist Church grow in size and
11 influence, uplifting the community like the light of Bethlehem and serving as a spiritual
12 leader and place for fellowship; and
13 WHEREAS, Minister Berry Vernon Wren has spent a lifetime answering God's call to be His
14 messenger and to do His will; and
S. R. 145
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0122
15 WHEREAS, whether the task is preparing a sermon, visiting the sick, counseling the
16 troubled, baptizing a new believer, or acting as a theologian, educator, administrator, or
17 humanitarian, Minister Berry Vernon Wren has shown the energy, devotion, and
18 commitment truly reflective of divine guidance; and
19 WHEREAS, the unmatched spiritual assistance offered by Minister Berry Vernon Wren is
20 a source of strength and direction for persons in all walks of life; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this enduring example of God's message
22 of peace and love be recognized and congratulated for his innumerable good works rendered
23 on behalf of persons in need.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 recognize and commend Minister Berry Vernon Wren on the wonderful occasion of his
26 retirement and extend best wishes for future health, happiness, and success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
28 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Minister Berry
29 Vernon Wren.
