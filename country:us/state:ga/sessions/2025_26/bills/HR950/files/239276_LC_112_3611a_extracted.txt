Section 1:
25 LC 112 3611
House Resolution 950
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing May 2025 as Foster Parent Appreciation Month; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, foster parents in our community provide safe, loving, and nurturing homes for
3 children in need, offering them a second chance at a brighter future; and
4 WHEREAS, the selfless dedication and unwavering commitment of foster parents make a
5 profound difference in the lives of children, providing them with stability, support, and the
6 opportunity to thrive; and
7 WHEREAS, foster parents often face significant challenges and demands, yet they continue
8 to open their hearts and homes to children in need, demonstrating remarkable compassion
9 and resilience; and
10 WHEREAS, we recognize the importance of supporting foster families and ensuring that
11 they have the resources and assistance they need to succeed in their crucial role.
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
13 the members of this body recognize May 2025 as Foster Parent Appreciation Month and
H. R. 950
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3611
14 express their sincere gratitude and appreciation for the invaluable contributions made by
15 foster parents.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
17 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
18 public and the press.
H. R. 950
