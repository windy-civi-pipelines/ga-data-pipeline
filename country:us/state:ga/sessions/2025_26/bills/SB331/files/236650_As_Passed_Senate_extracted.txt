Section 1:
25 SB 331/FA
By: Senators Dixon of the 45th and Ginn of the 47th
AS PASSED SENATE
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To repeal an Act creating and establishing a joint airport authority for the City of Winder and
2 the County of Barrow, approved March 25, 1958 (Ga. L. 1958, p. 3087), as amended,
3 particularly by an Act approved March 28, 1986 (Ga. L. 1986, p. 5518) and an Act approved
4 March 16, 1989 (Ga. L. 1989, p. 3764); to provide for the assets and liabilities thereof; to
5 repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 An Act creating and establishing a joint airport authority for the City of Winder and the
9 County of Barrow, approved March 25, 1958 (Ga. L. 1958, p. 3087), as amended,
10 particularly by an Act approved March 28, 1986 (Ga. L. 1986, p. 5519) and an Act approved
11 March 16, 1989 (Ga. L. 1989, p. 3764), is hereby repealed in its entirety.
12 SECTION 2.
13 Any and all assets of the Barrow County Airport Authority shall devolve automatically and
14 without further action to Barrow County, Georgia. The Board of Commissioners of Barrow
15 County, Georgia, is authorized to execute any deeds or instruments of conveyance required
S. B. 331
- 1 -
25 SB 331/FA
16 to place title of such assets into the name of Barrow County and is authorized to do so in the
17 name of the Barrow County Airport Authority; provided, however, the Barrow County
18 Airport Authority shall remain in existence for the limited purpose of transferring assets to
19 Barrow County, after which the Barrow County Airport Authority shall automatically
20 dissolve. The obligations of the Barrow County Airport Authority, including but not limited
21 to leases and liabilities, shall be transferred to and assumed by Barrow County, Georgia, by
22 such instruments as may be required to maintain the same. The Board of Commissioners of
23 Barrow County, Georgia, is authorized to execute any such instruments required to transfer
24 such obligations into the name of Barrow County and is authorized to do so in the name of
25 the Barrow County Airport Authority; provided, however, the Barrow County Airport
26 Authority shall remain in existence for the limited purpose of transferring assets to Barrow
27 County, after which the Barrow County Airport Authority shall automatically dissolve.
28 SECTION 3.
29 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 331
