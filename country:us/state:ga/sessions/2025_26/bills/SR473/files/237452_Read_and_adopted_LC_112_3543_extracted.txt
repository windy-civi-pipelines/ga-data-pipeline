25 LC 112 3543
Senate Resolution 473
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the Black Man Lab for its dedication to empowering Black men and enriching the
2 community; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Black Man Lab was founded by four Black fathers—Mawuli Davis, Jarrod
4 Grant, Dana Watkins, and Melek Buford—along with their sons as a space to cultivate
5 leadership, wisdom, and support young Black men in their development; and
6 WHEREAS, recognizing the critical need for mentorship, guidance, and intergenerational
7 learning, these fathers established the Black Man Lab to ensure that young Black men had
8 access to positive role models, knowledge, and community-driven empowerment; and
9 WHEREAS, the Black Man Lab has since grown into a transformative movement, bringing
10 together Black men from all walks of life to engage in open dialogue, professional
11 development, and culturally enriching learning experiences; and
12 WHEREAS, the mission of the Black Man Lab is to provide a safe space for Black men to
13 develop authentic manhood, fostering personal growth, community leadership, and a
14 commitment to excellence; and
S. R. 473
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3543
15 WHEREAS, the vision of the Black Man Lab is to empower and inspire Black men to
16 positively transform the world, embracing their roles as warriors, healers, and builders within
17 their families and communities; and
18 WHEREAS, the Black Man Lab has consistently offered one of the largest weekly gatherings
19 focused on encouraging Black men in the country; and
20 WHEREAS, the Black Man Lab's dedication to fostering authentic manhood, mentorship,
21 and community leadership has had a profound and positive impact, shaping the lives of
22 countless Black men and strengthening families and communities across the state.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
24 honor the Black Man Lab for its outstanding contributions to the empowerment and
25 enrichment of Black men, its dedication to fostering intergenerational wisdom, and its role
26 in inspiring positive transformation.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
28 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Black Man
29 Lab.
