25 LC 112 3625
House Resolution 922
80th, Jackson of the 68th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending The Empowerment Workshop and its founder, Le'Dor Milteer; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, in 2022, Sandy Springs resident Le'Dor Milteer founded The Empowerment
4 Workshop, a nonprofit organization committed to connecting our diverse communities
5 through education and empowerment programs; and
6 WHEREAS, The Empowerment Workship (TEW) and Le'Dor Milteer have demonstrated
7 an unwavering commitment to equity and inclusion, striving to help everyone have an
8 opportunity to shape their own futures; and
9 WHEREAS, TEW places advocacy at the center of its mission, working to amplify the
10 voices of marginalized groups, challenge systemic inequalities, and create lasting change
11 through public policy and community action; and
12 WHEREAS, TEW supports organizations and events designed to educate and empower our
13 youth, small businesses, minority communities, and seniors; and
H. R. 922
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3625
14 WHEREAS, TEW has organized and sponsored cultural events such as Sandy Springs
15 Juneteenth, the Atlanta Caribbean Carnival and Parade, Black History Month featuring Black
16 artists, and International Women's Day Atlanta; and
17 WHEREAS, the organization actively engages the Atlanta community by offering
18 mentorship, skill-building programs, and access to vital resources, equipping people with the
19 tools needed to build confidence, resilience, and leadership; and
20 WHEREAS, TEW operates a Community Service and Volunteer Credit Hours Program,
21 providing students and individual volunteers with the opportunity to gain hands-on
22 experience at Mercedes-Benz Stadium while developing practical skills in teamwork,
23 customer service, and event management; and
24 WHEREAS, through its comprehensive and impactful programs, TEW continues to mobilize
25 and inspire individuals across Atlanta, embodying the true spirit of community engagement
26 and service; and
27 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable organization be
28 appropriately honored during this special time.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
30 the members of this body commend The Empowerment Workshop and founder Le'Dor
31 Milteer for their outstanding contributions to advocacy, empowerment, and community
32 development as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
H. R. 922
