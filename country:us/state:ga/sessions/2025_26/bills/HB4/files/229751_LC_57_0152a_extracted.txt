Section 1:
25 LC 57 0152
By: Representative Au of the 50th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Part 5 of Article 4 of Chapter 11 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia
2 Annotated, relating to Brady Law Regulations, so as to require a three-day waiting period for
3 the purchase or transfer of certain firearms; to provide for inspection; to provide for
4 exceptions; to provide for criminal penalties; to provide for related matters; to repeal
5 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 Part 5 of Article 4 of Chapter 11 of Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
9 relating to Brady Law Regulations, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as
10 follows:
11 "16-11-172.1.
12 (a) No purchase or transfer of a firearm conducted by a licensed importer, licensed
13 manufacturer, or licensed dealer shall be completed until the third day after the initiation
14 of such purchase or transfer; provided, however, that the calculation of the three days shall
15 not include Saturdays, Sundays, or state or federal holidays and provided, further, that the
H. B. 4
- 1 -
25 LC 57 0152
16 NICS check provided for under Code Section 16-11-172 may be conducted within the three
17 days.
18 (b) Licensed importers, licensed manufacturers, and licensed dealers shall, during normal
19 business hours, make records available for inspection by any law enforcement agency for
20 purposes of any criminal investigation.
21 (c) Subsection (a) of this Code section shall not apply when a firearm is being purchased
22 by or transferred to:
23 (1) The holder of a weapons carry license issued pursuant to Code Section 16-11-129;
24 or
25 (2) Any person provided for under Code Section 16-11-130.
26 (d) Any licensed importer, licensed manufacturer, or licensed dealer that violates this Code
27 section, or any purchaser or transferee who uses fraud or false representation to avoid the
28 requirements of subsection (a) of this Code section, shall be guilty of a felony and, upon
29 conviction, shall be punished by imprisonment for not less than one nor more than five
30 years."
31 SECTION 2.
32 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
