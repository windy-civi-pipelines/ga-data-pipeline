Section 1:
25 LC 61 0118/AP
By: Representative Greene of the 154th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act creating the board of commissioners of Baker County, approved August 8,
2 1917 (Ga. L. 1917, p. 306), as amended, so as to provide that the board shall meet on the first
3 Tuesday of each month and on such other days as it may determine; to provide for a meeting
4 location to be set by local ordinance; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting
5 laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 An Act creating the board of commissioners of Baker County, approved August 8, 1917 (Ga.
9 L. 1917, p. 306), as amended, is amended by revising Section 5 as follows:
10 "SECTION 5
11 The board of commissioners shall meet on the first Tuesday of each month and on such other
12 days as it may determine. The board shall, by local ordinance, set a location and time for
13 such meetings."
14 SECTION 2.
15 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 594
