Section 1:
25 LC 39 4510
By: Representatives Townsend of the 179th, DeLoach of the 167th, Sainz of the 180th,
Burchett of the 176th, Hagan of the 156th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 3 of Chapter 3 of Title 50 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to other state symbols, so as to designate Brunswick stew as the official state stew;
3 to provide for legislative findings; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws;
4 and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 The General Assembly of Georgia finds and determines that:
8 (1) Brunswick stew is a rich, thick stew noted for its signature ingredients of meat,
9 tomatoes, and fresh vegetables, such as sweet corn;
10 (2) A plaque on an old iron pot in Brunswick, Georgia, declares the first Brunswick stew
11 was made in it on July 2, 1898, on nearby St. Simons Island; and
12 (3) Contrary to claims by other states, Georgia, and specifically Brunswick, Georgia, is
13 the birthplace of Brunswick stew.
H. B. 233
- 1 -
25 LC 39 4510
14 SECTION 2.
15 Article 3 of Chapter 3 of Title 50 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to other
16 state symbols, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
17 "50-3-94.
18 Brunswick stew is designated as the official Georgia state stew."
19 SECTION 3.
20 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 233
