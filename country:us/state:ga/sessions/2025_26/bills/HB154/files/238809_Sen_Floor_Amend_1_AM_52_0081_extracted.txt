25 Sen Floor Amend 1 AM 52 0081
Kennedy of the 18th offered the following amendment #1:
1 Amend HB 154 (LC 46 1133S) by replacing lines 1 and 2 with the following:
2 To amend Title 31 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to health, so as to
3 define "rural hospital organization" and to designate ambulance services as essential services
4 By inserting between lines 5 and 6 the following:
5 SECTION .05
6 Title 31 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to health, is amended in
7 subparagraph (a)(3)(A) of Code Section 31-8-9.1, relating to eligibility to receive tax credits
8 and related items, by inserting "qualifies as a rural emergency hospital as defined in 42
9 C.F.R. Section 485.502 or" after "or".
