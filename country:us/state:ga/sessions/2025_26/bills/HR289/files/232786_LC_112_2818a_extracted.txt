25 LC 112 2818
House Resolution 289
of the 13th, Williams of the 168th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating The Woodbine Opry upon its 25th anniversary; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, The Woodbine Opry will celebrate 25 years of old-time music at the beautifully
3 renovated "Old School" in Woodbine, Georgia, during the week of February 10, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, the celebration will begin on Monday, February 10, with a tribute to the original
5 founders of The Woodbine Opry, featuring songs and stories reminiscent of its early days;
6 and
7 WHEREAS, on Thursday, February 13, the popular band Satilla Blue will perform, packing
8 the Old School Auditorium with a full floor of dancers; and
9 WHEREAS, the celebration will continue on Friday, February 14, with a bluegrass and
10 Gospel jam session, followed by an electric country music dance night on Saturday, February
11 15, to conclude the festivities; and
12 WHEREAS, although the Old School Auditorium seats fewer than 200 patrons, The
13 Woodbine Opry's livestream and social media platforms reach 15,000 to 20,000 views each
14 month from across the country and around the world; and
H. R. 289
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2818
15 WHEREAS, as one of the longest-running free live music venues in the State of Georgia,
16 The Woodbine Opry has provided weekly performances of bluegrass, Gospel, and classic
17 country music at the Old Woodbine School for 25 years; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable establishment be
19 appropriately honored during this special time.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body congratulate The Woodbine Opry upon the grand occasion of its
22 25th anniversary as well as extend best wishes for a jam-packed week of joyous musical
23 celebration.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to The
26 Woodbine Opry.
