25 LC 112 3499
House Resolution 763
Gilliard of the 162nd, Hitchens of the 161st, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Savannah State University men's basketball team for winning the 2025
2 Southern Intercollegiate Athletic Conference Basketball Tournament Championship; and for
3 other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Savannah State University men's basketball team has proudly captured the
5 2025 Southern Intercollegiate Athletic Conference (SIAC) Basketball Tournament
6 Championship, securing a thrilling 67-66 victory over Tuskegee University in the
7 championship game on Saturday, March 8, 2025; and
8 WHEREAS, this remarkable achievement marks the second SIAC Championship in just four
9 years, following their 2022 title, showcasing the team's continued excellence and resilience;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, Interim Head Coach Clevan Thompson, who took the helm in early January,
12 has guided the Tigers to an impressive 13-5 record, including a current eight-game winning
13 streak, demonstrating his leadership and strategic prowess; and
14 WHEREAS, junior guard and Savannah native Javonte Landy, along with senior guard
15 Ayjay Plain, were honored with All-Tournament Team selections for their outstanding

16 performances while senior guard/forward Qua King was named the Tournament's Most
17 Valuable Player, further exemplifying the team's individual and collective talent; and
18 WHEREAS, the Savannah State University men's basketball team represented the SIAC in
19 the 2025 NCAA Division II Men's Basketball Tournament as the No. 8 seed in the South
20 Region, continuing their pursuit of excellence on the national stage.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
