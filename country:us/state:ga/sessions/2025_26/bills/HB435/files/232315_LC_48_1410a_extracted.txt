Section 1:
25 LC 48 1410
By: Representatives Ballinger of the 23rd, Camp of the 135th, Rice of the 139th, O’Steen of
the 169th, Mathiak of the 82nd, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 16-12-100 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 sexual exploitation of children, reporting violation, civil forfeiture, and penalties, so as to
3 expand limitations to include the Division of Family and Children Services; to provide for
4 related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Code Section 16-12-100 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to sexual
8 exploitation of children, reporting violation, civil forfeiture, and penalties, is amended by
9 revising subsection (d) as follows:
10 "(d) The provisions of subsection (b) of this Code section shall not apply to:
11 (1) The activities of law enforcement and prosecution agencies in the investigation and
12 prosecution of criminal offenses;
13 (2) Legitimate medical, scientific, or educational activities; or
14 (3) Any person who creates or possesses a visual medium depicting only himself or
15 herself engaged in sexually explicit conduct. ; or
H. B. 435

25 LC 48 1410
16 (4) Any staff member of the Division of Family and Children Services in the
17 performance of his or her official duties."
18 SECTION 2.
19 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
