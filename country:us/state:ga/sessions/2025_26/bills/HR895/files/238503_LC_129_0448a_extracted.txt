25 LC 129 0448
House Resolution 895
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Pamela Brown; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, March is celebrated as Women's History Month, a time to honor and recognize
3 the invaluable contributions of women throughout history and across all fields and trades;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, Pamela Brown's contributions to the community reflect the core values of
6 Women's History Month; and
7 WHEREAS, Pam is an exemplary Board Of Education member, currently serving in her 3rd
8 term; and
9 WHEREAS, she is also the Georgia School Boards Association District IV Director,
10 representing the Rockdale County, Decatur City Schools, DeKalb County, and Newton
11 County Schools districts; and
12 WHEREAS, a well-respected leader in the Rockdale community, Pam is known for her
13 commitment to education, community service, and advocacy; and
H. R. 895
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0448
14 WHEREAS, she actively engages in legislative efforts and supports various community
15 causes, is a member of the Conyers-Rockdale Economic Development Council, and is a past
16 member on the Board of Governors of the Conyers-Rockdale Chamber of Commerce; and
17 WHEREAS, she is a Senior Transmission Analyst for Southern Company, a 2010 graduate
18 of Leadership Rockdale, and a proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc.; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
20 remarkable woman are recognized during Women's History Month.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body recognize and commend Pamela Brown for her unwavering
23 dedication to her community and extend sincere best wishes for her continued health and
24 happiness.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
27 Pamela Brown.
