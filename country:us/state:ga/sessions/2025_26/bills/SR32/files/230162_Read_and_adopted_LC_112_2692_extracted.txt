25 LC 112 2692
Senate Resolution 32
22nd
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Charles G. Newton IV on his outstanding public service; and
2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Charles G. Newton IV has long been recognized by the citizens of this state for
4 the vital role that he has played in leadership and his deep personal commitment to the
5 welfare of the citizens of McDuffie County, Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, he has diligently and conscientiously devoted innumerable hours of his time,
7 talents, and energy toward the betterment of his community and state, as evidenced
8 dramatically by his more than two decades of service as chairman of the McDuffie County
9 Board of Commissioners; and
10 WHEREAS, during his tenure, McDuffie County has undergone advancements in
11 infrastructure, economic development, health care, and community services that have greatly
12 strengthened the county's future and enhanced the quality of life for all its residents; and
13 WHEREAS, through his exemplary leadership and advocacy at the state level, Charles G.
14 Newton IV has secured resources for critical infrastructure projects such as the county-wide
15 water and sewer system, road improvements, and the East and West Bypass; and
S. R. 32
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2692
16 WHEREAS, his accomplishments further include the championing of the county-wide
17 broadband initiative, the creation of a Boys and Girls Club within the county, and the
18 construction of the county's new government complex; and
19 WHEREAS, he has additionally served his community as a member of the McDuffie County
20 Farm Bureau Board of Directors, the McDuffie County Board of Health, the
21 Thomson-McDuffie County Water-Sewer Commission, and the Forward McDuffie Board
22 of Directors; and
23 WHEREAS, Charles G. Newton IV has served McDuffie County with honor and distinction,
24 and his vision and unyielding commitment to excellence will be sorely missed upon his
25 retirement; and
26 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
27 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately recognized.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
29 recognize and commend Charles G. Newton IV for his efficient, effective, unselfish, and
30 dedicated public service to McDuffie County, Georgia; congratulate him upon the grand
31 occasion of his retirement; and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued health and
32 happiness.
33 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
34 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Charles G.
35 Newton IV.
S. R. 32
