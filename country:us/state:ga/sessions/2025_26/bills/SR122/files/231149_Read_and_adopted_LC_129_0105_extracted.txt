25 LC 129 0105
Senate Resolution 122
the 32nd and Cowsert of the 46th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Emergency Manager Marc Vincent; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, on January 28, 2025, Emergency Manager Marc Vincent received a Public
3 Safety Commendation from the Georgia World Congress Center Authority's (GWCCA)
4 Department of Public Safety; and
5 WHEREAS, this accolade reflects his exceptional leadership during the recent emergency
6 responses to severe winter weather that coincided with major events on the GWCCA
7 campus; and
8 WHEREAS, his ability to coordinate resources and make timely critical decisions ensured
9 the safety and well-being of GWCCA's employees and campus attendees; and
10 WHEREAS, his professionalism, quick thinking, and commitment to excellence have truly
11 made a profound impact on GWCCA's campus; and
12 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
13 remarkable and distinguished Georgian be appropriately honored.
S. R. 122
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0105
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
15 recognize and commend the selfless, dedicated, and courageous work of Emergency
16 Manager Marc Vincent and extend best wishes for continued health, happiness, and success.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
18 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Emergency
19 Manager Marc Vincent.
S. R. 122
