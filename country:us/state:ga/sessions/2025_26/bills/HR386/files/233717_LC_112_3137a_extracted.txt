25 LC 112 3137
House Resolution 386
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending the Black Child Development Institute, recipient of the
2 Georgia Reads Community Award, for its dedication to literacy advancement; and for other
3 purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council on Literacy is dedicated to improving literacy from birth
5 through adulthood and recognizes that communities play a critical role in addressing low
6 literacy rates; and
7 WHEREAS, the Georgia Reads Community Award was established to recognize
8 communities across Georgia that have formed partnerships of two or more organizations,
9 sustained for at least three years, with a strategic plan and measurable results in literacy
10 improvement; and
11 WHEREAS, the Black Child Development Institute is a recipient of the Georgia Reads
12 Community Award for its dedicated efforts in promoting early literacy and language
13 development for families with children from birth to age eight; and
H. R. 386
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3137
14 WHEREAS, the Read to Succeed program assists families in establishing home libraries
15 filled with culturally responsive and developmentally appropriate children's books to foster
16 a love of reading and learning; and
17 WHEREAS, its meaningful partnerships include the United Way of Greater Atlanta, the
18 Georgia Department of Early Care and Learning, the Georgia Early Education Alliance for
19 Ready Students, and the Atlanta Speech School's Rollins Center for Language and Literacy,
20 demonstrating a strong commitment to literacy development through collaboration and
21 resource-sharing; and
22 WHEREAS, these initiatives have led to measurable improvements in reading proficiency,
23 ensuring greater opportunities for success in education and career development; and
24 WHEREAS, the dedication and hard work of the Black Child Development Institute and its
25 partners serve as an inspiration to others, demonstrating the power of community-driven
26 solutions to address literacy challenges.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body congratulate and commend the Georgia Reads Community Award
29 recipients, particularly the Black Child Development Institute, for their exemplary service
30 and unwavering commitment to improving literacy in their communities.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
33 Black Child Development Institute.
H. R. 386
