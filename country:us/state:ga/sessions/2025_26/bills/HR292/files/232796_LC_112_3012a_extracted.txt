25 LC 112 3012
House Resolution 292
By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13,
3 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and
4 WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight
5 leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence";
6 and
7 WHEREAS, a strategic leader and entrepreneur, Dr. Whitney N. Blanco is the founder of
8 Acreage, a real estate analytics platform that leverages AI, machine learning, and GIS to
9 revolutionize urban development through data-driven solutions that promote economic
10 growth, sustainability, and responsible development; and
11 WHEREAS, with a multifaceted background in real estate, civil engineering, public health,
12 and international development, she has spent over a decade designing innovative strategies
13 that bridge technology, urban planning, and financial feasibility, making her a sought-after
14 consultant for transformative initiatives worldwide; and
H. R. 292
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, her career spans the corporate, government, and nonprofit sectors, where she
16 has led cross-functional teams, advised on multi-million-dollar real estate and international
17 programs, and established high-impact partnerships with government agencies, private sector
18 leaders, and global organizations; and
19 WHEREAS, she has further served as a World Health Organization (WHO) global
20 coordinator and project management officer, leading international development strategies,
21 health infrastructure planning, and a $1 billion Ebola response plan in collaboration with G7,
22 G20, and major world health funders; and
23 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be
24 appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place
25 in which to live and work.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body commend Dr. Whitney N. Blanco for her many remarkable career
28 accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on
29 behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
32 Whitney N. Blanco.
