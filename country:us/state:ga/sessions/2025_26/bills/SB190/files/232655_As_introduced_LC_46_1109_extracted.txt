25 LC 46 1109
By: Senators Wicks of the 34th, Merritt of the 9th, Harbison of the 15th, McLaurin of the
14th, Jones of the 10th and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Part 1 of Article 1 of Chapter 3 of Title 12 of the Official Code of Georgia
2 Annotated, relating to general provisions of parks, historic areas, memorials, and recreation,
3 so as to provide certain fee waivers for active duty members of the military, veterans, and
4 family members; to provide for rules and regulations; to provide for related matters; to repeal
5 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 Part 1 of Article 1 of Chapter 3 of Title 12 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
9 relating to general provisions of parks, historic areas, memorials, and recreation, is amended
10 by revising Code Section 12-3-9.1, relating to discount for certain disabled veterans, as
11 follows:
12 "12-3-9.1.
13 (a) As used in this Code section, the term:
14 (1) 'Active duty member' means a member of the United States armed forces serving on
15 a full-time basis.
S. B. 190
- 1 -

16 (1) 'Disabled veteran' means a veteran discharged under honorable conditions from any
17 branch of the armed forces of the United States who has a physical disability which was
18 incurred during the period of service in the armed forces and who is a resident of the
19 State of Georgia.
20 (2) 'Armed forces' means the United States army, navy, marine corps, space force, coast
21 guard, air force, Georgia National Guard, or other reserve unit of the United States
22 military.
23 (2)(3) 'Fee' means the charge or fee established by the Board of Natural Resources for
24 the use or occupancy of any state park, historic site, or recreational area and specifically
25 includes, but is not limited to, entrance or admittance fees, rental fees for cabins or other
26 overnight lodgings, rental fees for campsites, and fees for the use of golf courses or other
27 recreational facilities.
28 (4) 'Gold Star family member' means an immediate family member of a deceased
29 member of the armed forces who died in the line of duty.
30 (5) 'Immediate family member' means a parent, child, spouse, brother, or sister.
31 (6) 'Veteran' means a former member of the armed forces who received an honorable
32 discharge.
33 (b) Any disabled veteran Gold Star family member may apply to the commissioner of
34 veterans service for certification as a disabled veteran Gold Star family member as defined
35 by paragraph (1) (4) of subsection (a) of this Code section. The commissioner of veterans
36 service is authorized and directed to certify such applicants applicant as disabled veterans
37 a Gold Star family member and to issue to such applicants applicant such proof of
38 certification as the commissioner finds appropriate. The commissioner of veterans service
39 may shall by rule or regulation require such documentation as may be necessary to certify
40 disabled veterans a Gold Star family member as provided in this subsection.
41 (c) Any disabled veteran, active duty member, or Gold Star family member who is
42 certified as such by the commissioner of veterans service as provided in subsection (b) of
S. B. 190
- 2 -
25 LC 46 1109
43 this Code section shall be granted a discount of 25 percent waiver of the fee otherwise
44 applicable at any state park, historic site, or recreational area operated by or pursuant to the
45 authority of the Department of Natural Resources or any division or other agency of said
46 department.
47 (d) The discount waiver provided for in subsection (c) of this Code section:
48 (1) Shall apply to rental fees for cabins, campsites, or other overnight accommodations
49 for the disabled veteran and members of the disabled veteran's immediate family who
50 occupy any disabled veteran, active duty member, or Gold Star family member who
51 occupies the overnight accommodations with the disabled veteran; and
52 (2) Shall not apply to rental or use fees for any group facilities or accommodations.
53 (e) The Department of Natural Resources shall promulgate rules and regulations for
54 verifying eligibility, issuing passes, and promoting the program described in this Code
55 section."
56 SECTION 2.
57 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 190
