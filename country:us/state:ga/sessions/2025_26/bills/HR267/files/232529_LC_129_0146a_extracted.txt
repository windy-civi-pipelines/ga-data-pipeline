25 LC 129 0146
House Resolution 267
By: Representative Crawford of the 89th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Brother Keiton Robinson is a senior at the University of West Georgia, where
3 he is completing a Bachelor of Science in biology with aspirations of earning a Doctor of
4 Veterinary Medicine; and
5 WHEREAS, he was initiated into the Kappa Kappa Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity Inc.
6 in fall 2023, where he currently serves as Chapter Basileus; and
7 WHEREAS, in spring 2024, he received the Dr. Moses C. Norman Sr. Education Scholarship
8 Award at the 87th District Meeting in Biloxi, Mississippi; and
9 WHEREAS, Brother Keiton Robinson was elected 2nd Vice State Representative of the State
10 Organization of Georgia at the 61st Georgia State Meeting and was granted the Bro. Alonzo
11 A. Bennett Scholarship Award in recognition of his leadership and academic excellence; and
12 WHEREAS, an exemplary student-leader, he further serves as the Secretary of the University
13 of West Georgia Pan-Hellenic Council, where he makes a positive impact on his peers
14 though his dedication and commitment to the community; and

15 WHEREAS, he additionally serves as an involved member of numerous organizations,
16 including the Pre-Vet Club, Wildlife Club, Emerging Healthcare Leaders, and the Chemistry
