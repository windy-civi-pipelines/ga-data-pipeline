25 LC 112 3430
House Resolution 625
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Hardaway Hawks varsity girls basketball team for winning the 2025
2 GHSA Class 2A Girls Varsity Basketball State Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Hardaway Hawks varsity girls basketball team has established and
4 maintained a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics;
5 and
6 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school by winning the 2025 GHSA
7 Class 2A Girls Varsity Basketball State Championship against the Murray County Indians
8 with a final score of 47 to 34, marking their first-ever state championship victory in school
9 history; and
10 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
11 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
12 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Head Coach Kelly
13 Ellison; and
H. R. 625
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3430
14 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
15 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
16 abundantly proper to call them champions.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
18 the members of this body congratulate the Hardaway Hawks varsity girls basketball team for
19 winning the 2025 GHSA Class 2A Girls Varsity Basketball State Championship and extend
20 best wishes for their continued success.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
22 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
23 Hardaway Hawks varsity girls basketball team.
H. R. 625
