Section 1:
25 LC 44 2896
By: Representatives Bell of the 75th, Hagan of the 156th, Huddleston of the 72nd, Stinson of
the 150th, and Cox of the 28th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Article 4 of Chapter 2 of Title 26 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated,
2 relating to advertising and sale of meat generally, so as to prohibit selling, offering for sale,
3 trading, or distributing lab-grown meat; to provide for definitions; to provide for related
4 matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Article 4 of Chapter 2 of Title 26 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
8 advertising and sale of meat generally, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as
9 follows:
10 "26-2-162.
11 (a) As used in this Code section, the term:
12 (1) 'Animal' means any animal, including cattle, swine, sheep, goats, fish, and poultry,
13 including eggs, raised for the production of an edible product or products intended for
14 human consumption. Such term also includes game animals, as defined in Code Section
15 27-1-2.
16 (2) 'Lab-grown meat' means cell cultured meat.
H. B. 201

17 (3) 'Meat' means the flesh, offal, or other by-product of any part of the carcass of an
18 animal that has been slaughtered.
19 (b) It shall be unlawful for any person to sell, offer for sale, trade, or distribute lab-grown
20 meat."
21 SECTION 2.
22 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
