25 LC 129 0400
House Resolution 826
of the 113th, and Moore of the 91st
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Rashan Ali Smith; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, with a passion for sports and entertainment, Rashan Ali Smith, born in Atlanta,
3 Georgia, and raised in Decatur, has established herself as a revered multimedia personality;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, an honor graduate of Florida A&M University, she has had candid perspective
6 on local Atlanta radio for nearly 15 years on HOT 107.9, V-103 and Streetz 94.5, helping to
7 propel those stations' ratings to top five in the market; and
8 WHEREAS, lending her talents to sports television, she worked as a sports anchor for
9 Headlines News and CNN as well as a sideline reporter for CBS Sports Network, covering
10 college football and basketball for four seasons; and
11 WHEREAS, Rashan delivered her unique and personable perspective as one of four co-hosts
12 on the nationally syndicated daily talk show, Sister Circle Live on TV One for three seasons;
13 and

14 WHEREAS, she currently hosts "The Cool Soror Podcast," a show in which she created to
15 highlight outstanding women and men in Black Greek Lettered Organizations; and
16 WHEREAS, her passion and purpose are deeply tied to the community; she is the founder
17 of Sporty Girls, Inc., a non-profit organization that encourages the development of life and
18 academic skills in girls ages 8-18 through participation in golf, tennis, soccer, swimming, and
19 lacrosse; and
20 WHEREAS, since its inception in 2006, Sporty Girls, Inc., has served nearly 2,000 girls in
21 the Metro Atlanta area; and
22 WHEREAS, Rashan is planning to relaunch Sporty Girls, Inc., in 2025; and
23 WHEREAS, Rashan has also published and released a vibrant children’s book called "Piper
24 Sky’s Pink Popsicle Shoes," based on the likeness of her own daughters as it follows the lead
25 character, Piper Sky, and her quest to help her friends find their own magic; and
26 WHEREAS, Rashan is a proud member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., where she
27 serves internationally on the honorary and awards committee; and
28 WHEREAS, she currently has a recurring role on a top-rated show under the Tyler Perry
29 print on BET+; and
30 WHEREAS, she has been married to Brian Smith for 22 years and they have two daughters;
31 Bailey, a freshman at Howard University, and Carter, a 9th grader; and
32 WHEREAS, Rashan lives by the motto, “Giving is the rent we pay for living."

33 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
34 the members of this body recognize and commend Rashan Ali Smith and extend sincere best
35 wishes for her continued health, happiness, and success.
36 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
37 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
38 Rashan Ali Smith.
