25 LC 112 2980
House Resolution 335
Davis of the 87th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 13, 2025, as Donor Day at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, nearly 3,100 Georgians provided the gift of renewed or improved life through
3 organ, eye, and tissue donation during 2024, including 176 who chose to become living
4 kidney donors, saving and improving the lives of tens of thousands through organ, eye, and
5 tissue transplantation; and
6 WHEREAS, these generous gifts were facilitated by organ, tissue, and eye recovery and
7 transplant organizations in Georgia that work together to educate the public about the
8 importance of organ, tissue, and eye donation as Donate Life Georgia; and
9 WHEREAS, LifeLink of Georgia partners with hospitals to support and guide families as
10 they establish their loved one's legacy through organ and tissue donation as well as honors
11 each donor by saving and improving the lives of as many patients as possible; and
12 WHEREAS, the Georgia Transplant Foundation bridges the gap between hope and
13 opportunity by providing financial, educational, emotional, and community support programs
14 and services that enrich the lives of those affected by organ transplantation in the State of
15 Georgia; and
H. R. 335
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2980
16 WHEREAS, LifeLink of Georgia and member organizations of Donate Life Georgia
17 encourage individuals to register their decision to save lives by joining the Donate Life
18 Georgia organ, eye, and tissue donor registry while obtaining a driver's license, online at
19 www.DonateLifeGeorgia.org, or when obtaining a hunting or fishing license through the
20 Department of Natural Resources; and
21 WHEREAS, LifeLink of Georgia and member organizations of Donate Life Georgia are
22 working tirelessly to ensure that the nearly 2,900 Georgians currently awaiting an organ
23 transplant receive a second chance at life; and
24 WHEREAS, by celebrating Donor Day at the state capitol and sharing the Donate Life
25 message, we can save and heal more lives and honor donors' legacies of generosity and
26 compassion; and
27 WHEREAS, we recognize those who have given the gift of life, both after death and through
28 living donation; those who have received the gift of life through organ, eye, and tissue
29 donation; those who are currently waiting for a lifesaving transplant; and those who died
30 waiting because an organ was not gifted in time.
31 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
32 the members of this body commend the selfless individuals and their families who make
33 donation possible as well as the member organizations of Donate Life Georgia for their
34 tireless dedication to educating Georgians about and saving lives through organ, eye, and
35 tissue donation and recognize February 13, 2025, as Donor Day at the state capitol.
H. R. 335
- 2 -
25 LC 112 2980
36 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
37 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
38 LifeLink of Georgia.
