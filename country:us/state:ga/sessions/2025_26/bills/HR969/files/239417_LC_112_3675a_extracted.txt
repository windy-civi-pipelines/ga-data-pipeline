25 LC 112 3675
House Resolution 969
and Momtahan of the 17th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Shayna Harris, South Paulding High School's 2025 Teacher of the Year; and
2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, nothing is more vital to the future of our state than the education of our young
6 people, and Shayna Harris exemplifies the selfless dedication that is characteristic of our
7 finest teachers; and
8 WHEREAS, Shayna Harris is a highly praised English teacher at South Paulding High
9 School, where she devotes her time and talents to guiding students to a brighter future
10 through subjects such as American Literature and Composition; Dramatic Writing for Film,
11 Television, and Stage; and Mythology; and
12 WHEREAS, she has earned distinction for teaching excellence as a result of her dedication
13 to her students and her profession and has been honored as South Paulding High School's
14 2025 Teacher of the Year; and
H. R. 969
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3675
15 WHEREAS, in recognition of such remarkable contributions to the education and welfare
16 of Georgia's students, it is abundantly fitting and proper for this body to honor the
17 extraordinary accomplishments and academic achievements of this outstanding educator.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body commend Shayna Harris, South Paulding High School's 2025
20 Teacher of the Year, and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
22 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
23 Shayna Harris.
