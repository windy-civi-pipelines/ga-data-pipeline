Section 1:
25 LC 44 2917
By: Representative Kelley of the 16th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act creating the Polk County Water Authority, approved April 14, 1967 (Ga.
2 L. 1967, p. 3108), as amended, so as to change certain provisions relating to the
3 reappointment of members of the Authority; to provide for related matters; to repeal
4 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 An Act creating the Polk County Water Authority, approved April 14, 1967 (Ga. L. 1967,
8 p. 3108), as amended, is amended by revising subsection (e) of Section 2 as follows:
9 "(e) No member shall be eligible for reappointment for a period of two years from the
10 expiration of his or her second consecutive previous term. Notwithstanding any other
11 provisions herein, all members of the Authority shall serve until their successors are
12 appointed and qualified."
13 SECTION 2.
14 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 412
