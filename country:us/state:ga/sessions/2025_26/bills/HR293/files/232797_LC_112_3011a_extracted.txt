House Resolution 293
By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13,
3 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and
4 WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight
5 leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence";
6 and
7 WHEREAS, Stacey Samedi is the founder and product engineer of Samedi Label, an
8 Atlanta-based technology company specializing in AI/ML integration and platform
9 development; and
10 WHEREAS, at Samedi Label, she bridges her deep engineering expertise with strategic
11 growth initiatives, specializing in user-centered design, systems integration, and data-driven
12 decision-making to optimize technical infrastructure and drive product success; and

13 WHEREAS, her comprehensive approach to technical leadership, combined with her track
14 record in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments, makes her a valuable contributor
