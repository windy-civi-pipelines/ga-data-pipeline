Section 1:
25 LC 112 3535
Senate Resolution 486
56th, Watson of the 1st and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Colt's Manufacturing Company for its commitment to safe and responsible
2 firearms ownership; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Colt's Manufacturing Company has been a trusted name in the firearms industry
4 for over 175 years; and
5 WHEREAS, Colt has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to promoting safe and
6 responsible firearms ownership through various initiatives and programs; and
7 WHEREAS, Colt has recently launched the Safety Impact Award, a platform recognizing
8 nonprofit organizations dedicated to firearm safety, and has engaged football legends Clay
9 Matthews, Adam Vinatieri, Joe Thomas, and Fletcher Cox as ambassadors for this initiative;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, each ambassador has made an initial donation of $5,000 to support their
12 respective charitable partners, including Project ChildSafe, Clays For Charity, Ducks
13 Unlimited, and the International Hunter Education Association, thereby fostering a culture
14 of safety and community education; and
S. R. 486
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3535
15 WHEREAS, the Safety Impact Award emphasizes the importance of education, training, and
16 community engagement in promoting safe and responsible firearm ownership; and
17 WHEREAS, Colt is further committed to encouraging secure storage solutions to prevent
18 unauthorized access to firearms and accidents; and
19 WHEREAS, Colt supports education and training initiatives for individuals of all experience
20 levels to foster a culture of safety and accountability.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 commend Colt's Manufacturing Company for its unwavering commitment to promoting safe
23 and responsible firearms ownership and for its efforts to enhance community education and
24 engagement through the Safety Impact Award and other initiatives.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
26 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Colt's
27 Manufacturing Company.
