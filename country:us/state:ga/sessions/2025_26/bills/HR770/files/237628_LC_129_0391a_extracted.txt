25 LC 129 0391
House Resolution 770
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Delano Page, Rockdale Magnet School for Science and Technology's Class
2 of 2025 valedictorian; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, a proper quality education for the young people of this state is the single most
4 important objective of the General Assembly; and
5 WHEREAS, Delano Page has shown by academic accomplishment that the pursuit and
6 attainment of scholastic success are within the reach of the students in this state; and
7 WHEREAS, this remarkable senior has earned distinction through his notable achievements
8 as a student at Rockdal Magnet School for Science and Technology where he is a member
9 of the Rockdale Magnet Math Team, Rockdale Peer Tutors, Rockdale Student Council,
10 ScholarsU, the Varsity Tennis team, Georgia Youth Justice Coalition, National Coalition
11 Against Censorship, Witness to Mass Incarceration, Prison Mathematics Program,
12 Generation Human Rights, and Statistics Without Borders; and
13 WHEREAS, he has been selected as valedictorian by earning exemplary grades in the most
14 challenging academic courses while maintaining a substantial extracurricular schedule
15 throughout his academic career; and
H. R. 770

16 WHEREAS, this achievement is the culmination of Delano Page's hard work and
17 determination, and he deserves much credit for his energy and dedication to academic
18 excellence; and
19 WHEREAS, he is an outstanding representative of the young people of this state and it is
20 only fitting and proper that he be recognized for his many accomplishments.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body heartily commend Delano Page for his spectacular scholastic
23 endeavors and express their most sincere best wishes for continued success.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
26 Delano Page.
