25 LC 112 3004
House Resolution 295
By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13,
3 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and
4 WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight
5 leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence";
6 and
7 WHEREAS, Alicia Lyttle currently serves as the CEO of AI Innovision, where she helps
8 organizations implement AI-driven solutions to enhance efficiency, sustainability, and
9 innovation; and
10 WHEREAS, widely known as the "Queen of AI," Alicia is the founder of the International
11 Association of AI Consultants (IAAIC) and the creator of a leading AI certification program
12 that equips professionals with the skills to apply AI in strategic procurement, sustainable
13 practices, and more; and

14 WHEREAS, she has collaborated with top companies, marketing agencies, and government
15 organizations to streamline operations, optimize resources, and build a future-ready approach
16 to sustainability and strategic purchasing; and
17 WHEREAS, as an expert in AI education and professional development, Alicia also leads
18 the AI Experts Club, a global community of individuals dedicated to advancing the practical
19 use of AI; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be
21 appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place
22 in which to live and work.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body commend Alicia Lyttle for her many remarkable career
25 accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on
26 behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Alicia
29 Lyttle.
