25 LC 46 9001
By: Senators Walker III of the 20th, Setzler of the 37th, Kennedy of the 18th, Strickland of
the 42nd, Brass of the 6th and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 15 of Title 43 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 professional engineers and land surveyors, so as to decouple the sequential order of
3 experience and examination requirements; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting
4 laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA
6 SECTION 1.
7 Chapter 15 of Title 43 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to professional
8 engineers and land surveyors, is amended by revising Code Section 43-15-9, relating to
9 professional engineer certificate of registration and eligibility, as follows:
10 "43-15-9.
11 To be eligible for a certificate of registration as a professional engineer, an applicant must
12 meet the following minimum requirements:
13 (1)(A) Obtain certification by the board as an engineer-in-training under paragraph (1)
14 of Code Section 43-15-8;
S. B. 125

15 (B) Acquire a specific record of not less than four years' experience in engineering
16 work of a character satisfactory to the board which indicates the applicant is competent
17 to practice professional engineering; and
18 (C) Subsequently pass Pass a written examination in the principles and practice of
19 engineering (professional engineer's examination);
20 (2)(A) Obtain certification by the board as an engineer-in-training under paragraph (2)
21 of Code Section 43-15-8;
22 (B) Acquire a specific record of not less than seven years' experience in engineering
23 work of a character satisfactory to the board which indicates the applicant is competent
24 to practice professional engineering; and
25 (C) Subsequently pass Pass a written examination in the principles and practice of
26 engineering (professional engineer's examination);
27 (3)(A) Obtain certification by the board as an engineer-in-training under paragraph (3)
28 of Code Section 43-15-8;
29 (B) Acquire a specific record of not less than seven years' experience in engineering
30 work of a character satisfactory to the board which indicates the applicant is competent
31 to practice professional engineering; and
32 (C) Subsequently pass Pass a written examination in the principles and practice of
33 engineering (professional engineer's examination); or
34 (4)(A) Graduate in an engineering or related science curriculum of not less than four
35 academic years;
36 (B) Acquire a specific record of not less than 16 years' experience in engineering work,
37 of which at least eight years have been in responsible charge of important engineering
38 work of a character satisfactory to the board, which indicates the applicant is competent
39 to practice professional engineering; and
40 (C) Subsequently pass Pass a written examination in the principles and practice of
41 engineering (professional engineer's examination)."
