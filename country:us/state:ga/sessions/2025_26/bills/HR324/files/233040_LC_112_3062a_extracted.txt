25 LC 112 3062
House Resolution 324
and Jones of the 47th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Student Leadership NORTH; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Student Leadership NORTH, formerly known as Student Leadership Johns
3 Creek, is a collaborative effort between business and civic leaders to provide leadership
4 training to local youth; and
5 WHEREAS, the mission of Student Leadership NORTH is to develop, energize, and activate
6 community leaders in Johns Creek, Roswell, and Alpharetta by providing students with
7 meaningful interactions with city, business, and government executives to inspire positive
8 change; and
9 WHEREAS, Student Leadership NORTH offers a two-year leadership program for emerging
10 leaders in their sophomore and junior years from Centennial, Chattahoochee, Johns Creek,
11 and Northview high schools; Mt. Pisgah School; Providence Christian Academy; Innovation
12 Academy; and Alpharetta High School; and
13 WHEREAS, in the first year of the program, students develop leadership skills through
14 structured learning experiences and apply them to community service projects, while the

15 second year focuses on individualized opportunities to implement these skills beyond high
16 school and college; and
17 WHEREAS, the program's success is evident in its graduates, with 17 percent attending Ivy
18 League schools, 93 percent attending top 50 colleges, and 100 percent attending top 100
19 colleges; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding organization be
21 appropriately honored.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Student Leadership NORTH for its
24 exceptional service in developing young leaders and extend best wishes for continued
25 success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
28 Student Leadership NORTH.
