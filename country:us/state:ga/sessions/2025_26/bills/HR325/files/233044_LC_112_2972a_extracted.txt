25 LC 112 2972
House Resolution 325
of the 29th, Cox of the 28th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the North Hall High School cross country program for its outstanding
2 achievements during the 2024 season; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the North Hall High School cross country program has long demonstrated a
4 commitment to excellence, developing student-athletes who embody perseverance,
5 discipline, and sportsmanship; and
6 WHEREAS, the 2024 cross country season was a historic and record-breaking year for North
7 Hall High School, highlighted by the girls team winning the GHSA Class 3A State
8 Championship—the first-ever girls state title in any sport in the school's history; and
9 WHEREAS, the North Hall girls secured the championship in dominant fashion, placing five
10 runners in the top 14 at the state meet, with senior Clodagh O'Bryant earning the individual
11 state championship title after an outstanding career as a four-time region champion and
12 school record holder in the 1600-meter and 3200-meter events; and
13 WHEREAS, the girls team saw additional all-state performances from Summer Muxlow,
14 who finished second, and Harleigh Smith, who placed fifth; and
H. R. 325
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, the North Hall boys cross country team also had an exceptional season,
16 finishing as the GHSA Class 3A State Runner-Up, led by senior Bryant Young, who secured
17 third place individually and earned all-state honors; and
18 WHEREAS, the success of the North Hall High School cross country program extends
19 beyond the high school level as the middle school boys team additionally captured its third
20 consecutive state championship; and
21 WHEREAS, the achievements of the North Hall cross country program are the result of the
22 dedication and hard work of its student-athletes as well as the leadership of coaches Sam
23 Borg, Art Dacosta, Jill Borg, and Lindsey Green; and
24 WHEREAS, as these magnificent teams earned their well-deserved reputations for
25 excellence by performing with courage and precision over months of training and
26 competition, it is abundantly proper to call them champions.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body congratulate the North Hall High School cross country program
29 for its outstanding achievements during the 2024 season and extend best wishes for
30 continued success.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to North
33 Hall High School.
