25 LC 112 2961
Senate Resolution 223
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending the Lamar County School System, recipient of the Georgia
2 Reads Community Award, for its dedication to literacy advancement; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council on Literacy is dedicated to improving literacy from birth
4 through adulthood and recognizes that communities play a critical role in addressing low
5 literacy rates; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Reads Community Award was established to recognize
7 communities across Georgia that have formed partnerships of two or more organizations,
8 sustained for at least three years, with a strategic plan and measurable results in literacy
9 improvement; and
10 WHEREAS, the Lamar County School System, utilizing the L4GA process, has strengthened
11 partnerships with local literacy organizations to boost community literacy engagement; and
12 WHEREAS, these meaningful partnerships include the City of Barnesville,
13 Barnesville-Lamar County Library, Milner Library, Ferst Readers of Lamar County, E.P.
14 Roberts Community Center, local daycare centers, and the Lamar County Health
15 Department; and
S. R. 223
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2961
16 WHEREAS, the Lamar County School System was selected as a Georgia Reads Community
17 Award recipient for its diligent efforts to develop and implement innovative programs that
18 have significantly increased literacy rates within its communities; and
19 WHEREAS, these initiatives have led to measurable improvements in reading proficiency,
20 ensuring greater opportunities for success in education and career development; and
21 WHEREAS, the dedication and hard work of the Lamar County School System and its
22 partners serve as an inspiration to others, demonstrating the power of community-driven
23 solutions to address literacy challenges.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
25 congratulate and commend the Georgia Reads Community Award winners, particularly the
26 Lamar County School System, for their exemplary service and unwavering commitment to
27 improving literacy in their communities.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
29 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Lamar County
30 School System.
