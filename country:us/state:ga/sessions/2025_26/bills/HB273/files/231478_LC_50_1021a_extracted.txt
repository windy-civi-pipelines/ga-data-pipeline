Section 1:
25 LC 50 1021
By: Representatives Stephens of the 164th, Kelley of the 16th, Smith of the 138th, Blackmon
of the 146th, Crowe of the 118th, and others
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 5C of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to title
2 ad valorem tax on motor vehicles, so as to change certain definitions regarding such taxation;
3 to provide for an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
5 SECTION 1.
6 Chapter 5C of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to title ad valorem
7 tax on motor vehicles, is amended in Code Section 48-5C-1, relating to alternative ad
8 valorem taxation of motor vehicles and title ad valorem tax fee, by revising paragraph (2) as
9 follows:
10 "(2) 'Immediate family member' means spouse, parent, child, sibling, grandparent, or
11 grandchild."
12 SECTION 2.
13 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
14 without such approval.
H. B. 273

15 SECTION 3.
16 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
