Section 1:
25 Floor Amend 3 AM 62 0005
ADOPTED
46th, Goodman of the 8th, and others offered the following amendment #3:
1 Amend the Senate Committee on Judiciary substitute to SB 57 (LC 62 0129S) by removing
2 "and private enforcement" on lines 3 and 4 and replacing it with "enforcement; to provide
3 for penalties"
4 By inserting between lines 32 and 33 the following:
5 Article 15 of Chapter 1 of Title 10 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
6 deceptive or unfair practices, is amended in Code Section 10-1-439.3, relating to role of
7 Attorney General in prosecuting violators and civil penalties, by adding a new subsection to
8 read as follows:
9 "(d)(1) In an action brought pursuant to this Code section for violation of Part 10 of this
10 article, the Attorney General:
11 (A) Notwithstanding paragraph (3) of subsection (a) of this Code section, shall seek
12 to recover up to $10,000.00 in civil penalties for the first violation, up to $50,000.00 in
13 civil penalties for a second violation, and $250,000.00 for a third or subsequent
14 violation;
15 (B) May bring such action without providing the notice required under subsection (a)
16 of this Code section; and
17 (C) May refer such violator to the Department of Banking and Finance for
18 investigation of any violations of Title 7 and the rules and regulations of the department
19 arising from such violation.

20 (2) If the Attorney General elects to not bring action for a violation of Part 10 of this
