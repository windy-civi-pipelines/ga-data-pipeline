25 LC 61 0197/AP
By: Representatives O’Steen of the 169th and Burchett of the 176th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act to provide a new charter for the City of Broxton, Georgia, in the County
2 of Coffee, approved April 6, 1981 (Ga. L. 1981, p. 3597), as amended, so as to increase the
3 compensation of the mayor and councilmembers; to provide for related matters; to repeal
4 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 An Act to provide a new charter for the City of Broxton, Georgia, in the County of Coffee,
8 approved April 6, 1981 (Ga. L. 1981, p. 3597), as amended, is amended by revising
9 Section 2.3 as follows:
10 "Section 2.3
11 The mayor shall receive as compensation for his or her services $625.00 per month. Each
12 councilmember shall receive as compensation for his or her services $425.00 per month. The
13 mayor and councilmembers shall also be entitled to be reimbursed for their actual and
14 necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties."
H. B. 742
- 1 -
25 LC 61 0197/AP
15 SECTION 2.
16 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 742
