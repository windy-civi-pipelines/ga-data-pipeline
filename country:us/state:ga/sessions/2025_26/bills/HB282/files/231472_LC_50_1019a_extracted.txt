Section 1:
25 LC 50 1019
By: Representatives Smith of the 138th, Williamson of the 112th, Blackmon of the 146th,
Kelley of the 16th, and Crowe of the 118th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 5 of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to ad
2 valorem taxation of property, so as to change certain definitions regarding such taxation; to
3 provide for related matters; to provide for an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and
4 for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 Chapter 5 of Title 48 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to ad valorem
8 taxation of property, is amended in Code Section 48-5-2, relating to definitions regarding
9 such taxation, by revising paragraph (1) as follows:
10 "(1) 'Current use value' of bona fide conservation use property means the amount a
11 knowledgeable buyer would pay for the property with the intention of continuing the
12 property in its existing use and in an arm's length, bona fide sale and shall be determined
13 in accordance with the specifications and criteria provided for in subsection (b) of Code
14 Section 48-5-269."
H. B. 282
- 1 -
25 LC 50 1019
15 SECTION 2.
16 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
17 without such approval.
18 SECTION 3.
19 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
