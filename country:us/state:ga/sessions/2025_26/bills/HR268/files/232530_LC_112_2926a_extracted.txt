Section 1:
25 LC 112 2926
House Resolution 268
of the 172nd, Jackson of the 128th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Georgia Green Industry Association; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Green Industry Association is a state-wide trade organization for
4 the horticulture industry in Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, members of the Georgia Green Industry Association include professionals in
6 the landscape trade, irrigation contractors, wholesale nursery growers, greenhouse operators,
7 retail garden centers, flower growers, and allied sales companies; and
8 WHEREAS, the Georgia Green Industry Association serves as the umbrella association for
9 this state's entire horticulture industry, providing leadership and advocacy for agriculture
10 businesses and enthusiasts; and
11 WHEREAS, the Georgia Green Industry Association and its members will be present at the
12 capitol on February 18, 2025, to speak with elected officials in order to promote and advance
13 the economic lifestyle and environmental benefits of horticulture in Georgia.
H. R. 268
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2926
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
15 the members of this body recognize and commend the Georgia Green Industry Association
16 for its commitment to Georgia horticulture and welcome members of this remarkable
17 organization to the state capitol.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
19 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
20 Georgia Green Industry Association.
H. R. 268
