Section 1:
25 LC 112 3029
Senate Resolution 204
41st, Anderson of the 43rd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the Georgia Vision Alliance for its exemplary contributions to the blind and
2 visually impaired community in Georgia; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Vision Alliance has been a steadfast advocate for the blind and
4 visually impaired community, working tirelessly to advance accessibility, equality, and
5 opportunities across the state of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, this distinguished organization has demonstrated unwavering commitment to
7 creating an inclusive society where individuals with visual impairments can lead
8 independent, fulfilling, and dignified lives; and
9 WHEREAS, the Georgia Vision Alliance has provided invaluable resources, education, and
10 advocacy for policies that promote accessibility, foster independence, and empower those
11 affected by visual impairments; and
12 WHEREAS, under the exceptional leadership of its chairman, Alice Ritchheart, and
13 co-chairmen, Stephanie Scott and McArthur Jarrett, the Georgia Vision Alliance has
14 championed the establishment of a Commission for the Blind in Georgia, a transformative
S. R. 204
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3029
15 initiative that has garnered widespread support and recognition for its potential to improve
16 lives; and
17 WHEREAS, the Alliance's efforts have inspired communities, legislators, and organizations
18 to prioritize the needs of the blind and visually impaired, fostering collaboration and progress
19 in addressing critical accessibility issues; and
20 WHEREAS, the dedication of the Georgia Vision Alliance exemplifies the spirit of
21 compassion, resilience, and service that defines the best of Georgia's citizenry.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 honor and commend the Georgia Vision Alliance for its outstanding service, advocacy, and
24 leadership in the blind and visually impaired community as well as extend best wishes for
25 continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Georgia Vision
28 Alliance.
