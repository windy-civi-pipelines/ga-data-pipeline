25 LC 112 3312
House Resolution 579
By: Representative Mathis of the 133rd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Lilla Lee Purser was born on March 14, 1925, in Cochran, Georgia, and this
3 year she enjoys the rare distinction of celebrating her 100th birthday; and
4 WHEREAS, she graduated from Cochran High School as a member of the Class of 1941,
5 where she played on the girls basketball team, and she continued her education at Middle
6 Georgia College, excelling in both basketball and tennis; and
7 WHEREAS, a devoted and active member of the First Baptist Church of Cochran, Lilla Lee
8 Purser has dedicated many years to teaching Sunday school and serving her fellow
9 congregants; and
10 WHEREAS, she was blessed with 72 years of loving marriage to the late Billy F. Purser, and
11 together they have raised two wonderful children; and
12 WHEREAS, she has brought joy and warmth to those around her through her love of
13 working in her yard, playing bridge, fishing, and cooking, and she now delights in playing
14 bingo, her favorite pastime; and
H. R. 579
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3312
15 WHEREAS, Lilla Lee Purser has witnessed a dramatic change in the world over the course
16 of her lifetime and we salute her contribution to its progress through her tireless efforts on
17 behalf of her community and extended family; and
18 WHEREAS, by the example Lilla Lee Purser has made of her life, she makes this world a
19 better place in which to live, and it is only fitting and proper that she be appropriately
20 honored during this very special and memorable time.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
22 the members of this body commend Lilla Lee Purser for her unselfish, dedicated, and loving
23 service to the members of her family and community; congratulate her on the remarkable
24 occasion of her 100th birthday; and extend their most sincere best wishes for continued
25 health and happiness.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Lilla
28 Lee Purser.
