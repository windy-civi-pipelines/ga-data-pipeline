25 LC 112 2843
House Resolution 150
of the 88th, and Moore of the 91st
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Ms. Corporate America Georgia and Trinity Girls Network Corp. for hosting
2 The Crown & Confidence Summit & Brunch; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the empowerment of women and girls through education, leadership, and
4 personal development is essential for fostering a more equitable and prosperous society; and
5 WHEREAS, Ms. Corporate America Georgia is dedicated to providing a platform for women
6 to excel in leadership, entrepreneurship, and community engagement as well as equipping
7 them with the skills and opportunities to succeed; and
8 WHEREAS, Trinity Girls Network Corp., a 501(c)(3) organization, is committed to
9 supporting women and girls through education, mentorship, and transformational programs
10 that cultivate leadership and service; and
11 WHEREAS, The Crown & Confidence Summit & Brunch serves as a signature event uniting
12 leaders, visionaries, and changemakers to educate, empower, and inspire women and girls
13 in leadership, entrepreneurship, and personal development through curated workshops,
14 keynote addresses, and interactive sessions; and
H. R. 150
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2843
15 WHEREAS, this outstanding event further celebrates the achievements of women and girls
16 who exemplify leadership, confidence, and resilience, providing participants with essential
17 tools, resources, and connections to unlock their full potential and contribute meaningfully
18 to their communities; and
19 WHEREAS, this partnership between Ms. Corporate America Georgia and Trinity Girls
20 Network Corp. exemplifies a shared commitment to uplifting women and girls, providing
21 opportunities for growth, and bridging gaps to create lasting change; and
22 WHEREAS, proceeds from the event support the ongoing work of Trinity Girls Network
23 Corp., enhancing its impact on local and global communities through educational initiatives
24 and mentorship programs; and
25 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that these exemplary organizations be
26 appropriately honored during this special time.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body commend Ms. Corporate America Georgia and Trinity Girls
29 Network Corp. for their shared dedication to empowering women and girls as well as
30 recognize The Crown & Confidence Summit & Brunch as a meaningful initiative advancing
31 leadership, education, and personal development.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
33 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Ms.
34 Corporate America Georgia and Trinity Girls Network Corp.
