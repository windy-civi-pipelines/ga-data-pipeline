25 LC 112 3402
House Resolution 613
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher on October 28, 2024; and
4 WHEREAS, Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher was born on December 6, 1936, in Jacksonville,
5 Florida, a beloved daughter of Joseph and Ophelia Clinton; and
6 WHEREAS, an accomplished educator and leader, Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher served as a
7 senior-level administrator for Boston Public Schools, a school psychologist and
8 post-secondary counselor in Michigan, a professor, a drama coach, a teacher, and the
9 Director of the Southern Region Education Legal Project of the NAACP; and
10 WHEREAS, she further served her community as a proud member of the Alpha Kappa
11 Alpha Sorority, Incorporated, sharing this legacy with her daughter, granddaughter, and
12 oldest sister; and
H. R. 613
- 1 -

13 WHEREAS, a woman of deep and abiding faith, she was an active member of St. Paul AME
14 Church from a young age, participating in Sunday school, the Allen Christian League, the
15 Youth Choir, and Vacation Bible School; and
16 WHEREAS, she was blessed with one remarkable daughter, Rhonda, two wonderful
17 grandchildren, Devin and Jasmyn, and five beloved great-grandchildren, Kamdyn, Kyler,
18 Jordan, Kinsley, and Kamyrah; and
19 WHEREAS, she gave inspiration to many through her high ideals, morals, and deep concern
20 for her fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and understanding she demonstrated to
21 her family and friends were admired by others; and
22 WHEREAS, she was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
23 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example she made of her life,
24 she made this world a better place in which to live; and
25 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous woman, Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher will long be
26 remembered for her love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
27 had the great fortune of knowing her.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher
30 and express their deepest and most sincere regret at her passing.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
32 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
33 family of Audrey Lee Clinton Fisher.
