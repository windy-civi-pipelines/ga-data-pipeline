25 LC 112 3609
House Resolution 915
By: Representative Townsend of the 179th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, a 12-year-old Georgia resident, Celia Young's passion for service was shaped
3 by her time spent with the ACCESS class, where she came to understand the limitations
4 faced by others and felt a calling to make a difference; and
5 WHEREAS, in 2021, she founded HPIN – Helping People in Need to fulfill her desire to
6 help those in need in her community; and
7 WHEREAS, Celia Young first created a team of HPIN Ambassadors by inviting her friends
8 to join the effort, equipping them with t-shirts and encouraging them to raise funds through
9 activities such as lemonade stands and cookie sales; and
10 WHEREAS, through these youth-led initiatives, HPIN has collected and distributed essential
11 goods including sleeping bags, canned food, and school supplies to local organizations such
12 as Saved by Grace, Communities in Schools, and Sparrow's Nest; and
H. R. 915
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3609
13 WHEREAS, Celia Young thoughtfully plans HPIN's annual projects prior to each summer,
14 and in 2024, she organized a successful silent art auction at Live Oak Fitness featuring
15 artwork by her and her Ambassadors, which raised $4,700 to support charitable efforts; and
16 WHEREAS, it is estimated that HPIN has raised between $25,000 and $30,000 in cash and
17 in-kind donations since its founding, making a meaningful impact on families and students
18 throughout the region; and
19 WHEREAS, with the belief that "some people need a little help sometimes," Celia Young
20 has embodied the highest ideals of civic responsibility and philanthropy, making it
21 abundantly fitting and proper that she be appropriately honored.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend Celia Young for her remarkable
24 leadership and service to her community as founder of HPIN – Helping People in Need as
25 well as extend best wishes for continued success.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Celia
28 Young.
