25 LC 112 3224
House Resolution 479
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Kathryn Fletcher for her service as chair of the Georgia
2 Society of Certified Public Accountants; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Kathryn Fletcher, CPA, MBA, CGMA, has demonstrated exceptional
4 leadership and dedication in her role as the chair of the Georgia Society of Certified Public
5 Accountants (GSCPA) for the 2024-2025 Fiscal Year; and
6 WHEREAS, throughout her tenure as chair, she has promoted the accounting profession
7 among young people, engaging with students of all ages to share the many opportunities
8 available within the field; and
9 WHEREAS, she has further served as a strong proponent of financial literacy, recognizing
10 its critical importance in daily life and championing increased awareness and education on
11 financial matters for individuals and communities alike; and
12 WHEREAS, her tenure as GSCPA chair has been marked by a commitment to innovation,
13 education, and advocacy, strengthening the profession's future and ensuring that CPAs
14 remain at the forefront of financial expertise and ethical responsibility; and
H. R. 479
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, her passion for the accounting profession began at an early age, leading her to
16 pursue a distinguished career in public accounting, where she has excelled as a trusted
17 advisor, mentor, and advocate for the profession; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding individual be
19 appropriately honored.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
21 the members of this body recognize and commend Kathryn Fletcher for her exemplary
22 service as chair of the Georgia Society of Certified Public Accountants and extend best
23 wishes for continued success in all future endeavors.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
25 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
26 Kathryn Fletcher.
