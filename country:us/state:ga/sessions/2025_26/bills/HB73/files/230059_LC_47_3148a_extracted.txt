Section 1:
25 LC 47 3148
By: Representative Greene of the 154th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act creating the Board of Education of Seminole County, approved March 14,
2 1983 (Ga. L. 1983, p. 3994), as amended, so as to provide that future elections for members
3 of the board of education shall be nonpartisan; to provide for related matters; to repeal
4 conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
6 SECTION 1.
7 An Act creating the Board of Education of Seminole County, approved March 14, 1983 (Ga.
8 L. 1983, p. 3994), as amended, is amended by revising Section 3 as follows:
9 "SECTION 3.
10 (a) All members who are elected to the board shall be elected in accordance with Chapter 2
11 of Title 21 of the O.C.G.A., the 'Georgia Election Code.' All elections for members of the
12 board conducted after August 1, 2025, shall be nonpartisan elections as provided for in
13 Code Section 21-2-139 of the O.C.G.A. Nothing in this subsection shall affect the terms
14 of office of the members of the board in office on the effective date of this subsection.
H. B. 73
- 1 -
25 LC 47 3148
15 Such sitting members shall serve out the terms of office for which such members were
16 elected and shall be eligible to succeed themselves.
17 (b)(1) Elections shall be held to elect board members from districts one, three, and five
18 at the nonpartisan general election held in 2026.
19 (2) Elections shall be held to elect board members from districts two and four at the
20 nonpartisan general election held in 2028.
21 (3) Successors to the members elected pursuant to paragraphs (1) and (2) of this
22 subsection shall be elected at the nonpartisan general election which is conducted in the
23 year in which the respective terms of office shall expire.
24 (c) Persons elected to the board shall take office on the first day of January following their
25 election and shall serve for a term of four years and until their successors are elected and
26 qualified."
27 SECTION 2.
28 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
