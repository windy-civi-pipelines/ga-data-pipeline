25 LC 112 2915
Senate Resolution 171
and Orrock of the 36th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing February 11, 2025, as the Interfaith Children's Movement's 14th Annual Day
2 at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Interfaith Children's Movement (ICM) has been a steadfast advocate for
4 Georgia's children since its founding in 2001, uniting people of all faiths to create a better
5 future by mobilizing communities across the state to champion policies that promote child
6 well-being, justice, and equity; and
7 WHEREAS, through its advocacy efforts, ICM has played a pivotal role in securing vital
8 protections for Georgia's children, from expanding access to healthcare to strengthening
9 juvenile justice reforms; and
10 WHEREAS, by fostering collaboration among faith leaders, policymakers, and grassroots
11 organizers, ICM has built a movement that speaks with a unified voice on behalf of the most
12 vulnerable; and
13 WHEREAS, the organization's dedication extends beyond policy work as it prepares the next
14 generation of advocates through youth engagement programs such as the Youth Alliance and
S. R. 171
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2915
15 the Youth Advocacy Internship, empowering young people to use their voices to shape a
16 more just and compassionate society; and
17 WHEREAS, on this 14th Annual Day at the state capitol, ICM's tireless commitment to
18 ensuring that all children in Georgia have the opportunity to thrive represents a call to action
19 for leaders, advocates, and communities across the state to continue working toward a future
20 where every child is nurtured, protected, and given the opportunity to succeed.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 recognize February 11, 2025, as the Interfaith Children's Movement's 14th Annual Day at the
23 state capitol in honor of the organization's unwavering efforts on behalf of the children of this
24 state as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
26 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Interfaith
27 Children's Movement.
