25 LC 112 3247
House Resolution 581
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing April 14, 2025, as Cherokee County 911 Foundation Day; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Board of Directors launched the Cherokee County 911 Foundation on April
4 14, 2024; and
5 WHEREAS, the Cherokee County 911 Foundation supports the Cherokee County E 9-1-1
6 Center, which is the primary public safety dispatch point for all law enforcement, fire and
7 emergency services, emergency management, and Reinhardt University in Cherokee County,
8 Georgia; and
9 WHEREAS, the Cherokee County 911 Foundation is the only public safety 501(c)(3)
10 nonprofit foundation in the State of Georgia solely focused on 911 operations; and
11 WHEREAS, the Cherokee County 911 Foundation and its board members have a mission
12 to support, advocate, and advance the Cherokee County E 9-1-1 Center; and
13 WHEREAS, the Cherokee County 911 Foundation and its board members have a vision to
14 be the voice for Cherokee County E 9-1-1; and
H. R. 581
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3247
15 WHEREAS, the Cherokee County 911 Foundation and its board members support, advocate,
16 and advance through programs and initiatives to assist employees of the Cherokee County
17 E 9-1-1 Center by creating an emergency fund, promoting mental and physical health,
18 certification, training, and education, and supporting the Community Outreach Unit; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this day be appropriately recognized in
20 honor of the Cherokee County 911 Foundation and its Board of Directors for their
21 dedication, passion, and hard work in supporting, advocating for, and advancing the
22 Cherokee County E 9-1-1 Center.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body recognize April 14, 2025, as Cherokee County 911 Foundation
25 Day.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
28 public and the press.
H. R. 581
