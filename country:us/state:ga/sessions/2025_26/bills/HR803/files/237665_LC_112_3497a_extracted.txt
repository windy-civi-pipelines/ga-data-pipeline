25 LC 112 3497
House Resolution 803
the 140th, Lupton of the 83rd, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dr. Charis Chambers; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Dr. Charis Chambers is a board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist and
3 specialty-certified pediatric and adolescent gynecologist whose career reflects excellence in
4 medical practice, education, and public advocacy; and
5 WHEREAS, Dr. Chambers graduated magna cum laude from Spelman College in 2010 with
6 a degree in biology, earned her medical degree from the University of Alabama at
7 Birmingham, and completed her residency in obstetrics and gynecology at Greenville
8 Memorial Hospital, where she was inducted into the prestigious medical honor society Alpha
9 Omega Alpha; and
10 WHEREAS, in 2019, she launched her educational platform The Period Doctor to address
11 the lack of accessible, medically accurate reproductive health information and minority
12 physician representation on social media; and
13 WHEREAS, her work as The Period Doctor has earned national attention through television,
14 digital media, and print publications, and her platform now reaches millions of viewers
15 monthly as well as boasts a following of over 270,000 individuals; and
H. R. 803

16 WHEREAS, in 2020, she pursued fellowship training in pediatric and adolescent gynecology
17 at Baylor College of Medicine, completing her training and beginning practice as a
18 physician; and
19 WHEREAS, in September 2022, she transitioned from hospital-employed medicine to
20 relocate to Atlanta, Georgia, with plans to open her own medical practice as well as to
21 continue her education and advocacy in reproductive health; and
22 WHEREAS, Dr. Chambers is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable
23 reputation for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
24 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
25 remarkable and distinguished individual be appropriately recognized.
26 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
27 the members of this body recognize and commend Dr. Charis Chambers for her many
28 outstanding contributions on behalf of the citizens of this state as well as extend the most
29 sincere best wishes for continued health and happiness.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dr.
32 Charis Chambers.
