25 LC 112 2734
House Resolution 103
By: Representative Williamson of the 112th
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Melvin G. Dye III has attained the rank of Eagle Scout, the most distinguished
3 rank conferred by the Boy Scouts of America; and
4 WHEREAS, a candidate for the most exceptional award in scouting must complete a
5 rigorous program of training and demonstrate his proficiency in selected scouting techniques
6 and the attainment of the highest ideals of scouting; and
7 WHEREAS, only one scout out of 97 ever reaches this lofty level of achievement, and
8 Melvin has ably evidenced his aptitude and competency in mastering the various special
9 skills taught through the Scouts BSA program; and
10 WHEREAS, this hard-working young man has demonstrated great leadership skills and
11 initiative and has truly exhibited a commitment to the scouting principles of honesty and
12 fairness during his years with the Scouts BSA program; and
13 WHEREAS, a member of Troop 705, Melvin earned the rank of Eagle Scout by
14 demonstrating leadership, organization, and hands-on skills in the restoration and repair of
H. R. 103
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2734
15 the fence at the McDaniel Tichenor House in Monroe, Georgia, the historic home of Henry
16 D. McDaniel, the 52nd Governor of Georgia; and
17 WHEREAS, as further testament to his excellent scouting, he has earned 27 Merit Badges
18 and a Bronze Palm as well as attended High Adventure Camps at both the Florida National
19 High Adventure Sea Base Camp and Philmont Scout Ranch in New Mexico; and
20 WHEREAS, the perseverance and determination displayed by this exceptional young leader
21 set an example for all Georgians, and great success is surely in this young man's future.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body commend Melvin G. Dye III for his diligence and dedication in
24 pursuing the highest honor awarded by the Boy Scouts of America and congratulate him on
25 attaining the prestigious rank of Eagle Scout.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
27 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
28 Melvin G. Dye III.
