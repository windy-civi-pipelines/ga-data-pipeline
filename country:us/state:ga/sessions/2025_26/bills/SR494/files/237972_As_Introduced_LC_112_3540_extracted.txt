25 LC 112 3540
Senate Resolution 494
18th, Anavitarte of the 31st and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Daniel O'Connor for his service in redistricting and
2 celebrating his birthday; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Daniel O'Connor has demonstrated an exceptional commitment to public
4 service through his extensive career in legislative analysis, policy research, and redistricting
5 in the State of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, Daniel O'Connor has served in various roles over decades, including with the
7 Georgia Republican Party and the Georgia House of Representatives Reapportionment
8 Office, contributing his expertise to the complex and critical task of redistricting legislative
9 districts; and
10 WHEREAS, he has provided invaluable support and guidance to legislators, produced
11 detailed analysis of voting patterns and demographics, and helped ensure fair and effective
12 representation for the people of Georgia; and
13 WHEREAS, his work has been instrumental during multiple redistricting cycles, including
14 the post-2010 cycle and the 2006 adjustments, during which he helped transition Georgia to
15 single-member districts and supported efforts that shaped the legislative map of the state; and
S. R. 494
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3540
16 WHEREAS, Daniel O'Connor's efforts reflect a dedication not only to sound governance and
17 data-informed decision-making but also to upholding the democratic principles of
18 representative government; and
19 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to honor Daniel O'Connor on the occasion of his
20 birthday, celebrated each year on March 25, and to recognize his many years of devoted
21 public service.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 recognize and commend Daniel O'Connor for his outstanding contributions to the State of
24 Georgia through his service in redistricting and reapportionment and extend heartfelt
25 congratulations to him on the occasion of his birthday, March 25, 1963.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Daniel O'Connor.
