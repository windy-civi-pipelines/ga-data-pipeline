25 LC 129 0231
House Resolution 485
Williams of the 168th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Combat Boots 2 Boardroom for its dedication to empowering veterans and
2 recognizing March 11, 2025, as Combat Boots 2 Boardroom Day at the state capitol; and for
3 other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia recognizes the invaluable contributions of our nation's
5 veterans, whose service and sacrifice have safeguarded our freedoms and strengthened our
6 communities; and
7 WHEREAS, many veterans face unique challenges when transitioning from military service
8 to civilian careers, including navigating professional development, entrepreneurship, and
9 leadership opportunities; and
10 WHEREAS, the Combat Boots 2 Boardroom's Veteran Pathway program is a distinguished
11 initiative dedicated to equipping veterans with the skills, knowledge, and resources necessary
12 to thrive in business, corporate leadership, and entrepreneurial endeavors; and
13 WHEREAS, this program provides tailored mentorship, professional training, networking
14 opportunities, and strategic guidance to help veterans translate their military experience into
15 successful civilian careers; and
H. R. 485
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0231
16 WHEREAS, Combat Boots 2 Boardroom has positively impacted the lives of many veterans
17 across Georgia, fostering economic growth, leadership development, and a strong support
18 network for those who have served our country; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding program be
20 appropriately honored for its unwavering commitment to empowering veterans and
21 strengthening our communities.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body commend Combat Boots 2 Boardroom for its dedicated service to
24 Georgia's veteran community, extend best wishes for its continued success in supporting
25 those who have honorably served our nation, and recognize March 11, 2025, as Combat
26 Boots 2 Boardroom Day at the state capitol.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
29 Combat Boots 2 Boardroom.
