25 LC 112 2741
House Resolution 75
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Phillip Benson Ham Sr.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Phillip Benson Ham Sr. on January 11, 2025; and
4 WHEREAS, Phillip Benson Ham Sr. was born on January 6, 1934, in Smarr, Georgia, a
5 beloved son of John Wesley Ham Sr. and Laura Belle Benson Ham; and
6 WHEREAS, he dedicated over six decades to his legal career in Forsyth, Georgia, serving
7 as president of the Flint Judicial Circuit Bar Association and as part of the Board of
8 Governors of the Georgia Bar Association as well as playing an instrumental role in the
9 creation of the Towaliga Judicial Circuit; and
10 WHEREAS, he further served the State of Georgia as a state representative for 10 years, and
11 during his tenure as vice-chair of the State Board of Elections, he championed education
12 reform and economic development in Monroe County; and
H. R. 75
- 1 -

13 WHEREAS, during his 13 years as chairman of the Monroe County Board of Education, he
14 guided the county through desegregation, secured funding for a new high school, and
15 implemented higher salary supplements to attract and retain top teachers; and
16 WHEREAS, he additionally served as president of the Monroe County Community Action
17 Committee and director of the Monroe County Economic Development Authority, bringing
18 major projects such as Plant Scherer and the Georgia Public Safety Training Center to
19 Monroe County; and
20 WHEREAS, Phillip Benson Ham Sr. was a leader in rural electrification and agricultural
21 advocacy, serving as board chairman of the Central Georgia Electric Membership
22 Cooperative, president of the National Rural Utilities Cooperative Finance Corporation, and
23 legislative director of the Georgia Farm Bureau; and
24 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man, Phillip Benson Ham Sr. will long be
25 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
26 had the great fortune of knowing him.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Phillip Benson Ham Sr.
29 and express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 family of Phillip Benson Ham Sr.
H. R. 75
