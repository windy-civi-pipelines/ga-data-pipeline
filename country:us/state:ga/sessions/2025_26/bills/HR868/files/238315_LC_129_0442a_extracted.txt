25 LC 129 0442
House Resolution 868
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Creekside High School girls basketball team for winning the 2025 GHSA
2 Class 4A State Basketball Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Creekside High School girls basketball team has established and maintained
4 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team has completed another triumphant season of basketball
6 competition by posting an outstanding record of 32 wins and only no losses; and
7 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
8 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
9 2025 campaign, the team captured its first state basketball championship; and
10 WHEREAS, the team brought special recognition to its school by winning the 2024 GHSA
11 Class 4A State Basketball Championship against Maynard Jackson with a final score of 57
12 to 37; and
13 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
14 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the

15 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Head Coach
16 Akennia Ellis; and
17 WHEREAS, Head Coach Akennia Ellis was named Region 4-AAAA Coach of the Year,
18 GPB Sports 4A Coach of the Year, Sandy’s Spiel 4A Coach of the Year and Georgia Athletic
19 Coaches Association Coach of the Year; and
20 WHEREAS, the team included all star performers Jacy Mitchell, Skye Cleveland, and
21 C’India Dennis; and
22 WHEREAS, C’India Dennis and Skye Cleveland both surpassed 1,000 career points this
23 season; and
24 WHEREAS, Skye Cleveland was selected as All State by the Georgia Athletic Coaches
25 Association and for the Georgia Basketball Coaches Association All Star Team; and
26 WHEREAS, C’India Dennis has been recognized as Morgan and Morgan 4A State
27 Championship MVP, Georgia Athletic Coaches Association 4A South Player of the Year,
28 Sandy’s Spiel 4A Player of the Year and 1st Team All-State, Region 4-AAAA Player of the
29 Year, Atlanta Tip Off Club 3rd Team Honoree, and is scholar athlete with a 3.45 GPA; and
30 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
31 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
32 abundantly proper to call them champions.
33 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
34 the members of this body congratulate the Creekside High School girls basketball team for

39 Creekside High School girls basketball team.
