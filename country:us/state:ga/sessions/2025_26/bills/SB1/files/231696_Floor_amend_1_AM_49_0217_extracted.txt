3 (6) The provisions of this subsection shall not apply to a student who identifies as a
4 transgender female and who has not undergone endogenous, or naturally occurring, male
5 puberty due to the clinically supervised use of puberty-blocking medication at or before
6 the onset of puberty, as evidenced by an affidavit from such student's licensed healthcare
7 provider confirming the administration of such treatment and its effectiveness in
8 preventing the physiological changes associated with male puberty.
