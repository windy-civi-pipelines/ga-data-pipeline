25 LC 112 3194
House Resolution 447
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Nya Aaliyah Floyd; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Nya Aaliyah Floyd, a distinguished and accomplished young woman from
3 Lithonia, Georgia, has demonstrated exceptional academic achievement, leadership, and
4 dedication to service throughout her educational journey; and
5 WHEREAS, as a first-generation college student, she earned an associate degree in business
6 administration from Herzing University with an outstanding 3.9 GPA and is currently
7 pursuing a Bachelor of Science in agriculture at Fort Valley State University, with plans to
8 graduate this year and pursue her passion for working with animals; and
9 WHEREAS, deeply committed to student engagement and community service, she is an
10 active member of the Truth Poetry Club, New Student Orientation Leaders, Fort Valley State
11 University's Choir, Royal Elite Modeling Troupe, and Miss Mary Magdalene; and
12 WHEREAS, she has further distinguished herself through membership in esteemed
13 organizations, having been initiated into the Alpha Phi Omega National Service Fraternity,
14 Incorporated, Phi Zeta Chapter in Spring 2023 as well as the Delta Sigma Theta Sorority,
15 Incorporated, Eta Chapter in Spring 2024; and
H. R. 447
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3194
16 WHEREAS, her leadership and poise have earned her numerous prestigious titles, including
17 serving as the 87th Miss Fort Valley State University and being crowned the 39th Miss
18 National Black College Alumni Hall of Fame; and
19 WHEREAS, she previously served with distinction as the 2023-2024 Miss Junior on the Fort
20 Valley State University Royal Court and as Miss Black for the Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity,
21 Incorporated, Gamma Zeta Chapter; and
22 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
23 remarkable and distinguished student leader be appropriately honored.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
25 the members of this body recognize and commend Nya Aaliyah Floyd for her exceptional
26 achievements, leadership, and contributions to her community as well as extend the most
27 sincere best wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Nya
30 Aaliyah Floyd.
