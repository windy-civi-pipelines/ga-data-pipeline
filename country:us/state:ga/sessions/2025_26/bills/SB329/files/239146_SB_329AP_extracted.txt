Section 1:
25 LC 62 0167
By: Senators Strickland of the 42nd, Davenport of the 17th, Williams of the 25th and Jones
of the 10th
AS PASSED
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act providing supplements to the salaries of the judges of superior court, the
2 district attorney, and the chief assistant district attorney of the Flint Judicial Circuit, approved
3 March 21, 1984 (Ga. L. 1984, p. 705), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 9,
4 1999 (Ga. L. 1999, p. 4169), so as to provide the chief judge with an annual stipend in
5 addition to county compensation to superior court judges; to provide for related matters; to
6 repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 An Act providing supplements to the salaries of the judges of superior court, the district
10 attorney, and the chief assistant district attorney of the Flint Judicial Circuit, approved
11 March 21, 1984 (Ga. L. 1984, p. 705), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 9,
12 1999 (Ga. L. 1999, p. 4169), is amended by adding a new section to read as follows:
13 "SECTION 1.1.
14 The governing authority of Henry County, such county comprising the Flint Judicial
15 Circuit, shall provide from county funds an annual stipend in addition to the compensation
16 received from state funds for the chief judge of the superior court in the amount of
S. B. 329
- 1 -
25 LC 62 0167
17 $10,000.00 per year, to be paid in 12 equal monthly payments. The stipend provided by
18 this section shall be in addition to any county supplements provided to each judge of the
19 superior court."
20 SECTION 2.
21 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
S. B. 329
