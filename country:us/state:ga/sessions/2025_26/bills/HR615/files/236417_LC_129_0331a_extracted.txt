25 LC 129 0331
House Resolution 615
142nd, and Reese of the 140th
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr.; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of one of its most distinguished citizens
3 with the passing of Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr.; and
4 WHEREAS, Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr. was a beloved son of Bryant and Ruth Herring;
5 and
6 WHEREAS, he served as a guardian of this nation's freedom and liberty with the United
7 States Army, valiantly and courageously protecting his fellow Americans as a paratrooper
8 during the Vietnam War; and
9 WHEREAS, a man of deep and abiding faith, Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr. served as the
10 Senior Pastor of Glorious Hope Missionary Baptist Church in Macon, Georgia for 16 years;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, he loved his community and served as a Tubman Torchbearer, a fundraising
13 arm of the Tubman African American Museum, a 20-year coach of Macon Little League, and
14 as a mentor for hundreds of young people across Middle Georgia; and
H. R. 615
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, he was united in love and marriage to his wife, Lena, and was blessed with four
16 remarkable children, Wallace Jr., Lisa, John, and Moses; and
17 WHEREAS, Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr. gave inspiration to many through his high ideals,
18 morals, and deep concern for his fellow citizens, and the devotion, patience, and
19 understanding he demonstrated to his family and friends were admired by others; and
20 WHEREAS, he was a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation
21 for integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness, and by the example he made of his life, he
22 made this world a better place in which to live; and
23 WHEREAS, a compassionate and generous man who demonstrated his commitment to
24 witnessing Christ through word and deed, Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr. will long be
25 remembered for his love of family, friendship, and community and will be missed by all who
26 had the great fortune of knowing him.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
28 the members of this body join in honoring the life and memory of Pastor Wallace B. Herring
29 Sr. and express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 family of Pastor Wallace B. Herring Sr.
H. R. 615
