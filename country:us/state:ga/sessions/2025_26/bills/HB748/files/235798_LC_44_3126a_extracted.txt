Section 1:
25 LC 44 3126
By: Representatives Smith of the 18th, Smith of the 70th, Huddleston of the 72nd, and Howard
of the 71st
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act placing the coroner of Carroll County on an annual salary, approved
2 April 17, 1973 (Ga. L. 1973, p. 2924), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 4,
3 1997 (Ga. L. 1997, p. 3716), so as to change the compensation of the coroner; to provide for
4 related matters; to provide an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other
5 purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 An Act placing the coroner of Carroll County on an annual salary, approved April 17, 1973
9 (Ga. L. 1973, p. 2924), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 4, 1997 (Ga. L.
10 1997, p. 3716), is amended by revising Section 2 as follows:
11 "SECTION 2.
12 The coroner of Carroll County shall receive an annual base salary of $43,620.00, payable
13 monthly from county funds. The coroner shall also receive an expense allowance of
14 $3,100.00 per year, payable monthly from county funds under such conditions as the county
H. B. 748

15 governing authority may provide. The amounts set forth in this section shall be subject to
16 annual adjustments as provided for under O.C.G.A. Section 36-5-28."
17 SECTION 2.
18 This Act shall become effective on July 1, 2025.
19 SECTION 3.
20 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
