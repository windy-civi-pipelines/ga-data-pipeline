Section 1:
25 LC 44 3088
By: Representatives Jones of the 47th, Thomas of the 21st, and Martin of the 49th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Code Section 25-10-2 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 prohibited fireworks activities and application of noise ordinances, so as to authorize local
3 governments to prohibit the use or ignition of consumer fireworks in proximity to certain
4 facilities housing equines; to provide for special event permits; to provide for definitions; to
5 provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 Code Section 25-10-2 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to prohibited
9 fireworks activities and application of noise ordinances, is amended by adding a new
10 subsection to read as follows:
11 "(d)(1) As used in this subsection, the term 'equine' shall have the same meaning as set
12 forth in Code Section 2-8-121.
13 (2) The governing authority of a county or municipal corporation may adopt an
14 ordinance prohibiting the use or ignition of consumer fireworks within 100 yards of a
15 commercial facility housing equines in a confined area.
H. B. 614
- 1 -
25 LC 44 3088
16 (3) An ordinance adopted by the governing authority of a county or municipal
17 corporation pursuant to paragraph (2) of this subsection may include provisions
18 authorizing the issuance by such governing authority of special event permits to allow the
19 temporary use or ignition of consumer fireworks within 100 yards of a commercial
20 facility housing equines in a confined area."
21 SECTION 2.
22 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
