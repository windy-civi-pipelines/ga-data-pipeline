25 LC 129 0454
House Resolution 944
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending V&L Research and Consulting; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, V&L Research and Consulting will be celebrating the 35th anniversary of its
3 founding; and
4 WHEREAS, under the guiding hand of its owners, Mrs. Lomax Taylor and Mrs. Virgil, and
5 with the exemplary work ethic demonstrated daily by its dedicated employees, this
6 outstanding company has grown from its humble beginnings in 1991 to become one of the
7 top 50 market research firms proudly certified by the American Marketing Association; and
8 WHEREAS, V&L Research and Consulting has established a glowing reputation of renown
9 throughout Georgia for its ability to empower clients by providing vital insights to enhance,
10 develop, and assess their services through comprehensive data analysis, focus groups,
11 surveys, and other innovative research methodologies; and
12 WHEREAS, throughout the company's 35 year history, it has conducted hundreds of studies
13 to uncover key consumer insights and assess public perceptions in a wide range of sectors,
14 from local, state, and national government agencies, such as the United States Department
15 of Homeland Security, the Department of Commerce, and the Centers for Disease Control

16 and Prevention, to private-sector giants like Bank of America, Coca-Cola USA, Delta
17 Airlines, McDonald's, Procter & Gamble, Target, Walmart, and Visa; and
18 WHEREAS, V&L Research and Consulting has been recognized for its leadership and
19 innovation with numerous awards, including the Atlanta Business League's Success Against
20 the Odds Award, the DeKalb Chamber of Commerce Women's Business of the Year
21 Stargazers Award, the American Marketing Association Innovative Leadership Award, and
22 Minority Enterprise Development Service Firm of the Year. We were also honored to serve
23 as a delegate for the White House Conference on Small Business; and
24 WHEREAS, a testament to its legacy of excellence and impact in the field of market
25 research, V&L Research and Consulting was inducted into the Atlanta Business League
26 Business Hall of Fame for Pioneering Entrepreneurial Endeavors; and
27 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of this
28 extraordinary company be appropriately recognized.
29 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
30 the members of this body congratulate V&L Research and Consulting upon the grand
31 occasion of its 35th anniversary, commend it for its many outstanding contributions on behalf
32 of the citizens of Georgia, and extend best wishes for its continued growth, development, and
33 success.
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
35 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to V&L
36 Research and Consulting.
