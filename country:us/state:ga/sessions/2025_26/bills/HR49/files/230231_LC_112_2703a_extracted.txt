25 LC 112 2703
House Resolution 49
75th, Park of the 107th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing January 18, 2025, as Manuel Estevan Paez Teran Day; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Manuel Estevan Paez Teran was a passionate environmentalist who was
3 committed to their advocacy for nature and envisioned a more just world that cared for
4 people and the environment; and
5 WHEREAS, Manuel Teran lived a life dedicated to that vision, working tirelessly to protect
6 forests and nature; and
7 WHEREAS, on January 18, 2023, Manuel Teran was killed in Atlanta while peacefully
8 protesting for the environment; and
9 WHEREAS, on January 18, 2025, family, friends, and supporters will gather to honor the
10 second anniversary of their passing; and
11 WHEREAS, we must honor the life of Manuel Teran and support the environmentalists who
12 continue this important work; and
H. R. 49
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2703
13 WHEREAS, we must continue Manuel Teran's legacy of fighting for justice, peace, and the
14 preservation of our environment, which is essential for life on Earth.
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
16 the members of this body recognize January 18, 2025, as Manuel Estevan Paez Teran Day
17 in honor of their life's work to protect our environment and build a better future for all.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
19 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
20 public and the press.
H. R. 49
