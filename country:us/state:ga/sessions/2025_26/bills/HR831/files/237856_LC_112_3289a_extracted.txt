25 LC 112 3289
House Resolution 831
and Reeves of the 99th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Alpharetta Raiders Volleyball Team for winning the 2024 GHSA 6A
2 Volleyball State Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Alpharetta Raiders Volleyball Team has established and maintained a proud
4 tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team completed a triumphant season of volleyball competition
6 by posting an outstanding, undefeated record of 36 wins and zero losses; and
7 WHEREAS, outstanding play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this
8 team a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its
9 remarkable 2024 campaign, the team captured back-to-back state titles and secured the third
10 volleyball state championship in school history; and
11 WHEREAS, the team's phenomenal performance is a clear indication of the outstanding
12 athletic ability, dedication, and hard work of each member, as well as their unity of purpose
13 and spirit, superb offensive and defensive talents, and the astute direction of Head Coaches
14 Marshall King and Grace Fossier; and
H. R. 831
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
16 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
17 abundantly proper to call them champions.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
19 the members of this body commend the Alpharetta Raiders Volleyball Team for winning the
20 2024 GHSA 6A Volleyball State Championship and extend their most sincere best wishes
21 for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
24 Alpharetta Raiders Volleyball Team.
