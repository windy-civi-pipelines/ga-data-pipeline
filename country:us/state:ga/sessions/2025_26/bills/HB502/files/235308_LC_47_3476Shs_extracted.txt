Section 1:
25 LC 47 3476S
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 2 of Title 21 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to
2 elections and primaries generally, so as to provide for preferential treatment during advance
3 voting to voters accompanied by children two years of age or younger; to revise provisions
4 related to voters who need assistance; to provide for preferential treatment during certain
5 hours on election day to voters accompanied by children two years of age or younger; to
6 revise provisions related to voters who need assistance; to provide for related matters; to
7 repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
9 SECTION 1.
10 Chapter 2 of Title 21 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to elections and
11 primaries generally, is amended by revising Code Section 21-2-385.1, relating to preferential
12 treatment for older and disabled voters, as follows:
13 "21-2-385.1.
14 During the period of advance voting established pursuant to subsection (d) of Code
15 Section 21-2-385, each elector who is 75 years of age or older, who is accompanied by one
16 or more children two years of age or younger, or who is disabled and requires assistance
H. B. 502 (SUB)

17 in casting an absentee ballot in person at the registrar's office, absentee ballot clerk's office,
18 or other locations as provided for in Code Section 21-2-382 such that standing in line
19 would be impracticable shall, upon request to a designated office employee or other
20 individual, be authorized to vote immediately at the next available voting compartment or
21 booth without having to wait in line if such location utilizes direct recording electronic
22 voting systems or be authorized to go to the head of any line necessary to cast a written
23 absentee ballot. Notice of the provisions of this Code section shall be prominently
24 displayed in the registrar's office or absentee ballot clerk's office."
25 SECTION 2.
26 Said chapter is further amended by revising Code Section 21-2-409.1, relating to voting by
27 electors who are 75 years of age or older or disabled electors without having to wait in line,
28 as follows:
29 "21-2-409.1.
30 On election day between the hours of 9:30 A.M. and 4:30 P.M., each elector who is 75
31 years of age or older, who is accompanied by one or more children two years of age or
32 younger, or who is disabled and requires assistance in voting as authorized by Code
33 Section 21-2-409, such that standing in line would be impracticable shall, upon request to
34 a poll officer, be authorized at any primary or election to vote immediately at the next
35 available voting compartment or booth without having to wait in line. Notice of the
36 provisions of this Code section shall be prominently displayed in the voting place."
37 SECTION 3.
38 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
