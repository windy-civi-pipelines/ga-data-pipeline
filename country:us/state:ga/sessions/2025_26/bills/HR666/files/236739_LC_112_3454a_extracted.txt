25 LC 112 3454
House Resolution 666
of the 110th, and Mitchell of the 88th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Stacia Mac; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Stacia Mac is a distinguished serial entrepreneur, entertainment executive, and
3 community advocate whose vision and dedication have profoundly impacted the music
4 industry, business sector, and underserved communities; and
5 WHEREAS, as the Founder and CEO of House of Legends Records, Stacia Mac has
6 demonstrated extraordinary leadership in the entertainment industry, providing strategic
7 guidance and opportunities for artists, including her pivotal role in negotiating her son Polo
8 G's record deal with Columbia Records, thus solidifying a path for his rise in the music
9 industry; and
10 WHEREAS, beyond the realm of entertainment, Stacia Mac has shown a steadfast
11 commitment to empowering communities through education, mentorship, and philanthropy,
12 as evidenced by her recognition as one of Billboard's Women in Music Top Executives in
13 2022; and

14 WHEREAS, her entrepreneurial acumen extends into new business ventures, including the
15 launch of her candle line in 2024, further demonstrating her versatility and innovative spirit;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, her passion for social justice and humanitarian efforts led to the founding of the
18 "Find Her Way" bail bond project, an initiative dedicated to supporting incarcerated women
19 in Chicago, and her philanthropic work includes organizing food, clothing, school fee, and
20 prom dress giveaways to uplift and support her community; and
21 WHEREAS, through The Mac Effect, Stacia Mac continues to serve as a beacon of hope and
22 empowerment, inspiring youth by providing mentorship, financial literacy education,
23 business development programs, and entrepreneurial resources to foster self-sufficiency and
24 generational wealth; and
25 WHEREAS, her unwavering dedication to empowerment, leadership, and community service
26 has left an indelible mark on the lives of many, exemplifying the values of perseverance,
27 integrity, and servant leadership.
28 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
29 the members of this body recognize and commend Stacia Mac for her outstanding
30 contributions to the entertainment industry, business sector, and community empowerment
31 initiatives as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
33 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Stacia
34 Mac.
