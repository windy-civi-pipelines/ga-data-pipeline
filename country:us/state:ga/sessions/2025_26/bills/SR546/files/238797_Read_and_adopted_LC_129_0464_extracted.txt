25 LC 129 0464
Senate Resolution 546
and Harbison of the 15th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Nyala Allen on Women Veterans Appreciation Day at the
2 Capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Nyala Allen served as a guardian of this nation's freedom and liberty with the
4 United States Army, valiantly and courageously protecting her fellow Americans from 1996
5 to 2012 and
6 WHEREAS, as CEO of Key Coaching & Consulting, LLC, and Founder of ACE Veteran
7 Realty, LLC, she delivers innovative, wellness-centered solutions that elevate leadership
8 performance, enhance emotional intelligence, and drive sustainable, purpose-driven success;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, Key Coaching & Consulting, LLC, founded in 2023, delivers personalized
11 coaching programs focusing on wellness, mindset transformation, and sustainable habit
12 development; and
13 WHEREAS, ACE Veteran Realty, LLC, is a veteran-owned real estate firm supporting
14 diverse clients, with a focus on military families and veterans using VA loan benefits for
15 homeownership and wealthbuilding strategies; and
S. R. 546
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0464
16 WHEREAS, as a testament to her outstanding entrepreneurship and service, Nyala has
17 received the President's Lifetime Achievement Award, in 2024, and is a member of Northeast
18 Atlanta Metro Association of Realtors' Million Dollar Club; and
19 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
20 exemplary veteran are appropriately honored.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 recognize and commend Nyala Allen for the dedication, bravery, and sacrifices she has made
23 to serve her country and extend sincere best wishes for her continued health, happiness, and
24 success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
26 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Nyala Allen.
