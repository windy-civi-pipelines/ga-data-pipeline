Section 1:
25 LC 62 0166
By: Representative DeLoach of the 167th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act providing for the election of the Board of Education of Long County,
2 approved March 23, 1977 (Ga. L. 1977, p. 3293), as amended, particularly by an Act
3 approved June 3, 2003 (Ga. L. 2003, p. 4390), so as to provide compensation for board of
4 education members, the vice chairperson, and the chairperson; to provide for related matters;
5 to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
7 SECTION 1.
8 An Act providing for the election of the Board of Education of Long County, approved
9 March 23, 1977 (Ga. L. 1977, p. 3293), as amended, particularly by an Act approved June
10 3, 2003 (Ga. L. 2003, p. 4390), is amended by revising Section 4 as follows:
11 "Section 4. Each member of the board of education shall be compensated in the amount
12 of $500.00 per month. The vice chairperson shall be compensated $600.00 per month. The
13 chairperson shall be compensated $700.00 per month."
14 SECTION 2.
15 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 799
