25 LC 129 0018
Senate Resolution 34
Dixon of the 45th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating the Grayson High School football team for winning the 2024 GHSA Class
2 6A State Football Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Grayson High School football team has established and maintained a proud
4 tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
5 WHEREAS, this superlative team has completed another triumphant season of football
6 competition by posting an outstanding record of 14 wins and only one loss; and
7 WHEREAS, excellent play, superior teamwork, and extraordinary resilience made this team
8 a spectacular one to observe throughout the season, and as a fitting finale to its remarkable
9 2024 campaign, the team captured its fourth state football championship; and
10 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
11 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
12 team; its superb offensive and defensive talents; and the astute direction of Head Coach
13 Santavious Bryant; and
S. R. 34
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0018
14 WHEREAS, this magnificent team performed with precision during the season and earned
15 its well-deserved reputation for high standards of athletic achievement, the quality of its play,
16 and the stiff competition it presented to its opponents.
17 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
18 congratulate the Grayson High School football team for winning the 2024 GHSA Class 6A
19 State Football Championship and extend best wishes for continued success.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
21 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the Grayson High
22 School football team.
S. R. 34
