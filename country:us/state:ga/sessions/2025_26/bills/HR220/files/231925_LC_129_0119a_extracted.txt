25 LC 129 0119
House Resolution 220
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Dereck Isaiah Reed; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Dereck Isaiah Reed is an 11th grade student at Global Impact Academy with
3 a deep passion for civil engagement, politics, neuroscience, debate, and philosophy; and
4 WHEREAS, he actively participates in numerous academic and extracurricular activities,
5 including the debate club, the Fulton County Youth Commission, the Future Business
6 Leaders of America, the AJC Black and Jewish Teen Initiative, tutoring, the National Honor
7 Society, and the Fulton County Advisory Council, while also playing basketball for
8 Creekside High School; and
9 WHEREAS, dedicated to community service and involvement, he is currently interning with
10 State Representative Lydia Glaize, where he engages in political initiatives and helps those
11 in need by distributing food and essentials; and
12 WHEREAS, beyond his academic and civic pursuits, he enjoys playing basketball, watching
13 podcasts, reading nonfiction, playing chess, and spending time with his family, friends, and
14 pet cat; and
H. R. 220
- 1 -

15 WHEREAS, Dereck aspires to attend Howard University or another Historically Black
16 College or University (HBCU) as he pursues his goal of becoming a neurosurgeon; and
17 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding achievements of this
18 remarkable student be appropriately honored.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
20 the members of this body recognize and commend Dereck Isaiah Reed for his exceptional
21 student leadership and extend best wishes for future success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
23 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to
24 Dereck Isaiah Reed.
