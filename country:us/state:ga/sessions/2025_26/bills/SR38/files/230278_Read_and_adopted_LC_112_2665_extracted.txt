25 LC 112 2665
Senate Resolution 38
49th, Watson of the 11th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the University of Georgia 4-H Program, Kingston Ryals, Melanie Biersmith,
2 and the 2024-2025 4-H Leadership Teams and recognizing February 25, 2025, as 4-H Day
3 at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, 4-H members consistently demonstrate the ability and leadership necessary to
5 be champions and serve as outstanding representatives and officers of the young people of
6 Georgia; and
7 WHEREAS, Kingston Ryals of Toombs County serves with distinction and dedication as
8 State 4-H President; and
9 WHEREAS, Melanie Biersmith of the University of Georgia Cooperative Extension has
10 worked diligently as State 4-H Leader/Director of 4-H; and
11 WHEREAS, the leadership, dedication, and hard work of Robert Thomas of Putnam County,
12 Ceci Ward of Catoosa County, Chloe Bray of Johnson County, and Max Nelson of Tift
13 County as District Presidents have been acknowledged by the 4-H membership in their
14 election of each of these young people to serve as president for 2024-2025; and
S. R. 38

15 WHEREAS, the excellent record of accomplishments in citizenship and leadership of Tiffani
16 McClain of Jasper County, Tyson Mathis of Oconee County, Shubham Badve of Cobb
17 County, Kingston Ryals of Toombs County, Thomas Holt of Ben Hill County, and Colin
18 Williamson of Tift County for their selection as delegates for the 2024 National 4-H
19 Conference; and
20 WHEREAS, Lauren Coley of Bleckley County and Sophia Scott of Pickens County were
21 named 2024 Leadership in Action winners; and
22 WHEREAS, Leila Rimes of Tift County, Amiyah Elam of Ware County, Haya Fatmi of
23 Cobb County, Brittney Bryant of Toombs County, Tiffani McClain of Jasper County, and
24 Carolyn Thomas of Putnam County were named state winners in the 2024 Deans Awards
25 competition; and
26 WHEREAS, Patrick Willis of Berrien County was named the 2024 William H. Booth Award
27 Honoree; and
28 WHEREAS, Laura Mirarchi of Troup County received the 2024 Ryles Rising Star Award;
29 and
30 WHEREAS, Kristen Quinton of Bibb County was named the 2024 Volunteer of the Year;
31 and
32 WHEREAS, CJ Lee of Glynn County received the 2024 Outstanding Lifetime Volunteer
33 Award.
34 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
35 commend the University of Georgia 4-H Program; Mr. Kingston Ryals, State 4-H President;
S. R. 38

36 Ms. Melanie Biersmith, State 4-H Leader and Director of 4-H; and the 2024-2025 4-H
37 District Presidents, 2024 National 4-H Conference delegates, 2024 Leadership in Action
38 winners, 2024 Deans Awards winners, 2024 William H. Booth Award honoree, 2024 Ryles
39 Rising Star Award recipient, 2024 Volunteer of the Year, and 2024 Outstanding Lifetime
40 Volunteer Award recipient for their numerous accomplishments as well as recognize
41 February 25, 2025, as 4-H Day at the state capitol.
42 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
43 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the public and the
