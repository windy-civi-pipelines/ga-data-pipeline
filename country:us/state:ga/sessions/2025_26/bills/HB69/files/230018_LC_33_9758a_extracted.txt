Section 1:
25 LC 33 9758
By: Representatives Burns of the 159th, Jones of the 47th, Efstration of the 104th, and Hatchett
of the 155th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend an Act making and providing appropriations for the State Fiscal Year beginning
2 July 1, 2024, and ending June 30, 2025, known as the "General Appropriations Act," Act No.
3 709, approved May 7, 2024, so as to make, provide, and change certain appropriations for
4 the operation of the state government and its departments, boards, bureaus, commissions,
5 institutions, and other agencies, for the university system, common schools, counties,
6 municipalities, and political subdivisions, for all other governmental activities, projects, and
7 undertakings authorized by law, and for all leases, contracts, agreements, and grants
8 authorized by law; to provide for the control and administration of funds; to provide an
9 effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
11 PART I
12 An Act making and providing appropriations for the State Fiscal Year beginning July 1,
13 2024, and ending June 30, 2025, known as the "General Appropriations Act," Act No. 709,
14 approved May 7, 2024, is amended by striking everything following the Part I designation
15 up to but not including the Part II designation and by substituting in lieu thereof the
16 following:
H. B. 69
- 1 -
25 LC 33 9758
17 "The sums of money hereinafter provided are appropriated for the State Fiscal Year
18 beginning July 1, 2024, and ending June 30, 2025, as prescribed hereinafter for such fiscal
19 year:"
20 PART II
21 This Act shall become effective upon its approval by the Governor or upon its becoming law
22 without such approval.
23 PART III
24 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
H. B. 69
- 2 -
