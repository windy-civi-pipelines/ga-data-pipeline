25 LC 112 2963
Senate Resolution 222
16th and Setzler of the 37th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Marietta City Schools, recipient of the Georgia Reads
2 Community Award, for its dedication to literacy advancement; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Council on Literacy is dedicated to improving literacy from birth
4 through adulthood and recognizes that communities play a critical role in addressing low
5 literacy rates; and
6 WHEREAS, the Georgia Reads Community Award was established to recognize
7 communities across Georgia that have formed partnerships of two or more organizations,
8 sustained for at least three years, with a strategic plan and measurable results in literacy
9 improvement; and
10 WHEREAS, Marietta City Schools is a recipient of the Georgia Reads Community Award
11 for its city-wide collaboration Literacy & Justice For All, which began in Marietta to support
12 language and literacy from the third trimester of pregnancy through age five, with the goal
13 of building the deep reading brain of every child; and
14 WHEREAS, its meaningful partnerships include Marietta City Schools, United Way of
15 Greater Atlanta, Rollins Center for Language and Literacy, Cobb Collaborative, and
S. R. 222
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2963
16 Wellstar, demonstrating a strong commitment to literacy development through early
17 childhood education and community collaboration; and
18 WHEREAS, Marietta City Schools has diligently developed and implemented innovative
19 programs that have significantly increased literacy rates within its communities; and
20 WHEREAS, these initiatives have led to measurable improvements in reading proficiency,
21 ensuring greater opportunities for success in education and career development; and
22 WHEREAS, the dedication and hard work of Marietta City Schools and its partners serve as
23 an inspiration to others, demonstrating the power of community-driven solutions to address
24 literacy challenges.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
26 congratulate and commend the Georgia Reads Community Award recipients, particularly
27 Marietta City Schools, for their exemplary service and unwavering commitment to improving
28 literacy in their communities.
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
30 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Marietta City
31 Schools.
S. R. 222
