25 LC 112 3200
House Resolution 460
of the 70th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending the Carroll County Chamber of Commerce on its 70th anniversary, Leadership
2 Carroll on its 33rd anniversary, and Carroll Tomorrow on its 25th anniversary and
3 recognizing the Leadership Carroll Class of 2025; and for other purposes.
4 WHEREAS, the Carroll County Chamber of Commerce has served as a vital organization
5 for businesses and community leaders, fostering economic growth, advocacy, and
6 collaboration for the past 70 years; and
7 WHEREAS, since its founding in 1954, the Carroll County Chamber of Commerce has
8 played a key role in supporting local businesses, workforce development, and regional
9 prosperity, making significant contributions to the economic success of Carroll County and
10 West Georgia; and
11 WHEREAS, Leadership Carroll, established 33 years ago, has been instrumental in
12 identifying, educating, and developing leaders within the community, equipping them with
13 the knowledge and skills to address the challenges and opportunities of Carroll County; and
H. R. 460
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3200
14 WHEREAS, Carroll Tomorrow, celebrating 25 years of economic and community
15 development, has worked to strengthen the county's business climate, enhance job creation,
16 and improve quality of life through strategic initiatives and partnerships; and
17 WHEREAS, the Leadership Carroll Class of 2025 represents the next generation of leaders
18 who are dedicated to serving Carroll County, engaging in meaningful dialogue, and
19 contributing to the continued growth and success of the region; and
20 WHEREAS, these organizations and programs exemplify a commitment to leadership,
21 innovation, and progress, ensuring that Carroll County remains a thriving community for
22 businesses, families, and future generations.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body commend the Carroll County Chamber of Commerce on its 70th
25 anniversary, Leadership Carroll on its 33rd anniversary, and Carroll Tomorrow on its 25th
26 anniversary for their outstanding service and dedication to the citizens of Carroll County.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Leadership Carroll Class of 2025 is recognized and
28 encouraged to continue their leadership journey with excellence and commitment to the
29 betterment of Carroll County and the State of Georgia.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 Carroll County Chamber of Commerce, Leadership Carroll, Carroll Tomorrow, and the
33 Leadership Carroll Class of 2025.
