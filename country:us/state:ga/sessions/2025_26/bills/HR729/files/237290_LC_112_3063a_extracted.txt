25 LC 112 3063
House Resolution 729
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the life and memory of Kaspyen Rhys Tidwell-Krantz; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the State of Georgia mourns the loss of a precious young citizen with the
3 passing of Kaspyen Rhys Tidwell-Krantz; and
4 WHEREAS, Kaspyen Rhys Tidwell-Krantz was born at 2:31 PM in Warner Robins, Georgia,
5 on August 15, 2024, weighing eight pounds, four ounces, to Mr. James Hayden Tidwell and
6 Mrs. Olivia Marie Krantz, who showed him immense love and devotion throughout his life;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, Kaspyen was a little warrior whose resilient spirit and contagious joy touched
9 the hearts of everyone he encountered, facing every challenge with strength and a smile; and
10 WHEREAS, he found joy in the simple pleasures of life, from feeling the sand between his
11 toes at the beach to giggling on his play mats, roaring like a lion with his father, splashing
12 in the bath, and playing with his favorite rattles; and
H. R. 729
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3063
13 WHEREAS, Kaspyen's love for cartoons brought him endless wonder, and his hours spent
14 mastering tummy time stand as further testament to his perseverance as well as the boundless
15 love and care demonstrated by his mother and father; and
16 WHEREAS, his infectious smile lit up every room, and he found delight in gazing at himself
17 in the mirror, fascinated by the person he was becoming; and
18 WHEREAS, although his time on earth was brief, Kaspyen's spirit and joyful presence will
19 forever live on in the hearts of those who knew and loved him; and
20 WHEREAS, he is cherished in memory by his parents, Mr. James Hayden Tidwell and Mrs.
21 Olivia Marie Krantz; grandparents, Donald and Darlene Krantz and Heather and Darrell
22 Krantz; aunts, Vivian Rehahn, Malayu Palmer, and Shiria Palmer; uncle, AJ Palmer; cousins,
23 Jamir Palmer, Jadie Palmer, Jayden Palmer, and Bucky Palmer; and godmother, La'Trecia
24 Cuffie.
25 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
26 the members of this body honor the life and memory of Kaspyen Rhys Tidwell-Krantz and
27 express their deepest and most sincere regret at his passing.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
30 family of Kaspyen Rhys Tidwell-Krantz.
H. R. 729
