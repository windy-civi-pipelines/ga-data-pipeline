2 2024 Class 1A/Division 1 State Cross Country Championship; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Bleckley County High School girls' cross country team has established and

6 WHEREAS, this superlative team has completed another triumphant season of cross country

15 WHEREAS, this championship team included four All-State runners: Ella Gibbs, 3-time

24 extend best wishes for continued success.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
26 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
27 Bleckley County High School girls' cross country team.
