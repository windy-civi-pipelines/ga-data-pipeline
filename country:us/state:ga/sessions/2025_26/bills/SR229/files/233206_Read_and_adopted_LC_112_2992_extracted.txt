25 LC 112 2992
Senate Resolution 229
Dixon of the 45th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Rudy Bowen for his service on the Georgia Department of
2 Transportation Board; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Rudy Bowen has demonstrated a lifelong commitment to service, leadership,
4 and community development, earning him the distinguished honor of being named the 2024
5 Gwinnett Citizen of the Year by the Gwinnett Chamber of Commerce; and
6 WHEREAS, a native of Atlanta, he and his wife, Jean Kirby, purchased their first home in
7 Gwinnett County in 1964; and
8 WHEREAS, a former lumber salesman, he founded Bowen Family Homes in 1969, and over
9 the next five years, he constructed an average of 50 homes a year, earning his reputation as
10 a builder of high-quality mid-range houses; and
11 WHEREAS, his contributions to the county extend beyond homebuilding and include service
12 on boards such as the Lake Lanier Recreational Authority, the Gwinnett Airport Authority,
13 the Gwinnett Board of Appeals, and the Johns Creek Hospital Board in addition to his role
14 as a deacon at Johns Creek Baptist Church; and
S. R. 229
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2992
15 WHEREAS, in 2007, he was elected to the Georgia Department of Transportation Board,
16 where he provided steady leadership through unprecedented times, ensuring that critical
17 roadwork and maintenance projects remained on or ahead of schedule, and twice served as
18 chairman during his 19-year-long tenure; and
19 WHEREAS, his dedication to both his family and his business has fostered a
20 multigenerational legacy, with his son, David, and sons-in-law, Mike Phelps and Tip Cape,
21 carrying on his homebuilding tradition, followed by his grandchildren who have also joined
22 the company; and
23 WHEREAS, Bowen Family Homes continues to construct entire communities and provide
24 customers with a total home buying experience to this day; and
25 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable individual be
26 appropriately honored for his enduring contributions to Gwinnett County.
27 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
28 recognize and commend Rudy Bowen for his service on the Georgia Department of
29 Transportation Board as well as extend best wishes for future health and happiness.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
31 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Rudy Bowen.
