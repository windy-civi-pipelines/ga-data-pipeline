25 LC 129 0316
House Resolution 645
61st, and Bell of the 75th
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Dora Din Whittley and recognizing March 20, 2025, as TrailBlazing Women
2 in Georgia Day; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day acknowledges the philanthropic, social,
4 and cultural contributions of women in the state; and
5 WHEREAS, Dora Din Whittley has long been recognized for the vital role that she has
6 played in leadership and her deep personal commitment to the welfare of the citizens of
7 Georgia; and
8 WHEREAS, she is a distinguished brand strategist, business advisor, and executive leader
9 with a proven track record of driving growth, visibility, and strategic partnerships for
10 emerging and established brands; and
11 WHEREAS, as the founder and co-principal of The Whittley Agency, Dora specializes in
12 brand amplification, corporate strategy, and market expansion, helping businesses navigate
13 high-growth transitions while building sustainable brand equity; and
H. R. 645
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0316
14 WHEREAS, her leadership and impact in business and branding have earned her numerous
15 accolades, including recognition as a 40 Under 40 Award Recipient by Upscale Magazine;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, beyond her work in brand strategy, Dora is passionate about economic
18 empowerment, leadership development, and fostering diverse business ecosystems; and
19 WHEREAS, she is a person of magnanimous strengths with an unimpeachable reputation for
20 integrity, intelligence, fairness, and kindness; and
21 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding contributions of this
22 remarkable individual are appropriately honored.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
24 the members of this body commend Dora Din Whittley for her efficient, effective, unselfish,
25 and dedicated public service to the State of Georgia; recognize March, 20, 2025, as
26 TrailBlazing Women in Georgia Day; and extend the most sincere best wishes for continued
27 health and happiness.
28 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
29 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Dora
30 Din Whittley.
