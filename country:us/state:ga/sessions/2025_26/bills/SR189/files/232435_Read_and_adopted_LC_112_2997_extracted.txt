25 LC 112 2997
Senate Resolution 189
8th, Still of the 48th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Commending Mercer University and recognizing February 20, 2025, as Mercer University
2 Day at the state capitol; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Mercer University was founded in 1833 and has provided 192 years of
4 educational service in Georgia; and
5 WHEREAS, the university moved its campus to Macon in 1871; and
6 WHEREAS, Mercer University is a dynamic and comprehensive center of undergraduate,
7 graduate, and professional education; and
8 WHEREAS, the university enrolls more than 9,200 students across 12 schools and colleges,
9 offering programs in liberal arts and sciences, law, pharmacy, medicine, health professions,
10 business, engineering, education, theology, music, nursing, and professional advancement;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, Mercer University's mission is "To teach, to learn, to create, to discover, to
13 inspire, to empower, and to serve"; and
S. R. 189
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2997
14 WHEREAS, the university challenges its community members to meet and exceed high
15 standards in teaching, learning, research, scholarship, and service; and
16 WHEREAS, Mercer University strives to change the world one student at a time through
17 various domestic and international programs; and
18 WHEREAS, the university has made a significant cultural impact in Macon and the
19 surrounding Middle Georgia area; and
20 WHEREAS, Mercer University's alumni have made substantial contributions to society and
21 culture across a wide range of fields.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
23 commend Mercer University as well as its administration, faculty, staff, trustees, students,
24 and alumni for the important contributions they have made to the State of Georgia and
25 recognize February 20, 2025, as Mercer University Day at the state capitol.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
27 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Mercer University.
