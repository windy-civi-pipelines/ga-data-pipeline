25 LC 112 3565
Senate Resolution 506
44th
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating Sarah Paisley Owen; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, the Midtown High School girls swim relay team has established and maintained
3 a proud tradition of excellence with equal emphases on character and academics; and
4 WHEREAS, the Midtown High School girls swim relay team brought special recognition to
5 its school and to Fulton County by winning first place in the 400 Free Relay, breaking the
6 school record by an impressive seven seconds with a time of 3:31.75; and
7 WHEREAS, this record-breaking relay team included the exceptional talents of Rhea
8 El-Deiry, Dabney Bond, Katya Tumanova, and Sarah Paisley Owen; and
9 WHEREAS, as additional testament to her exemplary performance, Sarah Paisley Owen
10 placed first in both the 50 Free and 100 Free events, breaking the state record in the 100 Free
11 and now holding state records in both events; and
12 WHEREAS, the phenomenal performance of the team is a clear indication of the outstanding
13 athletic ability, dedication, hard work, and unity of purpose and spirit of each member of the
14 team; and
S. R. 506
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3565
15 WHEREAS, as this magnificent team earned its well-deserved reputation for excellence by
16 performing with courage and precision over months of training and competition, it is
17 abundantly proper to call them champions.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
19 congratulate Sarah Paisley Owen for claiming first place in the 400 Free Relay and for her
20 individual achievements in the 50 Free and 100 Free events as well as extend best wishes for
21 continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
23 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Sarah Paisley
24 Owen.
