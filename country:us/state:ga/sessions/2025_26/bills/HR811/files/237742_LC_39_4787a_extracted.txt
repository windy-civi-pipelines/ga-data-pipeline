15 WHEREAS, during his over five decades of leadership at Connor's Temple Baptist Church,

21 Relations, a WSOK Image Award, and a Fannie Lou Hamer Award; and

23 be recognized appropriately by dedicating a bridge in his memory.
24 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AND ENACTED BY THE GENERAL
25 ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA that the bridge on State Route 405 over State Route 204 in
26 Chatham County is dedicated as the Rev. Dr. Bennie R. Mitchell, Jr. Memorial Bridge.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation is authorized and
28 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs dedicating the Rev. Dr. Bennie R. Mitchell,
29 Jr. Memorial Bridge.
30 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
31 and directed to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the
32 family of Reverend Dr. Bennie R. Mitchell, Jr., and to the Department of Transportation.
