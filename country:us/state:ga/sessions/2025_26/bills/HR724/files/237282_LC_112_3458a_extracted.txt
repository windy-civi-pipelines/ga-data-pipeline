25 LC 112 3458
House Resolution 724
of the 110th, Lim of the 98th, and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending the Historic Norcross Business Association; and for other
2 purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Historic Norcross Business Association (HNBA), a 501(c)(3) organization,
4 was formally established in March 2019 and has since become a pivotal force in fostering
5 the prosperity and growth of Norcross' Historic District by amplifying the collective voice
6 of local businesses; and
7 WHEREAS, the HNBA represents the common business interests of the Historic District,
8 which is composed of local, family-owned businesses that preserve and build upon the city's
9 rich history, ensuring the district's cultural and economic vitality; and
10 WHEREAS, the HNBA actively engages with the Norcross City Council, the Downtown
11 Development Authority, and city officials to promote collaboration and strategic
12 development efforts that support the district's continued success; and
13 WHEREAS, the HNBA is dedicated to strengthening the community by organizing and
14 hosting numerous free, family-friendly events throughout the year, including Irish Fest,
15 Witches Night Out, and the Victorian Christmas Village; and
H. R. 724
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3458
16 WHEREAS, recognizing the importance of a unified and strategic online presence, the
17 HNBA has branded itself as "The Heart of Norcross," creating an online hub through
18 theheartofnorcross.com and social media platforms to promote local businesses and
19 community events; and
20 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable association be
21 appropriately honored during this special time.
22 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that
23 the members of this body recognize and commend the Historic Norcross Business
24 Association for its outstanding contributions to the Historic District of Norcross, its
25 dedication to business advocacy, and its unwavering commitment to community engagement
26 as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized
28 and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to the
29 Historic Norcross Business Association.
