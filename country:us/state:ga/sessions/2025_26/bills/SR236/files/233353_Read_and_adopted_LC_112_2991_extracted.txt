25 LC 112 2991
Senate Resolution 236
Kemp of the 38th
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Sam M. Bromberg; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Sam M. Bromberg recently retired from a long career in public accounting and
3 personal financial planning, marking a significant milestone in his distinguished career; and
4 WHEREAS, he began his career in the accounting industry after earning a degree in
5 accounting from the University of Georgia; and
6 WHEREAS, upon obtaining his degree, Sam M. Bromberg joined the firm of Bates, Betts
7 & Carter, where he gained valuable experience in the field; and
8 WHEREAS, he continued his accounting career with the firm of Metcalf, Frix & Company,
9 where he served as a partner; and
10 WHEREAS, he later embarked on an entrepreneurial journey to co-found Bromberg &
11 Cohen, PC, with his partner, Jeffrey Cohen, which provided accounting and tax services to
12 individuals in Atlanta and the surrounding areas; and
S. R. 236
- 1 -

13 WHEREAS, he embarked on another entrepreneurial journey by co-founding Nova Wealth
14 Management Group, LLC, offering personal financial planning services to individuals in
15 Atlanta and the surrounding areas; and
16 WHEREAS, he later sold this business to Buckingham Strategic Wealth, ensuring that the
17 individuals he served would continue to receive financial guidance for years to come; and
18 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that the outstanding accomplishments of Sam
19 M. Bromberg be appropriately honored.
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
21 commend Sam M. Bromberg, CPA/PFS, for his outstanding service and extend best wishes
22 for his continued success in all future endeavors.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Sam M.
25 Bromberg.
