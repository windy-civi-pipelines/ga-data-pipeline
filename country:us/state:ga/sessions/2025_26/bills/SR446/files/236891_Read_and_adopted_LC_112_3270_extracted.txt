25 LC 112 3270
Senate Resolution 446
Wicks of the 34th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Honoring the exceptional service and sacrifices of veterans and recognizing November 2025
2 as Veterans Month; and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, Veterans Day, originally known as Armistice Day, marks the end of World War
4 I and the signing of the Armistice agreement between Germany and the Allies on November
5 11, 1918, at 11:00 AM; and
6 WHEREAS, in 1954, President Dwight D. Eisenhower renamed Armistice Day as Veterans
7 Day to honor all veterans of the United States; and
8 WHEREAS, veterans have made immeasurable contributions toward the defense of this
9 nation, as brave soldiers, sailors, airmen, and marines have risked their lives to protect the
10 freedoms that all Americans hold dear; and
11 WHEREAS, in addition to physical injuries, many veterans experience psychological and
12 emotional trauma that requires ongoing care and support; and
S. R. 446
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3270
13 WHEREAS, while veterans are formally honored on Veterans Day, dedicating the entire
14 month of November to their recognition will increase awareness of the hardships veterans
15 endure both during and after combat; and
16 WHEREAS, the observance of Veterans Month will provide an opportunity to show respect,
17 gratitude, and dedication to our veterans.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
19 honor the exceptional service and sacrifices of veterans, recognize November 2025 as
20 Veterans Month, and encourage all Georgians to engage in celebratory activities and
21 programs aimed at recognizing and supporting veterans.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
23 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to the public, veterans
24 organizations, and the press.
S. R. 446
