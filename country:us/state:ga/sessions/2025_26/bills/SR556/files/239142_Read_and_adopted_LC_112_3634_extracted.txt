25 LC 112 3634
Senate Resolution 556
By: Senator Mallow of the 2nd
A RESOLUTION
2 WHEREAS, Bishop Pierce Moore Jr. has demonstrated his commitment to teaching the
3 Gospel, witnessing Christ through word and deed, and addressing the physical,
4 psychological, and spiritual needs of others; and
5 WHEREAS, Bishop Pierce Moore Jr. was called to serve at Savannah Deliverance Center
6 of the Evangelistic Centers of Georgia Inc., where he has pastored for more than 16 years;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, Savannah Deliverance Center is the headquarters church for Deliverance
9 Evangelistic Centers of Georgia Inc., which was founded in 1965; and
10 WHEREAS, with the support of his wife, Minister Barbara N. Moore, First Lady and District
11 Mother, this spiritual team has provided guidance and leadership for the worshipers of
12 Savannah Deliverance Center, which has continued to grow and expand its outreach to
13 worshipers across the coastal region; and
S. R. 556
- 1 -
25 LC 112 3634
14 WHEREAS, a religious mentor who is dedicated to the awareness and spiritual development
15 of all people, Bishop Moore is a highly respected spiritual leader and shining example to
16 others to follow God's righteous path; and
17 WHEREAS, this accomplished community servant has received many awards, citations, and
18 commendations for his life's work, and it is abundantly fitting and proper that this enduring
19 Messenger of God be appropriately honored on the occasion of his 16th Pastoral
20 Anniversary.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
22 recognize and commend Pastor and Overseer Bishop Pierce Moore Jr. for his many good
23 works to improve the spiritual growth of his church and the surrounding community.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
25 to make appropriate copies of this resolution available for distribution to Bishop Pierce
26 Moore Jr. and the Savannah Deliverance Center family.
