25 LC 112 2761
Senate Resolution 89
22nd, Harrell of the 40th and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Congratulating and commending Ms. Patria Brown; and for other purposes.
2 WHEREAS, Ms. Patria Brown is the state recipient of the Technical College System of
3 Georgia 2024 Exceptional Adult Georgian in Literacy Education (EAGLE) award, which
4 recognizes adult learners in pursuit of excellence; and
5 WHEREAS, EAGLE celebrates and recognizes adult learners from across the state who have
6 demonstrated exceptional achievement in adult education classes; and
7 WHEREAS, for 31 years, the EAGLE program has created a greater awareness of adult
8 education opportunities available in local communities across the state and has fostered
9 involvement in lifelong learning pursuits; and
10 WHEREAS, Ms. Brown was selected from among 25 EAGLE delegates who have
11 demonstrated excellent academic achievements within their local adult education programs;
12 and
S. R. 89
- 1 -
25 LC 112 2761
13 WHEREAS, Ms. Brown will serve as an ambassador for adult education in Georgia,
14 representing nearly 22,000 students who benefit from adult education programs throughout
15 the state; and
16 WHEREAS, in pursuing her education, Ms. Brown has brought honor to herself, her family,
17 the Cobb County School District, and the entire Technical College System of Georgia, which
18 she represents as Georgia's exceptional adult education student for 2024.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 congratulate and commend Ms. Patria Brown for her exceptional achievements and efforts
21 to promote adult education in Georgia as well as extend best wishes for continued success.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
23 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Ms. Patria Brown.
