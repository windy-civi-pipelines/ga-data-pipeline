Section 1:
25 LC 129 0459
Senate Resolution 518
of the 55th, Mallow of the 2nd and others
A RESOLUTION
1 Recognizing and commending Ascend Church on the occasion of its third anniversary; and
2 for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the seeds which were sown by the loving and humble efforts of the devoted
4 original worshipers of Ascend Church have blossomed, establishing a rich tradition and
5 history; and
6 WHEREAS, Ascend Church has grown under the divine direction of Pastor Brian Guerin,
7 allowing the Word of God to spread throughout the community; and
8 WHEREAS, the congregation of this church has compiled an unparalleled record of
9 constructive, compassionate, and humanitarian service which it demonstrates daily through
10 open minds, open hearts, and open doors; and
11 WHEREAS, the members of Ascend Church have a sincere desire to serve the Lord and are
12 wholeheartedly committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ; and
S. R. 518
- 1 -
25 LC 129 0459
13 WHEREAS, the story of Ascend Church is one of triumph through the grace and sustaining
14 power of God, and it stands as an immovable witness to the truth that God keeps His
15 promise; and
16 WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this enduring example of God's message
17 of peace and love be recognized and congratulated for its innumerable good works rendered
18 on behalf of persons in need.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE that the members of this body
20 recognize the rich history of Ascend Church, commend its three years of dedicated service
21 to the Lord and community, and extend their warmest best wishes for its continued growth
22 and success.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is authorized and directed
24 to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Ascend Church.
S. R. 518
