Section 1:
25 LC 57 0184
By: Representatives O’Steen of the 169th, Burchett of the 176th, Campbell of the 171st,
Corbett of the 174th, and LaHood of the 175th
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
1 To amend Chapter 2 of Title 31 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to the
2 Department of Community Health, so as to provide for a grant program for the purpose of
3 acquiring and installing backup generators; to provide that the department shall consult with
4 the Georgia Emergency Management and Homeland Security Agency in establishing grant
5 criteria; to provide for the department to establish grant amounts; to provide for a definition;
6 to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA:
8 SECTION 1.
9 Chapter 2 of Title 31 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to the Department
10 of Community Health, is amended by adding a new Code section to read as follows:
11 "31-2-20.
12 (a) As used in this Code section, the term 'critical access hospital' shall have the same
13 meaning as provided in Code Section 31-8-9.1.
14 (b) Subject to appropriations by the General Assembly or other available funding, the
15 department shall establish a grant program to provide funding to critical access hospitals
16 for the purpose of acquiring and installing backup generators. The department, in
H. B. 262

17 consultation with the director of emergency management and homeland security shall
18 establish grant criteria and make award decisions; provided, however, that the department
19 and the director of emergency management and homeland security shall, to the greatest
20 extent possible, prioritize the approval of applications of those critical access hospitals
21 lacking a backup generator. The department shall be authorized to establish the grant
22 amount awarded to each recipient on a case-by-case basis."
23 SECTION 2.
24 All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
