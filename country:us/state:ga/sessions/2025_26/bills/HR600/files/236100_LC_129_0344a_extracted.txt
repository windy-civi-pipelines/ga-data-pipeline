2 and for other purposes.
3 WHEREAS, the Georgia Latino Film Alliance has been supporting, educating, and
4 empowering Latinos in film, entertainment, and arts since 2011; and
5 WHEREAS, the alliance ensures that the Latino community has a dedicated space in Georgia
6 where Latino film executives, directors, and artists are celebrated; and
7 WHEREAS, the Georgia Latino Film Alliance is a conduit to Georgia Latino International
